John Chapter 14

1“Don’t worry. You believe in God; believe in me too. 2There are many rooms in my Father’s house. If it weren’t so, I would’ve told you, because I’m going to get a place ready for you. 3If I go to do that, I’ll come back and get you so you can be where I am. 4You know the way to get to where I am.”

5Thomas said, “We don’t know where you’re going; how can we know the way to get there?” 6“I am the way, the truth, and the life. Nobody comes to the Father except through me. 7If you know me, you know my Father too. From now on you know him and have seen him.”

8Philip said, “Lord, show us the Father, that will be enough.” 9“Have I been with youpl this long, Philip, and yousg haven’t seen me?” Whoever has seen me has seen the Father. How can yousg say, ‘Show us the Father’? 10Don’t yousg believe I’m in the Father and the Father’s in me? What I say I don’t say on my own, but the Father present in me does his work. 11Believepl that I’m in the Father and the Father’s in me. If not, believepl because of the works themselves. 12Whoever believes in me will do what I do and more, because I’m going to the Father. 13I’ll do whatever you ask in my name so I may glorify the Father. 14I repeat, if you ask anything in my name, I’ll do it. 15“If you love me, you’ll keep my commandments. 16I’ll ask the Father to give you another Helper to be with you permanently, 17the Spirit of truth that the world can’t receive, because they don’t see him or know him. But you know him, because he stays with you and will be in you. 18I won’t leave you orphans; I’ll come to you. 19In a little while, the world won’t see me, but you’ll see me; you’ll live because I live. 20Then you’ll know I’m in the Father and you’re in me and I’m in you. 21Whoever has my commandments and keeps them is the one that loves me. Whoever loves me my Father will love, and I’ll love him and show myself to him.”

2Judas (not Iscariot) said, “Lord, what has happened that you’re going to show yourself to us but not to the world?”

3Jesus answered, “If a person loves me, he’ll keep my word, and my Father will love him. We’ll come and make our home in him. 24Whoever does not love me does not keep my words. The word you hear is not mine, but the Father’s. 25“I’ve said this while I’m with you. 26The Helper—the Holy Spirit the Father will send in my name—will teach you everything and remind you of everything I’ve told you. 27I leave you peace; I give you my peace, not the kind the world gives. Don’t worry or be afraid. 28You heard me say, ‘I’m going away and I’ll return to you.’ If you loved me, you would be happy that I’m going to the Father because the Father is greater than I am. 29“I’ve told you now before it happens so when it does happen, you’ll believe. 30Many things I won’t tell you now, because the ruler of this world is coming, and he doesn’t have anything to do with me. 31But that the world may know I love the Father, I’m doing what he commanded me. “Let’s go.” `

Jn 14:1-31
