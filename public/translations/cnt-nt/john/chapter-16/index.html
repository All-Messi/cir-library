John Chapter 16

1“I’ve told you this so you won’t get caught off guard. 2They’ll excommunicate you from the synagogues, but—more than that—the time’s coming when anybody that kills you will think he’s serving God. 3They’ll do it because they don’tknow the Father or me. So when it happens, remember that I told you it would. 4“I didn’t tell you these things at first, because I was with you. 5But now I’m going to the One that sent me, and none of you needs to ask, ‘Where are you going?’ 6Because I’ve told you, sorrow has filled your heart. 7But I tell you the truth, it’s to your benefit that I go away. If I don’t, the Helper won’t be coming to you. If I go, I’ll send him. 8When he comes, he’ll convict the world of sin and righteousness and judgment—9of sin, because they don’t believe in me; 10of righteousness, because I’m going to the Father and you won’t see me anymore; 11and of judgment, because the ruler of this world has been judged. 12“I still have so much to tell you, but you can’t deal with it now. 13But when the Spirit of truth comes, he’ll lead you into all truth. He won’t speak on his own, but will tell you what he has heard, and will inform you about what is coming. 14He’ll honor me because he’ll take from me to tell you. 15Everything the Father has is mine. That’s why I’ve said the Spirit will take from me to tell you. 16“In a little while you won’t see me; and again in a little while you’ll see me.”

7Some disciples said to one another, “What’s this, ‘In a little while you won’t see me; again in a little while you’ll see me’; and, ‘because I’m going to the Father’? 18What’s this ‘little while’? We don’t understand.

19Jesus knew they wanted to ask him, “Are you talking about what I said, ‘In a little while you won’t see me; again in a little while you’ll see me’? 20You’ll mourn, but the world will be glad. You’ll grieve, but your grief will turn to joy. 21When a woman’s in labor, she has grief; but when she has the child, she no longer remembers the anguish because of the joy of bringing a person has been born into the world. 22You’ll grieve, but I’ll see you again, and you’ll be glad. Nobody will take your gladness from you. 23In that day you won’t ask me for anything. Whatever you ask the Father in my name now he’ll give you. 24Up till now you haven’t asked for anything in my name. Ask and you’ll receive so your joy will be complete. 25“I’ve been using illustrations. The time’s coming when I’ll speak plainly about the Father. 26Then you’ll ask in my name; and, rest assured, I’ll ask the Father for you. 27The Father himself loves you because you’ve loved me and have believed I came from him. 28I came from him into the world. Again, I’m leaving the world and going back to him.”

9The disciples said, “Now you’re speaking clearly. 30Now we know that you know everything and don’t need anybody to ask you. By this we believe you’ve come from God.”

1Jesus responded, “Do you believe now? 32The time’s coming—and is almost here—when each of you will scatter to your own things and leave me alone. But I’m not alone, because the Father’s with me. 33I’ve told you these things so you’ll have peace. In the world you have trouble, but take heart, I’ve overcome the world.” Jn 16:1-33 The High Priestly Prayer
