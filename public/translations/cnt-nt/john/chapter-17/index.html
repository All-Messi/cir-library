John Chapter 17

1Jesus looked up to heaven, “Father, it’s time. Honor your Son so he can honor you. 2You gave him authority over everybody so he could give eternal life to everybody you’ve given him. 3Eternal life means knowing you, the only true God, and Jesus the Messiah, the One you sent. 4I’ve honored you on the earth and completed the work you gave me to do. 5Now, honor me and yourself with the honor I had with you before the earth existed. 6“I’ve shown you to the ones you gave me out of the world, and they’ve kept your word. 7 Now they know that the message I’ve given them came from you, 8and they’ve received it. They know truly that I came from you, and believe you sent me. 9I’m not asking about the world, but about the ones you gave me. 10My things are yours and yours are mine, and I’ve been honored in them. 11I’m not in the world anymore, but they’re in the world and I’m coming to you. Holy Father, keep them in your name so they can be one like we are. 12When I was with them, I kept them in your name, and none of them has perished except the son of perdition—to fulfill scripture. 13I’m saying these things so they can fully experience my joy. 14The world hates them because they’re not of the world. 15I’m not asking you to take them out of it, but to keep them from the evil one. 16They’re not of the world as I’m not of the world. 17I’ve sanctified them with the truth—with your word. 18As you sent me into the world, I’ve sent them into the world. 19I’ve sanctified myself for them so the truth can sanctify them. 20“Father, I’m not asking just that these be one, but that those also be one who believe in me through their word, 21so the world can know you sent me. 22I’ve given them the honor you gave me so they can be one like us—23so the world may know you love them like you love me. 24I want them to be with me where I am so they can see the honor you gave me because you loved me before the the world existed. 25“Righteous Father, the world didn’t know you, but I knew you, and these know you sent me. 26I’ll continue to make you known to them so the love you loved me with may be in them as I am in them.”

Jn 17:1-26

Agony in the Garden; The Arrest; Peter’s First Denial
