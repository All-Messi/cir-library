John Chapter 15

1“I’m the true vine; my Father is the vinedresser. 2He takes out every branch in me that doesn’t bear fruit. Every branch that does bear fruit he prunes so it’ll bear more fruit. 3Already you are pruned by the word I’ve spoken to you. 4Remain in me and I in you. A branch can’t bear fruit by itself unless it remains in the vine; neither can you unless you remain in me. 5I’m the vine; you’re branches. Whoever remains in me and I in him bears lots of fruit. Without me you can’t do anything. 6f anybody does not remain in me, he’s taken out like a branch. It dries up, and they gather it up and throw it in the fire and it’s burned up. 7 If you remain in me and my words remain in you, ask for whatever you want and you’ll receive it. 8This is what honors my Father: your bearing lots of fruit and becoming my disciples. 9As the Father has loved me, I’ve loved you. Remain in my love. 10If you keep my commandments, you’ll be remaining in my love as I’ve kept my Father’s commandments and remain in his love. 11“I’ve said this so you can have the complete joy I have. 12This is my commandment: love one another as I’ve loved you. 13Nobody has greater love than this: to lay down his life for his friends. 14You’re my friends if you do what I tell you. 15I don’t call you servants anymore, because a servant doesn’t know what his master’s doing. I call you friends because I tell you everything I’ve heard from my Father. 16You didn’t choose me; I chose you and appointed you to go and bear fruit that lasts, so my Father will give you whatever you ask for in my name. 17I command you these things so you’ll love one another. 18“If the world hates you, understand that it hated me first. 19If you were like the world, it would love its own kind. But because you’re not like the world, it hates you—but I’ve chosen you out of the world. 20Remember what I told you, ‘A servant’s not better off than his master.’ If they persecuted me, they’ll persecute you. If they kept my word, they’ll keep yours. 21They’ll do this to you because of me, because they don’t know the One that sent me. 22“If I hadn’t come and spoken to them, they wouldn’t have any sin; but now they have no excuse for their sin. 23Whoever hates me also hates my Father. 24If I hadn’t done among them what no one else has done, they wouldn’t have any sin; but now they’ve seen and have hated both me and my Father. 25But, they’ve done this in fulfillment of what’s written in their law, ‘They hated me for no reason.’ 26“When the Helper comes that I’ll send from the Father—the Spirit of truth who proceeds from him, he’ll bear witness of me. 27You’ll bear witness too, because you’ve been with me from the beginning.

Jn 15:1-27
