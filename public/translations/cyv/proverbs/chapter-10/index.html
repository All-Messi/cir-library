Proverbs Chapter 10

1Proverbs of Solomon A wise son makes a happy father, but a foolish one grieves his mother.

2Ill-gotten gain doesn’t gain you anything, but goodness delivers you from death.

3Yahveh won’t let good people go hungry; he thwarts what the wicked want.

4A lazy hand makes for poverty; a diligent one produces wealth.

5They who gather crops in the summer are smart; those who sleep at harvest time are shameful.

6Blessings come to the good, but the sinful harbor violent intentions.

Prov 10:1-6

7Good people leave blessed memories behind, but sinful people’s names rot away.

8The wise in heart accept commands, but babbling fools come to ruin.

9If you live with integrity, you’ll live with confidence, but people soon find out if you’re crooked.

10The one that winks causes trouble; babbling fools come to ruin.

11Good people’s words are life-giving fountains; What sinners harbor discord.

12Hatred stirs up conflict, but love atones for all sins.

13Wisdom comes from people that have insight; but a rod is for the back of fools.

14The wise store up knowledge, but ruin is at hand for foolish people.

Prov 10:7-14

15For the rich, wealth is a fortress; For the poor, poverty is their ruin.

16Life is good people’s pay; punishment is bad people’s payback.

17Listening to instruction puts you on the route to life; abandoning correction gets you lost.

18Hiding hatred makes you a liar; spreading slander makes you a fool.

19Talking too much makes you likely to sin; so be wise and limit what you say.

20The words of good people are like fine silver; what sinners say isn’t worth repeating.

Prov 10:15-20

21What good people say feeds many; foolish people starve to death from ignorance.

22Yahveh’s blessing makes you rich, and he doesn’t add sorrow to it.

23Sin is fun for fools; so is wisdom for the wise.

24What sinners fear tends happen; what good people want they tend to receive.

25After a windstorm passes, sinners have been blown away, but the godly have a firm foundation.

26To those who hire them, lazy people irritate like vinegar on teeth and smoke in the eyes.

Prov 10:21-26

27Respecting Yahveh lengthens your life, sinfulness cuts it short.

28Good people’s hopes result in happiness, but the expectations of the sinful fade away.

29Yahveh’s way is your fortress if you’re godly, but it destroys sinners.

30Good people won’t be displaced, but the sinful will be expelled from their land.

31The mouth of good people overflows with wisdom, but the perverse tongue of the wicked will be cut off.

32The good say what’s acceptable, but the sinful say perverse things.

Prov 10:27-32
