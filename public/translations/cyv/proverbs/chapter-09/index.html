Proverbs Chapter 9

1 Wisdom has built her house; She’s carved its seven columns.

2 She’s done her butchering, mixed her wine, and set her table.

3 She’s sent her maids to call out from the heights of the city: 4"Whoever is naïve, turn in here!" She says to the one that lacks good judgment, 5"Come in and eat my food; drink the wine I’ve mixed.

6 Abandon your foolish ways and start having a life; conduct yourself with good judgment."

7 If you correct a scoffer, you’ll be abused; if you reprove sinners, you’ll get insulted.

8 Don’t reprove scoffers; they’ll hate you. reprove the wise, and they’ll love you.

9 Instruct the wise, and they’ll be wiser still; teach the good, and they’ll be better yet.

10 Respect for Yahveh is basic to wisdom; knowing the Holy One clarifies your thinking.

11 It’ll multiply your days and add years to your life.

12 If you’re wise, you’re wise for yourself; if you scoff at wisdom, it’ll be to your detriment.

13 A foolish woman is loud; she’s naïve and ignorant.

14 She sits in the doorway of her house or takes a seat on the high places in town.

15 She calls out to those who are going by, who are following straight paths: 16"Whoever is naïve, turn in here," she claims to the one that doesn’t know any better. 17"Stolen water is refreshing; bread eaten in secret tastes good."

18 But they don’t know that the dead are there, that her guests lie in Sheol.
