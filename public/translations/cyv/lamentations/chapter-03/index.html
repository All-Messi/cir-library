Lamentations Chapter 3

1I have seen suffering under the rod God used in his anger.

2He has driven me out and made me walk in the dark;

3he has turned his hand against me repeatedly all day.

4He made my skin and body waste away, and broke my bones.

Lam 3:1-4

5He besieged me with bitterness and hardship.

6He made me live in dark places like the dead from long ago.

7He walled me in so I couldn’t escape and put heavy chains on me.

8Even when I call for help, he shuts out my prayer.

Lam 3:5-8

9He has blocked my road with hewn stones and made my paths crooked.

0He’s like a bear lying in wait for me, like a lion in unexpected places.

1He dragged me off the road, tore me to pieces; and made me desolate.

2He bent his bow and made me a target for his arrow;

Lam 3:9-12

13he shot deep into me the arrows from his quiver.

4I’m a laughingstock to my people, mocked in musical ditties all day.

5He filled me with bitterness and made me drink wormwood.

6He cracked my teeth with gravel and made me grovel in the dust.

Lam 3:13-16

7I’ve been set apart from peace and forget what happiness is like.

8So I say, “My strength is gone, and so is my hope in Yahveh.”

9Remember my suffering and homelessness.

0I certainly remember and am humbled within.

Lam 3:17-20

1I recall this and have hope from it:

22Yahveh love never stops coming; his mercies don’t end.

23His faithfulness is great; it’s renewed every morning.

24I say to myself, “Yahveh is my portion; so I have hope in him.”

Lam 3:21-24

25Yahveh is good to those who depend on him, people that look for him.

26They do well to wait silently for his salvation.

27It’s good for people to bear the yoke when they’re young,

28to sit alone in silence, since God has laid it on them,

29to put their mouth in the dust—maybe there’s hope,

30to turn their cheek to their abuser, to be filled with disgrace;

31because Yahveh won’t reject forever.

Lam 3:25-31

32If he causes grief, he’ll have mercy according to his enduring love;

33because he doesn’t willingly hurt people or grieve them. 34-36God doesn’t approve of crushing every prisoner in the land under his feet, of depriving people of justice in the presence of the Most High, of defrauding people in his lawsuit.

37Who is there that speaks and it happens unless the Lord has commanded it?

38Don’t both good and bad come from the Most High?

Lam 3:32-38

9Why should we complain in light of our sins?

0Let’s examine our ways and return to Yahveh.

1Let’s lift up our heart and hands toward God in heaven;

42we’ve sinned and rebelled; you haven’t pardoned us.

3You’ve covered yourself with anger and chased us; you’ve slain without pity.

4You’ve wrapped yourself in a cloud so no prayer can get to you.

Lam 3:39-44

5You’ve made us residue and refuse among the nations.

6Our enemies rail out against us.

7Panic and pitfall descend on us, devastation and destruction.

8My eyes run with streams of tears because my people are being destroyed.

9My eyes will keep pouring out

50till Yahveh looks down from heaven and sees.

1My eyes bring pain to my soul because of all the residents in my city.

2For no reason my enemies hunted me down like a bird;

53they’ve silenced me in the pit, and put a stone over me;

54water closed over my head; I said, “I’m lost.”

55I called to you, Yahveh, from down in the pit.

Lam 3:45-55

56You heard my plea, and didn’t hide from my prayer for relief, my call for help.

7You came and said, “Don’t be afraid!”

8Lord, you pled my case and redeemed my life.

9Yahveh, you’ve seen my oppression; judge my case.

0You’ve seen their revenge, their schemes against me.

1You’ve heard their taunts.

2My assailants’ whispers aim at me all day.

3Look at their sitting and rising; I’m what they mock in their song.

4Pay them back, Yahveh, according to what they’ve done;

65harden their heart so your curse will fall on them.

6Chase them in anger and destroy them from under the sky!

Lam 3:56-66

Yahveh’s Anger Satisfied
