Leviticus Chapter 3

1“If you’re offering a peace offering, offer from the herd a male or female without defect. 2Put your hand on its head and slaughter it at the entrance to the Tent of Meeting. The sons of Aaron will sprinkle its blood around on the altar. 3From the sacrifice the priest will present an offering by fire to Yahveh: the fat around and on the entrails, 4the two kidneys in the loins and their fat, and the lobe of the liver that he’ll remove with the kidneys. 5The sons of Aaron will offer up in smoke the burnt offering that’s on the wood over the fire, an aroma pleasing to Yahveh.

Lev 3:1-5
