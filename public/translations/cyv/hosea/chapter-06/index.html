Hosea Chapter 6

1 They’ll say, ‘Let’s return to Yahveh. He has torn us, but he’ll heal us; he has wounded us, but he’ll bandage us up.

2 He’ll revive us in two days, and raise us up the third, so we can live in his presence.

3 So let’s press on to know Yahveh. He’ll respond to us as sure as dawn comes; he’ll come to us like rain, like the spring rain that waters the ground.’ 4"What am I going to do with you, Ephraim, or with you, Judah? Your loyalty is like a morning cloud, like dew that evaporates early.

5 I’ve cut both of you up in pieces by the prophets; I’ve slain you with my words; the condemnations on you are as inescapable as light.

6 I delight in loyalty more than sacrifice, in knowing me more than in burnt offerings.

7 Like Adam you’ve broken covenant and dealt deceitfully with me. 8"Gilead is a city of sinners, tracked with bloody footprints.

9 Like raiders waiting for victims, a band of priests commits murder on the way to Shechem; and every shameful crime imaginable.

10 I’ve seen a horrible thing in Israel: Ephraim’s prostitution, Israel’s self-defilement.

11 Judah, I’ve appointed a harvest time for you when I restore my people’s fortunes.
