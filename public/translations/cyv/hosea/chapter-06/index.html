Hosea Chapter 6

1“They’ll say, ‘Let’s return to Yahveh. He has torn us, but he’ll heal us; he has wounded us, but he’ll bandage us up.

2He’ll revive us in two days, and raise us up the third, so we can live in his presence.

3So let’s press on to know Yahveh. He’ll respond to us as sure as dawn comes; he’ll come to us like rain, like the spring rain that waters the ground.’

Hos 6:1-3

4“What am I going to do with you, Ephraim, or with you, Judah? Your loyalty is like a morning cloud, like dew that evaporates early.

5I’ve cut both of you up in pieces by the prophets; I’ve slain you with my words; the condemnations on you are as inescapable as light.

6I delight in loyalty more than sacrifice, in knowing me more than in burnt offerings.

7Like Adam you’ve broken covenant and dealt deceitfully with me. 8“Gilead is a city of sinners, tracked with bloody footprints.

9Like raiders waiting for victims, a band of priests commits murder on the way to Shechem; and every shameful crime imaginable.

10I’ve seen a horrible thing in Israel: Ephraim’s prostitution, Israel’s self-defilement.

11Judah, I’ve appointed a harvest time for you when I restore my people’s fortunes.

Hos 6:4-11
