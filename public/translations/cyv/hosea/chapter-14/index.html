Hosea Chapter 14

1 Israel, come back to Yahveh your God; you’ve stumbled because you sinned.

2 Come back to Yahveh and bring your confession with you. Say, “Take away our sin; receive us graciously so we can offer our praises to you.

3 Assyria can’t save us; neither can our warhorses. We won’t say ‘our god’ anymore to something we’ve made; only from you can we orphans receive mercy."

4 Yahveh says, “I’ll heal your apostasy and love you freely because my anger will be gone.

5 I’ll be like dew to you; you’ll blossom like a lily.

6 Your roots will spread out like the cedars in Lebanon.

7 Your people that live in your shade, will raise grain again, and blossom like grapevines. You’ll be as fragrant as Lebanon’s wine. 8"Ephraim, I don’t have any connection with idols. I directly am the One that answers your prayers and takes care of you. I’m like a green cypress; your fruit all comes from me."

9 Whoever is wise, understand— whoever perceives, be aware: the Lord’s way is the right way; good people follow it, but sinners trip over it.
