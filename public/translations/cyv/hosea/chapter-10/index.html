Hosea Chapter 10

1 Israel is a luxurious vine that grows his own fruit. The more his fruit, the more altars he makes; the better his land, the better the sacred pillars he sets up.

2 He’s been unfaithful; now he must pay for his guilt. Yahveh is going to tear down his altars and pull over his sacred pillars.

3 Then they’ll say, “We don’t have a king because we didn’t respect Yahveh; even if we had one, what could he do for us?"

4 They just talk; they make treaties they don’t intend to keep, so condemnations sprout up like noxious weeds in the ditches in their fields.

5 The residents in Samaria will fear for the calf idol at Beth-aven [Bethel]. Its people will mourn for it; its idolatrous priests will cry over its departed beauty.

6 The idol itself they will carry off to Assyria as tribute to their great king; Ephraim will be disgraced for depending on it.

7 Samaria and its king will float away like driftwood on the ocean tide.

8 The high places of Beth-aven, the place of Israel’s sin, will fall into ruin; thorns and thistles will grow up around their altars. Then they’ll call to the mountains, “Cover us!” and to the hills, “Fall on us!"

9 Yahveh says, “Ever since Gibeah, they’ve just kept on sinning! Wasn’t it right for war to break out against them?

10 When I so desire, I’ll attack those sinners; I’ll gather the nations’ armies against them to punish them for their extreme guilt. 11"Ephraim is a trained heifer that loves to tread out grain, and I used to spare her fair neck. But I’ll put a yoke on Ephraim; I’ll make Judah plow and Jacob harrow by himself.

12 Sow seeds of goodness; harvest a crop of lasting love. Break up your heart’s hardened, fallow ground; it’s time to look to Yahveh so he’ll come to shower goodness on you. 13"You’ve cultivated sinfulness and harvested injustice; you’ve eaten the produce of lies because you’ve trusted in going it on your own with your big army.

14 So the loud noise of war will arise among your people and destroy your fortresses the way Shalman destroyed Beth-arbel in battle, and mothers were dashed to pieces with their children.

15 That’s what’s going to happen to you, too, Bethel, because of your extreme sinfulness. Israel’s king will be killed at dawn. Yahveh’s Love for Israel
