Hosea Chapter 4

1Israel, listen to Yahveh’s message; he’s filing charges against you: “There’s no faithfulness, kindness, or awareness of God there.

2There’s swearing, deception, murder, stealing, and adultery; they use violence, so there’s murder after murder.

3So the land is in mourning. Everybody that lives in it is wasting away, along with the animals in the field and the birds in the sky; even the fish are disappearing.”

Hos 4:1-3

4“But don’t anybody point a finger at somebody else; I’m suing you priests.

5You’ll stumble in the daytime; prophets will stumble with you at night; I’ll destroy your mother Israel.

6My people are getting destroyed because they don’t know me. Since you priests have refused to know me, I’ll refuse to acknowledge you as my priests. Since you’ve forgotten about my Law, I’ll forget about your children.

7The more priests there are, the more they sin against me; I’ll turn their honor into shame. Hos 4:4-7 8“They feed on my people’s sin because their sacrifices for it mean more food for the priests!

9It will be, like people like priest; I’ll punish them for their ways, repay them for their deeds.

10They’ll eat, but not have enough; they’ll carry on prostitution, but won’t gain anything by it, because they’ve stopped listening to Yahveh.

11Prostitution, wine, and new wine have robbed my people of understanding.

Hos 4:8-11

12“My people consult their wooden idols; their fortunetellers toss sticks to give them oracles. A spirit of prostitution has led them astray; they’ve carried on prostitution by leaving their God.

13They offer sacrifices to idols on mountaintops and burn incense on hills under oaks, poplars, and terebinth trees, because they make pleasant shade. That’s why your daughters become prostitutes and your brides commit adultery.

14I won’t punish their daughters when they carry on prostitution or their brides when they commit adultery, because you men visit prostitutes and offer sacrifices with temple prostitutes; so you foolish people will be ruined.

Hos 4:1

2-14 15“Though you carry on prostitution, Israel, don’t draw Judah into your guilt. Don’t go to Gilgal or up to Beth-aven [Bethel]; don’t swear, ‘As sure as Yahveh lives!’

16Israel is like a stubborn heifer; so how can Yahveh feed it like a lamb in the pasture?

7Leave Ephraim alone because she carries on idolatry.

18When their rulers are done drinking, they go visit prostitutes; they have no shame whatsoever.

19The wind will carry them off; their altars will bring them disgrace.

Hos 4:15-19

Judgment Against Israel’s Leaders
