Ezekiel Chapter 12

1 A message from Yahveh came to me: 2"Son of man, you’re living with a rebellious people. They have eyes but don’t see, and ears but don’t hear. 3So get together baggage for exile and go into exile in the daytime so they can watch; go from one place to another. Maybe they’ll understand, though they’re a rebellious house. 4Bring your baggage out in the daytime. Then go out in the evening as if you’re going into exile. 5Make a hole in the wall so they can watch you go out through it. 6"Put the baggage on your shoulder and carry it out in the dark. Cover your face so you can’t see where you’re going. I’m using you as an object lesson for Israel."

7 So in the daytime I brought out my baggage like an exile. In the evening I made a hole in the wall with my hands. I went out in the dark and carried the baggage on my shoulder while they watched.

8 In the morning a message from Yahveh came to me: 9"Son of man, hasn’t the rebellious House of Israel asked you what you’re doing? 10Tell them, ‘The Lord Yahveh says, “This oracle is about the official in Jerusalem and the House of Israel that’s there.” 11I’m an object lesson for you. As I’ve done, you’ll do. you’ll go into exile.’ 12The official that’s among you will put his baggage on his shoulder in the dark and go out. He’ll make a hole in the wall to take it out. He’ll cover his face so he can’t see where he’s going. 13’I’ll throw my net over him and take him to Babylon among the Babylonians, but he won’t see it even though he’s going to die there. 14I’ll scatter in every direction his helpers and troops, and chase them with a sword. 15They’ll know I’m Yahveh when I scatter them among the countries. 16I’ll spare a few from sword, famine, and disease so they can confess their detestable acts to the nations where they go."

17 A message from Yahveh came to me: 18"Son of man, eat your bread cringing; drink your water shuddering with anxiety. 19Then tell the people the Lord Yahveh says, ‘The people in Jerusalem will eat their bread with anxiety and drink their water with alarm, because everybody living there will strip them violently. 20The populated towns will be laid waste; the land will be desolate. They’ll know I’m Yahveh.""

21 A message from Yahveh came to me: 22"Son of man, what’s this proverb you people have about Israel, ‘The days are long and every vision fails’? 23Tell them the Lord Yahveh says, ‘I’ll make this proverb stop being used. The time’s coming soon for fulfilling every vision. 24From now on, there won’t be false vision or flattering fortunetelling in Israel. 25I’ll speak and it’ll happen without delay. In your days, rebellious people, what I’ll say I’ll do,’"

26 A message from Yahveh came to me: 27"Son of man, Israel is saying, ‘The vision he sees is for years from now.’ 28Tell them, ‘None of what I’m saying will delay any longer. It will happen soon,’" the Lord Yahveh says. False Prophets Condemned
