Psalms Chapter 18

1 I love you, Yahveh, my strength;

2 my fortress, my deliverer, my God, my Rock, my Refuge; my shield, the place that saves me, my stronghold."

3 I called on him who deserves praise, and he saved me from my enemies.

4 The waves of death entangled me; the torrents of destruction flooded over me.

5 the cords of Sheol tied me down; the snares of death had entrapped me.

6 In my desperation, I cried out to Yahveh, and he heard me in his Temple.

7 Then the earth quaked and the mountains shook because he was angry.

8 Smoke poured out of his nostrils; consuming fire flared from his mouth; glowing coals erupted from him.

9 He lowered the sky too and came down with dense darkness under his feet.

10 He rode on a cherub and came flying swiftly on the wings of the wind.

11 He covered himself with darkness like a pavilion, with dark rain clouds veiling his advance

12 and hiding the brightness around him, while hailstones and lightning bolts broke through his clouds.

13 Yahveh thundered from the sky; the Most High raised his voice; hailstones and lightning bolts resounded.

14 He shot his arrows and scattered my enemies; lightning flashes routed them.

15 Then the water in the ocean receded; the seabed was exposed at his rebuke, at the blast from his nostrils.

16 He reached down from on high and grabbed me and pulled me out of the path of the raging tsunami.

17 He delivered me from enemies that hated me, that were too strong for me.

18 They attacked me at a time of distress, but Yahveh was my buttress.

19 He brought me out to a safe place and rescued me because he delighted in me.

20 Yahveh rewarded me for my goodness, for my innocence;

21 I’d kept his ways and hadn’t sinned by leaving him;

22 his ordinances all stayed with me; I hadn’t shoved any of his statutes away.

23 I stood blameless in his presence because I’d kept myself from sinning.

24 So Yahveh rewarded me for my goodness, for my innocence.

25 To the kind you’re kind; with the blameless you’re blameless;

26 with the pure you’re pure; with the treacherous you’re shrewd.

27 You rescue the humble but bring down the haughty.

28 You’re my lamp, Yahveh my God, and shine light into my darkness.

29 With your help, I can crush a whole battalion I can scale any wall.

30 God’s way is perfect; Yahveh’s promise proves true; he’s a shield for everyone that depends on his protection.

31 Who is God except Yahveh? Who is a rock but our God,

32 who wraps me in strength and makes my way safe?

33 He makes my feet as surefooted a red deer’s hooves and stands me securely on high ground.

34 He trains my hands for battle and strengthens my arms to draw a bronze bow.

35 He’s given me his deliverance as a shield; his help has made me great.

36 He’s widened the path where I step so my feet don’t slip.

37 I chased my enemies and overtook them; I didn’t turn back till I’d eliminated them.

38 I thrust them through so they couldn’t get up again; they fell under my feet

39 because you wrapped me in strength for battle and subdued my assailants.

40 You’ve made my enemies retreat; I destroyed the ones that hated me.

41 They looked around for help, but there wasn’t any; they even cried out to Yahveh, but he didn’t answer.

42 I pulverized them like dust in the wind, emptied them out like dirt on the street.

43 You delivered me from conflict with the people and placed me at the head of nations; people I didn’t know now serve me.

44 Foreigners submit as soon as they hear me;

45 they lose heart and come cringing out of their fortresses.

46 Yahveh lives; praise be to my Rock; exalted be God that delivers me,

47 that avenges me, that subdues nations under me.

48 that rescues me from my enemies, lifts me above my attackers, and delivers me from violent people.

49 So I’ll thank you among the nations, Yahveh, and sing your praises.

50 You give victory for your king and show enduring love to your anointed one, to me [David] and my descendants forever.
