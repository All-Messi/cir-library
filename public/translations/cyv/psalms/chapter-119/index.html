Psalms Chapter 119

1How blessed they are who follow his way blamelessly, who follow the Law of Yahveh,

2who observe his testimonies and look to him with all their heart.

3They do no evil who follow his ways.

4You’ve charged us to keep your commandments diligently.

5Oh that my ways may become secure by reflecting on your statutes!

6I won’t be disgraced if I keep my eye on everything you’ve commanded.

7I’ll thank you by living with a godly heart as I learn your good ordinances.

8I’ll keep your statutes; don’t give up on me!

Ps 119:1-8

Bet ב

9How can the young keep their conduct pure? By guarding it with what you say.

10I’ve looked to you wholeheartedly; don’t let me stray from your commandments.

11I’ve treasured up your word in my heart so I won’t sin against you.

12You’re blessed, Yahveh; teach me your statutes.

13I’ve recited all the ordinances you’ve given

14and delighted in your testimonies as much as in wealth.

15I’ll study your precepts and fix my attention on your ways.

16I’ll delight in your decrees and commit your word to memory.

Ps 119:9-16

Gimel ג

17Deal well with me so I can live and keep your word.

18Open my eyes so I can see the wonderful things in your Law.

19I’m a foreigner in the world; so make your commandments clear to me.

20I feel a burden to always keep your ordinances.

21You rebuke the insolent, the accursed that wander off from your commandments.

22Take their insults and contempt away from me because I observe your decrees.

23Even though officials sit around plotting against me, I still think about your statutes

24and delight in your decrees; they’re my counselors.

Ps 119:17-24

Dalet ד

25I’m lying in the dust; revive me with your message.

26I’ve told you my intentions and you responded; now teach me your statutes!

27Make me understand what your commandments mean so I can think about your wonderful deeds.

28I melt away with sorrow; encourage me with your message.

29Put false ways far away from me and graciously teach me your Law.

30I’ve chosen to be faithful and keep your ordinances at hand.

31I hold onto your testimonies, Yahveh; don’t let me feel humiliated!

32I’ll follow your commands because by them you increase my understanding.

Ps 119:25-32

He ה

33Teach me, Yahveh, the way your statutes direct, and I’ll keep them to the end.

34Help me understand so I can observe your Law properly.

35Lead me the way your commandments dictate because I delight in them.

36Incline my heart to your testimonies rather than to dishonest profit.

37Turn my attention away from pointless things and give me life in your ways.

38Confirm to me your servant your promise to those who respect you.

39Turn away the disgrace I dread, because your ordinances are good.

40I want to obey your commandments; so in your goodness give me life.

Ps 119:33-40

Vav ו

41Direct your enduring love toward me, Yahveh, the salvation you offer in your word,

42so I can answer whoever tries to discredit me, because I trust what you say.

43Don’t take your message out of my mouth, because I’ve put my hope in your ordinances

44so I can keep your Law ever and always.

45I’m free because I look to your precepts.

46I’ll talk to kings about your testimonies without feeling self-conscious.

47I delight in your commandments;

48I lift up my hands to them and study your statutes.

Ps 119:41-48

Zayin ז

49Remember your promise that you made for me to trust in.

50That’s my reassurance in adversity; your promise revives me.

51Godless people utterly deride me; yet I won’t turn aside from your Law.

52When I remember your ordinances from of old, I find comfort.

53Burning indignation has seized me because of sinners that abandon your Law.

54Your statutes are my songs wherever life leads me.

55I think about you at night, about keeping your instructions.

56This has been my way of life: observing your commandments.

Ps 119:49-56

Het ח

57Yahveh, you’re my portion; I promise to obey what you say.

58I ask for your blessing with all my heart; be gracious to me like you promised.

59When I think about your ways, I turn to your testimonies.

60I hurry to obey your commandments.

61Although sinful people’s cords ensnare me, I won’t let them pull me away from your Law.

62At midnight I get up to thank you for your good ordinances.

63I’m a friend to everyone that respects you and keeps your precepts.

64The earth is full of your enduring love, Yahveh; teach me your regulations.

Ps 119:57-64

Tet ט

65You’ve dealt well with me, Yahveh, like you promised.

66Give me discernment and insight because I believe in your commandments.

67Before I suffered I went astray, but now I keep your word.

68You’re good and do good; teach me your regulations.

69The godless have smeared me with slander; but I’ll still observe your precepts with all my heart.

70Their hearts are dull, but I delight in your Law.

71Suffering was good for me because from it I learned to obey your statutes.

72Your Law is more valuable to me than thousands of pieces of silver and gold.

Ps 119:65-72

Yod י

73You made me and fashioned me; help me understand so I can appreciate your commandments.

74May the ones that respect you see me and be happy because I wait for your promise.

75Yahveh, I know your judgments are right and that it was in your faithfulness that you oppressed me.

76Let your enduring love comfort me like you promised.

77Send me your mercy so I can live, because I delight in your Law.

78Discredit godless people because they’re undermining my welfare with deceit; meanwhile, I’ll stay focused on your precepts.

79May those who respect you return to me, the ones that know your testimonies.

80May my heart be blameless in regard to your statutes so I won’t have any regrets.

Ps 119:73-80

Kaph כ

81I’m weary waiting for your rescue, but I’ve put my hope in your message.

82My eyes strain to see your promises come true, while I say, “When will you comfort me?”

83Though I’ve shriveled up like a wineskin in smoke, I don’t lose touch with your regulations.

84How long will I live? When will you punish my persecutors?

85Godless men have dug pits to capture me, men that don’t conform to your Law.

86Your commandments are reliable; protect me from people that harass me with lies!

87They almost ruined me, but I didn’t give up on your precepts;

88in your enduring love, spare my life so I can keep obeying them.

Ps 119:81-88

Lamed ל

89Yahveh, your word is settled forever in heaven.

90Your faithfulness continues through every generation; you built the earth to stand.

91Your ordinances stand today because everything serves your purposes.

92If the Law hadn’t delighted me, I would’ve died in my misery.

93But I’ll never forget your instructions because you’ve given me life by them.

94I belong to you; save me because I’ve relied on your precepts.

95Bad people lie in ambush to destroy me, so I’ll diligently keep thinking about your testimonies.

96I’ve seen a limit to perfection in everything else, but your commandments don’t have any.

Ps 119:89-96

Mem מ

97How I love your law! I think about it all the time.

98Your commandments make me wiser than my enemies because they’re my constant guide.

99I have more insight than my teachers because your testimonies inform my thinking.

100I understand more than my elders because I’ve observed your precepts.

101I’ve stayed off of every errant path so I could keep your word.

102I haven’t turned aside from your ordinances because you yourself have taught me.

103Your words taste so sweet! they’re sweeter than honey!

104I gain insight from your precepts; so I detest every false way of living.

Ps 119:97-104

Nun נ

105Your word is a lamp for my feet, a light for my path.

106I’ve confirmed the oath I swore to keep your good ordinances.

107I’m hurting so much, Yahveh; give me life like you promised.

108Accept my praise and teach me your regulations.

109My life is constantly at risk; yet I’m still faithful to your law.

110Sinners have laid a snare for me; yet I won’t stray from your precepts.

111I’ve inherited your testimonies forever; they make me happy.

112I’m determined to keep your commandments to the very end.

Ps 119:105-12

Samek ס

113I detest people with divided loyalties, but I love your Law.

114You’re my shield and hiding place; I’ve put my hope in what you say.

115Go away, bad people, so I can observe God’s commands.

116Sustain me like you promised; don’t let me lose hope.

117Uphold me so I’m safe, so I can always regard your testimonies.

118You reject those who stray from your statutes because there’re just fooling themselves.

119Sinners you consider dross, so I love your testimonies.

120My body trembles for fear of you; I stand in awe of your judgments.

Ps 119:113-20

Ayin ע

121I’ve done what‘s just and true; don’t abandon me to my oppressors.

122Be my your servant’s guarantee for good so godless people won’t oppress me.

123My eyes strain to see your deliverance come and your promise come true.

124Deal with me according to your enduring love and teach me your statutes.

125I’m your servant; give me insight so I can understand your testimonies.

126It’s time for you to act because they’ve broken your Law.

127I love your commandments more than gold, more than fine gold.

128I care about your precepts about everything; that’s why I hate every false way.

Ps 119:121-28

Pe פ

129Your testimonies are wonderful; so I observe them.

130Explaining your message gives light and understanding to simple people.

131I panted with my mouth wide open, longing for your commandments.

132Turn to me and be gracious the way you do with those who love you.

133Steady my steps with your word; don’t let any sin get control of me.

134Ransom me from human oppression so I can keep your precepts.

135Smile on me and teach me your statutes.

136I shed tears over people that don’t observe your Law.

Ps 119:129-36

Tsade צ

137You’re good, Yahveh; your judgments are fair.

138You’ve commanded your testimonies in goodness and faithfulness.

139My zeal has consumed me because my adversaries have forgotten your words.

140Your promise is pure; that’s why I love it. 1411’m small and despised; yet I don’t forget your precepts.

142Your goodness is everlasting; your Law is true.

143Trouble and anguish have come on me; yet I still delight in your commandments.

144Your testimonies are good forever; help me understand them so I can live.

Ps 119:137-44

Qoph ק

145I pray with all my heart; answer me, Yahveh! I’ll observe your statutes.

146I’ve appealed to you; deliver me, and I’ll keep your testimonies.

147I get up before dawn to call for help; I hope you’ll answer.

148I look forward to the night watches so I can think about what you say.

149Listen to me in your enduring love; in your justice preserve my life.

150The ones that persecute me are getting closer; they’re far from your Law.

151You’re near me, and your commandments are true.

152For a long time I’ve known that you’ve laid down your testimonies to last forever.

Ps 119:145-52

Resh ר

153Look at my difficulties and deliver me because I don’t forget your Law.

154Argue my case and acquit me; give me life like you promised.

155Salvation is far from sinners because they don’t rely on your statutes.

156Your mercies are great, Yahveh; give me life like you promised.

157I have lots of adversaries and persecutors; yet I don’t swerve from your testimonies.

158When I look at the faithless, I loathe them because they don’t listen to what you say.

159Consider how I love your commandments; give me life like you promised.

160Everything you say is true; every one of your just ordinances lasts forever.

Ps 119:153-60

Shin שׁ

161Officials harass me for no reason, but I only stand in awe of what you say.

162Your message makes me happy like someone that finds lots of spoil.

163I despise falsehood, but I love your Law.

164I praise you seven times a day because of your good ordinances.

165The ones that love your Law have peace; nothing makes them stumble.

166I look forward to your salvation, Yahveh, and do your commandments.

167I keep your testimonies; I love them so much.

168I keep your precepts, as you’re well aware.

Ps 119:161-68

Tav ת

169Let my call come to you, Yahveh; give me insight like you promised.

170Listen to my petition and give me the deliverance you promised.

171Let praise flow from my lips so you can teach me your regulations.

172I’ll sing about your word because your commandments are right.

173Be ready to help me because I’ve chosen your precepts.

174I long for your deliverance and delight in your Law.

175Let me live to praise you; let your ordinances help me.

176I’ve wandered off like a lost sheep; look for me because I haven’t forgotten your commands.

Ps 119:169-76
