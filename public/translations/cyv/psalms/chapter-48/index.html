Psalms Chapter 48

1Yahveh is great and deserves great praise in the city of our God on his holy mountain.

2Mount Zion, the heights of Zaphon, is high and beautiful— the joy of the whole earth, the city of the great King.

3In its towers God reveals himself as a strong defense.

4Kings joined forces and advanced against that strong defense.

5But as soon as it came into view, they panicked and fled.

6Panic seized them like the anguish of a woman in childbirth.

7Yahveh destroyed them like ships of Tarshish capsized by an east wind.

8As we’ve heard, so we’ve seen in the city of Yahveh of Hosts, our God; he’ll make it safe forever.

Ps 48:1-8

9In your Temple we think about your enduring love.

10Like your name, God, your praise is everywhere; your right hand is filled with goodness.

11Let Mount Zion be happy, and the residents of Judah celebrate your goodness.

12Walk around Zion and count its towers;

13inspect its ramparts and tour its fortresses so you can tell the next generation;

14because God is God forever; he’ll guide us till we die.

Ps 48:9-14
