Psalms Chapter 37

1Don’t worry about bad people or envy those who do wrong;

2they’ll wither like grass and fade like flowers.

3Trust in Yahveh and do good so you can live in the land and enjoy security.

4Delight in Yahveh, and he’ll give you what your heart desires.

5Commit your way to him and trust in him, and he’ll act on your behalf.

6He’ll bring forth your goodness like the dawn and your vindication like the midday sun.

Ps 37:1-6

7Rest in Yahveh and wait patiently for him; don’t worry about people that prosper by carrying out dishonest schemes.

8Stop being mad at them; set anger aside; don’t concern yourself with them; that only leads to your own sinning.

9They’ll be destroyed, but those who depend on Yahveh will inherit the land.

10Sinners will soon be gone; you can look all over for them, but they won’t be there.

11It’s the humble will inherit the land and delight in abundant prosperity.

Ps 37:7-11

12The wicked plot against the good and gnash their teeth at them.

13But Yahveh laughs at them, because he sees what’s going to happen to them.

14Sinners draw the sword and bend the bow to bring down the poor and needy, and slay the godly;

15but their sword will pierce their own heart, and their bow will break.

Ps 37:12-15

16The little that good people have is better than the abundance sinners possess;

17because Yahveh will break the arms of sinners and sustain the good.

18He knows the time of the blameless; their inheritance lasts forever.

19They aren’t discouraged in hard times and have plenty during famine.

20But the sinful perish; Yahveh’s enemies are like flowers in a pasture; they dissipate like smoke.

21The wicked borrow and don’t repay, but the good are generous and give.

22Those he blesses will inherit the land; the ones he curses will die.

Ps 37:16-22

23Yahveh guides the steps of the godly and delights in their lives.

24If they fall, they won’t fall headlong because Yahveh will hold their hand.

25I was young once; now I’m old; yet I’ve not seen good people abandoned or their children beg for bread.

26They’re gracious and generous; their descendants are a blessing.

27Quit doing bad things and do good, so you’ll live forever.

Ps 37:23-27

28Yahveh loves justice and doesn’t abandon his holy people; he keeps them forever, but cuts off the descendants of the wicked.

29The godly will inherit the land and live there permanently.

30The godly utter wisdom and speak justice.

31The Law of their God is in their heart; their feet don’t slip.

32Sinners spy on them and try to kill them.

33Yahveh won’t abandon the godly to their power or let them be condemned when they’re judged.

34Wait for Yahveh and keep his way, and he’ll raise you up to inherit the land; you’ll see the wicked destroyed.

Ps 37:28-34

35I’ve seen a violent, wicked people spread themselves out like tall trees in their native soil.

36Then they passed away and were gone; I looked for them, but they were nowhere to be found.

37Take note of blameless, watch the godly because peaceful people prosper;

38but sinners will be destroyed along with their future.

39The salvation of the godly comes from Yahveh, their refuge when trouble comes.

40Yahveh helps them and delivers them, protects them from sinners and saves them because they take refuge in him.

Ps 37:35-40
