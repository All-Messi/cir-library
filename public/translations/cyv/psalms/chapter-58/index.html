Psalms Chapter 58

1Leaders, do you decree what’s right? Do you judge justly?

2No, you plot injustice in your hearts and spread violence in the land.

3The sinful go astray from the womb; they lie from the time they’re born.

4They have venom like vipers, and stop up their ears like deaf cobras

5so they don’t hear their charmers’ voices or those skilled at casting spells.

Ps 58:1-5

6Pull out their fangs, God; break the teeth out of the young lions.

7Make them disappear like water that drains away; make their arrows shafts without points.

8Make sinners be like snails that dissolve as they go along, like a miscarriage that never sees the sun.

9Before your pots can feel the heat of burning briers; he’ll sweep them away with a whirlwind, whether green or on fire.

Ps 58:6-9

10Good people will celebrate when they see God pay them back; he’ll wash their feet in the sinners’ blood.

11People will say, “There’s surely a reward for good people; there’s certainly a God that judges on earth!”

Ps 58:10-11
