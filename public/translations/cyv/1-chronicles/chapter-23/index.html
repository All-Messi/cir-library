1 Chronicles Chapter 23

1When David got old, he appointed Solomon king over Israel. 2He gathered the leaders with the priests and Levites. 3The Le

vites 30

years old and up numbered 38,000. 4Of these, 24,000 oversaw the work, 6,000 were officers and judges, 54,000 were gatekeepers, and 4,000 praised Yahveh with instruments David made for that purpose. 6He divided them according to the sons of Levi: Gershon, Kohath, and Merari. 1 Ch 23:1-6

7The sons of Gershon were Ladan and Shimei.

8Three sons of Ladan were Jehiel the oldest, Jetham, and Joel.

9Three sons of Shimei were Shelomoth, Haziel, and Haran. These were the heads of families in Ladan/Libni. 10-11The sons of Shimei were Jahath, first; Zina/Zizah, second. Jeush and Beriah didn’t have very many sons, so they were all counted as one family.

1 Ch 23:7-11

12The sons of Kohath were Amram, Izhar, Hebron, and Uzziel.

3The sons of Amram were Aaron and Moses. Aaron and his sons were set apart permanently to sanctify Yahveh as most holy, to burn incense to him, to perform service to him, and to bless in his name.

14The sons of Moses, the man of God, were included in the tribe of Levi.

15The sons of Moses were Gershom and Eliezer.

16The son of Gershom was Shebuel the chief.

17The only son of Eliezer was Rehabiah. Rehabiah had lots of sons.

8The son of Izhar was Shelomith the chief.

9The sons of Hebron were Jeriah, first; then Amariah, then Jahaziel, then Jekameam.

0The sons of Uzziel were Micah, first, then Isshiah.

21The sons of Merari were Mahli and Mushi. The sons of Mahli were Eleazar and Kish.

22Eleazar died and had only daughters, so their cousins, Kish’s sons, married them.

3The sons of Mushi were Mahli, Eder, and Jeremoth.

1 Ch 23:12-23

24Those were Levi’s descendants by heads of families as counted in their census. They served in Yahveh’s Temple

from 20

years old and up. 25David said, “Yahveh God of Israel has given his people rest, and he lives in Jerusalem permanently. 26The Levites won’t need to carry the Tabernacle and the utensils for its service.” 27By David’s last words, Levi’s descendants were counted from 20 years old and up. 28They were to assist Aaron’s descendants with the service in the courts and rooms in Yahveh’s Temple. They were to purify the holy things 29and help with the showbread, the fine flour for grain offerings, wafers without yeast, what’s baked in the pan, or what’s well-mixed, and all measures of volume and size. 30They were to stand each morning and evening to thank and praise Yahveh. 31They offered burnt offerings on Sabbaths, new moons, and scheduled festivals in the number set by the Law. 32They maintained the Tent of Meeting and the Holy Place and helped Aaron’s descendants with the service in Yahveh’s house. 1 Ch 23:24-32 Duties of Priests
