1 Chronicles Chapter 3

1 Ch 3:21-24

2 the third was Absalom Ben-Maacah Bat-Talmai, king of Geshur; the fourth was Adonijah Ben-Haggith;

3 the fifth was Shephatiah by Abital; the sixth was Ithream by his wife Eglah.

4 He ruled in Hebron for 7½ years and in Jerusalem for 33.

5 These were born in Jerusalem: Shimea, Shobab, Nathan, and Solomon—four by Bath-shua Bat-Ammiel; 6and Ibhar, Elishama, Eliphelet, 7Nogah, Nepheg, and Japhia, 8Elishama, Eliada, and Eliphelet—nine. 9All of those were sons of David besides the sons of concubines. Tamar was their sister. 1 Ch 3:1-9

10 Solomon’s son was Rehoboam Avi-Abijah Avi-Asa Avi-Jehoshaphat Avi-11Joram Avi-Ahaziah Avi-Joash Avi-12Amaziah Avi-Azariah Avi-Jotham Avi-13Ahaz Avi-Hezekiah Avi-Manasseh Avi-14Amon Avi-Josiah Avi-15Johanan his firstborn, Jehoiakim second, Zedekiah third, and Shallum fourth.

16 The sons of Jehoiakim: Jeconiah his son and Zedekiah his son

17 The sons of Jeconiah the prisoner were Shealtiel, 18Malchiram, Pedaiah, Shenazzar, Jekamiah, Hoshama, and Nedabiah.

19 The sons of Pedaiah were Zerubbabel and Shimei. The sons of Zerubabbel were Meshullam and Hananiah; Shelomith was their sister; 20Hashubah, Ohel, Berechiah, Hasadiah, and Jushab-hesed—5. 1 Ch 3:10-20

21 The sons of Hananiah were Pelatiah and Jeshaiah, the sons of Rephaiah, the sons of Arnan, the sons of Obadiah, and the sons of Shecaniah,

22 The son of Shecaniah was Shemaiah. The sons of Shemaiah were Hattush, Igal, Bariah, Neariah, and Shaphat—6.

23 The sons of Neariah were Elioenai, Hizkiah, and Azrikam—3.

24 The sons of Elioenai were Hodaviah, Eliashib, Pelaiah, Akkub, Johanan, Delaiah, and Anani—7.
