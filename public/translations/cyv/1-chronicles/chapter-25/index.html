1 Chronicles Chapter 25

1David and the commanders of the army set apart some sons of Asaph, Heman, and Jeduthun to prophesy with lyres, harps, and cymbals. Those who performed their service were as follows:

2from the sons of Asaph: Zaccur, Joseph, Nethaniah, and Asharelah were under the direction of Asaph, who prophesied under the direction of the king;

3from the sons of Jeduthun: Gedaliah, Zeri, Jeshaiah, Shimei, Hashabiah, and Mattithiah—6 were under the direction of their father Jeduthun with the harp, who prophesied by giving thanks and praising Yahveh;

4from the sons of Heman: Bukkiah, Mattaniah, Uzziel, Shebuel, Jerimoth, Hananiah, Hanani, Eliathah, Giddalti, Romamti-ezer, Joshbekashah, Mallothi, Hothir, Mahazioth.

5Those were sons of Heman the king’s seer to honor him according to God’s message, because God gave

Heman 14

sons and 3 daughters.

6Those were under their father’s direction to sing in Yahveh’s Temple, with cymbals, harps, and lyres for the service. Asaph, Jeduthun, and Heman served under the king’s direction. 7There

were 288

trained in singing to Yahveh with their relatives, talented men. 8They cast lots for their duties, least and greatest, teacher and pupil. 1 Ch 25:1-8
