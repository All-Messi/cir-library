1 Chronicles Chapter 2

1 The sons of Israel: Reuben, Simeon, Levi, Judah, Issachar, Zebulun, 2Dan, Joseph, Benjamin, Naphtali, Gad, and Asher

3 The sons of JUDAH: Er, Onan, and Shelah by Bath-shua the Canaanitess Er, Judah’s firstborn, was sinful; so Yahveh took his life.

4 Tamar Judah’s daughter-in-law bore him Perez and Zerah. Judah had five sons in all.

5 The sons of Perez were Hezron and Hamul.

6 The sons of Zerah were Zimri, Ethan, Heman, Calcol, and Dara—five. (7The son of Carmi was Achar, the troubler of Israel, who violated the ban.)

8 The son of Ethan was Azariah. 1 Ch 2:1-8

9 The sons of Hezron were Jerahmeel, Ram, Chelubai, and Caleb.

10 Ram was the Avi-Amminadab Avi-Nahshon, leader of the sons of Judah.

11 Nahshon was the father of Salma Avi-

12 Boaz Avi- Obed Avi-

13 Jesse Avi-Eliab, then Abinadab, then Shimea,

14 then Nethanel, then Raddai, 15then Ozem, then David 16and sisters Zeruiah and Abigail. Zeruiah bore Abshai, Joab, and Asahel.

17 Abigail bore Amasa by Jether the Ishmaelilte.

18 Caleb Ben-Hezron had sons by Azubah his wife and by Jerioth. Her sons were Jesher, Shobab, and Ardon.

19 When Azubah died, he married Ephrath mother of Hur.

20 Hur was father of Uri Avi-Bezalel.

21 Afterward Hezron married the daughter of Machir (Avi- Gilead). He married her when he was 60 and she bore Segub.

22 Segub was father of Jair, who governed 23 towns in Gilead. 23But Geshur and Aram captured the towns of Jair from them along with Kenath and its 60 surrounding villages. Those were sons of Machir Avi-Gilead.

24 After the death of Hezron in Caleb-ephrathah, Abijah his wife bore him Ashhur Avi-Tekoa. 1 Ch 2:9-24

25 The sons of Jerahmeel the firstborn of Hezron were Ram, then Bunah, Oren, Ozem, and Ahijah. 26Jerahmeel had another wife, Atarah mother of Onam.

27 The sons of Ram, Jerahmeel’s firstborn, were Maaz, Jamin, and Eker.

28 The sons of Onam were Shammai and Jada. The sons of Shammai were Nadab and Abishur.

29 Abishur’s wife was Abihail Em- Ahban and Molid.

30 The sons of Nadab were Seled and Appaim; Seled died without sons.

31 The son of Appaim was Ishi Avi-Sheshan Avi-Ahlai.

32 The sons of Jada brother of Shammai were Jether and Jonathan. Jether died without sons.

33 The sons of Jonathan: Peleth and Zaza. Those were the descendants of Jarahmeel. 1 Ch 2:25-33

34 Sheshan had only daughters. He had an Egyptian servant named Jarha. 35He gave his daughter to Jarha in marriage, and she bore 36Attai Avi-Nathan Avi-37Zabad Avi-Ephlal Avi-38Obed Avi-Jehu Avi-39Azariah Avi-Helez Avi-40Eleasah Avi-Sismai Avi-41Shallum Avi-Jekamiah Avi-Elishama. 1 Ch 2:34-41

42 The sons of Caleb, brother of Jerahmeel, were Mesha, his firstborn, Avi-Ziph Avi-Mareshah Avi-Hebron.

43 The sons of Hebron were Korah, Tappuah, Rekem, and Shema.

44 Shema was father of Raham Avi-Jorkeam. Rekem was father of Shammai 45Avi-Maon Avi-Beth-zur. 1 Ch 2:42-45

46 Caleb’s concubine Ephah bore Haran, Moza, and Gazez. Haran was father of Gazez. 47The sons of Jahdai were Regem, Jotham, Geshan, Pelet, Ephah, and Shaaph.

48 Caleb’s concubine Maacah bore Sheber and Tirhanah, 49as well as Shaaph Avi-Madmannah, Sheva Avi- Machbena and Gibea. Caleb’s daughter was Achsah. 50Those were the sons of Caleb. The sons of Hur, the firstborn of Ephrathah, were Shobal founder of Kiriath-jearim, 51Salma founder of Bethlehem, and Hareph Avi-Beth-gader. 52Shobal, founder of Kiriath-jearim, was father of Haroeh, half of the Manahathites, 53and the families in Kiriath-jearim: the Ithrites, Puthites, Shumathites, and Mishraites; from them came the Zorathites and Eshtaolites. 54The sons of Salma were Bethlehem and the Netophathites, Atroth-beth-joab, and half of the Manahathites, the Zorites. 55The families of scribes that lived at Jabez were the Tirathites, Shimeathites, and Sucathites. Those are the Kenites that came from Hammath, father of the House of Rechab. 1 Ch 2:46-55
