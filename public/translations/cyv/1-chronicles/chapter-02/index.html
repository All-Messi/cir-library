1 Chronicles Chapter 2

1The sons of Israel: Reuben, Simeon, Levi, Judah, Issachar, Zebulun, 2Dan, Joseph, Benjamin, Naphtali, Gad, and Asher

3The sons of JUDAH: Er, Onan, and Shelah by Bath-shua the Canaanitess Er, Judah’s firstborn, was sinful; so Yahveh took his life.

4Tamar Judah’s daughter-in-law bore him Perez and Zerah. Judah had five sons in all.

5The sons of Perez were Hezron and Hamul.

6The sons of Zerah were Zimri, Ethan, Heman, Calcol, and Dara—five. (7The son of Carmi was Achar, the troubler of Israel, who violated the ban.)

8The son of Ethan was Azariah.

1 Ch 2:1-8

9The sons of Hezron were Jerahmeel, Ram, Chelubai, and Caleb.

10Ram was the Avi-Amminadab Avi-Nahshon, leader of the sons of Judah.

11Nahshon was the father of Salma Avi-

12Boaz Avi- Obed Avi-

13Jesse Avi-Eliab, then Abinadab, then Shimea,

14then Nethanel, then Raddai, 15then Ozem, then David 16and sisters Zeruiah and Abigail. Zeruiah bore Abshai, Joab, and Asahel.

17Abigail bore Amasa by Jether the Ishmaelilte.

18Caleb Ben-Hezron had sons by Azubah his wife and by Jerioth. Her sons were Jesher, Shobab, and Ardon.

19When Azubah died, he married Ephrath mother of Hur.

20Hur was father of Uri Avi-Bezalel.

21Afterward Hezron married the daughter of Machir (Avi- Gilead). He married her when he

was 60

and she bore Segub.

22Segub was father of Jair, who gov

erned 23

towns in Gilead. 23But Geshur and Aram captured the towns of Jair from them along with Kenath and its 60 surrounding villages. Those were sons of Machir Avi-Gilead.

24After the death of Hezron in Caleb-ephrathah, Abijah his wife bore him Ashhur Avi-Tekoa.

1 Ch 2:

9-24

25The sons of Jerahmeel the firstborn of Hezron were Ram, then Bunah, Oren, Ozem, and Ahijah. 26Jerahmeel had another wife, Atarah mother of Onam.

27The sons of Ram, Jerahmeel’s firstborn, were Maaz, Jamin, and Eker.

28The sons of Onam were Shammai and Jada. The sons of Shammai were Nadab and Abishur.

29Abishur’s wife was Abihail Em- Ahban and Molid.

30The sons of Nadab were Seled and Appaim; Seled died without sons.

31The son of Appaim was Ishi Avi-Sheshan Avi-Ahlai.

32The sons of Jada brother of Shammai were Jether and Jonathan. Jether died without sons.

33The sons of Jonathan: Peleth and Zaza. Those were the descendants of Jarahmeel.

1 Ch 2:25-33

34Sheshan had only daughters. He had an Egyptian servant named Jarha. 35He gave his daughter to Jarha in marriage, and she bore 36Attai Avi-Nathan Avi-37Zabad Avi-Ephlal Avi-38Obed Avi-Jehu Avi-39Azariah Avi-Helez Avi-40Eleasah Avi-Sismai Avi-41Shallum Avi-Jekamiah Avi-Elishama.

1 Ch 2:34-41

42The sons of Caleb, brother of Jerahmeel, were Mesha, his firstborn, Avi-Ziph Avi-Mareshah Avi-Hebron.

43The sons of Hebron were Korah, Tappuah, Rekem, and Shema.

44Shema was father of Raham Avi-Jorkeam. Rekem was father of Shammai 45Avi-Maon Avi-Beth-zur.

1 Ch 2:42-45

46Caleb’s concubine Ephah bore Haran, Moza, and Gazez. Haran was father of Gazez. 47The sons of Jahdai were Regem, Jotham, Geshan, Pelet, Ephah, and Shaaph.

48Caleb’s concubine Maacah bore Sheber and Tirhanah, 49as well as Shaaph Avi-Madmannah, Sheva Avi- Machbena and Gibea. Caleb’s daughter was Achsah. 50Those were the sons of Caleb. The sons of Hur, the firstborn of Ephrathah, were Shobal founder of Kiriath-jearim, 51Salma founder of Bethlehem, and Hareph Avi-Beth-gader.

52Shobal, founder of Kiriath-jearim, was father of Haroeh, half of the Manahathites, 53and the families in Kiriath-jearim: the Ithrites, Puthites, Shumathites, and Mishraites; from them came the Zorathites and Eshtaolites.

54The sons of Salma were Bethlehem and the Netophathites, Atroth-beth-joab, and half of the Manahathites, the Zorites.

55The families of scribes that lived at Jabez were the Tirathites, Shimeathites, and Sucathites. Those are the Kenites that came from Hammath, father of the House of Rechab.

1 Ch 2:46-55
