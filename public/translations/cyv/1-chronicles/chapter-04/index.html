1 Chronicles Chapter 4

1The sons of JUDAH: Perez, Hezron, Carmi, Hur, and Shobal

2Reaiah Ben-Shobal was father of Jahath Avi-Ahumai and Lahad. These were the families of the Zorathites.

3These were the sons of Etam: Jezreel, Ishma, and Idbash; their sister was Hazzelelponi.

4Penuel was father of Gedor and Ezer Avi-Hushah. Those were the sons of Hur firstborn of Ephrathah ancestor of Bethlehem.

5Ashhur father of Tekoa had two wives: Helah and Naarah. 6Naarah bore him Ahuzzam, Hepher, Temeni, and Haahashtari. 7The sons of Helah were Zereth, Izhar, Ethnan,

8and Koz, ancestor of Anub and Zobebah and the families of Aharhel Ben-Harum.

9A man named Jabez had more honor than his brothers, and his mother named him Jabez because she bore him “in pain.” 10Jabez called on the God of Israel, “Oh that you’d bless me and make my territory bigger, be with me, and keep me from harm so I won’t suffer!” God gave him what he asked.

1 Ch 4:1-10

1Chelub brother of Shuhah was father of Mehir Avi-Eshton.

12Eshton was father of Beth-rapha, Paseah, and Tehinnah Avi-Irnahash. These were the men of Recah.

3The sons of Kenaz were Othniel and Seraiah. The son of Othniel was Hathath.

4aMeonothai was father of Ophrah.

5The sons of Caleb Ben-Jephunneh were Iru, Elah, and Naam. The son of Elah was Kenaz.

6The sons of Jehallelel were Ziph and Ziphah, Tiria and Asarel.

7The sons of Ezrah were Jether, Mered, Epher, and Jalon. These are the sons of Bithia Pharaoh’s daughter that Mered married. She bore Miriam, Shammai, and Ishbah Avi-Eshtemoa. 18His Jewish wife bore Jered Avi-Gedor, Heber Avi-Soco, and Jekuthiel Avi-Zanoah. 19The sons of the wife of Hodiah sister of Naham were the fathers of Keilah the Garmite and Eshtemoa the Maacathite.

1 Ch 4:11-19

0The sons of Shimon were Amnon and Rinnah, Benhanan and Tilon. The sons of Ishi were Zoheth and Benzoheth.

1The sons of Shelah Ben-Judah were Er Avi-Lecah, Laadah Avi-Mareshah, the families of the linen workers at Beth-ashbea, 22Jokim the men of Cozeba, Joash Saraph, who ruled in Moab; and Jashubi-lehem. The records are ancient. 23These were the potters and people of Netaim and Gederah. They lived there with the king for his work.

1 Ch 4:20-23

24The sons of SIMEON: Nemuel, Jamin, Jarib, Zerah, Shaul

5Shaul was Avi-Shallum Avi-Mibsam Avi-Mishma.

26Mishma was father of Hammuel, Zaccur, and Shimei.

27Shimei

had 16

sons and 6 daughters, but his brothers didn’t have many sons. Their whole family didn’t multiply like the descendants of Judah. 28They lived in Beersheba, Moladah, and Hazar-shual, 29at Bilhah, Ezem, Tolad, 30Bethuel, Hormah, Ziklag, 31Beth-marcaboth, Hazar-susim, Beth-Birei, and Shaaraim. Those were their towns till David’s reign. 32Their villages were Etam, Ain, Rimmon, Tochen, and Ashan—5 towns, 33along with the villages that were around them as far as Baal. These were their settlements, and they kept a genealogy. 34Meshobab, Jamlech, Joshah Ben-Amaziah; 35Joel, Jehu Ben-Joshibiah Ben-Seraiah Ben-Asiel; 36Elioenai, Jaakobah, Jeshohaiah, Asaiah, Adiel, Jesimiel, Benaiah, 37Ziza Ben-Shiphi Ben-Allon Ben-Jedaiah Ben-Shimri Ben-Shemaiah: 38these named were leaders in their clans, and their families increased greatly. 39They went to the entrance of Gedor, to the east side of the valley, to look for pasture for their flocks. 40They found good pasture, and the land was large and quiet and peaceful; the previous residents were Hamites. 41These, according to the records, came at the time of Hezekiah, king of Judah, and attacked their tents as well as the Meunites that were there, and destroyed them till today, and lived in place of them, because there was pasture for their flocks. 42From them—from the descendants of Simeon—500 men went to Mount Seir with Pelatiah, Neariah, Rephaiah, and Uzziel, sons of Ishi as their leaders. 43They destroyed the remnant of the Amalekites that escaped and have lived there ever since. 1 Ch 4:24-43
