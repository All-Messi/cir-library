Jonah Chapter 2

1 Jonah prayed to Yahveh his God from the fish’s stomach, 2"I called to you in my distress, and you answered me; I cried out for help from the belly of Sheol, and you heard me.

3 You threw me into the deep, into the heart of the sea; the current swirled over me; your waves and breakers overwhelmed me.

4 So I said, ‘I’ve been driven away from you, but I’ll still look toward your holy Temple.’

5 Water closed in over me; the great deep surrounded me; seaweed wrapped around my head.

6 I sank down to the base of the mountains; the earth imprisoned me forever, but you brought me up from the Pit, Yahveh my God.

7 As my life was fading away, I remembered you; and prayed to you in your holy Temple.

8 Those who pay respects to pointless idols forfeit your faithfulness,

9 but I’ll sacrifice to you with thanksgiving. I’ll do what I’ve vowed; salvation comes from youY."

10 Then Yahveh had the fish cough Jonah up onto the shore. Jonah’s Trip to Nineveh
