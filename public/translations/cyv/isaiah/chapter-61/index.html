Isaiah Chapter 61

1The Spirit of the Lord Yahveh is on me; he’s anointed me to bring good news to the downtrodden. He’s sent me to bandage up the brokenhearted, to liberate captives, to free prisoners,

2to announce that this is the year Yahveh shows blessing, the day he sets things right, to comfort anyone that’s grieving,

3to give to the sad in Zion a crown in place of ashes, the oil of happiness instead of mourning, the mantle of praise rather than a fainting spirit so they’ll be like Terebinths of Goodness Yahveh has planted to demonstrate his glory.

Is 61:1-3

4Then they’ll rebuild the ancient ruins, set back up what was torn down, repair destroyed towns deserted for generations.

5Foreigners will pasture your flocks, do your farming, and tend your vineyards.

6But you’ll be Yahveh’s priests; people will speak of you as his ministers. You’ll feed on the wealth of nations and take pride in their prosperity.

7Instead of humiliation you’ll have a double portion; rather than dishonor you’ll celebrate over your lot. You’ll have everlasting joy in your land. 8“I, Yahveh, love justice and despise short-changing in burnt offering; I’ll make an everlasting contract with you and faithfully pay you for your work.

9Then the nations will honor your offspring worldwide; everyone will recognize you as the offspring Yahveh blessed.”

Is 61:4-9

0I’m really happy in Yahveh; I celebrate over my God because he’s dressed me in salvation and wrapped me in goodness like a robe, like a groom dresses himself in a garland, and a bride adorns herself with jewels.

1As the ground produces seeds and a garden makes sprouts grow, so the Lord Yahveh makes goodness and praise spring up for all the world to see.
