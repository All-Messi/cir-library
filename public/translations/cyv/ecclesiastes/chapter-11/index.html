Ecclesiastes Chapter 11

1Toss your bread on the water; it’ll come back around a long time later.

2Diversify your investments seven or eight ways; you don’t know what risks lie ahead.

3Heavy clouds drop rain; a tree stays where it falls.

4If you keep worrying about the weather, you won’t sow; if you keep watching the clouds, you won’t reap.

5Just like you don’t know where the wind will blow or how bones form in a womb, you don’t understand what God’s doing who makes everything happen.

6Plant your seed in the morning; don’t be idle in the afternoon; you don’t know if planting in the morning or afternoon is best, or if one’s as good as the other.

Eccl 11:1-6

7Light is pleasing; sunrise is pleasant to behold.

8If you live a long time, enjoy all your years; just remember, there’ll be lots of dark days, and nothing will seem important in the end.

9Young people, enjoy life while you’re young; stay pleasant. Go ahead and follow your impulses and desires; just remember, God will judge you for all of them.

0So don’t worry; take care of your health, because childhood and the prime of life soon pass.

Eccl 11:7-10
