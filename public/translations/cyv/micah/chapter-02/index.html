Micah Chapter 2

1 Misfortune awaits those who devise sin, Who lie awake plotting schemes in bed! In the morning they do them because they can.

2 They covet fields and seize them; they desire horses and take them. They rob people and confiscate the property they’ve inherited.

3 So Yahveh says, “I’m devising a catastrophe for this family; you won’t get your necks out of the noose. You won’t keep carrying on arrogantly; it’ll be a terrible time for you.

4 Your enemies will taunt you with a proverb and wail a bitter dirge in mockery, ‘We’re ruined! God is reapportioning our things. He’s taking them away from us and giving our fields to apostates!’

5 So you won’t have anyone stretching out a surveying line for you by lot in Yahveh’s assembly. 6”‘Don’t preach about such things,’ they say; ‘disgrace won’t catch up with us.’

7 Are you really saying such a thing, House of Jacob? Isn’t Yahveh’s Spirit patient? Does he do such things? Don’t his words do good for those who do right?

8 My people rise up against my people; they strip robes off the peaceful, off of people that go along innocently with no thought of fighting.

9 They evict my people’s women from their pleasant homes and forever strip away from their children the splendor I would give them.

10 Get up and get out of here! this is no longer a place to rest; there’s sinfulness here that brings painful destruction.

11 If a liar full of hot air tells you he’ll preach about the fun of wine and alcohol, he’ll certainly get a hearing from you! 12"I’ll assemble you, Jacob, remnant of Israel, and put you together like sheep in a fold; like a flock in its pasture, you’ll be a noisy crowd in your land.

13 The One that opens the gate will lead you out; you’ll break out of exile through it. Your King will go out ahead of you, Yahveh at the lead." Judgment Against Israel’s Leaders
