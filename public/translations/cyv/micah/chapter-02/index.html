Micah Chapter 2

1Misfortune awaits those who devise sin, Who lie awake plotting schemes in bed! In the morning they do them because they can.

2They covet fields and seize them; they desire horses and take them. They rob people and confiscate the property they’ve inherited.

3So Yahveh says, “I’m devising a catastrophe for this family; you won’t get your necks out of the noose. You won’t keep carrying on arrogantly; it’ll be a terrible time for you.

4Your enemies will taunt you with a proverb and wail a bitter dirge in mockery, ‘We’re ruined! God is reapportioning our things. He’s taking them away from us and giving our fields to apostates!’

5So you won’t have anyone stretching out a surveying line for you by lot in Yahveh’s assembly.

Mic 2:1-5

6“‘Don’t preach about such things,’ they say; ‘disgrace won’t catch up with us.’

7Are you really saying such a thing, House of Jacob? Isn’t Yahveh’s Spirit patient? Does he do such things? Don’t his words do good for those who do right?

8My people rise up against my people; they strip robes off the peaceful, off of people that go along innocently with no thought of fighting.

9They evict my people’s women from their pleasant homes and forever strip away from their children the splendor I would give them.

10Get up and get out of here! this is no longer a place to rest; there’s sinfulness here that brings painful destruction.

11If a liar full of hot air tells you he’ll preach about the fun of wine and alcohol, he’ll certainly get a hearing from you!

Mic 2:6-11

12“I’ll assemble you, Jacob, remnant of Israel, and put you together like sheep in a fold; like a flock in its pasture, you’ll be a noisy crowd in your land.

13The One that opens the gate will lead you out; you’ll break out of exile through it. Your King will go out ahead of you, Yahveh at the lead.”

Mic 2:12-13

Judgment Against Israel’s Leaders
