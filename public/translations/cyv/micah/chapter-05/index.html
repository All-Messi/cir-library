Micah Chapter 5

1 Muster your troops, Jerusalem; they’re laying siege against you; they’ll hit Israel’s leader in the face with a rod.

2 But you, Bethlehem Ephrathah, though you’re small among Judah’s clans, you’ll have a ruler come out of you on my behalf in Israel. His origins are from long ago, from ancient times."

3 Israel will be abandoned till the one that’s in labor has the child; then the rest of his relatives will come back from exile.

4 He’ll stand up and shepherd his flock with the power and majesty of Yahveh his God; they’ll be able to remain secure because at that time he’ll be great everywhere.

5 He’ll give us peace. Mic 5:1-5a When the Assyrians invade our land and overrun our fortresses, we’ll fight back with seven shepherds, eight princes leading us.

6 They’ll shepherd Assyria with the sword, Nimrod’s land with swords drawn. Yahveh will deliver us from them when they attack our land and try to tramp all over our territory. A Remnant to Be Delivered

7 Then the remnant of Jacob left in the land will live among many nations like dew from Yahveh, like showers on grass that mortals can’t restrain, that no one can delay. Mic 5:5b-7

8 The remnant left in Jacob will live among many nations like a young lion in a flock of sheep. He mauls and mangles as he goes; no one can stop him.

9 He’ll raise his hand against his enemies and wipe them out. 10"At that time," Yahveh says, “I’ll get rid of your horses and destroy your chariots.

11 I’ll demolish your towns and tear down your defenses.

12 I’ll abolish sorcery; you won’t have fortunetellers anymore.

13 I’ll get rid of your carved images and sacred pillars so you won’t worship what you’ve made.

14 I’ll pull up your Asherah poles and destroy your pagan cities.

15 I’ll carry out angry revenge on nations that refuse to obey.” Yahveh’s Case Against Israel
