Joel Chapter 1

1A Message from Yahveh to Joel Ben-Pethuel Mourning over a Locust Invasion

2Listen to this, elders; pay attention, everybody in the land. Has anything like this ever happened in your lifetime or your ancestors’ lifetime?

3Tell your descendants about it; they need to tell their descendants, and so on.

Joel 1:1-3

4What the cutting locusts left the swarming locusts have eaten. What the swarming locusts left the hopping locusts have eaten. What the hopping locusts left the stripping locusts have eaten.

5Wake up, you drunks, and cry; wail, wine drinkers, for the sweet wine you can’t find anymore.

6A nation has invaded my land, powerful and without number. Its teeth are like a lion’s teeth; it has fangs like a lioness.

7It’s destroyed my grapevines and ruined my fig trees. It’s stripped off their bark and thrown it away, so their branches are bare and white.

Joel 1:4-7

8Wail like a virgin wearing burlap for her young groom.

9The grain offering and liquid offering have stopped in Yahveh’s Temple. The priests, Yahveh’s ministers, mourn.

10The fields are ruined, the land is bare because the grain has been destroyed, the new wine has dried up, the fresh oil has failed.

11Despair, farmers; wail, vinedressers, for the wheat and barley; the harvest is ruined.

12The grapevines have shriveled up, the fig trees have withered; the pomegranate, palm, and apple trees as well. The fruit trees in the orchard have dried up, the joy among the people too.

Joel 1:8-12

A Call to Repentance

13Put on burlap and chant a dirge, priests; wail, ministers at the altar! Come, spend the night in burlap, ministers of God, because the grain offerings and liquid offerings have stopped in his Temple.

14Consecrate a fast, declare a solemn assembly; gather the elders and the people to Yahveh’s Temple, and cry out to him.

15Dread the day! The day of Yahveh is upon us, a day of destruction that’s coming from the Almighty.

16Hasn’t food disappeared in front of us, and happiness left the Temple?

17Seeds shrivel under the clods; barns stand empty; granaries are abandoned because the crops have failed.

Joel 1:13-17

18The cattle are bawling! Herds are wandering aimlessly because there’s no pasture; flocks of sheep are bleating in misery.

19I’m calling out to you, Yahveh, because fire has burned off the open range; flames have burned up the trees in the fields.

20Even the wild animals pant for you because the streams have dried up and fire has scorched the pastures.

Joel 1:18-20

The Day of Yahveh
