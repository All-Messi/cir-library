Jeremiah Chapter 47

1 A Message from Yahveh to Jeremiah About the Philistines Before Pharaoh Conquered Gaza

2 Yahveh says, “A flood is swelling up from the north; it will become a tsunami. It’ll flood the land and everything in it, the cities and those who live in them. People will cry out; everybody in the land will wail.

3 Because of the noise of stallions, rushing chariots, wheels, fathers won’t look back for their children; they’ll be too overwhelmed

4 by what’s coming to destroy the Philistines, to cut off from Tyre and Sidon every ally that’s left. Yahveh will destroy the Philistines, the remnant along the coast of Crete.

5 Gaza will be bald; Ashkelon will lie in ruins. Remnant in the plain, how long will you gash yourselves?

6 Sword of Yahveh, how long before you’re still, before you go back into your sheath?

7 But how can it be still when Yahveh has ordered it to attack against Ashkelon and the seacoast?"
