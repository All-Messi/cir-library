Numbers Chapter 34

1-2Yahveh told Moses to say to Israel, “When you go into Canaan, 3your south border will run from the Wilderness of Zin alongside Edom from the end of the Dead Sea eastward. 4Your border will turn from the south to the ascent of Akrabbim and go on along to Zin. It will end south of Kadesh-barnea, go out to Hazaraddar, and on along to Azmon. 5The border will turn from Azmon to where the River of Egypt empties into the Mediterranean Sea.

Num 34:1-5

6“Your west border will be the Mediterranean Sea. 7“Your north border will stretch from the Mediterranean Sea to Mount Hor, 8Lebo-hamath, Zedad, 9Ziphron, and end at Hazar-enan. 10“Your east border will run from Hazar-enan to Shepham 11down to Riblah on the east of Ain down to the slopes east of the Sea of Chinnereth. 12It will go down to the Jordan and on down to the Dead Sea.”

3Moses told Israel, “That’s the land you’ll inherit by lot, land Yahveh told you to give to the-nine-and-a-half tribes. 14-15Reuben, Gad, and the Manasseh half-tribe have received their inheritance 15beyond the Jordan.”

Num 34:6-15

Leaders for Dividing the Land

16Yahveh told Moses, 17“Eleazar the priest and Joshua Ben-Nun will divide up the land. 18Name one leader from each tribe to divide it up:

19from Judah, Caleb Ben-Jephunneh;

20from Simeon, Samuel Ben-Ammihud;

21from Benjamin, Elidad Ben-Chislon;

22from Dan, Bukki Ben-Jogli;

23from Joseph—Manasseh, Hanniel Ben-Ephod; 24 —Ephraim, Kemuel Ben-Shiphtan;

25from Zebulun, Elizaphan Ben-Parnach;

26from Issachar, Paltiel Ben-Azza;

27from Asher, Ahihud Ben-Shelomi;

28from Naphtali, Pedahel Ben-Ammihud.”

29Yahveh commissioned those men to divide up Canaan for Israel.

Num 34:16-29
