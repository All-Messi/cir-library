Numbers Chapter 3

1 The Generations of Aaron and Moses When Yahveh Spoke to Moses on Mount Sinai Duties of Levites

2 The sons of Aaron were Nadab, the firstborn, Abihu, Eleazar, and Ithamar—3anointed and ordained as priests. 4In the Wilderness of Sinai, Nadab and Abihu died from offering before Yahveh fire different from what he’d commanded. They didn’t have any children, so Eleazar and Ithamar served as priests during Aaron’s lifetime.

5 Yahveh told Moses, 6"Set the tribe of Levi aside to serve Aaron. 7They’ll perform duties for him and the whole community at the Tent of Meeting. They’ll service the Tabernacle 8and keep the furnishings of the Tent of Meeting in assisting with the duties of the people. 9Give the Levites to Aaron and his sons. 10Appoint Aaron and his sons to carry out the duties of the priesthood; execute any layman that tries to come near."

11 Again Yahveh told Moses, 12"I’ve taken the Levites from the people in place of the firstborn. 13The firstborn belong to me. When I struck down the firstborn in Egypt, I set apart to myself the firstborn people and animals in Israel. The Levites belong to me; I’m Yahveh."

14 Yahveh told Moses in the Wilderness of Sinai, 15"Count the descendants of Levi by families—every male a month old and up." 16Moses counted them:

17 the sons of Levi: Gershon, Kohath, and Merari.

18 the clans descended from Gershon: Libni and Shimei.

19 the clans descended from Kohath: Amram, Izhar, Hebron, and Uzziel.

20 the clans descended from Merari: Mahli and Mushi.

21 The Libnites and Shimeites descended from Gershon. 22Their males a month old and up numbered 7,500. 23They camped west of the Tabernacle, behind it. 24Eliasaph Ben- Lael was family leader among the Gershonites. 25The sons of Gershon serviced the Tent of Meeting, including the Tabernacle and tent, its covering, the screen for the door to the Tent of Meeting, 26the hangings around the court, and the screen for the door to the court around the Tabernacle, the altar, and its cords.

27 The Amramites, Izharites, Hebronites, and Uzzielites descended from Kohath. 28Their males a month old and up numbered 8,600; they serviced the sanctuary. 29They camped south of the Tabernacle. 30Elizaphan Ben-Uzziel was family leader among the Kohathites. 31The sons of Kohath serviced the Ark, the table, the lampstand, the altars, the utensils of the sanctuary they perform service with, and the screen.

32 Eleazar Ben-Aaron, the chief leader from Levi, oversaw the ones that performed duties in the sanctuary.

33 The Mahlites and Mushites descended from Merari. 34Their males a month old and up numbered 6,200. 35Zuriel Ben-Abihail was family leader of Merari. They camped north of the Tabernacle. 36The sons of Merari serviced the frames of the Tabernacle, its bars, posts, bases, equipment, 37together with the posts around the court with their bases, pegs, and cords.

38 Moses, Aaron, and his sons camped east of the Tabernacle and performed duties in the sanctuary for the obligation of Israel. Any layman that would come near was to be executed. 39The Levites that Moses and Aaron counted a month old and up numbered 22,000. Redemption of the Firstborn

40 Yahveh told Moses, “Count by name every firstborn male in Israel a month old and up. 41Take the Levites for me in place of Israel’s firstborn sons, and take the Levites’ livestock in place of Israel’s firstborn livestock."

42 Moses counted Israel’s firstborn: 4322,273 males a month old and up.

44 Yahveh told Moses, 45"The Levites belong to me; I’m Yahveh. 46For the ransom of the 273 firstborn of Israel above the number of Levites, 47take 2 oz 5 shekels per head, shekels of the sanctuary (20 gerahs to a shekel). 48Give the ransom money to Aaron and his sons."

49 Moses took the ransom money from the ones above the number ransomed by the Levites. 50From the firstborn he took the money in shekels of the sanctuary (1,365 shekels 34 lbs), 51and gave it to Aaron and his sons. Duties of the Kohathites
