Numbers Chapter 29

1 The first day, seventh month, hold a holy convocation; don’t work. Celebrate the Festival of Trumpets. 2Offer a burnt offering as an aroma pleasing to Yahveh: 1 bull, 1 ram, and 7 male lambs a year old without defect. 3Offer with them their grain offerings of fine flour mixed with olive oil—.3 of a measure for the bull, .2 for the ram, 4and .1 for each lamb. 5Offer 1 male goat for a sin offering to atone for yourselves. 6Offer them in addition to the burnt offering at the new moon and its grain offering, and the daily burnt offering and its grain offering. Offer their liquid offerings. Day of Atonement Offerings 7"The tenth day, seventh month, hold a holy convocation; fast and don’t work. 8Present a burnt offering to Yahveh as a pleasing aroma: 1 bull, 1 ram, and 7 male lambs a year old without defect. 9Offer them together with the grain offering of fine flour mixed with olive oil—.3 of a measure for the bull, .2 for the ram, 10and .1 for each lamb. 11Offer one male goat for a sin offering in addition to the sin offering of atonement and the daily burnt offering, grain offering, and liquid offering. 12"The fifteenth day, seventh month, hold a holy convocation; don’t work. Observe a festival to Yahveh for a week. 13Present a burnt offering as an aroma pleasing to Yahveh: 13 bulls, 2 rams, and 14 male lambs a year old without defect. 14Offer them together with their grain offerings of fine flour mixed with olive oil—.3 of a measure for each bull, .2 for each ram, 15and .1 for each lamb. 16Offer one male goat as a sin offering in addition to the daily burnt offering, grain offering, and liquid offering. 17"The second day, offer 12 bulls, 2 rams, and 14 male lambs a year old without defect. 18Offer them together with the grain offering and liquid offering for each bull, ram, and lamb in standard fashion. 19Offer 1 male goat as a sin offering in addition to the daily burnt offering, its grain offering, and liquid offering. 20-22"The third day, offer 11 bulls. Do the rest like the second day. 23-25"The fourth day, offer 10 bulls. Do the rest like the second day. 26-28"The fifth day, offer 9 bulls. Do the rest like the second day. 29-31"The sixth day, offer 8 bulls. Do the rest like the second day. 32-34"The seventh day, offer 7 bulls. Do the rest like the second day. 35"The eighth day, hold a solemn assembly; don’t work. 36Present a burnt offering as an aroma pleasing to Yahveh: 1 bull, 1 ram, and 7 male lambs a year old without defect. [37-38] 39"Present those to Yahveh at your scheduled times in addition to your offerings for fulling a vow, free will offerings, burnt offerings, and the liquid offerings for your peace offerings."

40 Moses told Israel everything Yahveh commanded him. Men’s Vows
