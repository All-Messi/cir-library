Numbers Chapter 1

1 On the first day, second month, second year after they left Egypt, Yahveh told Moses at Sinai in the Tent of Meeting, 2"Take a census by families. Count the men 320 years old and up that can go to war. You and Aaron count them by armies. 4To help you, appoint one man from each tribe as head of his ancestor’s house. 5They’ll stand with you: from Reuben, Elizur Ben-Shedeur;

6 from Simeon, Shelumiel Ben-Zurishaddai;

7 from Judah, Nahshon Ben-Amminadab;

8 from Issachar, Nethanel Ben-Zuar;

9 from Zebulun, Eliab Ben-Helon;

10 from Joseph’s son Ephraim, Elishama Ben-Ammihud; from Joseph’s son Manasseh, Gamaliel Ben-Pedahzur;

11 from Benjamin, Abidan Ben-Gideoni;

12 from Dan, Ahiezer Ben-Ammishaddai;

13 from Asher, Pagiel Ben-Ochran;

14 from Gad, Eliasaph Ben-Deuel;

15 from Naphtali, Ahira Ben-Enan.

16 Those are the appointed leaders by tribes, the heads of clans in Israel."

17 Moses and Aaron took the men God had designated 18and gathered the community on the first day, second month. [19]

20 The count came out as follows: 2146,500 from Reuben, Israel’s firstborn; 22-2359,300 from Simeon, 24-2545,650 from Gad, 26-2774,600 from Judah, 28-2954,400 from Issachar, 30-3157,400 from Zebulun, 32-3340,500 from Ephraim, 34-3532,300 from Manasseh, 36-3735,400 from Benjamin, 38-3962,700 from Dan 40-4141,500 from Asher, 42-4353,400 from Naphtali, 44-46The total was 603,550.

47 They didn’t count the Levites in the military census. 48Yahveh told Moses, 49"Don’t count the Levites. 50Put them in charge of the Tabernacle and everything associated with it. They’ll transport it and its furnishings, take care of it, and camp around it. 51When it sets out, they’ll dismantle it. When it camps, they’ll assemble it. Execute any layman that comes near. 52The Israelites will camp in groups by their standard in their armies. 53But the Levites will camp around the Tabernacle so there won’t be any wrath on the Israelites. [54a] Israel did everything Yahveh commanded Moses. The Layout of Israel’s Camp
