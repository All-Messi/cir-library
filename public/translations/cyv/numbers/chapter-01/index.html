Numbers Chapter 1

1On the first day, second month, second year after they left Egypt, Yahveh told Moses at Sinai in the Tent of Meeting, 2“Take a census by families. Count the men 320 years old and up that can go to war. You and Aaron count them by armies. 4To help you, appoint one man from each tribe as head of his ancestor’s house. 5They’ll stand with you: from Reuben, Elizur Ben-Shedeur;

6from Simeon, Shelumiel Ben-Zurishaddai;

7 7from Judah, Nahshon Ben-Amminadab;

8 8from Issachar, Nethanel Ben-Zuar;

9 9from Zebulun, Eliab Ben-Helon;

10from Joseph’s son Ephraim, Elishama Ben-Ammihud; from Joseph’s son Manasseh, Gamaliel Ben-Pedahzur;

11from Benjamin, Abidan Ben-Gideoni;

12from Dan, Ahiezer Ben-Ammishaddai;

13from Asher, Pagiel Ben-Ochran;

14from Gad, Eliasaph Ben-Deuel;

15from Naphtali, Ahira Ben-Enan.

6Those are the appointed leaders by tribes, the heads of clans in Israel.”
