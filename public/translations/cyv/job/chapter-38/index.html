Job Chapter 38

1God answered Job out of the whirlwind: 2“Who is this that confuses my counsel with ignorant words?

3Gird up* like a man; I’ll ask you some questions, and you answer me.

4Where were you when I laid the foundation for the earth? Tell me if you have any idea.

5Who specified its measurements or stretched out a line on it? Surely you know!

6What did its pylons rest on? Who laid its cornerstone

7when the morning stars sang together and all God’s angels shouted for joy?

Job 38:1-7

8“Who shut the ocean in boundaries, when it came bursting out of the womb,

9when I clothed it with clouds and swaddled it with deep darkness,

10when I set its boundaries, bolted its doors,

11and said, ‘You can’t go beyond this point; here’s where your proud waves stop’?

Job 38:8-11

12“Have you ever commanded the morning and made dawn happen

13so it can spread to the ends of the earth and bring to an end night’s evil?

14The earth is stamped like clay under a seal; its features stand out like folds in a robe.

15Light is withheld from sinners, and their uplifted arm is broken.

Job 38:12-15

16“Have you traveled to the springs of the ocean or walked in the recesses of the deep?

17Have the gates of death been revealed to you; have you seen the doors into deep darkness?

18Do you have any idea how big the earth is? Tell me if you know that.

Job 38:16-18

19“Where’s the road to where light lives? Where’s the place for darkness,

20so you can take it to its territory and make out the paths to its home?

21You know because you were born there, right! and you’ve lived so long!

22Have you entered the storehouses for snow or seen where I keep the hail

23I reserve for troubled times, for the time of war and battle?

24Where’s the road to the source of light or where the east wind scatters over the earth?

Job 38:19-24

25“Who carved a channel for the flood or laid out the course of a thunderbolt

26to bring rain on land with no people— on a vacant desert—

27to satisfy desolate land and make grass sprout?

28Does rain have a father? Who has begotten the drops of dew?

29Whose womb did ice come out of? Who gave birth to frost from the sky?

30Water gets as hard as rock; its surface freezes over and imprisons the deep.

Job 38:25-30

31“Can you tie up the chains of the Pleiades or untie the cords of Orion?

32Can you lead out the signs of the Zodiac in their seasons and guide the Bear with her cubs?

33Do you know the laws of the sky or manage the earth with them? 34“Can you shout to the clouds to get enough water to cover you?

35Do you send out lightning bolts on their way? Do they report to you, ‘We’re here’?

Job 38:31-35

36“Who endowed the heart with wisdom or gave intelligence to the mind?

37Who can count the clouds or tip over the water jars in the sky

38when dust hardens into clumps and forms clods?

Job 38:36-38

39“Can you stalk prey for a lion or satisfy the appetite of young lions

40when they crouch in their dens and lie in wait in their lairs?

41Who fixes feed for the ravens when their young call out to God and wander around with nothing to eat?

Job 38:39-41
