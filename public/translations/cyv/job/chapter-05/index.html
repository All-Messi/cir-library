Job Chapter 5

1“Call out for help now; will anybody answer you? Which of the angels will you turn to?

2Adversity slays the foolish; anger kills the simpleminded.

3I’ve seen the foolish taking root and having their progress immediately cursed.

4Their children are never safe, they’re oppressed in the courts, and there’s nobody to deliver them.

5The hungry eat up their harvest; briers take over it; schemers are eager to get their wealth;

6 6because adversity doesn’t sprout from the dirt, and trouble doesn’t grow out of the ground. Job 5:1-6 7“Mortals are born for trouble like sparks fly up from burning wood. 8“I would seek God; I’d place my cause in front of him

9 9who does great, unsearchable things, wonders without number.

10 10He sends rain to water the fields.

11 11He sets the humble up high and lifts mourners to safety.

12 12He frustrates devious plots so they can’t succeed.

13 13He catches the wise in their own shrewdness and quickly thwarts wily people’s schemes.

14 14They run into darkness in the daytime and grope at noon as at night.

15 15But he saves people from the sword and the poor from the powerful.

16So the helpless have hope; the sinful have to shut their mouths.

Job 5:

7-16 17“How happy they are that God corrects; so don’t despise discipline by the Almighty,

18because he inflicts pain but gives relief, he wounds in order to heal.

9He’ll deliver you from six troubles; even in seven, tragedy won’t touch you.

0In famine he’ll redeem you from death; in war he’ll save you from the sword.

1He’ll hide you from the scourge of the tongue; you won’t panic when violence comes.

2You’ll laugh at destruction and famine; wild animals won’t scare you.

Job 5:17-2

3You’ll be at peace with the rocks in the field; the wild animals will be at peace with you.

4You’ll know your tent is secure; you’ll inventory your holdings and find nothing missing.

5You’ll have lots of children, offspring like grass on the ground.

6You’ll come to your grave at a ripe old age, like a bundle of wheat at harvest time.

7We’ve examined this and found it’s true; listen to it for your own good.”
