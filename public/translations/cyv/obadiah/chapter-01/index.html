Obadiah 1

CYV Translation

Obadiah Chapter 1

1The Vision of Obadiah 2“I’ll make you a minor player among the nations

3Your arrogance has deceived you, and tell yourselves that nobody can bring you down.

4Though you’ve built high like an eagle I’ll bring you down.

Obad 1-4

5“If thieves came to you at night—

6But how you’ll be pillaged, Esau,

7Your allies will force you out of your borders;

8At that time,” Yahveh says, “won’t I destroy and those with understanding from Esau’s mountain?

9Your strong troops will be afraid, Teman; will be cut down in the slaughter.

Obad 5-9

10“Because of your violence against Jacob your brother, you’ll be covered with disgrace and permanently eliminated.

11You stood aloof

12You shouldn’t have gloated over your brother’s misfortune; you shouldn’t have celebrated over Judah’s destruction

13You shouldn’t have entered my people’s gate

14You shouldn’t have stood at the fork in the road or have betrayed the survivors in their misfortune.

Obad 10-14

15“Yahveh’s judgment is coming on all the nations.

16You and those nations will drink the same punishment

17But on Mount Zion some will escape, Jacob’s house will return to reclaim its possession.

18Then Jacob’s house will become a fire, Yahveh has said it.

Obad 15-18

19Then my people in the Negev will take over Esau’s mountains; the ones in the western foothills will occupy the Philistine plain. They’ll take over the fields of Ephraim and Samaria;

20Exiles from Israel will replace the Phoenicians as far north as Zarephath; the captives from Jerusalem that are in Sepharad [north]

21All those who were rescuedLXX will go up to Mount Zion The kingdom will belong to Yahveh.

Obad 19-21
