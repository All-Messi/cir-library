Partial Conquest of Remaining Territory

1:1After Joshua died, the people asked Yahveh, “Who will go up first to fight the Canaanites?”

2He said, “Judah. I’ll deliver the land to him.”

3Judah said to his brother Simeon, “Go with me to my territory to fight the Canaanites, and I’ll go with you to yours.”

So he went with him.

4Judah went up and Yahveh delivered 10,000 Canaanites and Perizzites to him at Bezek. 5-6They attacked Adoni-bezek 6and he fled, but they caught him and cut off his thumbs and big toes. 7He said, “Seventy kings with their thumbs and big toes cut off used to pick up scraps under my table. God has done to me what I’ve done.” They took him to Jerusalem, where he died.

8Judah fought Jerusalem and took it. They struck it with the sword and set it on fire. 9Then they went down to fight the Canaanites in the hill country, the Negev, and the western foothills. 10Judah attacked the Canaanites in Hebron (called Kiriath-arba before) and defeated Sheshai, Ahiman, and Talmai. Wives for Benjamites

We’ve sworn by Yahveh not to give them our daughters for wives.” Jud 21:1-7

8They said, “What tribe didn’t come up?” 9When the people mustered, not one person from Jabesh-gilead was there. 10The community sent 12,000 of their bravest men, “Go strike the people in Jabesh-gilead with the sword, including women and children. 11Destroy every male and every woman that has been with a man.” 12They found 400 young virgins in Jabesh-gilead and brought them to the camp at Shiloh in Canaan.
