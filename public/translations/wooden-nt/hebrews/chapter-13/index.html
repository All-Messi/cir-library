Hebrews Chapter 13

1 Let brotherly-love continue. 2Do┘not└forget the love-of-strangers, for through this some were-unaware, having-entertained angels. 3Remember the-ones bound as bound-with (them), the-ones being-mistreated as also yourselves being in (the) body. 4(Let) [the] marriage (be) honorable in all-people and the bed (be) undefiled, for [the] God will-judge fornicators and adulterers. 5(Let) the way-(of life of you be) without-love-of-silver, being-satisfied with-the-things being-present, for he has said, “I will┘not└abandon you; neither certainly will-I-leave┘[not] you└behind,” 6so-that, being-confident, we say,

7 Remember the leaders of-you, who spoke to-you the word of-[the] God; considering the outcome of-the way-of-life of-whom, imitate the/their faith. 8Jesus Christ (is) the same yesterday and today and unto the ages3/forever3. 9Do┘not└be-carried-away by-various and new (doctrines), for (it is) good for the heart to-be-established by-grace, not by-foods in-which the-ones walking/conducting-themselves were┘not└profited. 10We-have (an) altar of-which the-ones serving the tabernacle do┘not└have (a) right to-eat; 11for the blood of-which animals is-carried concerning sin into the (Holy of-) Holies by the high-priest; the bodies of-these are-burned-up outside the camp. 12Wherefore also Jesus, in-order-that he-might-sanctify the people through [the] his-own blood, suffered outside the gate. 13For-that-very-reason-then, let-us-go to him outside the camp, bearing the reproach of-him; 14For we-do┘not└have here (a) remaining city, but we-look-for the coming (city). 15Through him [therefore]ms let-us-offer-up (a) sacrifice of-praise to-[the] God through everything, that is, (the) fruit of-(the)-lips, confessingpl the name of-him. 16And do┘not└forget [the] doing-good and sharing, for by such “sacrifices” [the] God is-well-pleased.

18 Pray concerning us, for we-are-persuaded that we-have (a) clear conscience, desiring in all-things to conduct (ourselves) well. 19And I-encourage (you) to-do this more-exceedingly, in-order-that I-may-be-restored to-you more-quickly/sooner.

22 Now I-encourage you, brothers, to-bear-with the word of-exhortation, for I-sent/wrote to-you even through brief-things2/briefly2.

23 Know (that) the brother of us, Timothy has-been-released, with whom, if he-comes soon, I-will-see you.

24 Greet all the leaders of-you and all the saints; the-ones from [the] Italy greet you.
