Hebrews Chapter 1

1 In-many-parts and in-many ways [the] God of-old, having-spoken to-the falters in the prophets 2at (the) last of-[the] these days he-spoke to-us in (a) Son, whom he-appointed heir of-all-things, through whom also he-made the worlds-ages; 3who being (the) direct-shining of-the/his glory and (the) exact-image of-the substance/nature of-him, and upholding [the] all-things by-the word of-the power of-him, having-made purification of-[the] sins, sat-down on (the) right-(hand) of-the Majesty in (the) high-things, 4having-become as-much better than-the angels as he-has-inherited (a) more-excellent name than they.

5 For to-which of-the angels did-he┘ever└say,

6 And again when he-brings the Firstborn into the world, he-says,

7 And to the angels he-says,

8 But to the Son (he says),

11 They will-perish, but you will-remain,

13 To which of-the angels did-he┘ever└say,

14 Are-they not all ministering spirits sent for ministry on-account-of the-ones going to-inherit salvation?
