Ephesians Chapter 1

1 Paul, (an) apostle of-Christ Jesus through the will of-God, to-the saints [the] being [in Ephesus]ms and faithful in Christ Jesus. 2Grace to-you and peace from God (the) Father of-us and (the) Lord Jesus Christ.

3 Blessed (be) the God and Father of-the Lord of-us Jesus Christ, the-One having-blessed us with every spiritual blessing in-regard-to heavenly matters in Christ, 4even-as he chose us in him before (the) foundation of-(the)-world for-us to-be holy and blameless in-the-presence-of him in love, 5having-predestined us to sonship through Jesus Christ to himself, according-to the good-pleasure of-the will of-him, 6to (the) praise of-(the) glory of-the grace of-him that he graced┘us└with in the Beloved; 7in whom we-have [the] redemption through the blood of-him, the forgiveness of-[the] trespasses, according-to the wealth of-the grace of-him 8that he-lavished on us in all wisdom and insight, 9having-made-known to-us the mystery of-the will of-him, according-to the good-pleasure of-him that he-purposed in him 10for responsibility of-the fullness of-[the] times, to-sum-up [the] all-things in [the] Christ—the-things in the heavens and the-things in the earth in him; 11in whom also we-were-made-an-inheritance, having-been-predestined according-to (the) purpose of-the-One working [the] all-things according-to the counsel of-the will of-him, 12unto [the] our being to (the) praise-(the)-glory of-him the-ones having-hoped in [the] Christ; 13in whom you also, having-heard the word of-[the] truth, the good-news of-the salvation of-us, in whom also having-believed you-were-sealed with-the Holy [the] Spirit of-[the] promise; 14[who is] (the) pledge of-the inheritance of-us, unto (the) redemption of-the possession, to (the) praise of-the glory of-him.

15 On-account-of this I-also, having-heard the according-to you faith in the Lord Jesus and the love [the] toward all the saints, 16do┘not└cease giving-thanks for you, making mention (of you) in the prayers of-me; 17in-order-that the God of-the Lord of-us Jesus Christ, the Father of-[the] glory, might-give you (a) spirit of wisdom and revelation in (the) knowledge of-him, 18the eyes of-the heart [of-you]ms having-been-enlightened unto [the] your knowing what is the hope of-[the] calling of-him, what (is) the wealth of-[the] glory of-the inheritance of-him among the saints, 19and what (is) the unlimited greatness of the power of-him toward us the-ones believing according-to the working of-the might of-the strength of-him, 20which he-worked in [the] Christ, having-raised him from (the) dead and having-seated (him) on the right (hand) of-him in-regard-to the heavenly (places), 21far-above all rule and authority and power and lordship/dominion and every name named, not only in [the] this age but also in the next, 22and he-put all-things under the feet of-him, and gave him (to be) head above all-things to–the church, which is the body of-him, the fullness of-the-One filling [the] all-things in all.
