1 Peter Chapter 4

1So Christ having-suffered in-(the)-flesh, you also arm-yourselves-with the same attitude, because the-one having-suffered in-(the)-flesh has-ceased sins 2unto [the] no-longer living the remaining time in (the) flesh by (the) desires of-men, but (the) will of-God. 3For the having passed-by time (is) sufficient to-have-carried-out the will of-the Gentiles, having-pursued-(a)-course/pattern in indecencies, lusts, drunkenness[es], revelries, drunken orgies, and abominable idolatries, 4in which they-think-strange your not running-with (them) in the same excess of-[the]-reckless-living, slandering (you), 5who-will-give-(an)-account to-the-One having-prepared to-judge (the) living and (the) dead. 6For to this (end) the-gospel-was-preached even to-(the)-dead in-order-that they-might-be-judged according-to men in-(the)-flesh, but living according-to God in-(the)-spirit.

7Now the end of-all-things has-come-near. So be-of-sound-judgment and be-sober unto prayer 8before all-things, having love constant to each another, because love covers (a) multitude of-sins, 9(being) hospitable to one-another without complaining, 10even-as each received (a) gift, ministering it to one-another as good stewards of-(the)-manifold/manifold grace of-God. 11If anybody speaks, (let it be) as utterances of-God; if anybody serves (let it be) as out-of (the) strength that [the] God supplies, in-order-that in all-things [the] God may-be-glorified through Jesus Christ, to-whom is [the] glory and [the] might forevermore5. Amen.

19so-that also let┘the-ones suffering according-to the till of-[the] God└entrust their souls to-the faithful creator in doing-good.
