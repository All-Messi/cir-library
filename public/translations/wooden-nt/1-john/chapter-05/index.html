1 John Chapter 5

1 Every the-one believing that Jesus is the Christ has-been-begotten by [the] God, and every the-one loving the-One having-begotten loves also the-One having-been-begotten by him. 2By this we-know that we-love the children of-[the] God: when we-love [the] God and do the commandments of-him. 3For this is the love of-[the] God: [in-order]-that we-keep the commandments of-him, and the commandments of-him are not heavy/hard. 4Because every the-one having-been-begotten by [the] God overcomes the world, and this is the victory [the] having-overcome the world: the faith of-us. 5And who is the-one overcoming the world unless2 the-one believing that Jesus is the Son of-[the] God.

6 This is the-one having-come by water and blood—Jesus Christ, not by [the] water only, but by [the] water and by [the] blood; and the Spirit is the-One testifying because the Spirit is the truth. 7Because three are the-ones testifying: 8the Spirit and the water and the blood, and the three are for the one-thing. 9If we-receive the testimony of-[the] men, the testimony of-[the] God is greater, because this is the testimony of-[the] God that he-has-testified about the Son of-himself. 10The-one believing in [the] Son of-[the] God has the testimony in him; the-one not believing [the] God has-made him (a) liar, because he-has┘not└believed in the testimony that [the] God has-testified about the Son of-him. 11And this is the testimony: that [the] God gave to-us eternal life, and this [the] life is in the Son of-him. 12The-one having the Son has [the] life; the-one not having the Son of-[the] God does┘not└have [the] life.

13 I-wrote these-things to-you, in-order-that you-may-know that you-have eternal life, the-ones believing in the name of-the Son of-[the] God. 14And this is the boldness that we-have toward him: that if we-ask anything according-to the will of-him, he-hears us. 15And if we know that he-hears us with-respect-to whatever2 we-ask, we-know that because we-have the requests that we-have-asked from him.

16 Whoever2 sees the brother of-him sinning (a) sin not unto death, he-will-ask and he-will-give life to-him, to-the-ones sinning not unto death. (A) sin unto death exists; I-am┘not└speaking about that (sin) [in-order]-that he-should-ask. 17Every injustice is sin, and (a) sin not unto death exists.

18 We-know that every the-one having-been-begotten by [the] God does┘not└sin; but the-one begotten by [the] God keeps himself, and the evil-one does┘not└touch him. 19We-know that we-are of/from [the] God, and the whole world lies in the evil-one. 20But we-know that the Son of-[the] God has-come, and he-has-given us understanding, in-order-that we-may-know the true-One, and we-are in the true-(One). In the Son of-him Jesus Christ—this is the true God and eternal life. 21Little-children, guard/keep yourselves from [the] idols.
