Mark Chapter 11

1And when they-are-nearing [to] Jerusalem, to Bethphage and Bethany, to the Mount of-[the] Olives, he-sends two of-the disciples of-him, 2and he-says to-them, “Go into the village [the] across-from you, and immediately, entering into it, you-will-find (a) donkey-colt tied, on which nobody of-men [not-yet]ms sat. 4And they-went-away and found (a) donkey-colt tied at (the) gate outside by the street, and they-untie it. 5And some of-the-ones standing there began-to-say to-them, “What are-you-doing, untying the donkey-colt?” 6And the/they said to-them even-as [the] Jesus said, and they-let them.

11And he-entered into Jerusalem into the temple; and having-looked-around-at all-things, the hour already being late, he-entered into Bethany with the twelve.

12And the next-day, they having-come from Bethany, he-was-hungry. 13And having-seen (a) fig tree from-afar having leaves, he-came (to see) whether then he-would-find anything on it. 18And the chief-priests and the scribes heard and began-to-seek how they-might-destroy him, for they-were-afraid-of him, for all the crowd was-being-amazed at the teaching of-him. 19And when evening came, they-would-travel outside the city.

0And passing-by in-the-morning, they-saw the fig-tree withered from (the) root. 21And having-been-reminded, [the] Peter says to-him, “Rabbi, behold, the fig-tree that you-cursed has-withered.

7And they-come again into Jerusalem; and in the temple, he walking, the chief-priests and the scribes and the elders come to him, 28and they-begin-by-saying, “By what authority are-you-doing these-things, and who gave you [the] this authority in-order-that you-may-do these-things?”

29And [the] Jesus said to-them, “I-will-ask you one matter, and you-answer me and I-will-tell you by what authority I-do these-things. 30The baptism [the] of-John—was-it from heaven or from man? Answer me.”
