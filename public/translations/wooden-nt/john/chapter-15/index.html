John Chapter 15

As the branch is┘not└able to-bear fruit from itself unless2 it-remains in the vine; so you neither unless2 you-remain in me. 5I am the vine; your (are) the branches. If they-kept the word of-me, they-will┘also└keep [the] yours. 21But they-will-do all these-things to you on-account-of the name of-me, because they-do┘not└know the-One having-sent me. 22If I-had┘not└come and spoken to-them, they-would┘not└have sin. 26When the Comforter comes whom I will-send you from the Father, the Spirit of-[the] truth that comes from the Father—that-one will-bear-witness with me. 27And you also bear-witness because you-are with me.
