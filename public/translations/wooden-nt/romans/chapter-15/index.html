0Now I-urge you, [brothers]ms, by the Lord of-us Jesus Christ and by the love of-the Spirit to-strive-with me in [the] prayers for me to [the] God, 31in-order-that I-may-be-delivered from the-ones disobeying in [the] Judaea and (that) the ministry of-me [the] in Jerusalem may-be acceptable to-the saints, 32in-order-that with joy, having-come to you by (the) will of-God I-may-have-a-time-of-rest-with you. 33Now the God of-[the] peace (be) with all of-you.
