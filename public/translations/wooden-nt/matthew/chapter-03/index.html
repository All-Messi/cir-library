Matthew Chapter 3

1 And in [the] those days, John the Baptist comes preaching in the desert of-[the] Judaea 2[and]ms, saying, “Repent, for the kingdom of-[the] heaven came-near.” 3For this is the-one having-been-spoken through Isaiah the prophet, saying,

7 And having-seen all the Pharisees and Sadducees coming to the baptism of-him, he-said to-them, “Generations of-snakes! He will-baptize you with (the) Holy Spirit and fire, 12the winnowing-shovel of-whom (is) in-the hand of-him, and he-will-thoroughly-clean-up the threshing-floor of-him, and he-will-gather the grain of-him into the/his granary, but the chaff he-will-burn-up with unquenchable fire."

16 And [the] Jesus, having-been-baptized, came-up immediately from the water, and behold the sky[s] was-opened [to-him]ms, and he-saw [the]ms Spirit of-[the]ms God coming-down like (a) dove [and]ms coming onto him. 17And, behold, (a) voice (came) out-of the sky[s], saying, “This is the beloved [the] Son of-me, in whom I-delighted."
