Revelation Chapter 19

1After these-things I-heard as-it-were (the) loud voice of-(a)-large multitude in [the] heaven, saying, “Halleluia; [The] salvation and [the] glory and the power of-the God of-us,

2 2because the judgments of-you (are) true and just, because he-judged the great [the] prostitute that corrupted the earth with the immorality of-her, and the blood of-the servants of-him he-avenged from (the) hand of-it.

3And (a)-second-(time) they-said, “Halleluia, and the smoke of-it goes-up unto the ages of the ages/forevermore.”

4And the twenty four [the] elders and the four living-beings fell-down and worshiped [the] God the-One sitting on the throne, saying, “Amen. Halleluia.”

5And (a) voice from the throne came-out, saying, “Praise the God of-us, all the servants of-him, [and/even]ms the-ones fearing him, the small and the great.”

6And I-heard as-it-were (the) sound of-(a)-large crowd and as-it-were (the) sound of-many-waters and as-it-were (the) sound of thunders, saying, “Halleluia, because (the) Lord reigned/reigns, the God [of-us]ms, the Almighty.

7 7Let-us-rejoice and let-us-be-glad, and let-us-give [the] glory to-him; because the wedding/marriage of-the Lamb came, and the wife of-him prepared herself;

8 8and it-was-given to-her in-order-[that] she-clothe-herself with bright, clean linen, for linen is the righteousness[es] of-the saints.”

9And he-says to-me, “Blessed (are) the-ones called to the dinner/marriage-feast of-the Lamb.” And he-says to-me, “These [the] words of-[the] God are true.”

0And I-fell-(down) before the feet of-him to-worship him, and he-says to-me, “See not (to do it). I-am (a) fellow-servant of-you and the brothers of-you the-ones having the testimony of-Jesus. Worship God, for the testimony of-Jesus is the spirit of-[the] prophecy.”

1And I-saw the sky/[the] heaven opened, and behold (there was a) white horse and the-One sitting on it (was) [called]ms faithful and true, and in righteousness he-judges and makes-war. 12And the eyes of-him (were) as-it-were (a) flame of-fire, and on the head of-him (were) many diadems, having (a) name written that nobody knows except2 himself. 13And (he was) clothed with (a) garment dipped/bathed in/with-blood, and the name of-him has-been-called the word of-[the] God. 14And the armies [the-ones] in [the] heaven were-following him on white horses, dressed in clean white linen. 15And out-of the mouth of-him (a) sharp sword was-going-out/was-sticking-out, in-order-that with it he-might strike the nations, and he-himself/he will-shepherd hem with (an) iron rod, and he-himself/he walks/tramples the winepress of-the wine of-the wrath of-the anger of-[the] God the Almighty. 16And he-has on the garment and on the thigh of-him (a) name written: “KING OF-KINGS” and “LORD OF-LORDS.”

7And I-saw one/an angel standing on the sun, and he-shouted [with]ms (a) loud voice, saying to-all the birds [the-ones] flying in (the) midst-of-heaven/(the)-sky, “Come, be-gathered to the great [the] feast of-[the] God, 18in-order-that you-may-eat the flesh[es] of-kings and (the) flesh[es] of-tribunes and (the) flesh[es] of-strong-ones and (the) flesh[es] of-horses and of–the-ones sitting on them and (the) flesh[es] both of-all freemen and slaves and small and great.”

19And I-saw the beast and the kings of-the earth and the armies of-them gathered to-make war with the-One sitting on the horse and with the army of-him. 20And the beast was-seized and with him the false prophet the-one having-done the signs before him, by-which he-deceived the-ones having-received the mark of-the beast and the-ones worshiping the image of-him. And the two were-thrown, living, into the lake of-[the] fire burning with sulphur. 21And the rest were-killed by the sword the-one having-come out-of the mouth of-him, of-the-One sitting on the horse, and all the birds were-fully-satisfied from the flesh[es] of-them.
