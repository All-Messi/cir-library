5from (the) tribe of-Judah twelve thousand having-been-sealed, from (the) tribe of Reuben twelve thousand, from (the) tribe of-Gad twelve thousand,

6from (the) tribe of-Asher twelve thousand, from (the) tribe of Naphtali twelve thousand, from (the) tribe of Manasseh twelve thousand,

7from (the) tribe of Simeon twelve thousand, from (the) tribe of Levi twelve thousand, from (the) tribe of Issachar twelve thousand,

8from (the) tribe of Zebulun twelve thousand, from (the) tribe of Joseph twelve thousand, from the tribe of Benjamin twelve thousand having-been-sealed.

9After these-things I-looked, and, behold, a great crowd that nobody was-able to number [it], out-of every nation and tribe and peoples and languages, standing before the throne and before the Lamb, clothed with-white robes, and palm-branches/fronds (were) in the hands of-them. 10And they-are-shouting with-(a)-loud voice, saying, “[The] salvation to-the God of-us [the-one] sitting on the throne and to-the Lamb.”

11And all the angels stood in-a-circle/around the throne and the elder and the four living-beings, and they-fell-down before the throne on the faces of-them and worshiped [the] God, 12saying, “Amen. [The] blessing and [the] glory and [the] wisdom and [the] thanksgiving and [the] honor and [the] power and [the] strength to-the God of-us unto the ages of-the ages/forevermore5. Amen.”

3And one of the elders answered, saying to-me, “Who are these [the] wrapped/dressed with-respect-to-[the] white [the] robes and whence did-they-come?”

4And I-said, “My lord, you know.” And he-said to-me, “These are the-ones coming out-of the great [the] tribulation and they-washed the robes of-them and made┘them└white in the blood of-the Lamb. 15On-account-of this they-are before the throne of-[the] God and serve him day and night in the sanctuary of-him; and the-One sitting on the throne tabernacles/dwells with them. 16They-will┘not└hunger anymore nor thirst anymore, neither will┘the sun└fall on them nor any heat. 17Because the Lamb [the] in-the-midst-of the throne will-shepherd them, and he-will-lead them to springs of-living water.”
