Micah Chapter 1

1 A Message from the LORD to Micah from Moresheth

2 Listen, every nation;

3 He’s coming down from his place

4 The mountains will mash down under his feet

5 That’s because Jacob rebelled

6 I’ll make Samaria a pile of ruins in the countryside,

7 I’ll smash its idols,

8 Because of this, I’ll mourn and wail,

9 My people’s wound is too deep to heal;

10 Don’t “tell” it in Gath;

11 Just move on into captivity

12 Those living in Maroth grow weak waiting for relief,

13 Harness a “team of horses” to a chariot and flee,

14 Send farewell gifts to Moresheth-gath;

15 I’ll bring a “conqueror” on you, people of Mareshah;

16 Shave off your hair in despair for the children you delight in;
