Micah Chapter 5

1 Muster your troops, Jerusalem;

2 But you, Bethlehem Ephrathah,

3 Israel will be abandoned

4 He’ll stand up and shepherd his flock

5 He’ll give us peace. Mic 5:1-5a

6 They’ll shepherd Assyria with the sword,

7 Then the remnant of Jacob left in the land

8 The remnant left in Jacob

9 He’ll raise his hand against his enemies

11 I’ll demolish your towns

12 I’ll abolish sorcery;

13 I’ll get rid of your carved images and sacred pillars

14 I’ll pull up your Asherah poles

15 I’ll carry out angry revenge
