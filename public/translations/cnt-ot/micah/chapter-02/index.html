Micah Chapter 2

1 Misfortune awaits those who devise sin,

2 They covet fields and seize them;

3 So the LORD says,

4 Your enemies will taunt you with a proverb

5 So you won’t have anyone stretching out a surveying line

7 Are you really saying such a thing, House of Jacob?

8 My people rise up against my people;

9 They evict my people’s women from their pleasant homes

10 Get up and get out of here!

11 If a liar full of hot air tells you

13 The One that opens the gate will lead you out;
