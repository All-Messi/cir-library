Nahum Chapter 1

2 The LORD is an exclusive God

3 He doesn’t get mad quick,

4 At his command seas and rivers dry up,

5 In his presence, mountains quake and hills melt;

6 Who can withstand his indignation?

7 The LORD is good, a fortress in times of trouble;

8 But with an overwhelming flood,

9 Whatever you’re plotting against the LORD

10 His enemies, like tangled thornbushes and staggering people,

11 Who is this counselor of yours

12 This is what the LORD says,

13 I’ll break their yoke off your neck

14 The LORD has issued an edict about you, Assyria:

15 Over the mountains, a messenger is coming with good news,
