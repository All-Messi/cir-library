Psalms Chapter 50

1God, the Mighty One, the LORD, has spoken and calls to earth from sunrise to sunset.

2He shines forth out of Zion in perfect beauty.

3Our God comes; he doesn’t stay quiet; a fire consumes in front of him; a storm rages around him.

4He calls on the sky above and the earth beneath to witness the judgment of his people. 5“Gather my people to me that I’ve made a covenant with by sacrifice.”

6The heavens declare his justice because God himself is judge.

Ps 50:1-6

7“Listen, my people, and I’ll speak; Israel, I’ll bring my charges against you. I’m God, your God.

8I don’t have any complaint about your sacrifices or burnt offerings that are always in front of me.

9But I don’t need any bulls from your barns or male goats from your pens;

10every animal in the forest already belongs to me, the livestock on a thousand hills.

11I know every bird in the mountains; everything that moves in the field is mine.

12If I was hungry, I wouldn’t say anything to you about it, because the whole world belongs to me and everything in it.

13Will I eat the meat of bulls or drink the blood of male goats?

14Offer to me a sacrifice of thanksgiving; keep your vows to the Most High.

15Call on me when there’s trouble; then I’ll rescue you, and you’ll honor me.”

Ps 50:7-15

16But God says to the sinful, “What reason do you have to recite my statutes or discuss my covenant?

17You hate discipline and disregard what I tell you.

18Thieves are your friends; you associate with adulterers.

19You give your mouth free reign; your tongue tells lies.

20You sit around and gossip about your brother; you slander your mother’s son.

21I didn’t speak up when you did those things, so you thought it didn’t matter to me; but now I’ll charge you and lay out my case against you.

Ps 50:16-21

22“Consider this, you that forget about me, or I’ll tear you to pieces, and there won’t be anyone to save you.

23Giving thanks is the sacrifice that honors me; I’ll show you my deliverance if you keep to my path.”

Ps 50:22-23
