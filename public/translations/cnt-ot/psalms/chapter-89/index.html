Psalms Chapter 89

1 I’ll sing forever about the LORD’s enduring love

3 The LORD said, “I’ve made a covenant with my chosen one;

5 The sky praises your wonders

6 Who in the sky can compare with you?

8 Lord GOD of Hosts, who is like you?

9 You rule the raging sea;

10 You’ve crushed Rahab like one that’s slain

11 The sky is yours; the earth belongs to you;

12 You’ve created north and south;

13 You have a powerful arm and a strong hand;

14 Goodness and justice make the foundation for your throne;

15 How blessed people are who know the sound of joy,

16 In your name they celebrate all day;

17 They glory in your strength;

18 Their shield belongs to the LORD;

19 Long ago you said in a vision to your holy people,

20 I found my servant David

21 My hand will make him secure;

22 His enemies won’t outsmart him;

23 I’ll crush his adversaries

24 My faithfulness and enduring love will be with him,

25 I’ll establish his rule as far as the sea

26 He’ll call out to me, ‘My Father,

27 I’ll make him my firstborn,

28 I’ll keep my enduring love for him forever

29 I’ll make his descendants permanently secure,

30 If his descendants leave my Law

33 But I won’t break off my enduring love from him

34 I won’t violate my covenant

35 Once I’ve sworn by my holiness,

36 His descendants will continue permanently,

37 It’ll be secure as permanently as the moon,

38 But, you’ve thrown him away and rejected him;

39 You’ve spurned the covenant with him

40 You’ve broken down his walls

41 Everyone that passes by plunders him;

42 You’ve raised up his adversaries

43 You’ve turned back the edge of his sword

44 You made his splendor cease

45 You’ve shortened his youth

46 How long, LORD,

47 Remember how long life is.

48 Who can live without dying?

49 Where’s your former enduring love

50 Remember the scorn your servants endure,

52 Bless the LORD forever!
