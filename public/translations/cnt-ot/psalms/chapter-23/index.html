Psalms Chapter 23

1The LORD is my shepherd; I’ll have everything I need.

2He beds me down in green pasture and leads me beside still water.

3He refreshes me and guides me along good paths for his honor.

4Even though I walk through the darkest valley, I won’t be afraid, because he’s with me; his rod and staff reassure me.

5You set a table for me in front of my enemies; you anoint my head with oil; my cup runs over.

6Goodness and mercy will follow me all my life, and I’ll live in the LORD’s house forever.

Ps 23:1-6
