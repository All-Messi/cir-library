Psalms Chapter 32

1 How blessed they are whose sin God forgives,

3 When I didn’t acknowledge my sin,

5 When I acknowledged it and said, “I’ll confess my sin to the LORD,”

6 So everyone that’s godly needs to pray to you

7 You’re our hiding place

8 I’ll teach you the way to go;

9 Don’t be like a senseless horse or mule

10 Sinful people incur lots of sorrow,

11 Be happy and celebrate in him;
