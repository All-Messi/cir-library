Genesis Chapter 15

1 After that, a message from the LORD came to Abram in a vision, “Don’t worry, Abram; I’m your shield. You’ll have a great reward."

2 Abram asked, “LORD God, what will you give me? I don’t have any children; Eliezer of Damascus is my heir. 3Since you haven’t given me any offspring, a man born in my household will inherit."

4 A message from the LORD came to him, “He won’t inherit. Your heir will come from your body.” 5He took him outside, “Look at the sky and count the stars if you can. That’s how many descendants you’ll have,"

6 Abram trusted him, and the LORD considered him good because of it.

7 God said, “I’m the LORD who brought you out of Ur in Chaldea to give you this land.” 9"Bring me a three-year-old heifer, goat, and ram, and a turtledove and young pigeon."

10 He brought them, cut them in half—except for the birds—and laid the halves across from each other. 11Birds of prey came down on the carcasses, but he shooed them away.

12 As the sun was going down, he fell into a deep sleep, and an eerie darkness came over him. 13God told him, “Your descendants will be foreigners in a country that enslaves them and oppresses them for 400 years. 14I’ll judge that nation, and your descendants will come out with lots of possessions. 15You will pass on to your ancestors in peace and be buried at a good old age. 16In the fourth generation, they’ll come back here. The sinfulness of the Amorites hasn’t climaxed yet."

17 After sundown, it was pitch dark. A fire pot and flaming torch passed between the halves of the carcasses. 18That day the LORD made a covenant with Abram, “I’ll give your descendants this land from the River of Egypt to the Euphrates River, along with 19the Kenites, Kenizzites, Kadmonites, 20Hittites, Perizzites, Rephaim, 21Amorites, Canaanites, Girgashites, and Jebusites."
