Zephaniah Chapter 3

1 Misfortune awaits the rebellious, defiled, tyrannical city, Jerusalem,

3 Its officials are roaring lions;

4 Its prophets are reckless, devious men;

5 The LORD is good within it;

7 I said, ‘Surely, they’ll respect me now

9 Then I’ll purify the people’s lips

10 From beyond the rivers in Nubia,

11 At that time, you won’t need to be ashamed

12 But among you I’ll leave humble, common people

13 The ones remaining in Israel will do no wrong,

14 Shout for joy, Jerusalem!

15 The LORD has canceled your condemnation

16 At that time, he’ll tell Jerusalem,

17 The LORD your God is among you

19 At that time, I’ll deal with your oppressors,

20 Then I’ll bring you home,
