Jonah Chapter 2

1Jonah prayed to the LORD his God from the fish’s stomach, 2“I called to you in my distress, and you answered me; I cried out for help from the belly of Sheol, and you heard me.

3You threw me into the deep, into the heart of the sea; the current swirled over me; your waves and breakers overwhelmed me.

4So I said, ‘I’ve been driven away from you, but I’ll still look toward your holy Temple.’ Jon 1:17-2:4

5Water closed in over me; the great deep surrounded me; seaweed wrapped around my head.

6I sank down to the base of the mountains; the earth imprisoned me forever, but you brought me up from the Pit, LORD my God.

7As my life was fading away, I remembered you and prayed to you in your holy Temple.

8Those who pay respects to pointless idols forfeit your faithfulness,

9but I’ll sacrifice to you with thanksgiving. I’ll do what I’ve vowed; salvation comes from you.’”

10Then the LORD had the fish cough Jonah up onto the shore.

Jon 2:5-10
