Lamentations Chapter 3

1 I have seen suffering

2 He has driven me out and made me walk in the dark;

4 He made my skin and body waste away,

5 He besieged me with bitterness and hardship.

6 He made me live in dark places

7 He walled me in so I couldn’t escape

8 Even when I call for help,

9 He has blocked my road with hewn stones

10 He’s like a bear lying in wait for me,

11 He dragged me off the road, tore me to pieces;

12 He bent his bow and made me a target for his arrow;

14 I’m a laughingstock to my people,

15 He filled me with bitterness

16 He cracked my teeth with gravel

17 I’ve been set apart from peace

18 So I say, “My strength is gone,

19 Remember my suffering and homelessness.

20 I certainly remember

21 I recall this and have hope from it:

22 The LORD’s love never stops coming;

23 His faithfulness is great;

24 I say to myself, “The LORD is my portion;

25 The LORD is good to those who depend on him,

26 They do well to wait silently for his salvation.

27 It’s good for people to bear the yoke when they’re young,

32 If he causes grief, he’ll have mercy

37 Who is there that speaks and it happens

38 Don’t both good and bad come from the Most High?

39 Why should we complain in light of our sins?

40 Let’s examine our ways and return to the LORD.

41 Let’s lift up our heart and hands toward God in heaven;

43 You’ve covered yourself with anger and chased us;

44 You’ve wrapped yourself in a cloud

45 You’ve made us residue and refuse among the nations.

46 Our enemies rail out against us.

47 Panic and pitfall descend on us, devastation and destruction.

48 My eyes run with streams of tears

49 My eyes will keep pouring out 51My eyes bring pain to my soul 52For no reason my enemies hunted me down like a bird; 55I called to you, LORD, from down in the pit. 56You heard my plea 57You came and said, “Don’t be afraid!” 58Lord, you pled my case and redeemed my life. 59 LORD, you’ve seen my oppression; judge my case. 60You’ve seen their revenge, their schemes against me. 61You’ve heard their taunts. 62My assailants’ whispers aim at me all day. 63Look at their sitting and rising; 64Pay them back, LORD, according to what they’ve done; 66Chase them in anger and destroy them from under the sky!
