Zechariah Chapter 9

1 A Message from the LORD Against the Land of Hadrach [Aram] The message from the LORD is against Damascus—for the eyes of all people, including the tribes of Israel, are on the LORD—

3 Tyre built a fortress,

4 The LORD will confiscate its possessions,

5 Ashkelon will see it and be afraid.

6 Ashdod will house a mongrel race;

7 I’ll remove the blood from their mouths

8 I’ll camp around my Temple to protect it

9 Celebrate, Zion!

10 I’ll get rid of chariots in Ephraim,

11 Because of my covenant with you, sealed with blood,

12 Come back to the fortress, all you prisoners that still have hope;

13 I’ll bend Judah like my bow;

14 The LORD will appear above them;

15 The LORD of Hosts will defend his people;

16 The LORD their God will save them then

17 How nice things will be!
