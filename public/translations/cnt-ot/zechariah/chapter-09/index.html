Zechariah Chapter 9

1 9:1A Message from the LORD Against the Land of Hadrach [Aram] The message from the LORD is against Damascus—for the eyes of all people, including the tribes of Israel, are on the LORD—

2against Hamath, which borders it, and against Tyre and Sidon, though they’re so very wise.

3Tyre built a fortress, piled up silver like dust and gold like dirt on the streets.

4The LORD will confiscate its possessions, throw its wealth into the sea, and burn it down.

5Ashkelon will see it and be afraid. Gaza will writhe in anguish; Ekron, too, because it’s lost hope; Gaza won’t have a king; Ashkelon will be empty.

6Ashdod will house a mongrel race; I’ll destroy the Philistines’ pride.

7I’ll remove the blood from their mouths and the disgusting things from between their teeth. They’ll be a remnant for our God, like a clan in Judah; Ekron will be like Jebusites.

Zech 9:1-7

8I’ll camp around my Temple to protect it so armies can’t walk all over my people; oppressors won’t run over them anymore because I’m watching. Zion’s Coming King

9Celebrate, Zion! Shout, Jerusalem! Your king is coming to you, just and able to save, humble and riding on a donkey, on a colt, a donkey’s foal.

10I’ll get rid of chariots in Ephraim, warhorses in Jerusalem, and the bow used in battle. I’ll offer peace to the nations and rule from sea to sea, from the Euphrates River to the ends of the earth.

Zech 9:8-10

11Because of my covenant with you, sealed with blood, I’ve freed your prisoners from the dry cistern.

12Come back to the fortress, all you prisoners that still have hope; I promise to restore you double today. like a flock of his sheep, like jewels in a crown, sparkling in his land.

13I’ll bend Judah like my bow; Ephraim will be my arrow. I’ll stir up Zion against Greece and wield you like a sword.

14The LORD will appear above them; his arrows will shoot like lightning. He’ll blow the bugle and march in the windstorm from the south.

Zech 9:11-14

15The LORD of Hosts will defend his people; they’ll trample the stone slingers. They’ll drink their bloodlxx and be boisterous as with wine, filled like a sacrificial basin, drenched like the horns on the altar.

16The LORD their God will save them then

17How nice things will be! Grain will make the young men thrive; new wine will make the maidens flourish.

Zech 9:15-17

The LORD Will Restore His People
