Nehemiah Chapter 9

1 On the twenty-fourth of that month, the Israelites gathered with fasting in burlap and doused with dust. 2They separated from foreigners and confessed their and their ancestors’ sins. 3While they stood in their place, they read from the Law for a fourth of the day. For another fourth they confessed and worshiped the LORD their God. 4On the Levites’ platform stood Jeshua, Bani, Kadmiel, Shebaniah, Bunni, Sherebiah, Bani, and Chenani, and appealed to the LORD their God.

5 Then the Levites Jeshua, Kadmiel, Bani, Hashabneiah, Sherebiah, Hodiah, Shebaniah, and Pethahiah said,

6 You alone are the LORD.

7 You’re the LORD God

8 You found him faithful

10 You performed signs and wonders against Pharaoh,

11 You separated the sea

12 You led them during the day with a pillar of cloud

13 Then you came down on Mount Sinai

14 You told them about your holy Sabbath

15 You gave them bread from the sky for their hunger

17 They didn’t remember the awesome deeds

18 Even when they made a calf out of molten metal

20 You gave them your good Spirit to teach them;

21 For 40 years you provided for them in the desert,

22 You delivered kingdoms and nations to them

23 You made their descendants as numerous as the stars

24 So their descendants went in and took possession of it;

25 They captured fortified towns and a fertile land;

27 So you delivered them to their oppressors;

28 But as soon as they got comfortable,

30 But you bore with them for many years

31 But in your great mercy

33 You’re justified in everything that’s happened to us,

34 Our kings, leaders, priests, and ancestors

35 But they, in their own kingdom,

36 We’re slaves today

37 Its abundant produce is for the kings

38 Because of that, we’re signing a covenant;
