2 Samuel Chapter 1

1 After Saul died, David came back from massacring the Amalekites and stayed two days in Ziklag. 2The third day, a man came from Saul’s camp with torn clothes and dust on his head. He prostrated himself to David.

2 SAMUEL

3 David said, “Where have you come from?” “They’ve fled from the battle; many have fallen. Saul and his son Jonathan are dead.” 6"I happened to be on Mount Gilboa, and Saul was leaning on his spear. The chariots and cavalry were coming up on him. 7When he looked back, he called out to me, 8’Who are you?’ 10"So I stood beside him and killed him. I knew he couldn’t live. I took the crown off his head and the armband from his arm and brought them to you."

11 David and his men tore their clothes. 12They mourned and cried and fasted till evening for Saul, Jonathan, the LORD’s people, and Israel, because they’d fallen by the sword.

13 David asked the young man, “Where are you from?"

14 David said, “How is it you weren’t afraid to destroy the LORD’s anointed?"

15 He called one of the young men, “Cut him down."

16 David told him, “Your blood is on your own head. You testified against yourself that you killed the LORD’s anointed.” 17David chanted this dirge over Saul and Jonathan. 18He told them to teach Judah the Son of the Bod; the Book of Jashar records it:

20 Don’t tell it in Gath

21 Mountains of Gilboa,

22 From the blood of the slain, from the fat of the strong,

23 Saul and Jonathan, beloved and pleasant in life,

24 Young women of Israel, mourn for Saul,

25 How the strong have fallen in battle!

26 I’m distressed over you, Jonathan my brother;

27 How the powerful have fallen,
