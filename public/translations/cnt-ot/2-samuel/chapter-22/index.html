2 Samuel Chapter 22

1David sang this song to the LORD when he delivered him from his enemies and from Saul: 2“The LORD is my rock, my fortress, my deliverer,

3 3my God, my refuge, my shield, the horn of my salvation, my fortress, my savior who saves me from violence.

4I called on him who deserves praise, and he saved me from my enemies.

5The waves of death entangled me; the torrents of destruction flooded over me;

6 6the cords of Sheol tied me down; the snares of death trapped me.

7In my desperation I cried out to the LORD, and he heard me in his temple.

2 Sam 22:1-7

8Then the earth quaked and the mountains shook because he was angry.

9Smoke poured out of his nostrils; consuming fire flared from his mouth; glowing coals erupted from him.

0He lowered the sky too and came down with dense darkness under his feet.

1He rode on a cherub and came flying on the wings of the wind.

2He made blackness a canopy around him, a mass of moisture, dark clouds in the sky.

3Coals of fire flamed out from the brightness in front of him.

4The LORD thundered from the sky; the Most High raised his voice.

2 Sam 22:8-14

5He shot arrows and scattered my enemies; lightning flashes routed them.

6Then the water in the sea receded; the seabed was exposed at his rebuke, at the blast from his nostrils.

7He reached down from on high and grabbed me, and pulled me out of the path of the raging tsunami.

8He delivered me from powerful enemies that hated me, that were too strong for me.

9They attacked me in a time of catastrophe, but the LORD supported me.

0He brought me out into a safe place and rescued me because he delighted in me.

2 Sam 22:15-20

21“The LORD has rewarded me according to my goodness, according to my innocence—

22because I’d kept his ways and hadn’t sinned by leaving him,

23because his ordinances all stayed with me; I hadn’t shoved any of his statutes away.

4I stood blameless in front of him and kept away from sin.

5So the LORD has rewarded me according to my goodness— according to my godliness toward him. 26“With the kind you’re kind; with the blameless you’re blameless;

27with the pure you’re pure; the devious you torture.

8You rescue the humble, but bring down the haughty.

9You’re my lamp, LORD, and shine light into my darkness.

0With your help I can crush a battalion; I can scale a wall.

1God’s way is perfect; the LORD’s promise proves true; he’s a shield for everyone that depends on him for protection.

2 Sam 22:21-31

32“Who is God except the LORD? Who is a rock except our God,

33 33who wraps me in strength and makes my way safe,

34 34who makes my feet like red deer’s hooves and sets me securely on high ground,

35 35who trains my hands for battle and strengthens my arms to draw a bronze bow,

2 Sam 22:32-35

36 36who gives me his deliverance as a shield and helps make me great,

37 37who widens the path for my feet so they don’t slip.

8I chased my enemies and overtook them; I didn’t turn back till I’d eliminated them.

9I thrust them through so they couldn’t get up again. They fell under my feet

40because you’ve wrapped me in strength for battle; and subdued my assailants under me.

1You’ve made my enemies retreat; I destroyed the ones that hated me.

2They looked around for help, but there wasn’t any; they even cried out to the LORD, but he didn’t answer them.

3I pulverized them like dust, crushed and stomped on them like dirt in the street.

2 Sam 22:36-43

44“You delivered me from conflict with my own people and placed me at the head of nations; people I didn’t know serve me now.

5Foreigners pretend to submit as soon as they hear me;

6they retreat and come cringing out of their fortresses.

2 Sam 22:44-46

47“The LORD lives; blessed be my Rock; exalted be God, the rock that saves me,

48 48the God that carries out vengeance for me and subdues nations under me,

49 49who brought me out from my enemies and lifted me above my attackers, who rescued me from violent people.

0So I’ll thank you among the Gentiles, LORD, and sing praises to you.

1You’re a tower of victory for your king and show enduring love to your anointed one— David and his descendants forever.”

2 Sam 22:

2-51 [= Ps 18:2-50]
