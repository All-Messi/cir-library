Amos Chapter 3

1 Listen to what the LORD says against you, Israel, against the entire family he brought out of Egypt:

3 Can two men walk together unless they’ve agreed on their route?

4 Does a lion roar in the woods before it’s made a kill?

5 Does a bird fall into a trap on the ground if there’s no bait in it?

6 If a bugle blows in the city, won’t the people be afraid?

7 For certain the LORD God doesn’t do anything

8 A lion has roared! Who’s not afraid?

9 Declare on the fortresses in Ashdod and Egypt: “Gather in the mountains around Samaria and watch the great turmoil and oppression in it. 10They don’t know how to do what’s right; they store up violence and devastation in their fortresses."

11 So the Lord GOD says,

12 Like a shepherd snatches from a lion’s mouth

13 Listen and testify against Jacob’s house.

14 When I punish Israel’s sins, I’ll punish Bethel’s altars too.

15 I’ll strike their winter palaces and summer homes;
