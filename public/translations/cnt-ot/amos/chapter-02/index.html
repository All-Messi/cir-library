Amos Chapter 2

1The LORD says, “For three sins of Moab and for four, I won’t hold back its punishment: they burned the king of Edom’s bones into lime.

2So I’ll burn the fortresses in Kerioth; Moab will die amid turmoil, war cries, and bugle blasts.

3I’ll eliminate judges from him and slay his officials with him.”

Amos 2:1-3

God’s Judgment on Judah and Israel

4The LORD says, “For three sins of Judah and for four, I won’t hold back its punishment: he rejected the Law of the LORD and didn’t keep his statutes. The same lies have led them astray that their ancestors followed.

5So I’ll send down fire on Judah and burn the fortresses in Jerusalem.”

Amos 2:4-5

6The LORD says, “For three sins of Israel and for four, I won’t hold back its punishment: they sell the good for money, the poor for a pair of shoes.

7They walk on the heads of the poor in the dust and abandon those who are suffering. A man and his father sleep with the same girl to profane my holy name.

8They lie down beside every altar on cloaks taken as pledges. In God’s Temple they drink the wine of the people they’ve fined.

Amos 2:6-8

9“Yet I was the One that destroyed the Amorites for you, though they were as tall as cedars and strong as oaks. I destroyed their fruit above and their roots below.

10I was the One that brought you up from Egypt, and led you in the desert

for 40

years to take over the land of the Amorites.

1Then I raised up some of your offspring to be prophets and some of your young men to be Nazirites. Isn’t that so, Israel?

Amos 2:9-11

12“But you made Nazirites drink wine and told prophets not to prophesy!

3I’ll weigh you down like a wagon overloaded with bundles,

4The swift won’t be able to flee; the strong will lose their strength; the powerful won’t save their lives.

5The archers won’t stand their ground; the swift won’t escape; the cavalry won’t save themselves.

6The bravest among the troops will drop their weapons and run when that time comes.”

Amos 2:12-16

Witnesses Summoned Against Israel
