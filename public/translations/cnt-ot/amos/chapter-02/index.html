Amos Chapter 2

1 The LORD says,

2 So I’ll burn the fortresses in Kerioth;

3 I’ll eliminate judges from him

4 The LORD says,

5 So I’ll send down fire on Judah

6 The LORD says,

7 They walk on the heads of the poor in the dust

8 They lie down beside every altar

10 I was the One that brought you up from Egypt,

11 Then I raised up some of your offspring to be prophets

13 I’ll weigh you down like a wagon

14 The swift won’t be able to flee;

15 The archers won’t stand their ground;

16 The bravest among the troops
