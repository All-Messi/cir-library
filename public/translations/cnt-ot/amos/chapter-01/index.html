Amos Chapter 1

1 The Message of Amos, Shepherd from Tekoa, About Israel at the Time of Uzziah, King of Judah, and Jeroboam Son of Joash, King of Israel,

2 He said,

3 The LORD says,

4 So I’ll send down fire on the house of Hazael,

6 The LORD says,

7 So I’ll send down fire on the walls of Gaza

8 I’ll eliminate the people from Ashdod

9 The LORD says,

10 So I’ll send down fire on the walls of Tyre

11 The LORD says,

12 So I’ll send down fire on Teman

13 The LORD says,

14 So I’ll kindle a fire on the walls of Rabbah

15 Their king will go into exile with his officials."
