Amos Chapter 8

1 The Lord GOD showed me a basket of ripe fruit. “My people Israel are also ripe—for punishment. I won’t spare them any longer. 3The singing at the palace will turn into wailing. There’ll be lots of corpses silently thrown out everywhere."

4 Listen to this, you that run over the needy and trample on the humble, 5saying,

7 The LORD has sworn by Jacob’s arrogance,

9 At that time, I’ll make the sun go down at noon

10 I’ll turn your festivals into mourning

11 The Lord GOD says.

12 People will stagger from sea to sea,

13 At that time, beautiful virgins

14 The ones that swear by the guilt of Samaria,
