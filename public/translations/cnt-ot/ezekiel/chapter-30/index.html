Ezekiel Chapter 30

1 A message from the LORD came to me again:

3 The day of the LORD is near,

4 A sword will come at Egypt;

5 Nubia, Put, Lud, Arabia, Libya, and the people of any land allied with them will fall by the sword with them.

7 They’ll be desolate among desolate lands;

8 They’ll know I’m the LORD

9 At that time, I’ll send ambassadors in ships

11 Him and his people, the most ruthless of the nations,

12 I’ll dry up the Nile’s canals

14 I’ll make Pathros desolate,

15 I’ll pour out my anger on Sin, Egypt’s fortress;

16 I’ll set Egypt on fire.

17 The young men of On and Pi-beseth

18 It will be a dark day in Tehaphnehes

19 So I’ll carry out judgments on Egypt,

20 The seventh day, first month, eleventh year, a message from the LORD came to me: 21"Son of man, I’ve broken the arm of Pharaoh, Egypt’s king. It hasn’t been put in a sling to mend or wrapped with a bandage to get strong enough to hold a sword.’ 22So the Lord GOD says, ‘I’ll break both of his arms, the good arm and the broken one, and make the sword fall from his hand. 23I’ll scatter the Egyptians among the nations. 24I’ll strengthen the king of Babylon and put my sword in his hand. Pharaoh will groan from his wounds. 25I’ll strengthen the king of Babylon’s arms, but Pharaoh’s arms will fall. When the king of Babylon holds out his sword against Egypt 26and I scatter the Egyptians among the nations, they’ll know I’m the LORD."’"
