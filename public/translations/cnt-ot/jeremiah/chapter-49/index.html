Jeremiah Chapter 49

1 About Ammon

2 The time’s coming

4 Why do you brag about your fertile valleys?

5 I’m going to bring terror on you

6 Yet later I’ll restore Ammon’s fortunes."

7 About Edom

8 Turn around, run away,

9 If grape-gatherers come to you,

10 But I’ll strip bare Esau’s land

11 But leave its orphans behind; I’ll keep them alive;

12 The LORD says, “If the ones that didn’t deserve the cup have to drink it, will you be let off? You will drink it! 13I’ve sworn by myself that Bozrah will be something to abhor, a disgrace, a ruin, a curse. Its towns will lie in permanent ruins."

14 I’ve heard a message from the LORD;

15 I’ll make you small among the nations;

16 The terror you inspired 17"Edom will be something to abhor. Everybody that goes past it will be horrified and hiss at its wounds. 18Like the overthrow of Sodom, Gomorrah, and its neighbors, nobody will live there. 19Like a lion from the thickets by the Jordan suddenly attacking a sheepfold, I’ll chase everybody away from it. I’ll appoint over it anyone I choose. Who can take me to court? What shepherd can oppose me. 20So listen to the LORD’s plan for Edom, his intentions for Teman. They’ll drag off the little ones in the flock and empty the pasture. 21The ground will shake at their downfall. The outcry will be heard all the way to the Red Sea. 22It’s enemy will soar high and swoop down like an eagle and spread his wings against Bozrah. The hearts of Edom’s mighty men will be like the heart of a woman in labor."

23 About Damascus

24 Damascus is helpless;

25 How that famous city has been deserted,

26 Its young men will fall in its streets;

28 About Kedar and the Kingdoms of Hazor

29 They’ll take away their tents and flocks,

30 Run! Flee! Live in deep caves,

32 Their camels will be plunder;

34 A Message from the LORD to Jeremiah About Elam

36 I’m going to bring on Elam the four winds

37 I’ll scatter Elam ahead the enemies

38 Then I’ll set my throne in Elam

39 Yet later I’ll restore Elam’s fortunes.’"
