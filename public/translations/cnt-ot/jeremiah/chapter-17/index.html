Jeremiah Chapter 17

1 Judah’s sin is recorded with an iron stylus;

2 As well as they remember their children,

3 My mountain in the open country,

4 You’ll voluntarily let go of the inheritance I gave you;

5 The LORD says,

6 They’re like a tamarisk shrub in the desert

7 Fortunate are the people that trust in the LORD,

8 They’re like a tree planted by water

10 I, the LORD, search hearts and test minds

11 People that make a fortune unjustly

12 From the beginning, our sanctuary

13 LORD, hope of Israel,

14 LORD, I’ll be healed if you heal me;

15 They keep asking to me,

16 But I haven’t hurried away from serving as your shepherd;

17 Don’t be a terror to me;

18 Disgrace those who harass me;

19 The LORD told me, “Go stand in the gate that Judah’s kings use, and in the other gates around Jerusalem. 20Say, ‘Listen to the LORD’s message, Judah, kings of Judah, people living in Jerusalem who use these gates: 21For your own good, don’t carry any load on the Sabbath or bring anything in through these gates. 22Don’t carry a load out of your houses on the Sabbath or work. Set the Sabbath aside like I told your ancestors. 23But they stiffened their necks and wouldn’t follow directions. 24”‘Listen carefully and don’t carry any load through this city’s gates on the Sabbath. Set it aside by resting on it. 25Then through these gates will come kings and officials who sit on David’s throne, who ride in chariots and on horses, people of Judah and Jerusalem. People will live in this city permanently. 26They’ll come in from the towns in Judah and places around Jerusalem, from Benjamin, the western foothills, the hill country, and the Negev. They’ll bring thanksgiving sacrifices to the LORD’s Temple. 27If you don’t set the Sabbath aside, I’ll light a fire in Jerusalem’s gates. It will spread to the palaces in Jerusalem, and it won’t be put out.’"
