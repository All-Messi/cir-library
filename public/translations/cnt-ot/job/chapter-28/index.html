Job Chapter 28

1“There’s a mine for silver and a refinery for gold.

2Iron comes out of the ground, and people can smelt copper.

3 3They use a torch in the darkness and go in as far they can, searching for the ore in gloom and deep shadow.

4They sink mine shafts far from where people live and go down them, dangling on ropes, swinging back and forth.

5People grow food on top of the ground; but far below, the ground melts as if by fire.

Job 28:1-5

6Its rocks yield lapis lazuli, whose flecks glitter like gold.

7Birds of prey can’t get to it; no falcon has seen it.

8Proud animals don’t set foot in it; fierce lions don’t prowl there.

9Miners find flint and tear down mountains to get at it.

0They chisel out tunnels in the rock to discover precious stones.

1They dam up trickling streams and bring hidden treasure out to light.

Job 28:6-11

12“But where can people discover wisdom? Where is intelligence located?

3People don’t know what it’s worth; they can’t find it where people live.

4The deep says, ‘It’s not here’; the sea says, ‘It’s not inside me.’

5Pure gold doesn’t exchange for it; silver doesn’t weigh out equal to its price.

6Its value can’t be calculated in the gold from Ophir, in precious onyx or lapis lazuli.

Job 28:12-16

7Gold or crystal can’t match it; it can’t be traded for articles made from fine gold.

8Coral and crystal can’t be mentioned with it; acquiring wisdom is more important than buying pearls.

9The topaz in Nubia can’t compare to it; it can’t be measured in terms of pure gold.

0Where then does wisdom come from? Where’s it located?

1Nothing alive can see it; it’s hid from the birds in the sky.

2Abaddon and Death say, ‘We haven’t heard any report about it.’

Job 28:17-22

23“God knows where it is and understands how to get to it.

4He can see all the way to the end of the earth and everything that’s under the sky.

5When he decided how hard the wind should blow and how much rain should fall,

26when he decreed the way rain should form and the course a thunderbolt should follow,

27then he looked at wisdom and appraised it; he put it in place and tested it.

8He told humanity, ‘Respecting the Lord shows wisdom; getting away from sin brings insight.’”

Job 28:23-28
