Job Chapter 36

1Elihu continued: 2“Bear with me a little longer, and I’ll show you what I have to say on God’s behalf.

3I’ll bring my knowledge from afar and credit goodness to my Maker.

4What I’m saying is true; I know perfectly well what I’m talking about.

5God is powerful, but he doesn’t despise anybody; he’s mighty in his power to understand.

6He doesn’t keep sinners alive, but gives justice to the oppressed.

7He doesn’t take his eyes off of good people, but enthrones them with kings and exalts them permanently.

8If they’re bound in chains and held tight in a web of difficulties,

9 9he tells them the reason: it’s because they’ve acted arrogantly.

0He opens their ears to instruction and commands them to turn away from sin.

Job 36:1-10

1If they listen and serve him, they’ll end their days in prosperity, their years in pleasantness.

2But if they ignore him, they’ll perish by the sword and die ignorant.

3But the godless in heart store up anger; they don’t call out for help when he restrains them.

4They die young; their life perishes among the cult prostitutes.

5He delivers the afflicted by their afflictions and opens their ears with adversity.

Job 36:11-15

6So he’s luring you with distress into a safe space that’s not crowded and has set delicious food on your table. 17“But you’ve become obsessed with condemning sinners, obsessed with judgment and justice.

8Be careful that anger doesn’t lure you into scoffing; don’t let the great cost of the ransom turn you aside.

9Will your wealth or strength keep you away from troubles?

0Don’t long for the cover of night when people disappear unnoticed.

1Make sure not to turn to evil, which you’ve preferred to affliction.

2God is honored by his power; who can teach like him?

3Who has prescribed his conduct or said, ‘You’ve done something wrong’?

Job 36:16-23

24“Remember to extol his work that people sing about.

5Everybody has seen it; they can look at it from a long way off.

6God is so great we don’t understand him; we can’t find out how old he is.

7He evaporates water drops and distills his mist into rain,

28which the clouds pour down on people abundantly.

Job 36:24-28

9Can anyone understand how clouds spread out or how thunder claps in his pavilion?

0He spreads out lightning all around him and covers the depths of the ocean.

1They’re how he governs nations and supplies food abundantly.

2He fills his hands with lightning bolts and hurls them at their marks.

3Thunder announces his presence and warns livestock that a storm is coming.

Job 36:29-33
