Job Chapter 8

1 Bildad the Shuhite answered:

3 Does God pervert justice?

4 When your children sinned against him,

5 If you look for God and beg for mercy from the Almighty,

7 Though you started with little,

10 Won’t they teach you

12 While they’re still growing and not ready to be cut,

13 The paths of everyone that forgets God are like that;

14 What they depend on breaks down;

15 They trust it, but it doesn’t hold firm;

16 It’s like a lush plant that thrives in the sunshine

18 But if it’s pulled loose,

19 That’s the extent of its ‘joy’;

21 Yet he’ll fill your mouth with laughter,

22 In the end, those who hate you he’ll cover with disgrace;
