Job Chapter 27

1 Job continued his comments:

4 I won’t speak wickedness or lie.

5 I’ll never concede that you’re right;

6 I’ll hold onto my claim about my goodness,

8 What hope do the godless have when God cuts them off,

9 Will he listen to their outcry

10 Will they be able to delight in the Almighty

11 Let me teach you about God’s power;

12 You’ve all seen that;

16 Though sinners pile up silver like dust

18 The house they build is as fragile as a spider’s web

19 They go to bed rich, but only once;

20 Panic overwhelms them like a flood;

21 The east wind picks them up

23 People will clap their hands and jeer at them.
