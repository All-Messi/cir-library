Judges Chapter 5

1 Deborah and Barak sang that day,

3 Hear, you kings; listen, you officials;

4 LORD, when you went out from Seir,

5 The mountains shook in front of the LORD;

7 The rural people ceased in Israel;

8 When the people chose new gods,

9 My heart goes out to Israel’s commanders,

11 At the sound of those who separate flocks

13 The remnant of the nobles came down;

14 They set out to the valley from Ephraim;

15 The officials of Issachar came with Deborah;

16 Why did you linger among the sheepfolds?

17 Gilead stayed put beyond the Jordan.

18 Zebulun was a people that risked their lives,

20 The stars fought from the sky;

21 The current of the Kishon swept them away,

24 May Jael be most fortunate among women,

25 He asked for water; she gave him milk.

26 She took a tent peg

27 He sank, he fell;

29 Her wise ladies answer;
