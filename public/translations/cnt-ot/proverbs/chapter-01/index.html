Proverbs Chapter 1

1 Proverbs of Solomon Son of David, King of Israel

7 Respect for the LORD is the first thing about being wise;

8 Listen, son, to your father’s instruction;

10 Son, if sinners tempt you, don’t give in to them.

11 If they say, “Come on,

12 Let’s swallow them alive and whole like Sheol,

13 We’ll get precious wealth

14 Throw in your lot with us,

16 They hurry to do harm;

17 There’s no point in spreading a net

18 Sinners lie in wait for their own blood;

19 That’s how it is with people that profit from violence;

20 Wisdom shouts in the street;

21 It cries out from the top of the wall

23 Pay attention when I correct you;

24 I called and you wouldn’t listen;

25 You ignored my advice;

26 So I’ll laugh at your misfortune

28 Then you’ll call out to me, and I won’t answer;

30 You wouldn’t accept my advice;

32 The waywardness of simpleminded people gets them killed;

33 But the ones that listen to me, live securely;
