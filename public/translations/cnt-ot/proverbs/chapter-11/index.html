Proverbs Chapter 11

1 The LORD detests a false balance;

2 Dishonor follows pride,

3 The integrity of the wise gives them direction;

4 Wealth won’t help you when judgment comes,

5 Goodness smooths the way for the blameless,

6 Their goodness delivers the godly;

7 When sinners die, their aspirations die as well;

8 Good people leave trouble behind,

9 The godless destroy their neighbors with their mouths,

10 A city celebrates when things go well for the godly;

11 The godly are a blessing that builds up a city;

12 Belittling your neighbor doesn’t help anybody;

13 Gossips go around telling secrets;

14 Without wise leaders a nation falls;

15 Standing good for a stranger’s debt

16 A gracious woman earns respect;

17 Kindness repays you;

18 Sinners get rich by deception;

19 Steadfast goodness promotes life;

20 The LORD detests the perverse in heart;

21 Sinful people will certainly suffer for it,

22 A beautiful woman with no discretion

23 The godly look forward to good things;

24 Give liberally and grow richer;

25 The generous prosper;

26 People curse the one that hoards grain

27 Whoever seeks the good finds favor,

28 People that rely on their wealth will fall;

29 Bring trouble on your family,

30 Good people become a tree of life,

31 If the righteous are rewarded on earth,
