Proverbs Chapter 18

1 Unfriendly people pursue selfish ends;

2 Fools don’t delight in learning,

3 When sinners come, contempt comes with them;

4 Wise thoughts are deep water;

5 Don’t cater to bad people

6 Fools’ lips instigate conflict;

7 The mouths of fools bring about their ruin;

8 Rumors are like delicious morsels;

9 People that aren’t productive

10 The LORD’s name is a strong tower;

11 The wealth of the rich is their fortified city;

12 Pride goes before destruction;

13 Popping off an answer before listening to the question

14 The human spirit can survive a sick body,

15 The wise search for knowledge;

16 Generosity opens doors

17 The first to argue a case in court

18 Casting lots ends contentions;

19 Winning back an offended brother

20 What the mouth says satisfies the inner self;

21 The tongue wields the power of life and death;

22 A man that finds a wife finds something good

23 The poor ask for help;

24 Someone with lots of “friends” may come to ruin,
