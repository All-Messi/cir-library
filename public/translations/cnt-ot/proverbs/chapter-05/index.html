Proverbs Chapter 5

1 Pay attention to my wisdom, son;

3 An immoral woman’s lips drip honey;

5 Her feet go down to death;

6 She doesn’t ponder the path of life;

7 Listen to me, sons;

8 Stay away from her;

13 I didn’t listen to my teachers

14 I’ve come to the point of total ruin

15 Drink water from your own cistern,

16 Should your spring be spilled

17 Let it be for yours alone,

18 Let your fountain be blessed;

19 Like a lovely deer, a graceful doe,

20 Son, why be stimulated by an immoral woman?

21 The LORD watches everyone

22 Their own iniquities capture the wicked;

23 They die from ignorance
