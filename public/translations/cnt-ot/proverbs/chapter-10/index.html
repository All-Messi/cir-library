Proverbs Chapter 10

1 Proverbs of Solomon

2 Ill-gotten gain doesn’t gain you anything,

3 The LORD won’t let good people go hungry;

4 A lazy hand makes for poverty;

5 They who gather crops in the summer are smart;

6 Blessings come to the good,

7 Good people leave blessed memories behind,

8 The wise in heart accept commands,

9 If you live with integrity, you’ll live with confidence;

10 The one that winks causes trouble;

11 Good people’s words are life-giving fountains;

12 Hatred stirs up conflict,

13 Wisdom comes from people that have insight;

14 The wise store up knowledge,

15 For the rich, wealth is a fortress;

16 Life is good people’s pay;

17 Listening to instruction puts you on the route to life;

18 Hiding hatred makes you a liar;

19 Talking too much makes you likely to sin;

20 The words of good people are like fine silver;

21 What good people say feeds many;

22 The LORD’s blessing makes you rich,

23 Sin is fun for fools;

24 What sinners fear tends to happen;

25 After a windstorm passes, sinners have been blown away;

26 To those who hire them,

27 Respecting the LORD lengthens your life;

28 Good people’s hopes result in happiness,

29 The LORD’s way is your fortress if you’re godly,

30 Good people won’t be displaced,

31 The mouth of good people overflows with wisdom,

32 Good people say what’s acceptable,
