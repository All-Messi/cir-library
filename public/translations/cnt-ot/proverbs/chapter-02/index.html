Proverbs Chapter 2

1Son, if you accept what I say and value my commandments,

2if you’re attentive to wisdom and inclined to understanding,

3if you cry out for insight and call out for comprehension,

4if you look for it like silver and search for it like treasure—

5then you’ll understand the importance of reverence for the LORD and find out what it means to know God.

Prov 2:1-5

6The LORD gives wisdom; knowledge and insight come from him.

7He stores up wisdom for godly people and shields those who maintain integrity

8and guard justice. He preserves his saints.

9Then you’ll understand goodness and justice, equity and every proper course of action.

10Wisdom will come into your heart, and knowledge will be pleasant to you.

Prov 2:6-10

11Discretion will come to you; knowledge will watch over you

2to deliver you from the evil way, from people that say perverse things,

13who leave the paths of godliness to walk in the dark,

14who enjoy sinning and revel in its perversity,

15whose paths are crooked, whose ways are treacherous;

6to deliver you from immoral women, from the seductive woman

17that leaves the husband of her youth and forgets her covenant with God,

18whose house lies on the road to death, whose footprints lead to the dead.

19No man that enters her house ever comes back; they don’t reach the paths that lead to life.

Prov 2:11-19

20Follow the road good people take; keep to the paths they follow,

1because the godly will live in the land; the blameless will stay here.

2But sinners will be removed from the land; the treacherous will be uprooted from their country.

Prov 2:20-22
