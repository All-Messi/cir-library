Proverbs Chapter 16

1 We have intentions,

2 Our ways may seem pure to us,

3 Commit your work to the LORD,

4 The LORD has a purpose for everything,

5 The LORD detests arrogant people

6 Loyalty and faithfulness atone for sin;

7 When we please the LORD,

8 A little with goodness is better

9 You can make your plans,

10 A king needs to make inspired decisions;

11 The LORD requires accurate scales and balances;

12 An evil king is especially detestable;

13 Godly words delight a king;

14 A king’s anger is a death sentence;

15 There’s life in a king’s countenance;

16 Getting wisdom is better than getting gold!

17 The road of the upright leads away from evil;

18 Pride goes before destruction,

19 Better to nurture a humble spirit

20 By attending to the LORD’S word, we find what’s good;

21 The wise in heart we consider insightful;

22 Wisdom is a fountain of life to the wise,

23 The wise think carefully about what they say,

24 Pleasant words are like honeycomb,

25 There’s a way that seems right,

26 The workers’ appetite works for them

27 Worthless people plot evil;

28 Perverse persons spread conflict;

29 Violent people tempt their friends

30 Those who wink devise perverse things;

31 A gray head is a crown of honor

32 Those who are slow to lose their temper

33 We cast lots in the lap,
