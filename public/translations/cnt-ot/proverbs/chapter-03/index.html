Proverbs Chapter 3

1 Son, don’t forget what I’ve taught you;

3 Don’t let kindness and truth slip away from you;

4 That way you’ll find blessing

5 Trust in the LORD with all your heart;

6 Acknowledge him in everything you do,

7 Don’t get the idea that you’re smart;

8 It will heal your body and refresh your very bones.

9 Honor the LORD with your substance,

10 Your barns will be full;

11 Son, don’t reject the LORD’s discipline

13 They are blessed who find wisdom,

14 It’s more profitable than silver;

15 It’s more precious than jewels;

16 Its left hand holds wealth and honor;

17 Its ways are pleasant;

18 It’s a life-giving tree for people that pick its fruit;

19 The LORD founded the earth with wisdom

21 Son, keep sound wisdom and discretion nearby;

22 They’ll refresh your soul

23 You’ll walk along your road

24 When you lie down, you won’t feel afraid;

25 Don’t be afraid of sudden panic

27 Don’t withhold good from people that need it

28 Don’t tell them,

29 Don’t devise misfortune against the one

30 Don’t be difficult with people

31 Don’t envy the violent

32 The LORD detests crooked people,

33 He puts a curse on the house of the sinful,

34 He scoffs at scoffers,

35 The wise receive honor;
