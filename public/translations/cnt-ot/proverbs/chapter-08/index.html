Proverbs Chapter 8

1Wisdom calls out, and intelligence raises its voice.

2It takes its stand on high places where footpaths meet.

3It shouts at the city gates: 4“I’m calling out to you; my voice is aimed at everyone.

5Learn prudence, simple ones; gain wisdom, foolish people.

6Listen, so I can tell you nobler things, important things come from me.

Prov 8:1-6

7I tell the truth because I detest sinfulness.

8Everything I say is good; there’s nothing devious in it.

9What I say makes sense to anyone that has insight; it’s right to those who learn.

0Take instruction rather than silver; accept knowledge instead of gold

1because wisdom is worth more than jewels; nothing you want compares with it.

Prov 8:7-11

12“I, wisdom, and good judgment live together; I discover knowledge and discretion.

3Respecting the LORD means hating sin, pride and arrogance, corrupt behavior and perverse speech.

4I offer advice about wise living and provide insight and strength.

5Kings reign by me, and rulers decree justice.

6Officials and nobles govern by me as does everyone that judges justly.

7I love the ones that love me, that diligently search me out.

8I have wealth and honor to offer, lasting riches and goodness.

9My fruit has more value than pure gold; my yield outperforms the finest silver.

0I walk in the way of goodness, on the paths of justice,

21to endow with wealth the ones that love me.

Prov 8:12-21

22“The LORD created me at the very beginning, before anything else he did in ancient times.

23I was in place from everlasting, from the beginning of time on earth.

24I came before there were any oceans or any springs abounded with water,

25before the mountains were born or any hills existed,

26before he made soil and fields and dust on the ground.

Prov 8:22-26

27I was there when he put the heavens in place and drew the horizons around the ocean;

28when he set the sky above and established the fountains in the deep;

29when he laid shorelines for the seas so water couldn’t cross their boundaries; when he marked out the foundations for the earth.

0I stood beside him like a general contractor, serving as his daily delight, celebrating with him,

31rejoicing in the world he was making, delighting in humankind.

Prov 8:27-31

32“So now, my children, listen to me because I bless those who keep my ways.

3Obey instruction in order to be wise; don’t neglect it.

4Blessed are those who pay attention to what I say, who watch at my gates each day and wait at my doorpost.

5The ones that find me find life and gain the LORD’s blessing.

6But those who miss out on me hurt themselves; the ones that hate me love death.”

Prov 8:32-36
