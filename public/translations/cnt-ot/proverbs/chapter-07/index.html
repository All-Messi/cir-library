Proverbs Chapter 7

1 Take my advice, son;

2 Keep my commands so you can have a life;

3 Tie them on your fingers;

4 Call wisdom your sister and insight your friend,

6 At the window of my house

8 He was going down the street near her corner

11 She’s loud and rebellious, not content to stay home—

13 She grabs him and kisses him,

15 So I’ve come out to meet you,

16 I’ve decorated my couch with covers,

17 I’ve sprinkled my bed with myrrh, aloes, and cinnamon.

18 Come on, let’s drink our fill of love till morning;

19 My husband’s gone on a long trip;

21 She entices him with persuasion,

22 Finally he follows her like a bull to slaughter,

23 An arrow pierces his liver;

24 So pay attention to what I’m saying, sons.

25 Don’t let your heart turn to its own ways

27 Her house lies on the road to Sheol,
