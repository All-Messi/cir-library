Proverbs Chapter 15

1 A gentle answer deflects anger;

2 The wise dispense knowledge;

3 The LORD sees everything;

4 Gentle words are a tree of life,

5 Fools reject their father’s discipline,

6 There’s wealth in the house of the godly,

7 The lips of the wise give good advice;

8 The LORD detests the sacrifices of sinners,

9 He detests the way of the wicked,

10 Those who abandon the way incur harsh discipline;

11 Sheol and Abaddon lie open before the LORD;

12 Scoffers don’t like people that rebuke them;

13 A cheerful heart makes a happy face,

14 The mind of the wise is hungry for insight,

15 Negative people have nothing but bad days,

16 It’s better to have a little with reverence for the LORD

17 A plate of vegetables with someone you love tastes better

18 Hot-tempered people stir up quarrels,

19 Briers overgrow lazy people’s paths,

20 Wise children make a happy father;

21 Foolishness is fun for the foolish,

22 Without advice plans go awry,

23 Everyone enjoys giving a good answer,

24 The path of the wise leads upward

25 The LORD tears down the house of the proud

26 The LORD detests evil plans

27 Those greedy for gain bring trouble home;

28 The godly think before they speak;

29 The LORD stands far away from the sinful,

30 A cheerful look makes the heart happy;

31 If you listen to constructive correction,

32 If you ignore instruction, you only hurt yourself;

33 Respecting the LORD teaches wisdom;
