1 Chronicles Chapter 4

1 The sons of JUDAH: Perez, Hezron, Carmi, Hur, and Shobal

2 Reaiah son of Shobal was father of Jahath father of Ahumai and Lahad. These were the families of the Zorathites.

3 These were the sons of Etam: Jezreel, Ishma, and Idbash; their sister was Hazzelelponi.

4 Penuel was father of Gedor and Ezer father of Hushah. Those were the sons of Hur, firstborn of Ephrathah ancestor of Bethlehem.

5 Ashhur father of Tekoa had two wives: Helah and Naarah. 6Naarah bore him Ahuzzam, Hepher, Temeni, and Haahashtari. 7The sons of Helah were Zereth, Izhar, Ethnan, 8and Koz, ancestor of Anub and Zobebah, and the families of Aharhel son of Harum.

9 A man named Jabez had more honor than his brothers, and his mother named him Jabez because she bore him “in pain.” 10Jabez called on the God of Israel, “Oh that you’d bless me and make my territory bigger, be with me, and keep me from harm so I won’t suffer!” God gave him what he asked. 1 Ch 4:1-10

11 Chelub brother of Shuhah was father of Mehir father of Eshton.

12 Eshton was father of Beth-rapha, Paseah, and Tehinnah father of Irnahash. These

13 The sons of Kenaz were Othniel and Seraiah. 14bSeraiah was father of Joab, founder of Ge-harashim, so-called because they were craftsmen.

15 The sons of Caleb son of Jephunneh were Iru, Elah, and Naam.

16 The sons of Jehallelel were Ziph and Ziphah, Tiria and Asarel.

17 The sons of Ezrah were Jether, Mered, Epher, and Jalon. These are the sons of Bithia Pharaoh’s daughter that Mered married. She bore Miriam, Shammai, and Ishbah father of Eshtemoa. 18His Jewish wife bore Jered father of Gedor, Heber father of Soco, and Jekuthiel father of Zanoah. 19The sons of the wife of Hodiah sister of Naham were the fathers of Keilah the Garmite and Eshtemoa the Maacathite. 1 Ch 4:11-19

20 The sons of Shimon were Amnon and Rinnah, Benhanan and Tilon.

21 The sons of Shelah son of Judah were Er father of Lecah, Laadah father of Mareshah, the families of the linen workers at Beth-ashbea, 22Jokim the men of Cozeba, Joash Saraph, who ruled in Moab; and Jashubi-lehem. The records are ancient. 23These were the potters and people of Netaim and Gederah. They lived there with the king for his work. 1 Ch 4:20-23

24 The sons of SIMEON: Nemuel, Jamin, Jarib, Zerah, Shaul

25 Shaul was father of Shallum father of Mibsam father of Mishma.

26 Mishma was father of Hammuel, Zaccur, and Shimei.

27 Shimei had 16 sons and 6 daughters, but his brothers didn’t have descendants of Judah. 28They lived in Beersheba, Moladah, and Hazar-shual, 29at Bilhah, Ezem, Tolad, 30Bethuel, Hormah, Ziklag, 31Beth-Marcaboth, Hazar-susim, Beth-Birei, and Shaaraim. Those were their towns till David’s reign. 32Their villages were Etam, Ain, Rimmon, Tochen, and Ashan—5 towns, 33along with the villages that were around them as far as Baal. These were their settlements, and they kept a genealogy. 34Meshobab, Jamlech, Joshah son of Amaziah; 35Joel, Jehu son of Joshibiah son of Seraiah son of Asiel; 36Elioenai, Jaakobah, Jeshohaiah, Asaiah, Adiel, Jesimiel, Benaiah, 37Ziza son of Shiphi son of Allon son of Jedaiah son of Shimri son of Shemaiah: 38these named were leaders in their clans, and their families increased greatly. 39They went to the entrance of Gedor, to the east side of the valley, to look for pasture for their flocks. 40They found good pasture, and the land was large and quiet and peaceful; the previous residents were Hamites. 41These, according to the records, came at the time of Hezekiah, king of Judah, and attacked their tents as well as the Meunites that were there, and destroyed them till today, and lived in place of them, because there was pasture for their flocks. 42From them—from the descendants of Simeon—500 men went to Mount Seir with Pelatiah, Neariah, Rephaiah, and Uzziel, sons of Ishi as their leaders. 43They destroyed the remnant of the Amalekites that escaped and have lived there ever since. 1 Ch 4:24-43
