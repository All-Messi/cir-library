1 Chronicles Chapter 23

1 Ch 23:24-32

7 The sons of Gershon were Ladan and Shimei.

8 Three sons of Ladan were Jehiel the oldest, Jetham, and Joel.

9 Three sons of Shimei were Shelomoth, Haziel, and Haran. 10-11The other sons of Shimei were Jahath, first; Zina/Zizah, second. Jeush and Beriah didn’t have very many sons, so they were all counted as one family. 1 Ch 23:7-11

12 The sons of Kohath were Amram, Izhar, Hebron, and Uzziel.

13 The sons of Amram were Aaron and Moses.

14 The sons of Moses, the man of God, were included in the tribe of Levi.

15 The sons of Moses were Gershom and Eliezer.

16 The son of Gershom was Shebuel the chief.

17 The only son of Eliezer was Rehabiah.

18 The son of Izhar was Shelomith the chief.

19 The sons of Hebron were Jeriah, first; then Amariah, then Jahaziel, then Jekameam.

20 The sons of Uzziel were Micah, first, then Isshiah.

21 The descendants of Merari were Mahli and Mushi.

22 Eleazar died and had only daughters, so their cousins, Kish’s sons, married them.

23 The sons of Mushi were Mahli, Eder, and Jeremoth. 1 Ch 23:12-23

24 Those were Levi’s descendants by heads of families as counted in their census. They served in the LORD’s Temple from 20 years old and up. 25David said, “The LORD God of Israel has given his people rest, and he lives in Jerusalem permanently. 26The Levites won’t need to carry the Tabernacle and the utensils for its service.” 27By David’s last words, Levi’s descendants were counted from 20 years old and up. 28They were to assist Aaron’s descendants with the service in the courts and rooms in the LORD’s Temple. They were to purify the holy things 29and help with the showbread, the fine flour for grain offerings, wafers without yeast, what’s baked in the pan, or what’s well-mixed, and all measures of volume and size. 30They were to stand each morning and evening to thank and praise the LORD. 31They offered burnt offerings on Sabbaths, new moons, and scheduled festivals in the number set by the Law. 32They maintained the Tent of Meeting and the Holy Place and helped Aaron’s descendants with the service in the LORD’s house.
