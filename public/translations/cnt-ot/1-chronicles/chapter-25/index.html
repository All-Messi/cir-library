1 Chronicles Chapter 25

1 Ch 25:20-31

5 Those were sons of Heman, the king’s seer, to honor him according to God’s message, because God gave Heman 14 sons and 3 daughters.

6 Those were under their father’s direction to sing in the LORD’s Temple, with cymbals, harps, and lyres for the service. Asaph, Jeduthun, and Heman served under the king’s direction. 7There were 288 trained in singing to the LORD with their relatives, talented men. 8They cast lots for their duties, least and greatest, teacher and pupil. 1 Ch 25:1-8

9 The first lot came out for Asaph to Joseph—he and his relatives made 12; 19the twelfth came out for Hashabiah, who with sons and relatives made 12; 1 Ch 25:9-19 21for the fourteenth came out for Mattithiah, who with sons and relatives made 12; 24the seventeenth came out for Joshbekashah, who with sons and relatives made 12; 31the twenty-fourth came out for Romamti-ezer, who with sons and relatives made 12.
