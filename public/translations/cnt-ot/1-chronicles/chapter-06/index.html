1 Chronicles Chapter 6

1 The descendants of LEVI: Gershon, Kohath, and Merari

2 The descendants of Kohath were Amram, Izhar, Hebron, and Uzziel.

3 The children of Amram were Aaron, Moses, and Miriam.

4 Eleazar was father of Phinehas father of 5Abishua father of Bukki father of 6Uzzi father of Zerahiah father of 7Meraioth father of Amariah father of 8Ahitub father of Zadok father of 9Ahimaaz father of Azariah, father of 10Johanan father of Azariah (priest in the Temple that Solomon built in Jerusalem), and 11Azariah was father of Amariah father of 12Ahitub father of Zadok father of 13Shallum father of Hilkiah father of 14Azariah father of Seraiah father of 15Jehozadak, who went into exile when the LORD took Judah and Jerusalem away by Nebuchadnezzar. 1 Ch 6:1-15

16 The sons of Levi were Gershom, Kohath, and Merari.

17 The sons of Gershom were Libni/Ladan and Shimei.

18 The sons of Kohath were Amram, Izhar, Hebron, and Uzziel.

19 The sons of Merari were Mahli and Mushi.

20 The sons of Gershom were Libni, Jahath his son, Zimmah his son, 21Joah his son, Iddo his son, Zerah his son, Jeatherai his son.

22 The sons of Kohath were Amminadab, Korah his son, Assir his son, 23Elkanah his son, Ebiasaph his son, Assir his son, 24Tahath his son, Uriel his son, Uzziah his son, and Shaul his son.

25 The sons of Elkanah were Amasai and Ahimoth, 26The sons of Elkanah were Zophai, Nahath his son, 27Eliab his son, Jeroham his son, and Elkanah his son.

28 The sons of Samuel were Joel his firstborn and Ahijah second.

29 The sons of Merari were Mahli, Libni his son, Shimei his son, Uzzah his son, 30Shimea his son, Haggiah his son, and Asaiah his son. 1 Ch 6:16-30

31 These are the ones David appointed to sing in the LORD’s Tabernacle after the Ark rested there. 32They served in song at the Tent of Meeting till Solomon built the LORD’s Temple in Jerusalem. They served in their order.

33 These are the ones with their sons that served. From the Kohathites were Heman the singer son of Joel son of Samuel (34son of Elkanah son of Jeroham son of Eliel son of Toah 35son of Zuph son of Elkanah son of Mahath son of Amasai 36son of Elkanah son of Joel son of Azariah son of Zephaniah 37son of Tahath son of Assir son of Ebiasaph son of Korah 38son of Izhar son of Kohath) son of Levi son of Israel.

39 Heman’s brother Asaph stood on his right, that is, Asaph son of Berechiah (son of Shimea 40son of Michael son of Baaseiah son of Malchijah 41son of Ethni son of Zerah son of Adaiah 42son of Ethan son of Zimmah son of Shimei 43son of Jahath son of Gershom) son of Levi.

44 On the left were their relatives the sons of Merari: Ethan son of Kishi (son of Abdi son of Malluch 45son of Hashabiah son of Amaziah son of Hilkiah 46son of Amzi son of Bani son of Shemer 47son of Mahli son of Mushi son of Merari) son of Levi.

48 Their relatives the Levites were appointed for the service of the Tabernacle, God’s house. 1 Ch 6:31-48

49 Aaron and his sons offered on the altar of burnt offering and the altar of incense for the work of the Holy of Holies, and atoned for Israel like Moses had commanded.

50 These are the sons of Aaron: Eleazar, Phinehas his son, Abishua his son, 51Bukki his son, Uzzi his son, Zerahiah his son, 52Meraioth his son, Amariah his son, Ahitub his son, 53Zadok his son, and Ahimaaz his son. 54These are their settlements according to their camps within their borders. To the descendants of Aaron of the clans of the Kohathites (Theirs was the first lot.), 55they gave Hebron in Judah with its pastures around it. 56But the fields round the town and its villages they gave to Caleb son of Jephunneh. 57To Aaron’s descendants they gave the following cities of refuge: Hebron, Libnah with its pastures, Jattir, Eshtemoa with its pastures, 58Hilen with its pastures, Debir with its pastures, 59Ashan with its pastures, and Beth-shemesh with its pastures. 60From Benjamin they gave Geba with its pastures, Allemeth with its pastures, and Anathoth with its pastures—13 towns. 1 Ch 6:49-60 61To the rest of Kohath’s descendants, they gave 10 towns by lot from the Manasseh half-tribe. 62To Gershom’s descendants according to their families, they gave 13 towns in Bashan from the tribes of Issachar, Asher, Naphtali, and Manasseh. 63To Merari’s descendants according to their families, they gave by lot 12 towns from the tribes of Reuben, Gad, and Zebulun. 64Israel’s descendants gave the Levites the towns with their pastures. 65From the tribes of Judah, Simeon, and Benjamin, they gave the towns by lot. 66Some families of Kohath’s descendants had towns from the tribe of Ephraim. 67They gave them the following cities of refuge with their pastures: Shechem in the hill country of Ephraim, Gezer, 68Jokmeam, Beth-horon, 69Aijalon, and Gath-rimmon. 70From the Manasseh half-tribe they gave towns with their pastures: Aner and Bileam for the rest of the descendants of Kohath. 1 Ch 6:61-70 71To Gershom’s descendants they gave towns with their pastures from the family of the Manasseh half-tribe: Golan in Bashan and Ashtaroth. 72From the tribe of Issachar they gave towns with their pastures: Kedesh, Daberath, 73Ramoth, Anem. 74From Asher they gave towns with their pastures: Mashal, Abdon, 75Hukok, and Rehob. 76From Naphtali they gave towns with their pastures: Kedesh in Galilee, Hammon, and Kiriathaim. 77To the rest of the Levites, the sons of Merari, from the tribe of Zebulun they gave towns with their pastures: Rimmono and Tabor. 78On the east side of the Jordan, from Reuben they gave towns with their pastures: Bezer in the open country, Jahzah, 79Kedemoth, and Mephaath. 80From Gad they gave towns with their pastures: Ramoth in Gilead, Mahanaim, 81Heshbon, and Jazer. 1 Ch 6:71-81
