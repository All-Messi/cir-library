1 Chronicles Chapter 7

1 ISSACHAR had four sons: Tola, Puah, Jashub, and Shimron.

2 The sons of Tola were Uzzi, Rephaiah, Jeriel, Jahmai, Ibsam, and Samuel, heads of

3 The son of Uzzi was Izrahiah.

4 With them by their generations according to their families were 36,000 troops of the army, because they had many wives and sons. 5Their relatives among the descendants of Issachar were trained soldiers, enrolled by genealogy—87,000 in all. 1 Ch 7:1-5

6 BENJAMIN had three sons: Bela, Becher, and Jediael.

7 Bela had five sons: Ezbon, Uzzi, Uzziel, Jerimoth, and Iri. They were heads of families, trained soldiers enrolled by genealogy—22,034.

8 The sons of Becher were Zemirah, Joash, Eliezer, Elioenai, Omri, Jeremoth, Abijah,

10 The son of Jediael was Bilhan.

12 Shuppim and Huppim were sons of Ir.

13 The sons of NAPHTALI: Jahziel, Guni, Jezer, and Shallum, the descendants of Bilhah.

14 The sons of MANASSEH, borne by his Aramaean concubine: Asriel and Machir father of Gilead

15 Machir took a wife for Huppim and for Shuppim. His sister was Maacah. The name of

17 The son of Ulam was Bedan.

18 His sister Hammolecheth bore Ishbod, Abiezer, and Mahlah.

19 The sons of Shemida were Ahian, Shechem, Likhi, and Aniam. 1 Ch 7:13-19

20 The sons of EPHRAIM were Shuthelah and Bered, Tahath his son, Eleadah his son, Tahath his son, 21Zabad his son, Shuthelah his son, and Ezer and Elead thatpl the men in Gath who were born in the land killed because they came down to steal their livestock. 22Their father Ephraim mourned a long time, and his relatives came to comfort him. 23Then he slept with his wife, and she bore a son, and he named him Beriah, because “misfortune” had come on his family. 24His daughter was Sheerah—who built lower and upper Beth-horon—as well as Uzzen-sheerah. 25Rephah was his son along with Resheph, Telah his son, Tahan his son, 26Ladan his son, Ammihud his son, Elishama his son, 27Non/Nun his son, and Joshua his son. 28Their possessions and settlements were Bethel, Naaran to the east, Gezer to the west, and Shechem as far as Ayyah with their towns. 29Along their border with the Manassites, they had Beth-shean, Taanach, Megiddo, and Dor with their towns. The descendants of Joseph lived in those towns. 1 Ch 7:20-29

30 The sons of ASHER: Imnah, Ishvah, Ishvi, Beriah, as well as their sister Serah

31 The sons of Beriah were Heber and Malchiel father of Birzaith.

32 Heber was father of Japhlet, Shomer, and Hotham, as well as Shua their sister.

33 The sons of Japhlet were Pasach, Bimhal, and Ashvath.

34 The sons of Shemer were Ahi, Rohgah, Jehubbah, and Aram.

35 The sons of his brother Helem were Zophah, Imna, Shelesh, and Amal.

36 The sons of Zophah were Suah, Harnepher, Shual, Beri, and Imrah,

37 Bezer, Hod, Shamma, Shilshah, Ithran, and Beera.

38 The sons of Jether were Jephunneh, Pispa, and Ara.

39 The sons of Ulla were Arah, Hanniel, and Rizia.

40 These were descendants of Asher, heads of families elite soldiers, principal leaders. The number enrolled by genealogy for military service was 26,000 men. 1 Ch 7:30-40
