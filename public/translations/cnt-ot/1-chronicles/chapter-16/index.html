1 Chronicles Chapter 16

1 Ch 16:1-7

4 He appointed some Levites to serve at the Ark—to celebrate, thank, and praise the LORD God of Israel: 5Asaph the chief, second to him Zechariah, then Jeiel, Shemiramoth, Jehiel, Mattithiah, Eliab, Benaiah, Obed-edom, and Jeiel with musical instruments—harps and lyres. Asaph played the loud cymbals; 6Benaiah and Jahaziel the priests blew bugles constantly in front of the Ark.

7 That day David first assigned Asaph and his relatives to give thanks to the LORD.

8 Thank the LORD; call on him;

9 Sing to him, sing praises to him;

10 Take pride in his holy name;

11 Look to the LORD and his strength;

12 Remember the wonderful things he’s done,

14 He’s the LORD our God;

15 Remember his covenant forever,

17 He confirmed it to Jacob as a statute,

19 When they were just a few

21 He didn’t let anybody oppress them;

23 Sing to the LORD, all the earth;

24 Tell about his glory the world over,

25 The LORD is great, and deserves great praise;

26 The people’s gods are idols,

27 Honor and majesty are with him;

28 Attribute to the LORD, families of the nations,

29 Attribute to the LORD the honor that’s due him;

30 Tremble in his presence, all the earth;

31 Let the sky be happy and the earth celebrate;

32 Let the sea roar and everything that fills it;

33 Then the trees in the forest will sing for joy,

34 Give thanks to the LORD because he’s good;

35 Then say, “Save us, our saving God;

36 Bless the LORD God of Israel,

37 He left Asaph and his relatives at the Ark to serve continually as each day’s work required. 38He left Obed-edom with his 68 relatives and Hosah with the son of Jeduthun as gatekeepers. 39He left Zadok the priest and his relatives at the Tabernacle in the high place at Gibeon 40to offer burnt offerings every morning and evening, according to the Law the LORD commanded Israel. 41With them were Heman and Jeduthun and the rest that were designated by name to give thanks to the LORD because his love endures forever. 42With them were Heman and Jeduthun with trumpets and cymbals for the ones that sounded out loud, with instruments for the songs of God and the sons of Jeduthun for the gate. 43Then the people left for home and David went back to bless his household. 1 Ch 16:37-43
