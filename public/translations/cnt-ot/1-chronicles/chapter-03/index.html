1 Chronicles Chapter 3

1 Ch 3:21-24

4 He ruled in Hebron for 7½ years and in Jerusalem for 33.

5 These were born in Jerusalem: Shimea, Shobab, Nathan, and Solomon—four by Bath-shua daughter of Ammiel; 6and Ibhar, Elishama, Eliphelet, 7Nogah, Nepheg, and Japhia, 8Elishama, Eliada, and Eliphelet—nine. 9All those were sons of David besides the sons of concubines. Tamar was their sister. 1 Ch 3:1-9

10 Solomon’s son was Rehoboam father of Abijah father of Asa father of Jehoshaphat father of 11Joram father of Ahaziah father of Joash father of 12Amaziah father of Azariah father of Jotham father of 13Ahaz father of Hezekiah father of Manasseh father of 14Amon father of Josiah father of 15Johanan his firstborn, Jehoiakim second, Zedekiah third, and Shallum fourth.

16 The sons of Jehoiakim: Jeconiah his son and Zedekiah his son

17 The sons of Jeconiah the prisoner were Shealtiel, 18Malchiram, Pedaiah,

19 The sons of Pedaiah were Zerubbabel and Shimei.

21 The sons of Hananiah were Pelatiah and Jeshaiah, the

22 The son of Shecaniah was Shemaiah.
