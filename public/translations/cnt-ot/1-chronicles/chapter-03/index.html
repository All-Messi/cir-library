1 Chronicles Chapter 3

1These were David’s six sons born in Hebron: the firstborn was Ammon by Ahinoam the Jezreelitess; the second was Daniel by Abigail the Carmelitess;

2the third was Absalom son of Maacah daughter of Talmai, king of Geshur; the fourth was Adonijah son of Haggith;

3the fifth was Shephatiah by Abital; the sixth was Ithream by his wife Eglah.

4He ruled in Hebron

for 7

½ years and in Jerusalem for 33.

5These were born in Jerusalem: Shimea, Shobab, Nathan, and Solomon—four by Bath-shua daughter of Ammiel; 6and Ibhar, Elishama, Eliphelet, 7Nogah, Nepheg, and Japhia, 8Elishama, Eliada, and Eliphelet—nine. 9All those were sons of David besides the sons of concubines. Tamar was their sister.

1 Ch 3:1-9

10Solomon’s son was Rehoboam father of Abijah father of Asa father of Jehoshaphat father of 11Joram father of Ahaziah father of Joash father of 12Amaziah father of Azariah father of Jotham father of 13Ahaz father of Hezekiah father of Manasseh father of 14Amon father of Josiah father of 15Johanan his firstborn, Jehoiakim second, Zedekiah third, and Shallum fourth.

6The sons of Jehoiakim: Jeconiah his son and Zedekiah his son

17The sons of Jeconiah the prisoner were Shealtiel, 18Malchiram, Pedaiah, Shenazzar, Jekamiah, Hoshama, and Nedabiah.

19The sons of Pedaiah were Zerubbabel and Shimei. The sons of Zerubabbel were Meshullam and Hananiah; Shelomith was their sister; 20Hashubah, Ohel, Berechiah, Hasadiah, and Jushab-hesed—5.

1 Ch 3:10-20

21The sons of Hananiah were Pelatiah and Jeshaiah, the sons of Rephaiah, the sons of Arnan, the sons of Obadiah, and the sons of Shecaniah,

22The son of Shecaniah was Shemaiah. The sons of Shemaiah were Hattush, Igal, Bariah, Neariah, and Shaphat—6.

23The sons of Neariah were Elioenai, Hizkiah, and Azrikam—3.

24The sons of Elioenai were Hodaviah, Eliashib, Pelaiah, Akkub, Johanan, Delaiah, and Anani—7.

1 Ch 3:21-24
