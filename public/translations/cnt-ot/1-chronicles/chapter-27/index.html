1 Chronicles Chapter 27

1 These were the heads of families, commanders of thousands and hundreds, and their officers that served the king in regard to the divisions that came in and went out month by month all year. Each division numbered 24,000.

2 Jashobeam son of Zabdiel was responsible for the first division for the first month. 3He descended from Perez and was chief of the commanders of the army for the first month.

4 Dodai the Ahohite and his division were responsible for the second month with Mikloth as chief officer.

5 The third commander of the army for the third month was Benaiah, son of Jehoiada the priest, as chief. 6This Benaiah was the most elite soldier of the 30 and responsible for 30. His son Ammizabad was responsible for his division. 1 Ch 27:1-6

7 The fourth for the fourth month was Joab’s brother Asahel and his son Zebadiah.

8 The fifth for the fifth month was commander Shamhuth the Izrahite.

9 The six for the sixth month was Ira son of Ikkesh the Tekoite.

10 The seventh for the seventh month was Helez the Pelonite, descendants of Ephraim.

11 The eighth for the eighth month was Sibbecai the Hushathite from the Zerahites.

12 The ninth for the ninth month was Abiezer the Anathothite from the Benjamites.

13 The tenth for the tenth month was Maharai the Netophathite from the Zerahites.

14 The eleventh for the eleventh month was Benaiah the Pirathonite, descendant of Ephraim.

15 The twelfth for the twelfth month was Heldai the Netophathite of Othniel. 1 Ch 27:7-15

16 In charge of the tribes of Israel

23 But David didn’t count the ones under 20 because the LORD had said he would multiply Israel like stars in the sky. 24Joab son of Zeruiah had started to count them but didn’t finish. Wrath came on Israel because of it, and the number wasn’t included in The Chronicles of King David.

25 Azmaveth son of Adiel oversaw the king’s storehouses. Jonathan son of Uzziah oversaw the storehouses in the country, towns, villages, and

26 Ezri son of Chelub oversaw the farmers that plowed the ground.

27 Shimei the Ramathite oversaw vineyards.

28 Baal-hanan the Gederite oversaw the olive and sycamore trees in the western foothills.

29 Shitrai the Sharonite oversaw the cattle that grazed in Sharon.

30 Obil the Ishmaelite oversaw the camels.

31 Jaziz the Hagrite oversaw the flocks.

32 Jonathan, David’s uncle, was an intelligent advisor and secretary.

33 Ahithophel was advisor to the king.

34 Jehoiada son of Benaiah and Abiathar succeededpl Ahithophel.
