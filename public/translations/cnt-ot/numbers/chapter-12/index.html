Numbers Chapter 12

1 Miriam and Aaron criticized Moses for the Nubian woman he’d married. 2"Has the LORD indeed only spoken through you? Hasn’t he spoken through us too?" The LORD heard it. (3Moses was the most humble man in the world.) 4At once, the LORD told Moses, Aaron, and Miriam, “Come out to the Tent of Meeting.” 5He came down in a pillar of cloud and stood at the entrance to the Tent to call Aaron and Miriam. When they’d come forward, 6he said,

7 Not so with Moses;

8 I speak to him directly, clearly,

9 The LORD’s anger burned against them, and he left. 10When the cloud withdrew from over the Tent, Miriam had leprosy as white as snow. When Aaron turned and saw it, 11he told Moses, “Don’t hold that foolish sin against us. 12Don’t leave her like somebody dead, with flesh half eaten away at birth."

13 Moses cried out to the LORD, “God, heal her!"

14 The LORD said, “If her father had only spit in her face, wouldn’t she have borne her disgrace for a week? Quarantine her outside the camp for a week. Then she can come back."

15 So they did; the people didn’t move on till she returned. 16Then they moved out from Hazeroth to the Wilderness of Paran.
