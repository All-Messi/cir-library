Numbers Chapter 33

1 These are the segments of the journey Israel took when they left Egypt. 2The LORD told Moses to write them down. 3They traveled from Rameses on the fifteenth day, first month, the day after Passover. They left right in front of the Egyptians 4while they were burying the firstborn that the LORD struck. The LORD also carried out judgments against their gods.

5 They left from Ramses to Succoth,

8 They passed through the sea

9 Elim, where there were

10 They camped by the Red Sea,

13 Alush,

15 They camped in the Wilderness of Sinai,

16 Kibroth-hattaavah,

17 Hazeroth,

18 Rithmah,

19 Rimmon-perez,

20 Libnah,

21 Rissah,

22 Kehelathah,

23 Shepher,

26 Tahath,

27 Terah,

28 Mithkah

33 Jotbathah,

34 Abronah,

35 Ezion-geber,

38 The LORD told Aaron to go up to Mount Hor. He died the first day, fifth month, fortieth year after Israel left Egypt; 39he was 123. 40The Canaanite king of Arad, who lived in the Negev, heard Israel was coming.

41 Israel went from Mount Hor

43 Oboth,

44 Iyeabarim (Iyim) on the border of Moab,

46 Almon-diblathaim,

49 They camped by the Jordan from Beth-jeshimoth to Abel-shittim on the plains of Moab. “When you cross the Jordan into Canaan, 52drive out the people, destroy their carved stones and molten images, and tear down their pagan shrines. 53Take the land and live in it because I’ve given it to you. 54You’ll inherit land by allotment per clan. Bigger clans will inherit more; the smaller ones will inherit less. Their allotments will fall within their tribe. 55If you don’t drive out the people, they’ll be pricks in your eyes and thorns in your sides and cause you trouble. 56I’ll end up doing to you what I intend to do to them."
