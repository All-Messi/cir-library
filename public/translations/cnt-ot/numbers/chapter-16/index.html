Numbers Chapter 16

1Korah son of Izhar son of Kohath son of Levi, along with Dathan and Abiram sons of Eliab and On son of Peleth (descendants of Reuben)—2rebelled together with 250 prominent, chosen leaders of the community. 3They gathered against Moses and Aaron, and said, “You’ve gone far enough. The whole community is holy, every one of them, and the LORD’s among them. Why place yourselves above the LORD’s assembly?”

Num 16:1-3

4When Moses heard it, he fell on his face. 5Then he told Korah and his company, “Tomorrow morning the LORD will show who’s his and who’s holy. He’ll bring near the one he chooses. 6You and your company take censers 7tomorrow and put fire in them and incense on them in the LORD’s presence. The man he designates will be the one that’s holy. You’ve gone far enough, you descendants of Levi. [8] 9Aren’t you satisfied that God has set you apart from the community, and brought you near to service the Tabernacle and perform service to the community? 10Isn’t it enough that he’s brought you near? Are you trying to usurp the priesthood too? 11You and your company have gathered against the LORD. Why criticize Aaron?” N

um 16:4-11

12Moses sent for Dathan and Abiram, but they said, “We’re not coming. 13Isn’t it enough that you’ve brought us up out of a land flowing with milk and honey to have us die in a desert? You also want to lord it over us. 14You haven’t brought us to a land flowing with milk and honey. You haven’t given us fields and vineyards to inherit. Do you want to put out these men’s eyes? We’re not coming!”

15Moses was really mad and said to the LORD, “Don’t regard their offering! I haven’t taken a single donkey of theirs or harmed any of them.” 16Moses told Korah, “You and your company, along with Aaron, present yourselves to the LORD tomorrow. 17Each of you 250 and Aaron, take your firepans, put incense on them, and bring them to the LORD.”

18They took their censers and put fire and incense on them. They stood at the entrance to the Tent of Meeting with Moses and Aaron. 19Korah gathered the community against them there. The glory of the LORD appeared to the whole community.

Num 16:12-19

0He told Moses and Aaron, 21“Get away from the community so I can consume them instantly.”

2But they fell on their faces and said, “God, God of everyone’s spirit, when one person sins, will you direct your anger at the whole community?”

23He said to Moses and Aaron, 24“Tell them to get away from where Korah, Dathan, and Abiram live.”

Num 16:20-24

25Moses got up and went to Dathan and Abiram, and the elders followed him. 26He told the community, “Get away from these men’s tents. Don’t touch anything that’s theirs or you’ll get swept away in their sin.” 27They got away from where Korah, Dathan, and Abiram lived. Dathan and Abiram came out and stood at the entrance to their tents with their wives, sons, and little ones. 28Moses said, “This is how you’ll know the LORD has sent me to do this; it’s not my idea. 29If these men die naturally or suffer a typical fate, the LORD hasn’t sent me. 30But if he causes something entirely new and the ground opens up and swallows them and what’s theirs, you’ll understand that they’ve rejected the LORD.”

Num 16:25-30

31Just as he finished speaking, the ground under them gaped open. 32It swallowed them, their families, the people that belonged to Korah, and their possessions. 33They all went down to Sheol alive, and the ground closed back over them. 34Everybody around them fled at their outcry, “The ground may swallow us!” 35Fire from the LORD also consumed

the 250

men that were offering incense.

6The LORD said to Moses, 37“Tell Eleazar to pick up the censers from the blaze and scatter the embers. 38Hammer the censors into sheets for plating the altar, since they did present them to the LORD and they’re holy. They’ll serve as a sign for Israel.”

39Eleazar took the bronze censers and hammered them out as plating for the altar. 40That was to remind Israel that no layman—someone not descended from Aaron—should come to burn incense to the LORD; he’d end up like Korah and his company.

Num 16:31-40

41But the next day the community criticized Moses and Aaron, “You made the LORD’s people die.” 42However, Moses and Aaron turned toward the Tent of Meeting, and the cloud covered it, and the glory of the LORD appeared. 43They went to the front of the Tent, 44and the LORD told Moses, 45“Get away from the community so I can consume them all at once.” They fell on their faces. 46Moses told Aaron, “Take your censer, put fire in it from the altar, and add incense to it. Hurry and take it to the community and atone for them. The LORD’s anger has gone out and started a plague!”

47Aaron did that and ran into the assembly and put on the incense and atoned for them. 48He stood between the dead and living, and the plague stopped. 49
