Numbers Chapter 3

1The Generations of Aaron and Moses When the LORD Spoke to Moses on Mount Sinai Duties of Levites

2The sons of Aaron were Nadab, the firstborn, Abihu, Eleazar, and Ithamar—3anointed and ordained as priests. 4In the Wilderness of Sinai, Nadab and Abihu died from offering before the LORD, fire different from what he’d commanded. They didn’t have any children, so Eleazar and Ithamar served as priests during Aaron’s lifetime.

Num 3:1-4

5The LORD told Moses, 6“Set the tribe of Levi aside to serve Aaron. 7They’ll perform duties for him and the whole community at the Tent of Meeting. They’ll service the Tabernacle 8and keep the furnishings of the Tent of Meeting in assisting with the duties of the people. 9Give the Levites to Aaron and his sons. 10Appoint Aaron and his sons to carry out the duties of the priesthood; execute any layman that tries to come near.”

11Again the LORD told Moses, 12“I’ve taken the Levites from the people in place of the firstborn. 13The firstborn belong to me. When I struck down the firstborn in Egypt, I set apart to myself the firstborn people and animals in Israel. The Levites belong to me; I’m the LORD.” Num 3:5-13

4The LORD told Moses in the Wilderness of Sinai, 15“Count the descendants of Levi by families—every male a month old and up.” 16Moses counted them:

7the sons of Levi: Gershon, Kohath, and Merari. 18 the clans descended from Gershon: Libni and Shimei.

19the clans descended from Kohath: Amram, Izhar, Hebron, and Uzziel.

20the clans descended from Merari: Mahli and Mushi.
