Numbers Chapter 6

1 The LORD told Moses to say to Israel, “When men or women take a Nazarite vow to dedicate themselves to the LORD, 3they must abstain from wine, alcohol, and from vinegar made from them. They can’t drink grape juice or eat fresh grapes or raisins. 4They’re not to eat anything a grapevine produces from seeds to skin. 5"During that time, they have to let their hair grow. 6-7They can’t make themselves ceremonially unclean by going near a dead body even for a parent or sibling that dies. [8] 9"If someone dies suddenly beside them and defiles their dedicated head, they’ll shave it the same day and on the seventh day. 10The eighth day, they’ll take two turtledoves or young pigeons to the priest at the entrance to the Tent of Meeting. 11The priest will offer one as a sin offering and the other as a burnt offering; that will purify them from their defilement. That same day they’ll re-consecrate their head, 12dedicate to the LORD their days as a Nazirite, and take a male lamb a year old for a guilt offering. Their previous days of separation are void. 13"When Nazirites finish their time of separation, they’ll take an offering to the entrance to the Tent of Meeting 14and present it to the LORD:

16 The priest will present them all to the LORD. [17] 18Then the Nazirites will shave their head and put the dedicated hair on the fire that’s under the peace offering. 19The priest will take the ram’s boiled shoulder and one cake and wafer without yeast from the basket, and put them in the Nazirites’ hands. 20The priest will wave them in front of the LORD. They’re set apart for the priest, together with the chest offered by waving and the thigh offered by lifting up. Afterward Nazirites can drink wine again. 21"That’s the law for Nazirites that vow their offering to the LORD for their separation in addition to what else they can afford."

22 The LORD told Moses to have Aaron and his sons 23pronounce this blessing on Israel:

25 The LORD smile on you

26 The LORD lift up his countenance on you
