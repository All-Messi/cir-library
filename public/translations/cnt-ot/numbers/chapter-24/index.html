Numbers Chapter 24

1 When Balaam saw that it pleased the LORD to bless Israel, he didn’t go like before to look for omens, but faced the open country. 2He saw Israel camping by tribes, and God’s Spirit came on him. 3He took up this oracle:

7 Water will flow from Jacob’s buckets;

8 God is bringing him out of Egypt;

9 Israel lies down like a lion;

10 Balak was mad at Balaam. He clapped his hands, and said, “I called you to curse my enemies, and you’ve persisted in blessing them three times! 11Go back home. I said I’d greatly honor you, but the LORD has prevented you from receiving your honor."

12 Balaam said, “Didn’t I tell your messengers, 13’Though Balak gives me his house full of silver and gold, on my own I can’t do anything contrary to the LORD’s command, good or bad. I’ll say what he says.’? 14I’m going to my people, but I’ll advise you what this nation will do to your people later."

15 He took up this oracle:

18 Edom will be a possession;

19 A ruler will arise from Jacob

20 He looked at Amalek and took up this oracle:

21 He looked at the Kenites and took up this oracle:

22 Nevertheless, Kain will be consumed;

23 He took up his oracle:

24 But ships will come from the coast of Kittim

25 Balaam went home and Balak left.
