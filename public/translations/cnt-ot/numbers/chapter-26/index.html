Numbers Chapter 26

1 After the plague the LORD told Moses and Eleazar, 2"Count by families the men 20 years old and up that can go to war." 3Moses and Eleazar told them in the plains of Moab by the Jordan across from Jericho, 4"Count the men 20 years old and up." The Israelites that came out of Egypt were as follows:

5 Reuben by families was Hanoch, Pallu, 6-7Hezron, and Carmi: 43,730

8 The son of Pallu was Eliab, 9father of Nemuel, Dathan, and Abiram. These are the Dathan and Abiram the community called on to oppose Moses and Aaron in the company of Korah, when they contended against the LORD. 10The ground opened up and swallowed them with Korah, when that company died, and the fire consumed 250 men. That served as a warning. 11The sons of Korah, however, didn’t die. 15-18Gad by families was Zephon, Haggai, Shuni, Ozni, Eri, Arod, and Areli. 40,500 The sons of Manasseh were Machir, father of Gilead, father of Jezer, Helek, Asriel, Shechem, Shemida, and Hepher. Hepher was father of Zelophehad, whose daughters were Mahlah, Noah, Hoglah, Milcah, and Tirzah. 52,700 The sons of Ephraim were Shuthelah, Becher, and Tahan. The son of Shuthelah was Eran. 32,500 52The LORD told Moses, 53"Parcel out the land among these for an inheritance in proportion to their numbers, 54a larger inheritance for larger groups, a smaller one for smaller groups. 55Divide it up by lot by tribes. [56]" 57These are the Levites numbered by families: Gershon, Kohath, and Merari. 58These are the families of Levi: the Libnites, Hebronites, Mahlites, Mushites, and the Korahites. Kohath became the father of Amram. 59Amram’s wife was Jochebed, daughter of Levi, born in Egypt. She bore Aaron, Moses, and their sister Miriam. 60Aaron became the father of Nadab, Abihu, Eleazar, and Ithamar. 61Nadab and Abihu died from offering before the LORD fire different from what he’d commanded. 62The number of male Levites a month old and up was 23,000. They weren’t counted with the Israelites because they didn’t inherit with them. 63Those were the ones Moses and Eleazar counted in the plains of Moab by the Jordan across from Jericho. 64But Moses and Aaron didn’t count anyone they’d counted in the Wilderness at Sinai. 65That was because the LORD had said they’d die in the desert. Only Caleb and Joshua would survive.
