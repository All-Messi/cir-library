Exodus Chapter 11

1 The LORD told Moses, “I’m going to bring one more plague on Pharaoh and Egypt. After that, he’ll let you go. When he does, he’ll drive you out completely. 2Tell each man and woman to ask their neighbors for silver and gold articles."

3 The LORD gave the Egyptians a favorable attitude toward the people. Furthermore, Pharaoh’s officers and the people in general respected Moses a lot.

4 Moses said, “The LORD says, ‘About midnight I’ll go out into Egypt, 5and all firstborn will die—the firstborn of Pharaoh on his throne, the firstborn of the slave girl behind the millstones, the firstborn among the livestock. 6There’ll be such an outcry everywhere in Egypt as has never been heard or will be. 7But among the Israelites, not even a dog will bark at people or animals. You’llsg see how the LORD distinguishes Israel from Egypt. 8’Yoursg officials will come and bow to me and say, “Leave, you and your people.” Then I’ll leave.’"

9 The LORD told Moses, “Pharaoh won’t listen; so I’ll multiply my wonders in Egypt.” 10Moses and Aaron performed those wonders for him, but the LORD hardened his heart, and he wouldn’t let the people go.
