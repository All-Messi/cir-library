Exodus Chapter 6

1The LORD told Moses, “Now you’ll see what I’ll do to Pharaoh. He’ll have to let them go. I’ll make him send them out of his country. 2I’m Yahveh. 3I appeared to Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob as ‘God Almighty’ [El-Shaddai]. I didn’t make myself known to them by my name ‘Yahveh.’ 4I made a covenant with them to give them Canaan, where they were living as nomads. 5I’ve heard the Israelites’ groaning because the Egyptians are holding them in slavery, and I’ve remembered my covenant. 6Tell the Israelites, ‘I’ll take you out from under Egyptian bondage and redeem you with power and great acts of judgment. 7I’ll take you to be my people and be your God. You’ll know I’m the LORD your God who brought you out from under the burdens the Egyptians put on you. 8I’ll take you to the land I swore to give Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob.’”

9Moses told the people, but they wouldn’t listen. They were beat down by the cruel slavery.

Ex 5:22-6

0The LORD said to Moses, 11“Go tell Pharaoh to let the Israelites leave his country.”

12But Moses said, “They haven’t listened to me. Why would Pharaoh listen to me? I’m not a good speaker.”

13The LORD gave Moses and Aaron a charge for Pharaoh and the people of Israel about getting the Israelites out of Egypt.
