Exodus Chapter 30

1“Make an altar out of acacia wood as a place to burn incense: 218 x 18 x 36in with horns in one piece with it. 3Overlay it with pure gold on its sides, top, and horns; make a molding around it. 4On two sides put two gold rings under its molding on opposite ends for carrying it. 5Make the poles out of acacia wood overlaid with gold. 6Put the altar in front of the curtain that’s near the Ark of Testimony—in front of the mercy seat that’s on top of the Ark—where I’ll meet you. 7-8Aaron will burn fragrant incense on it every morning and evening and trim the lamps. Burn incense to me continuously throughout your generations. 9Don’t offer foreign incense, burnt offering, or meal offering on this altar. Don’t pour any liquid offering on it. 10Throughout your generations, Aaron will make atonement on its horns once a year with the blood of the sin offering. It’s most holy to me.

Ex 30:1-10

Atonement Money [11] 12“When you take a census of the Israelites, each one will give a ransom for himself to the LORD so there won’t be any plague among them. 13-14Each person that’s counted from 20 years up will give .2 oz a half-shekel of the sanctuary as a contribution to the LORD (20 gerahs to a shekel). 15Rich and poor will contribute the same .2 oz a half-shekel of to make atonement. 16Give it for the service of the Tent of Meeting as a memorial to the LORD. Ex 30:11-16 Bronze Basin for Washing [17] 18“Make a bronze laver with a bronze base, put it between the Tent of Meeting and the altar, and put water in it. 19Aaron and his sons will wash their hands and feet from it 20when they go into the Tent of Meeting so they won’t die when they approach the altar to offer up in smoke a sacrifice by fire to me. 21That will be an ongoing statute for Aaron and his descendants. Ex 30:17-21 Anointing Oil [22] 23“Take the finest spices: 12½ lbs 500 shekels of liquid myrrh, 6¼ lbs 250 shekels of fragrant cinnamon, 6¼ lbs 250 shekels of fragrant cane, 2412½ lbs 500 shekels of cassia, and 1½ gallons hin of olive oil. 25From them, make a holy anointing oil, a perfume mixture, a perfumer’s work. 26With it, anoint the Tent of Meeting, the Ark of Testimony, 27the table and its utensils, the lampstand and its utensils, the altar of incense, 28the altar of burnt offering and its instruments, and the laver and its stand. 29Consecrate them so they’ll be holy. Whatever touches them will be holy. 30Anoint Aaron and his sons to consecrate them for performing service to me as priests. 31Tell the Israelites, ‘This will be an anointing oil holy to me throughout your generations. 32Don’t make any oil in the same proportions or pour it on anybody else. It’s holy, reserved for you. 33Whoever mixes any oil like it or puts any of it on a layman, will be excluded from the community.’” Ex 30:22-33 Incense 34“Take the spices stacte, onycha, galbanum, and pure frankincense in equal amounts 35to make incense, a perfumer’s work—salted, pure, and holy. 36Beat some of it very fine and put part of it in front of the Testimony in the Tent of Meeting, where I’ll meet you. It will be most holy to you. 37Any incense you make for yourselves can’t have the same proportions. My incense will be exclusively for the LORD. 38Whoever makes any perfume like it will be excluded from the community. Ex 30:34-38 Bezalel and Oholiab 31[:1] 2“I’ve specifically called Bezalel son of Uri son of Hur, from the tribe of Judah. 3I’ve filled him with my spirit in wisdom, understanding, and knowledge in all kinds of craftsmanship

4 4to make artistic designs for work in gold, silver, and bronze,

5in cutting stones for settings and carving in wood.

6I’ve appointed with him Oholiab son of Ahisamach from the tribe of Dan. In the hearts of everybody that’s skilled, I’ve put the skill to make everything:

7 7the Tent of Meeting, the Ark of Testimony, the mercy seat on top of it, the furniture in the tent,

8 8the table and its utensils, the pure gold lampstand with its utensils, the altar of incense,

9 9the altar of burnt offering with its utensils, the laver with its stand,

10 10the holy woven vestments for Aaron and his sons for carrying out their priesthood,

11the anointing oil, and the fragrant incense for the Holy Place. They’re to make them the way I told you.

Ex 31:1-11

The Sabbath [12] 13“Tell the Israelites to observe my Sabbaths. They’re a sign between you and me throughout your generations so you can know I’m the LORD who sanctifies you. 14The Sabbath is set aside for you. Execute anybody that profanes it. Exclude from the community whoever works on it. 15Work for six days, but the seventh day will be a complete rest set aside to me. 16Israelites will observe the Sabbath throughout their generations as a continuing covenant. 17It’s a permanent sign between them and me, because I made earth and sky in six days, but stopped working on the seventh day and was refreshed.”

18When God finished speaking to him on Mount Sinai, he gave him the two stone tablets of the Testimony that God wrote with his own finger.
