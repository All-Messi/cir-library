Isaiah Chapter 16

1 Send tribute lambs to the ruler of the land,

2 At the Arnon fords the women of Moab will be

4 Let our refugees from Moab stay among you;

6 We’ve heard about Moab’s excessive pride,

7 So everyone in Moab will moan

8 The fields of Heshbon will wither;

9 So I’ll weep bitterly for Jazer and the wine of Sibmah;

10 Happiness will be gone from the productive fields;

11 So my heart moans for Moab like a dirge on a harp;

12 When the people of Moab

13 The LORD spoke that message earlier about Moab. 14Now he says, “In three years, like a hired hand under contract would count them, I’ll humble Moab’s pride and decimate its sizeable population. Its survivors will be few and feeble."
