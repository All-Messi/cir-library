Isaiah Chapter 52

1 Wake up, wake up;

2 Get up, shake off the dust, captive Jerusalem;

4 In the beginning my people went down to Egypt to live;

5 Why are my people enslaved for nothing

6 Now my people will know my name;

7 How welcome on the mountains

8 Listen! Your watchmen are happy and singing;

9 Break out in joyful song together,

10 He’s bared his holy arm

11 Leave, leave there; don’t hang onto anything defiled;

12 You won’t go out quickly like fleeing refugees;

13 My servant will succeed;

14 But, even as many were astonished at you, my people,

15 He’ll cleanse many nations;
