Hosea Chapter 14

1Israel, come back to the LORD your God; you’ve stumbled because you sinned.

2Come back to the LORD and bring your confession with you. Say, “Take away our sin; receive us graciously so we can offer our praises to you.

3Assyria can’t save us; neither can our warhorses. We won’t say ‘our god’ anymore to something we’ve made; only from you can we orphans receive mercy.”

Hos 14:1-3

4The LORD says, “I’ll heal your apostasy and love you freely because my anger will be gone.

5I’ll be like dew to you; you’ll blossom like a lily.

6 6Your roots will spread out like the cedars in Lebanon.

7Your people that live in your shade, will raise grain again, and blossom like grapevines. You’ll be as fragrant as Lebanon’s wine.

Hos 14:4-7

8“Ephraim, I don’t have any connection with idols. I directly am the One that answers your prayers and takes care of you. I’m like a green cypress; your fruit all comes from me.”

9Whoever is wise, understand— whoever perceives, be aware: the Lord’s way is the right way; good people follow it, but sinners trip over it.

Hos 14:8-9
