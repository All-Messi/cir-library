Hosea Chapter 5

1 Hear this, priests!

2 You rebels have gone deep in slaughter,

3 I know what you’re doing, Ephraim;

4 Your deeds keep you from coming back to me.

6 They’ll go with their flocks and herds

7 They’ve dealt deviously with the LORD

9 Ephraim will become a pile of rubble when he’s rebuked;

10 Judah’s officials are like people that remove landmarks;

11 Ephraim will be oppressed, crushed in condemnation,

12 So I’ll be like a moth in Ephraim,

14 I’ll be like a lion to Ephraim,

15 I’ll go back to my place
