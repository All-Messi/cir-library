Hosea Chapter 6

1 They’ll say, ‘Let’s return to the LORD.

2 He’ll revive us in two days

3 So let’s press on to know the LORD.

5 I’ve cut both of you up in pieces by the prophets;

6 I delight in loyalty more than sacrifice,

7 Like Adam you’ve broken covenant

9 Like raiders waiting for victims,

10 I’ve seen a horrible thing in Israel:

11 Judah, I’ve appointed a harvest time for you
