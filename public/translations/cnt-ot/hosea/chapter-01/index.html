Hosea Chapter 1

1Messages from the LORD to Hosea Son of Beeri at the Time of Uzziah, Jotham, Ahaz, and Hezekiah, Kings of Judah, and Jeroboam Son of Joash/Jehoash, King of Israel Hosea’s Wife and Children

2When the LORD spoke the first time through Hosea, he said, “Marry a prostitute and have children, because the land is committing flagrant prostitution by abandoning me.” 3So he married Gomer daughter of Diblaim, and she had a son. 4The LORD said, “Name him Jezreel. Soon I’ll punish Jehu’s dynasty for the bloodshed in Jezreel, and put an end to Israel. 5At that time, I’ll break Israel’s military strength in the Valley of ‘Jezreel.’” Hos 1:1-5

6Then she had a daughter. The LORD told him, “Name her Lo-ruhamah. I ‘won’t have mercy’ any more on Israel by forgiving them. 7But I will have mercy on Judah and deliver them, but not by bow, sword, battle, horses, or horsemen.”

8When she’d weaned Lo-ruhamah, she had another son. 9The LORD said, “Name him Lo-ammi. You’re ‘not my people,’ and I’m not your God.

10Yet the number of Israelites will be like sand by the sea, that you can’t measure or count. Where I said, ‘You’re not my people,’ I’ll say, ‘You’re children of the living God.’

11Then Judah and Israel will unite and appoint one leader. They’ll return from the exile together because the day of Jezreel [sowing] will be great.”

Hos 1:6-11
