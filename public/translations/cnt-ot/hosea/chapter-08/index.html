Hosea Chapter 8

1 Put the bugle to your lips!

2 Israel pleads with me,

3 Israel has rejected what’s good;

4 He’s set up kings, but not by me;

6 Even that calf originated from Israel!

8 Israel has been swallowed up;

9 He’s gone up to Assyria

10 Even though he’s hired other nations as allies,

12 Though I wrote 10,000 laws for him,

13 The people offer the meat of my sacrifices and eat it,

14 Israel has forgotten his Maker and built palaces;
