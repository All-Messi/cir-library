Hosea Chapter 13

1 When Ephraim spoke, people cringed;

2 Now his people sin more and more

3 So they’ll be like morning mist,

5 I took care of you in the desert,

6 But when you got to pasture,

7 So I’ll be like a lion to you

8 I’ll attack you like a bear robbed of her cubs

10 Where’s your king

11 I gave you a king in my anger

13 The pains of childbirth have come on him;

15 Though he’s flourishing like a reed,

16 Samaria will bear the consequences of his guilt
