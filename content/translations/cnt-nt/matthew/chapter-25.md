## Matthew Chapter 25


### The Parable About Ten Virgins

<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The groom was delayed, and they all got drowsy and fell sleep. </span></i></span>

<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Later, the rest of the virgins came, ‘Sir, let us in.’ </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But he answered, ‘I don’t know you.’ </span></i></span>

<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He gave five talents to one, two to another, and one to another—according to their abilities—and left. Right away </span></i></span>

<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The one with two talents did the same thing and earned two more. </span></i></span>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“‘Well done, good, dependable servant. <p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“But the servant he gave one talent came up, ‘Master, I knew you were a hard man, reaping where you didn’t sow, gathering where you didn’t scatter. </span></i></span>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“‘You wicked, lazy servant. <span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Throw the worthless servant out into the dark. ### Discussion of the Final Judgment


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“When the Son of Man comes in his glory with the angels, he’ll sit on his glorious throne. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The nations will stand in front of him, and he’ll separate them like a shepherd separates sheep from goats. </span></i></span>
