## Matthew Chapter 8


### Cleansing a Leper


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Jesus came down from the hillside, big crowds followed him. </span></span>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus reached out and touched him, <i>“I’m willing; be cleansed.” </i>Instantly his leprosy disappeared. </span></span>
