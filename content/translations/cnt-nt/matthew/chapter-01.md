<p class="verse"><sup>17</sup>There are 14 generations from Abraham to David, 14 from David to the exile in Babylon, and 14 from the exile to Messiah.</p>

<p class="section-ref">Mt 1:1-17</p>

### Jesus' Birth Announced to Joseph

<p class="verse"><sup>18</sup>This is how the birth of Jesus the Messiah took place. His mother Mary was engaged to Joseph. Before they got married, it was discovered that she was pregnant (by the Holy Spirit). <sup>19</sup>Joseph, her husband, was a just man and not willing to disgrace her publicly, so he intended to break off the engagement privately. <sup>20</sup>As he was struggling with the matter, an angel of the LORD appeared in a dream, "Joseph son of David, don't have qualms about marrying her. She's pregnant by the Holy Spirit. <sup>21</sup>She'll have a son you're to name Jesus, because he'll 'save' his people from their sins." <sup>22</sup>That fulfilled what the LORD had said by the prophet Isaiah,</p>

<blockquote class="scripture-quote">
<sup>23</sup>"A virgin will have a son<br>
they'll call Emmanuel ('God with us')" [Is 7:14].
</blockquote>

<p class="verse"><sup>24</sup>Joseph got up and did what the angel said, <sup>25</sup>but didn't have relations with her till she had a son; he named him Jesus.</p>
