## Matthew Chapter 16


### Visit to Magadan;



### Demand for a Sign from the Sky


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Pharisees and Sadducees came, testing him, asking for a sign from the sky. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He answered, <i>“When evening comes, you say, ‘It’ll be fair weather; the sky’s red.’ </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In the morning, ‘It’ll be bad weather today; the sky’s red and dark.’ You can read the sky, but you can’t read the signs of the times. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>An sinful, adulterous generation keeps looking for a sign, but it’s not going to get one—except for the sign of Jonah.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> He left.                                                   </span></span></p>

### Fourth Withdrawal East of the Lake;

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The disciples went to the other side <i>of the lake </i>and had forgotten to take bread. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus told them, <i>“Watch out for the ‘yeast’ from the Pharisees and Sadducees.” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They started talking among themselves, <i>“He said that because we didn’t bring any bread.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus knew it, <i>“Men with little faith, why are you talking about not having any bread? </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t you understand or remember the 5 loaves for the 5,000 and how many medium-sized baskets full you picked up, </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>or the 7 loaves for the 4,000 and how many large baskets full you picked up? </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>How do you not know I wasn’t talking about bread!? Watch out for the ‘yeast’ from the Pharisees and Sadducees.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then they got it; he hadn’t warned them about the yeast in bread, but about the teaching of the Pharisees and Sadducees.  </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“John the Baptist, Elijah, Jeremiah, one of the prophets.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Who do <u>you</u> say I am?”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Simon Peter said, <i>“You’re the Messiah, the Son of the living God.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Bless you, Simon Barjonah. <span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As sure as you’re ‘Peter,’ I’ll build my church on that ‘rock.’ Even the gates of the grave won’t be strong enough to stop it. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll give you<sup>sg</sup> the keys to the kingdom of heaven. <span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then he charged them not to tell anybody he was the Messiah.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But Peter pulled him aside and got after him, <i>“That’s <u>not</u> going to happen to you!”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But Jesus turned away from him, <i>“Get behind me, Satan. You’re being a stumbling block to me. You don’t have God’s interests in mind, but people’s interests.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“If anybody wants to follow me, he has to set his own interests aside, pick up his cross, and follow me. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Whoever wants to save his life will lose it, and whoever loses it over me will find it.
</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>What good does it do to get the whole world and lose your life? What will you give in exchange for your life? </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Son of Man will come in the glory of his Father with his angels. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Some people standing here won’t die before they see the Son of Man coming in his kingdom.</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>                 </span></span></p>
