## 2 Corinthians Chapter 12


### Paul’s Revelation Experiences


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Being proud may be appropriate, but </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>it’s </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>not profitable. I’ll go on to visions and revelations from the Lord. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I know a man in Christ 14 years ago—whether in the body or not, I don’t know; God knows. He was caught up to the third heaven—</span><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[3]repeat 4</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>to Paradise—and heard things you can’t express in words. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll take pride in that man; I won’t boast about myself except in my weaknesses. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If I do boast, I won’t be foolish </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>about it</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>; I’ll talk about what’s real. ### The Thorn in the Flesh


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Because of the extraordinary greatness of </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>those</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> revelations, God gave me a thorn in the flesh—a messenger from Satan—to discipline me so I wouldn’t think too highly of myself. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I asked the Lord three times to take it away. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“My grace is sufficient for you; my power reaches its highest expression in weakness.” </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Gladly, then, I’ll take pride in my weaknesses so Christ’s power can live in me. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So I delight in weakness, mistreatment, need, persecution, and difficulty for Christ. When I’m weak I’m strong.  </span></span></p>
