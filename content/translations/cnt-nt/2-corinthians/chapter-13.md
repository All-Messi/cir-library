<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>This will be the third time I’m coming to you.</span><b><i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> “Establish everything by two or three witnesses” </span></i></b><b><span style='font-size: 9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Deut 19:15].</span></b><b><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></i></b></span>

<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>because you’re looking for proof that Christ is speaking in me. He’s not weak toward you but is strong among you. </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We are happy to be weak and you to be strong. We pray for this too: your perfection. </span></span>
