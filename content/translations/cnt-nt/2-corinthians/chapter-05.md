### Taking Courage in Christ


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So we always take courage. <span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We operate by faith more than by sight.) </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We have courage and prefer to be absent from the body and at home with the Lord. </span></span>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So, fearing the Lord, we persuade people. <span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We’re not trying to commend ourselves to you again; we’re giving you a chance to take pride in us so you can answer people that take pride in appearances instead of heart. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Whether we’re beside ourselves </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>is</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> for God </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>to decide</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>; whether we have a sound mind is for you</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> to decide</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>. </span></span>

Even though we’ve known Christ that way, we don’t anymore. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So if anybody’s in Christ, they’re a new creation. The old things are gone; they’ve become new; </span></span>
