## 2 Corinthians Chapter 7

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Since we have these promises, let’s “cleanse” ourselves from all defilement of flesh and spirit, and become perfect in holiness out of respect for God.                                                                                                                               </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">2 Cor 6:14-7:1</p>


### The Great Value of Repentance


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Make room for us <i>in your hearts</i>. We haven’t done anything wrong to anybody, misled anybody, taken advantage of anybody. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’m not criticizing <i>you</i>; like I said earlier, you’re in our hearts whether that means living or dying together. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’m being bold with you and expressing the great pride I take in you.                                                                                                              </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">2 Cor 7:2-4</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When we came to Macedonia, we couldn’t rest. We had troubles on every <i>side</i>—conflicts outside and fears inside. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But God, who comforts the downhearted, comforted us by Titus’ coming </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and by the comfort he felt over you. He reported your longing, your grieving, <i>and</i> your zeal for me, so I rejoiced even more. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Though I made you sad by </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>my</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> letter, I don’t regret </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>sending it</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>, though I felt bad that it made you sad for a while. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’m happy now, not because you were grieved, but because you were grieved to repentance. You were sorry in conformity with God’s will, so you didn’t suffer any loss because of us. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Sorrow that conforms</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> a person </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>to God’s </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>will</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> produces a repentance that leads to salvation without regret. The grief of this world leads to a dead end. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>What earnestness that grieving in conformity to God’s will has produced in you! What defense and indignation! What fear and longing! What zeal and avenging of wrong! In everything you’ve shown yourselves to be innocent in the matter. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I didn’t write to you just for the offender and the offended; I wrote for your earnestness toward us to become clear to you in front of God. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Because of that, we’ve been comforted.                                                                                                               </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">2 Cor 7:5-13a</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Besides being comforted, we rejoiced over Titus’ joy because you all refreshed his spirit. </span><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If I ever told him how proud I was about anything in you, I wasn’t embarrassed. As we told you the truth about everything, our comments to Titus proved to be true </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>as well</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His affection for you is all the greater when he remembers your obedience and how you received him with heartfelt respect. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’m glad I can be confident about you in everything.                                                                                                                     </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">2 Cor 7:13b-16</p>
