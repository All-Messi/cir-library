## Mark Chapter 4


### The Sermon in Parables


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height:normal'><b><span style='font-size:14.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>4</span></b><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>:</span></sup></b><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Another time he was trying to teach by the lake, but the crowd was<sup>p</sup> so big he got in a boat on the water and sat down. The crowd was on the shore. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He started teaching them with parables. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“<i>Listen. A sower went out to sow. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As he sowed, some seed fell along the path, and birds came and ate it. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Some fell on rocky ground, where there wasn’t much soil. It sprouted up right away because the soil was thin. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But when the sun climbed high, it scorched and shriveled up because it didn’t have roots. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Other seed fell among thorns, and the thorns grew up and choked it out, and it didn’t produce anything. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But other seed fell on good ground, grew up, and produced 30, 60, 100 times over. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Whoever has ears, listen!”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;line-height:normal'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When he was by himself, the ones around him with the Twelve asked him about the parables. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told them, <i>“You’re allowed to know the unrevealed things about God’s kingdom, but to people outside I’m doing things in parables </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>so </span></i></span></p>


### ‘They’ll certainly see, but not perceive;


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;line-height:normal'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        they’ll certainly hear, but not understand, </span></i></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;line-height:normal'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    be converted, and </span></i></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:1.5in;line-height:normal'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I forgive them’</span></i></b><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></b><b><span style='font-size:9.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Is 6:9-10].                                                               </span></b></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Mk 4:1-12</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;line-height:normal'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“You don’t understand this parable? How will you understand any parable? </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A sower sows the message. Some </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>deeds
<i>fall beside the path where the message is sown. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When they hear it, Satan comes at once and takes away what was sown in them. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Some fall on rocky soil. When they hear the message, they’re glad and accept it right away. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But they don’t have root in themselves and just last a while. When trouble or persecution comes on account of the message, they stumble. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Some </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>seeds<i> fall among thorns. They hear the message; </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>but the cares of this age, the deception of wealth, the desire for other things enter in and choke them out, and they don’t produce anything. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Others fall on good ground; they hear the message, accept it, and produce 30, 60, 100 times as much as was sown.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;line-height:normal'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></i><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“People don’t take a lamp and stick it under a basket, do they? They put it on a stand. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Nothing is hidden that won’t be revealed; nothing is secret that won’t come to light. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Whoever has ears, listen!”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Be careful what you listen to. The measure you measure with, you’ll be measured by—and more so.
</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Those who have, will receive more; those who don’t have, will lose even what they do have.” </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Mk 4:13-25</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;line-height:normal'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></i><span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“God’s kingdom is like a man that puts seed in the ground. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He sleeps and gets up day after day, but he doesn’t know how the seed sprouts and grows. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The ground produces crops on its own—the stalk, the head, then the seed in the head. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When the crop is ripe, he gets out his scythe to harvest it.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;line-height:normal'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></i><span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“What can we compare God’s kingdom to? What’s it like? </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>It’s</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> <i>like a mustard seed, which is smaller than any other seed people plant. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yet it grows up bigger than the other garden plants and puts out branches, so birds build nests in its shade.</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Only with parables like those was he delivering the message to them as they could understand. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Privately he’d explain them to his disciples.                                                                                                                                           </span></span></p>

### Stilling the Storm


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That evening he said<sup>p</sup>, <i>“Let’s go to the other side.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They left the crowd and took him along in the boat like he was; <i>some</i> other boats went along. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v37" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A dangerous storm came up<sup>p</sup> and was sloshing waves into the boat, and it was filling up. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v38" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> <u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> was in the back of the boat, sleeping on a cushion. They woke<sup>p</sup> him up, <i>“Rabbi, don’t you care that we’re going to die?” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v39" id="v39"><sup class="verse-num">39</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Now that he was awake, he rebuked the wind, and a general calm settled in. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v40" id="v40"><sup class="verse-num">40</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said, <i>“Why are you so scared? Don’t you trust me yet?”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v41" id="v41"><sup class="verse-num">41</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They were even more scared now, <i>“Who <u>is</u> this that even the wind and waves obey?”                                                                                                                     </i></span></span></p>
