## Mark Chapter 9


### The Transfiguration


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Six days later Jesus took<sup>p</sup> Peter, James, and John up on a mountain by themselves, and was transfigured in front of them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His clothes turned brilliant white like no laundryman can bleach them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Elijah appeared with Moses, talking to Jesus. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Peter responded<sup>p</sup>, <i>“Rabbi, it’s good to be here. Should we set up three tents—one each for you, Moses, and Elijah?” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He didn’t know what to say, because they were afraid. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A cloud surrounded them, and a voice came out of it, <i>“This is my dearly loved Son; listen to him.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Suddenly they looked around and didn’t see anybody but Jesus.                                                                                                                                               </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Mk 9:2-8</p>


### Discussing the Vision


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As they were coming down the mountain, he impressed on them not to pass on to anybody what they’d seen till the Son of Man arose from the dead. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They kept the matter to themselves and wondered what <i>“arising from the dead”</i> meant.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They asked him, <i>“Why do the scribes say Elijah has to come first?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Elijah does come first and restore everything, and how does the scripture say that the Son of Man has to suffer many things and be rejected? </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But Elijah has in fact come, and they treated him however they wanted to, like scripture says.”         </span></i><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Mk 9:9-13</p>


### Healing the Demoniac Boy


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When they got back to the rest of the disciples, they saw a big crowd around them and scribes arguing with them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When they saw him, the whole crowd was really surprised and ran to greet him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus asked, <i>“Why are you arguing with them?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A man in the crowd answered him, <i>“Rabbi, I brought you my boy. He has a spirit that makes him unable to talk. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Wherever it seizes him, it throws him on the ground. He foams at the mouth and grinds his teeth; and it makes him rigid. I spoke to your disciples about casting it out, but they couldn’t do it.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Unbelieving bunch, how long am I going to be with you? How long am I going to deal with you? Bring him to me.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They brought him; and when the spirit saw Jesus, it threw the boy into convulsions. He fell on the ground and rolled around and foamed at the mouth.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus asked his dad, <i>“How long has this been going on?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“Ever since he was little. </i></span><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Lots of times it’s thrown him into the fire or the water to destroy him. But if you can, help us; have sympathy for us.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus said, <i>“If you can? Everything’s possible for someone that believes.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His dad cried out,
<i>“I believe! Help my unbelief!”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Jesus saw the crowd quickly coming together, he commanded the unclean spirit, <i>“Mute and deaf spirit, I charge you, come out of him and don’t go into him again.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>It cried out and threw him into severe convulsions and came out. The boy looked like he was dead, so much so that many of them said he’d died. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But Jesus took his hand and raised him up, and he stood up.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Jesus went into a house, his disciples asked him privately<i>, “Why couldn’t <u>we</u> cast it out?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“This kind doesn’t come out except by prayer.”</span></i><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                                </span></b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Mk 9:14-29</p>


### Third Prediction of His Death


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They left and began traveling around Galilee. He didn’t want anybody to know it, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>because he was trying to teach his disciples, <i>“I’m going to be betrayed to authorities that will put me to death. Three days later I’ll resurrect.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They didn’t understand, but they were reluctant to ask him about it.                                                                                                                               </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Mk 9:30-32</p>


### Discussion About Rank


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When they came home to Capernaum, he asked them, <i>“What were you<sup>pl</sup> arguing about on the road?”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They kept still because they’d been arguing about who had higher rank. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He sat down and called the Twelve over, <i>“If anybody wants to be first, he’ll put himself last as servant of all.” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He took a little child and stood him among them and put his arms around him, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v37" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Whoever receives one of these children in my name receives me, and whoever receives me receives the One that sent me.”</span></i></span></p>


### The Unknown Exorcist


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v38" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>John said, <i>“Rabbi, we saw a fellow casting out demons in your name and tried to stop him because he wasn’t following us.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v39" id="v39"><sup class="verse-num">39</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“<i>Don’t stop<sup>pl</sup> him. Nobody that performs a miracle in my name</i> <i>will turn around right away and say something bad about me. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v40" id="v40"><sup class="verse-num">40</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Whoever is not against us is for us. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v41" id="v41"><sup class="verse-num">41</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Whoever gives you a cup of water in my name because you’re following the Messiah, will certainly not miss out on his reward. </span></i><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                                        </span></b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Mk 9:33-41</p>

<span class="verse verse-v43" id="v43"><sup class="verse-num">43</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If your hand causes you problems, cut it off. It’s better to go about life deformed than to have two hands and go to hell—into the unquenchable fire. [</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v44" id="v44"><sup class="verse-num">44</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>]<sup>ms</sup> </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v45" id="v45"><sup class="verse-num">45</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If your foot causes you problems, cut it off. It’s better to go about life deformed than to have two feet and be thrown into hell. [</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v46" id="v46"><sup class="verse-num">46</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>]<sup>ms</sup> </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v47" id="v47"><sup class="verse-num">47</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If your eye causes you problems, gouge it out. <span class="verse verse-v48" id="v48"><sup class="verse-num">48</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>where the worms don’t die, and the fire doesn’t burn out.</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v49" id="v49"><sup class="verse-num">49</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Everybody will be salted with fire. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v50" id="v50"><sup class="verse-num">50</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Salt’s good, but if it loses its saltiness, how will you make <u>it</u> salty?
