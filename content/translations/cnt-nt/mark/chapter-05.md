## Mark Chapter 5


### Healing the Gadarene Demoniacs


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height:normal'><b><span style='font-size:14.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>5<sup>:</sup></span></b><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They came to the other side of the lake, to the area of the Gerasenes. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As soon as he got out of the boat, a man with an unclean spirit met him from the tombs. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’d been living in the</span><span style='font-size:16.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>
</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>tombs, and nobody could</span><span style='font-size:16.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>
</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>tie him up anymore.</span><span style='font-size:16.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>People had often bound him with shackles and chains. He’d pulled the chains apart and break the shackles in pieces. Nobody was strong enough to subdue him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Constantly day and night in the tombs and in the hills, he’d scream and cut himself with stones. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When he saw Jesus a long way off, he ran and bowed to him </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and screamed<sup>p</sup>,
<i>“What is this, Jesus, Son of God Most High! I beg you by God not to torment me!”</i> (</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said that because Jesus had told the unclean spirit to come out of him<i>.</i>)</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He asked him, <i>“What’s your name?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“‘Legion’; there are lots of us.”</i> </span><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He begged Jesus not to send them away from the area. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A tremendous herd of pigs was feeding on the hillside. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Send us into the pigs.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He let them. They went into the herd of about 2,000, which stampeded down the steep hill into the lake and drowned.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The herdsmen ran away and reported it in town and in the countryside, and people came to see what had happened. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They came<sup>p</sup> over to Jesus and saw the demon-possessed man sitting there dressed and in his right mind, and they were afraid. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The people that had seen it described to them how it was with the demoniac and the pigs. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The people begged Jesus to leave. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As he was getting in the boat, the demoniac begged to go along; </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>but Jesus told<sup>p</sup> him, <i>“Go home to your family and tell them how much the Lord has done for you and how he was merciful to you.” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He went and told in the Decapolis what all Jesus had done for him, and everybody was amazed.
</span></span></p>

### Raising Jairus’ Daughter;



### Healing a Woman with Chronic Hemorrhaging


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Jesus crossed back over to the other side in the boat, a big crowd had gathered beside the lake. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A ruler of the synagogue, named Jairus, saw him and came<sup>p</sup> and fell down<sup>p</sup> to him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He pleaded<sup>p</sup>,
<i>“My little girl is at the point of death. Please come and lay hands on her so she’ll live.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus went with him. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            A big crowd was following him and pressing in on him. </span><span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A woman there had been hemorrhaging for twelve years. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She’d been put through a lot by doctors and spent everything she had without any benefit—but got worse. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When she heard about Jesus, she came up behind him in the crowd and touched his clothes. She’d said, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“If I can just touch his clothes, I’ll be cured.” </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Instantly her bleeding stopped. She could tell in her body that she was healed from the scourge she’d been dealing with for so long.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>At once Jesus sensed that power had gone out of him. He turned around in the crowd, <i>“Who touched my clothes?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His disciples said, <i>“You see the crowd pressing in on you and you say, ‘Who touched me?’”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He kept looking around to see who did it. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The woman, afraid and trembling, knew what had happened to her. She came and fell down to him and admitted the whole thing. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Daughter, your faith has cured you. Have a good day and stay healed from your ailment.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>While he was speaking, they came<sup>p</sup> from the synagogue ruler’s house, <i>“Your daughter has died; why bother the Rabbi anymore?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But Jesus overheard the message and told<sup>p</sup> the synagogue ruler, <i>“Don’t give up; just trust me.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v37" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He only let Peter, James, and his brother John follow him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v38" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When he came<sup>p</sup> to the man’s house and saw the crying and loud wailing, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v39" id="v39"><sup class="verse-num">39</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>he went in and said<sup>p</sup>, <i>“Why are you making such a scene? The girl’s not dead; she’s ‘asleep.’” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v40" id="v40"><sup class="verse-num">40</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They started ridiculing him. He put them all out and took her parents and the three with him into where the girl was. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v41" id="v41"><sup class="verse-num">41</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He took her hand, “<i>Talitha, qum</i> <i>(</i></span><i><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Girl, get up</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>).”
</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v42" id="v42"><sup class="verse-num">42</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She stood up right away and started walking around. (</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She was twelve.</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>) They were completely astounded. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v43" id="v43"><sup class="verse-num">43</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He gave them strict orders that nobody should know about this and told them to give her something to eat.                                                                              </span></span></p>
