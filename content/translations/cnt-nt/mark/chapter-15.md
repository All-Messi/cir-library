## Mark Chapter 15


### The Two Trials Before Pilate


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>At daylight, the chief priests held session with the elders, scribes, and full Sanhedrin. They bound Jesus and led him away to Pilate. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pilate asked him, <i>“Are you the ‘King of the Jews’?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“Yes.”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The chief priests accused him of all kinds of things.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pilate asked him again, <i>“You’re not saying anything? Look at all the things they’re accusing you of.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus still didn’t answer. Pilate was surprised.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>At each festival, Pilate would release a prisoner that they asked for. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A prisoner called Barabbas was incarcerated with insurgents that<sup>pl</sup> had committed murder in the uprising. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The crowd came forward and started asking Pilate to do as he customarily did for them.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pilate answered, <i>“Do you want me to release ‘the King of the Jews’?”</i> (</span><b><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>10</span></sup></b><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He knew the high priests had delivered him over because they envied him</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.)</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But the high priests stirred up the crowd to ask for Barabbas instead.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pilate answered again, <i>“What do you want me to do then with the one you call ‘the King of the Jews’?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Crucify him!”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“What’s he done?”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            They shouted louder, <i>“Crucify him!”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pilate wanted to quiet the crowd, so he released Barabbas and delivered Jesus to be flogged and crucified.        </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Mk 15:1-15</p>


### Torture by the Roman Soldiers


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The soldiers took him into the court—The Praetorium—and got the whole cohort together. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They dressed him in purple and twisted together a crown made out of thorns and put it on him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They began to acclaim him, “<i>Hail, King of the Jews!”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and were beating him on the head with a reed, spitting on him, and kneeling and bowing to him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When they were done making fun of him, they took off the purple robe and dressed him in his own clothes, and took<sup>p</sup> him away to crucify him.
</span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Mk 15:16-20</p>


### The Way to Golgotha


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They conscripted<sup>p</sup> Simon, a Cyrenian coming in from the countryside—father of Alexander and Rufus—to carry his cross. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They took<sup>p</sup> him to Golgotha (“The Skull”), </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and offered him wine mixed with myrrh, but he didn’t take it. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When they’d crucified him, they divided up his clothes by casting lots for what each soldier would take.                                                </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Mk 15:21-24</p>


### The Death of Messiah


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>It was 9 o’clock when they crucified him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pilate inscribed his accusation above him, <i>“The King of the Jews.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They also crucified</span><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>p</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> two insurgents with him, one on each side. <p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> </span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The people going past threw insults at him and shook their heads<i>, “Ha! You that tear down the sanctuary and rebuild it in three days, </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>save yourself; come down from the cross!”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The chief priests along with scribes made fun of him too, <i>“He saved other people; he can’t save himself! </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Messiah, King of Israel, come down now from the cross so we can see and believe!”</span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> Even the ones crucified with him were insulting him.                                            </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>At noon it got dark over the whole country till 3. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>At 3 o’clock Jesus cried out very loud, </span><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“<i>Eloi, Eloi, lama sabachthani</i></span></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></i><b><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>(</span></b><b><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>My God, my God, why have you deserted me</span></b><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>)</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span><b><span style='font-size:9.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Ps 22:1]?”</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Some of the people standing nearby heard it, <i>“He’s calling for Elijah.”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>One man ran and filled a sponge with sour wine, put it on a reed, and gave him a drink, <i>“Let’s see if Elijah will come and take him down.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v37" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus let out a loud cry and quit breathing.                                                      </span></span></p>

### Miracles Accompanying Messiah’s Death


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v38" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The curtain in the sanctuary tore in two, top to bottom. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v39" id="v39"><sup class="verse-num">39</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When the centurion standing beside him saw the way he quit breathing, he said, <i>“This man really was God’s Son.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v40" id="v40"><sup class="verse-num">40</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Some women watching from a distance included Mary the Magdalene; Mary, mother of James the Less and Joses; and Salome. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v41" id="v41"><sup class="verse-num">41</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When he was in Galilee, they traveled with him and helped him. There were some other women that came up with him to Jerusalem.       </span></span></p>

### The Burial of Jesus


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and Watch at the Tomb</span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height: normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v42" id="v42"><sup class="verse-num">42</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When evening came, since it was Preparation, the day before Sabbath, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v43" id="v43"><sup class="verse-num">43</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joseph of Arimathea—a respected member of the Sanhedrin, who was waiting for God’s kingdom—gathered the courage to go to Pilate and ask for Jesus’ body. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v44" id="v44"><sup class="verse-num">44</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pilate was surprised that he’d already died. He called the centurion to ask him if Jesus was dead already. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v45" id="v45"><sup class="verse-num">45</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When he understood from him that Jesus was dead, he let Joseph have the body. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v46" id="v46"><sup class="verse-num">46</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joseph had bought a linen sheet; he took Jesus down and wrapped him in it. He put him in a rock-hewn tomb and rolled a stone over the entrance. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v47" id="v47"><sup class="verse-num">47</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Mary the Magdalene and Mary, mother of Joseph, watched where they laid him.
