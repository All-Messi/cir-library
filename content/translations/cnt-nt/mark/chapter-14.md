### Peter’s Denials


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v66" id="v66"><sup class="verse-num">66</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Peter was down below in the courtyard. A servant girl of the high priest came. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v67" id="v67"><sup class="verse-num">67</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When she saw Peter warming himself, she looked at him closely, <i>“You were with the Nazarene, Jesus.” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v68" id="v68"><sup class="verse-num">68</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But he denied it, <i>“I don’t know what you’re talking about,” </i>and he went out to the entryway.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v69" id="v69"><sup class="verse-num">69</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The servant girl saw him again and started telling the ones standing around, <i>“This <u>is</u> one of them.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v70" id="v70"><sup class="verse-num">70</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He denied it again. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height:normal'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A little later the ones standing around said, <i>“You really <u>are</u> one of them; you’re a Galilean.”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v71" id="v71"><sup class="verse-num">71</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He started taking oaths and swearing, <i>“I don’t know the man you’re talking about.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v72" id="v72"><sup class="verse-num">72</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Immediately a rooster double-crowed, and Peter remembered: <i>“Before a rooster double-crows, you’ll deny me three times.”</i> He broke down sobbing.        </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Mk 14:66-72</p>
