## Acts Chapter 11


### Peter Reports to Jerusalem


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The apostles and believers in Judaea heard that Gentiles had accepted God’s message. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Peter went up to Jerusalem, members of the Circumcision took issue with him,</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> </span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“You went in and ate with uncircumcised men.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Peter explained step by step, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I was in Joppa praying when I saw a vision in a trance, something like a big sheet came down from the sky by four corners. It came to me, </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and I stared into it and saw four-footed animals, wild animals, reptiles, and birds. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I heard a voice, ‘Kill and eat them, Peter.’</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I said, ‘No, Sir; I’ve never eaten anything unclean.’</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“The voice spoke a second time out of the sky, ‘Don’t <u>you</u> consider unclean what <u>God</u> has cleansed.’ </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That happened three times, and everything went back up into the sky.       </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Right then, three men from Caesarea stood at the house where we were staying. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Spirit told me to go with them without scruples. These six brothers went along and entered the man’s house. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told us how he’d seen an angel standing in his house, saying, ‘Send to Joppa for Simon called Peter. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’ll tell you what will save you and your household.’ </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“While I was speaking, the Holy Spirit fell on them like on us in the beginning. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I remembered what the Lord had said, ‘John immersed in water, but <u>you’ll</u> be “immersed” in the Holy Spirit.’ </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If God gave <u>them</u> the same gift he gave us when <u>we</u> trusted in the Lord Jesus the Messiah (‘</span></i><i><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Christ’</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>), who was I to oppose God?”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When they heard that, they backed off and praised God, <i>“Then God has also offered Gentiles repentance to life.”</i></span></span></p>

### The Good News in Antioch


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The ones scattered by the ordeal surrounding Stephen traveled as far as Phoenicia, Cyprus, and Antioch, speaking the message just to Jews. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But some Cypriots and Cyrenians went to Antioch and started presenting the Lord Jesus to Greeks as well. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Lord was with them, and a large number turned to the Lord. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The church in Jerusalem heard about them and sent Barnabas to Antioch. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When he got there and saw <i>the evidence of </i>God’s grace, he was glad and encouraged them with heartfelt purpose to remain with the Lord. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He was a good man, full of the Holy Spirit and faith; and a significant number joined the Lord. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He went to Tarsus to look for Paul. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When he found him, he brought him to Antioch. For a year they gathered in the church and taught a sizable crowd. They called the disciples Christians first in Antioch.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>At that time, prophets from Jerusalem came down to Antioch. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>One, named Agabus, signified by the Spirit that a severe famine would come over the whole <i>Roman </i>empire, which took place in the <i>reign of</i> Claudius. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The believers, as they could, made up their mind to send relief to the Christians in Jerusalem. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They sent it to the elders by Barnabas and Saul.                                           </span></span></p>
