## Acts Chapter 10


### The Conversion of Cornelius


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A centurion in Caesarea named Cornelius, from the Italian cohort, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>was a devout God-fearer along with his household. He gave considerable charitable gifts to the people and prayed to God constantly. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In a vision about 3 o’clock, he saw an angel of God coming in and saying,<i> “Cornelius.”</i>   </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He looked at him and was afraid, <i>“Yes, Sir?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Your prayers and charitable gifts have come up as a memorial to God. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Send
</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>some
<i>men to Joppa and summon Simon called Peter. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’s staying with Simon a tanner whose house is by the sea.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When the angel left, he called two household servants—his personal assistants—and a devout soldier. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He went through everything with them and sent them to Joppa.             </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Acts 10:1-8</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The next day, as they were getting close to the town, Peter went up on the housetop about noon to pray. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He was getting hungry. While they were fixing lunch, he fell into a trance. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He watched the sky open up and something like a big sheet was let down to earth by four corners. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>It held reptiles, birds, and four-footed animals. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A voice said, <i>“Kill and eat them, Peter.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“No, Sir; I’ve never eaten anything unclean.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The voice spoke again, <i>“Don’t <u>you</u> consider unclean what <u>God</u> has cleansed.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That happened three times, and the vessel went back up into the sky.             </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Acts 10:9-16</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>While Peter was wondering what the vision could possibly mean, Cornelius’ men had asked around for Simon’s house and were standing at the gate. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They called out, asking if Simon called Peter was staying there.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>While he kept trying to figure out the vision, the Spirit said, <i>“Three men are looking for you. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Go down and go with them without scruples. I’ve sent them.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He went down and said, <i>“I’m the one you’re looking for. Why have you come?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They said, <i>“Cornelius is a centurion, a God-fearer with a good reputation in the Jewish nation. A holy angel directed him to invite you to his house to hear what you have to say.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So he invited them in and put them up for the night. The next day he went with them, and some brothers from Joppa went along.                                                                                                   </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Acts 10:17-23</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>On the second day, he came to Caesarea. Cornelius was expecting them and had called his relatives and close friends. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Peter came in, Cornelius met him and fell down to him in homage. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Peter raised him up, <i>“Please stand up; I’m just a man too.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As they talked, Peter went in and found a large group.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told them, <i>“You know a Jew’s not supposed to associate at home with a foreigner, but God has shown me not to consider anybody </i>ceremonially <i>unclean.
</i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So without objecting, I came when you called. Why did you send for me?”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Cornelius said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Four days ago about this time, I was observing 3 o’clock prayer in my house. A man stood in front of me in bright clothes </span></i><span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and said, ‘Cornelius, God has heard your prayer and remembered your charitable gifts. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Send to Joppa for Simon called Peter. He’s staying with Simon, a tanner next to the sea. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Right away I sent for you. Thank you for coming. We’re here in front of God to hear what the Lord has commanded you.”</span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Acts 10:24-33</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Peter said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I perceive that God doesn’t show favoritism with people. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But, in every nation he accepts people that respect him and live right. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He sent the message to the people of Israel, delivering good news about peace through Jesus the Messiah, who is Lord of all. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v37" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You know what took place all over Judaea, beginning in Galilee after the baptism that John preached. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v38" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God anointed Jesus the Nazarene with the Holy Spirit and power. He went around doing good and healing people the devil had oppressed, because God was with him. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v39" id="v39"><sup class="verse-num">39-40</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We witnessed what he did in the country of the Jews and in Jerusalem. On the third day God raised up the One they killed on a tree and presented him clearly, </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v41" id="v41"><sup class="verse-num">41</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>not to everybody, but to witnesses he’d previously chosen—to us who ate with him after his resurrection. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v42" id="v42"><sup class="verse-num">42</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He sent us to tell the people that he’s the One God appointed to judge the living and the dead. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v43" id="v43"><sup class="verse-num">43</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The prophets show that through him everybody who trusts in him has his sins forgiven.”</span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Acts 10:34-43</p>


### Gentiles Receive the Spirit


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v44" id="v44"><sup class="verse-num">44</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>While Peter was speaking, the Holy Spirit fell on the audience. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v45" id="v45"><sup class="verse-num">45</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The believers from the Circumcision who had come with Peter were surprised that God poured out the Holy Spirit on Gentiles too. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v46" id="v46"><sup class="verse-num">46</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They heard them speak in languages and praise God.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Peter responded, </span><span class="verse verse-v47" id="v47"><sup class="verse-num">47</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Nobody can forbid water, can they, for baptizing these who have received the Holy Spirit like we did?”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v48" id="v48"><sup class="verse-num">48</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He commanded to baptize them into Jesus the Messiah. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They asked him to stay for several days.                                                            </span></p>
