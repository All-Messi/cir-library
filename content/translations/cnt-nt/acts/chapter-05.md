## Acts Chapter 5


### Ananias and Sapphira


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A man named Ananias and his wife Sapphira also sold a piece of land. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He kept part of the price and took the rest to the apostles. (She was in on it.) </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Peter said, <i>“Ananias, why did Satan prompt you to lie to the Holy Spirit, when you kept part of the price of the field? </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>While you had it, wasn’t it yours? When you sold it, didn’t you control it? Why decide to do this? You didn’t just lie to people, but to God.” </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Ananias heard that, he collapsed and died. Great respect came on everybody that heard about it. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The young men got up, wrapped him up, carried him out, and buried him.                           </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Some three hours later his wife came in, not knowing what had happened. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Peter asked her, <i>“Did you<sup>pl</sup> sell the field for such and such?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“Yes.”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Peter said, <i>“Why’d you<sup>pl</sup> agree to test the Spirit of the Lord? The ones that buried your husband are at the door, and they’ll carry you out.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She collapsed right there and died. The young men carried her out to bury her beside her husband. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>People that heard about what happened came to greatly respect the church.                                                                                                                            </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Acts 5:1-11</p>


### Miracles Among the People


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The apostles did many signs and wonders among the people, and they were getting together in Solomon’s Portico. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>People hesitated to join them, but they held them in high regard. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Believers kept coming to the Lord, large numbers of men and women. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As a result, they were bringing sick people out into the streets and putting them on cots and stretchers so when Peter came, even his shadow might fall on some of them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Crowds even came from towns around Jerusalem, carrying sick people and people harassed by unclean spirits. They were all healed.                                                                                                                                                                         </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Acts 5:12-16</p>


### The Apostles Persecuted


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The high priest and his associates—Sadducees—were jealous. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They arrested the apostles and put them in public custody. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>During the night an angel of the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> opened the gates of the jail and let them out, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Go into the Temple and speak to the people the message about this life.” </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They went in about daylight and started teaching. The high priest and his associates convened Israel’s Sanhedrin, the senate, and sent to the jail to bring the apostles. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But the officers didn’t find them. They came back and reported,</span><b><span style='font-size: 14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> <sup>23</sup></span></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“We found the jail securely locked and the guards standing at the doors; but when we opened it, we didn’t find anybody inside.”</span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The captain of the Temple and the chief priests were dumbfounded about what this might lead to. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A fellow came and told them, <i>“They’re standing in the Temple, teaching the people.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The captain went with the officers and brought them without force because they were afraid the people would stone them. </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Acts 5:17-26</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They took them into the Sanhedrin, and the high priest asked them, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Didn’t we strictly charge you not to teach in this name? You’ve filled Jerusalem with your teaching and intend to bring this man’s blood on us.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Peter and the apostles said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“We have to obey God instead of you. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Our ancestors’ God raised up Jesus that you <u>did</u> murder on a tree. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God exalted him as Leader and Savior to his right hand to offer repentance and forgiveness to Israel. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We speak to that fact and so does the Holy Spirit God has given to ones that obey him.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Acts 5:27-32</p>


### Gamaliel’s Advice


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They were furious and wanted to execute them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But a Pharisee in the Sanhedrin named Gamaliel, a respected teacher of the Law, stood up and told them to put the men out <i>of the council chamber</i> for a while, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Israelites, be careful what you do to them. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Before now, Theudas rose up, claiming to be somebody. About 400 men joined him. He was killed, and everybody that obeyed him scattered and didn’t amount to anything.</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v37" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“After that, Judas the Galilean rose up during the census, and drew people to him. He perished too, and the ones that obeyed him scattered. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v38" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Now I tell you, leave these men alone. If this effort is coming from men, it’ll be overthrown. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v39" id="v39"><sup class="verse-num">39</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If it’s from God, you won’t overthrow them, and you can be fighting against God.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They agreed. </span><span class="verse verse-v40" id="v40"><sup class="verse-num">40</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They called the apostles, beat them, charged them not to speak in Jesus’ name, and released them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v41" id="v41"><sup class="verse-num">41</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They left the Sanhedrin, elated over being considered worthy of suffering for The Name. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v42" id="v42"><sup class="verse-num">42</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Every day in the Temple and from house to house, they continued to preach that Jesus was the Messiah.                                                                                                               </span></span></p>
