## Acts Chapter 8


### Paul Leads a General Persecution


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Severe persecution broke out against the church in Jerusalem.
