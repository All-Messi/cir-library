## Romans Chapter 5


### Extreme Grace


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We’ve been justified by trusting, and we express trusting God by trusting our Lord Jesus Christ. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Christ</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> gives us access to this grace that we stand in. <i>Because of it</i>, we rejoice in the hope that comes from <i>our</i> glorious God. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Not only that, but we can </span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>even rejoice in hardships because we know they produce <u>endurance</u>, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>which produces <u>character</u>, which produces a <u>hope</u> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>that doesn’t disappoint because God keeps pouring out his love into our hearts through the Holy Spirit he gave us. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When we were still weak, at the right time Christ<sup>Messiah </sup>died for the ungodly. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Hardly anybody is </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>willing</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> to die even for <u>somebody that’s</u> <u>good</u>. Somebody might, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>but God has shown us how much he cares about us by having Christ<sup>Messiah </sup>die for us while we were still <u>sinners</u>. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>More than that, if his blood has justified us, we’re safe from God’s displeasure. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If the <u>death</u> of his Son reconciled us to God while we were estranged, now that he’s reconciled us, his <u>life</u> will save us. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>All the while, we take pride in God through his Son who reconciled us.                                                                                                                     </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Rom 5:1-11</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>       </span></span></p>


### Adam and Christ in Contrast


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So, as through one man, sin—hence death—entered the world, death passed on to everybody because everybody has sinned. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Before the Law, there was sin in the world, but sin isn’t held against a person if there’s no law. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But, death did prevail from Adam to Moses even over the ones that hadn’t sinned like Adam did—who corresponds to the One that was to come.                                                                 </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rom 5:12-14</p>

In the one case, many died because one sinned. In the other case, many received abundant grace because of God’s gracious gift and the one man Jesus Christ. </span></span>

</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If
<u>death</u> prevailed because of one man’s sin, much more will <u>life</u> prevail—in the ones that receive abundant grace and bestowed righteousness—because of the One, Jesus Christ. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As by the sin of <u>one</u> <i>there was what led</i></span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> to condemnation for <u>all</u> people, so by one act of righteousness <i>there is</i> justification that leads to life for <u>all</u> people. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As by one man’s disobedience many became sinners, so by One <i>man’s</i> obedience many will become righteous. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Law came in besides to increase sin, but where sin <u>increased</u> grace <u>multiplied</u>.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So as sin prevailed in <u>death</u>, grace could prevail in righteousness to eternal <u>life</u> through Jesus Christ our Lord.                                                                       </span></span></p>
