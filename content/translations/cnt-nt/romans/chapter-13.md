## Romans Chapter 13


### Respect for Political Rulers


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-left:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Every person needs to submit to higher authorities, because there’s no authority except by God’s <i>approval</i>. God has established the authorities that exist. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So if a person resists them, he’s resisting what God has established, and he’ll be condemned for it. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Rulers don’t bring fear for good behavior, but for bad. You shouldn’t be afraid of authority. Do what’s right, and you’ll be praised for it. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They’re God’s ministers to you for good. If you do what’s wrong, be afraid; they don’t carry a sword for nothing. They punish people for doing bad things. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So you need to submit, not only to avoid punishment, but for conscience sake. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>For the same reason, pay <i>your</i> taxes, because they’re God’s ministers, who dedicate themselves to those very things. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pay everybody what’s due to them—taxes, customs, respect, honor.                  </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rom 13:1-7</p>


### Call for Renewed Goodness


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-left:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t owe people anything except to love them. The one that loves another fulfills the Law. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Do not commit adultery, murder, steal, covet”</span></i></b><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></b><b><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Ex 20:13-15, 17; Deut 5:17-19, 21]<i> </i></span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>or any other commandment is summed up in <b><i>“Love your neighbor as yourself”</i> </b></span><b><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Lev 19:18]</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>.</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Love doesn’t do anything bad to a “neighbor;” so it fulfills the Law.                                                       </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-left:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Do</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> that because you know it’s time to get up from sleeping. Your salvation’s nearer now than when you came to believe. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Nighttime is far gone and daytime’s coming; so lay aside what people do in the dark and pick up the tools used in daylight. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Live respectably in the daylight, not with carousing and indecency, rivalry and jealousy. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Put on the Lord Jesus Christ, and don’t cater to physical drives.                  </span></span></p>
