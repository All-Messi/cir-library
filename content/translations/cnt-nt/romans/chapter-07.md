## Romans Chapter 7


### The Marriage Comparison


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t you know, you that know the Law, that it applies to someone as long as the person’s alive? </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Law ties a woman to her husband as long as he’s alive. If he dies, she’s free from the law about husbands. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So while he’s alive, she’d be an adulteress if she joined herself to another man. But if her husband dies, she’s free from the law, and she’s not an adulteress if she joins herself to another man. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So, through the Christ’s body you also died to the Law and united with someone else—who resurrected—so you could bear fruit to God. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When you were in the flesh, <i>its</i> sinful desires that were through the Law worked in your members to bear fruit that led to death. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Now you’re released from the Law because you’ve died to what imprisoned you. As a result, you’re serving in the new spirit, not in the old letter.  </span></span></p>

### Indwelling Sin

Goodness, no! But I wouldn’t have known sin if it weren’t for the Law. I wouldn’t have known coveting if the Law hadn’t said, <b><i>“Do not covet”</i> </b></span><b><span style='font-size: 10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Ex 20:17; Deut 5:21]</span></b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>.</span><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></b></span>

<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I was alive apart from<i> </i>law at one time; but when the commandment came, sin came to life </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and I died. The commandment that was meant to bring me life turned out to bring <i>me </i>death.
</span></span>

### Inner Conflict


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So, did what was good kill me? <span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We know the Law is spiritual, but I’m physical, sold like a servant to sin. </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’m no longer doing it; sin’s doing it in me. </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I

<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If I’m doing what I don’t want to do, <u>I’m</u> not doing it; <u>sin’s</u> doing it in me. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I find the principle that evil lies in me who wants to do good. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In my heart of hearts, I delight in God’s Law, </span></span>

That pattern is imprisoning me in the sin that’s in my members. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Miserable man! Who’s going to rescue me from this death-filled body? </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Thank God, our Lord Jesus Christ!
