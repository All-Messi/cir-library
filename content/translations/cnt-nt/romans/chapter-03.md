## Romans Chapter 3


### The Advantage Jews Had

What’s the benefit of circumcision? </span></span>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        <b><i>“ . . . that you can be regarded </i></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If our unrighteousness highlights God’s righteousness, we wouldn’t say, would we, that God’s not fair in inflicting <i>his</i> displeasure? </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We wouldn’t say, would we, that </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>since our lie highlights God’s truth, he shouldn’t consider us sinners? </span></span>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        <b><i>“Not one is good,</i></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in'><b><i><sup><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    </span></sup></i></b><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They’ve all turned away;</span></i></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    as a group they’ve become depraved.</span></i></b></p>

### Goodness by Trust


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Now, aside from <u>law</u>, a goodness from <u>God</u> has come to light, <i>a goodness</i> the Law and prophets talk about: </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>a goodness from <u>God</u> through <u>trusting</u> in <u>Jesus Christ</u> whether by<i> </i><u>Jew or Gentile</u>. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Everybody has sinned and falls short of God’s glory; </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>his grace freely justifies them<i> </i>through the redemption that comes through Christ Jesus. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’s the One that God appointed <i>as a </i>basis for forgiveness by trusting in his blood. <i>Putting him forward</i> demonstrates God’s goodness in overlooking sins previously committed </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>during the time when he puts up with <i>them</i>. <i>Putting Christ forward</i> demonstrates <i>God’s

<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We reason that trust justifies us apart from what we do under law. </span></span>
