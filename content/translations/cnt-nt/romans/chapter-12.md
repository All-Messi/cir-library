## Romans Chapter **12**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><strong><sup>1</sup></strong>So I urge you by God’s mercies to present your bodies <em>as</em> living sacrifices, holy <em>and</em> pleasing to God; <em>that’s</em> your spiritual service. <strong><sup>2</sup></strong>Don’t conform yourselves to this world; transform yourselves by renewing your mind so you can discern what God’s will <em>is</em>—<em>what’s</em> good, pleasing, and perfect.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>By the grace God gave me, I say to everyone among you not to think more of yourselves than you should. Think properly as measured by the faith God has allotted to each <em>of you</em>. <strong><sup>4</sup></strong>There are many parts to one body, and they don’t all serve the same purpose. <strong><sup>5</sup></strong>That’s how it is with the many of us that are in Christ. Individually we’re interdependent parts with one another. <strong><sup>6</sup></strong>We have different gifts according to God’s grace. If <em>our gift is</em> prophecy, <em>let’s prophesy </em>in accordance with our faith. <strong><sup>7</sup></strong>If <em>it’s </em>service, <em>let’s carry out our commitment to him</em> in <em>our</em> service. If <em>it’s</em> teaching, <em>let’s do it</em> in teaching. <strong><sup>8</sup></strong>If <em>it’s </em>encouraging, <em>let’s </em><em>do it</em> in encouragement. If <em>it’s</em> sharing, <em>let’s do it</em> liberally. If<em> it’s </em>leading,<em> </em>let’s <em>do it</em><em> </em>diligently. If<em> </em><em>it’s</em> showing mercy, <em>let’s do it</em> cheerfully.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Rom 12:1-8</p>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><strong><sup>9</sup></strong>Love has to be genuine. Hate what’s bad; hold onto what’s good. <strong><sup>10</sup></strong><em>Devote</em><em> yourselves</em> to one another in brotherly love. Look up to one another in showing honor. <strong><sup>11</sup></strong>Be diligent,<em> be</em> enthusiastic in spirit, serve the Lord. <strong><sup>12</sup></strong>Rejoice in hope, endure adversity, devote yourselves to prayer, <strong><sup>13</sup></strong>share in the saints’ needs, show hospitality to strangers. <strong><sup>14</sup></strong>Bless people that persecute you; don’t curse them. <strong><sup>15</sup></strong>Rejoice with those who are rejoicing; cry with those who are crying. <strong><sup>16</sup></strong>Take the same attitude toward other people that <em>you take toward</em> yourself. Don’t be proud; associate with humble people. Don’t consider yourself smart. <strong><sup>17</sup></strong>Don’t pay back evil for evil. Be careful to do what’s good in front of everybody. <strong><sup>18</sup></strong>As much as comes from <u>your</u> part, live peacefully with everyone. <strong><sup>19</sup></strong>Don’t take revenge, dear friends; walk away from anger.
