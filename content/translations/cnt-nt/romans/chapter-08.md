## Romans Chapter 8


### Freedom Through Christ


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So
<i>there’s</i></span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> no condemnation in Christ Jesus. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The life-producing spirit that operates in him has freed you from law that produces sin and death. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>What the Law couldn’t do because it operated through weak flesh, God <i>did</i> by sending his Son in the form of sin-oriented flesh to deal with sin in the flesh and put it in its place. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So the goodness the Law called for, you can fulfill by living according to the spirit instead of the flesh. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>People that live in a flesh-oriented way focus on material things; people that live in a spirit-oriented way focus on things that are not material. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The fleshly mentality leads to a dead end; the spiritual mentality leads to life and peace. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So the flesh-oriented mentality opposes God because it doesn’t—and can’t—be in submission to his Law. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>People that live in a flesh-oriented way can’t be pleasing God. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But, we’re not flesh-oriented, but spirit-oriented if God’s Spirit lives in us. If we don’t have Christ’s spirit, we don’t belong to him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If Christ is in us, the body <i>is</i> dead because of sin, but the spirit <i>is</i> alive because of goodness. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If the Spirit of the one that resurrected Jesus is living in us, the One that resurrected Christ will make our mortal bodies alive by the Spirit that is living in us.                                                                     </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rom 8:1-11</p>


### Living Above the Flesh


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So, we’re not obliged to live in a flesh-oriented way. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We’ll die if we live that way. But, we’ll live if by spirit we put to death the misdeeds of flesh. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As many as God’s spirit leads, are God’s children. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We didn’t receive again a sense of bondage resulting in fear, but a sense of sonship that calls out <i>‘Father!’</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Spirit himself testifies with our spirit that we’re God’s children. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If
<i>we’re</i> children, then <i>we’re</i> heirs, God’s heirs, fellow heirs with Christ—<u>if</u> we suffer with <i>him</i> so we can be glorified with <i>him</i>.
     </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rom 8:12-17</p>


### Longing for the Eternal


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I don’t consider the sufferings of this time worth comparing to the glory that’ll be revealed. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Creation’s deep longing waits for <i>that</i> revelation to God’s children. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Creation’s been subjected to worthlessness, not willingly, but by the One who subjected it in hopes </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>of freeing it from bondage to decay <i>and delivering it</i> to the glorious freedom of God’s children. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Up till now the whole creation has been groaning in birth pains. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We, too, who have the firstfruits of the Spirit, long to be adopted as children, and to have our bodies redeemed. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Hope has saved us but hope that we can see isn’t hope. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We hope for what we don’t see, and patiently long for it.        </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>And, the Spirit helps our weaknesses. We don’t know what to pray for, but he intercedes for us with sighs we can’t say. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The One who examines hearts understands what our spirits mean and intercedes for the saints in a way that harmonizes with God’s will. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We know everything works out for good for those who love God and are called in a way that fits with his purpose. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The ones he foreknew he predestined to conform to the image of his Son so he’d be the oldest of many brothers. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The ones he predestined he called, justified, and glorified. </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rom 8:18-30</p>

If God’s for us, who’s against us? </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If he didn’t spare his own Son, but offered him up for us all, how will he not graciously give us everything we need? </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Who’s going to level accusation against God’s chosen people? He justifies; who condemns? </span></span>

</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the One that died and, beyond that, resurrected to God’s right hand to intercede for us. </span></span>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    we’re considered sheep for slaughter”</span></i></b><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></b><b><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Ps 44:22]</span></b><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>.</span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v37" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In all these things, we more than conquer through the One who loved us. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v38" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’m confident that death or life, angels or rulers, present or future things, powers, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v39" id="v39"><sup class="verse-num">39</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>height or depth, or any other created thing, can’t separate us from the love God has for us in Christ Jesus our Lord.</span></span></p>
