## Romans Chapter 1


### Salutation


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Paul, servant of Christ<sup>Messiah</sup> Jesus, called <i>to be </i>an apostle, and set apart to deliver the good news </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God promised through his prophets in the scriptures. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3-5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That news is about his Son, David’s son by descent—physically, and God’s Son by designation—spiritually as to holiness. <i>God</i> powerfully <i>designated him such</i> by raising him from the dead. Through Jesus Christ our Lord we’ve received grace and apostleship for making obedient faith available to Gentiles.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Among them you, too, are the called <i>people </i>of Jesus Christ.<i> </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>To God’s loved ones in Rome, called <i>to be</i> holy: grace and peace from God our Father and the Lord Jesus Christ.         </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rom 1:1-7</p>


### Desire to Visit the Romans


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>First, I’m grateful to my God for you, <i>grateful</i> through Jesus Christ because everywhere in the world people are talking about your faith. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God’s my witness—that I serve in my spirit for the good news about his Son—I think about you constantly. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In my prayers I always plead that, somehow by God’s will, I can eventually come to you. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I long to see you so I can share some spiritual gift that will help make you secure <i>in the faith</i>.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That way I’ll be encouraged among you by the faith we share. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I want you to know that I’ve often intended to come but couldn’t. <i>I’ve wanted</i> to accomplish something among you like I’ve done among the rest of the Gentiles. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14-15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’m obligated to preach to Gentiles and barbarians, to wise and foolish, and to you in Rome.                                                                                           </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rom 1:8-15</p>


### Life by Trust


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’m not ashamed of the good news. It’s God’s power for bringing salvation to everybody that trusts, to Jews first and to Gentiles. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The good news reveals a goodness that comes from God through a growing trust <i>in him.</i> The scripture says, <b><i>“A good person will have life by trusting” </i></b></span><b><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Hab 2:4]</span></b><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.</span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rom 1:16-17</p>


### Foolishness of Disbelief


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God reveals from heaven his displeasure at ungodliness and at unjust people that suppress the truth by </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>their </span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>injustice.<sup> </sup></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He does that because what they know about God is evident in them because he made it clear to them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ever since he created the universe, what he’s created clearly makes visible the invisible things about him, namely his eternal power and divine nature. So people don’t have any excuse. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They knew God but didn’t give him his rightful place as God or practice gratitude. They gave themselves over to foolish speculation and their senseless hearts went dark. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>While they claimed to be smart, they became dumb </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and exchanged the glory of the immortal God for images of mere mortals, birds, four-footed animals, reptiles.</span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rom 1:18-23</p>


### God’s Giving People Up


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So because of lusts in their hearts, God gave them up to unclean </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>behavior</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>—to dishonoring their bodies between themselves. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They exchanged the truth about God for a lie and worshiped and served what was created instead of the One that created, who is blessed forever. Amen. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God gave them up to dishonorable desires. Their females exchanged <i>their</i></span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> natural sexual relationships for what’s </span><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>contrary to nature</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Males left their natural sexual relationships with females and burned in lust for other males, males with males, committing shameful acts and receiving in themselves the payback that’s appropriate to their delusion. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Even as they didn’t see fit to have God in what they knew, God gave them over to a perverted mind. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They were </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>filled with sinfulness, wickedness, greed, malice, jealousy, murder, strife, deception, meanness, gossip, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>slander, hatred for God, arrogance, boasting, disloyalty, evil scheming, disobedience to parents. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They were</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> without understanding, untrustworthy, unloving, unmerciful. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They knew God’s standards and </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>knew</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> that people who practice such things deserve to die; but they did them </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>anyway</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> and delighted in people that did them.</span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rom 1:24-32</p>
