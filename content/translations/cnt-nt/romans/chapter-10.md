## Romans Chapter 10

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> My heart’s desire and prayer to God is for their salvation. <strong><sup>2</sup></strong>I admit they have a zeal for God, but they don’t know what they’re doing. <strong><sup>3</sup></strong>They don’t understand the righteousness that comes from God, and they try to establish their righteousness <u>by themselves</u>.
