## 1 Timothy Chapter 4


### Future Heresies

<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They’ll</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> forbid marriage and require abstinence from food that God created for faithful people to receive with gratitude. </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God’s word and </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>our </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>prayers sanctify everything he created.                                           </span></span></p>

### Timothy’s Interim Ministry


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t let anybody disregard you because you’re young. Set an example for the faithful in what you say and do—in love, faith, purity. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Till I get there, give attention to the </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>public</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> reading </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>of scripture</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>, to encouragement, to teaching. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t neglect your spiritual gift that you received by prophecy and the laying on of hands by the eldership. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Cultivate it; immerse yourself in it so everybody will see your progress. </span></span>
