## 1 Timothy Chapter 1


### Salutation


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Paul, apostle of Christ<sup>Messiah</sup> Jesus by the authority of God our Savior and Christ Jesus our hope, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>to Timothy <i>my</i> loyal son in the faith. Grace, mercy, <i>and</i> peace from God <i>the</i> Father and Christ Jesus our Lord.                       </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">1 Tim 1:1-2</p>


### Warning Against False Teachers


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When I left for Macedonia, I urged you to stay in Ephesus to instruct certain people not to teach false doctrine </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>or pay attention to myths and endless genealogies. Those things lead to speculation rather than God’s plan, which operates by faith. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The goal of instruction is love from a pure heart, a clear conscience, and sincere faith. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Some have turned away from these to empty talk and got lost. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They want to teach the Law, but they don’t know what they’re talking about so confidently.                                             </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">1 Tim 1:3-7</p>


### Using the Law Lawfully


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We know the Law is good if you use it ‘lawfully.’ </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>It’s not there <i>so much </i>for good people, as for lawless, disorderly, godless, sinful, unholy, profane <i>people</i>. It’s for murderers, for people that kill
<i>their </i>fathers and mothers, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>for immoral people, male homosexuals, kidnappers, liars, perjurers, and anything else that contradicts sound doctrine </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>as set forth in the glorious good news from our blessed God—the good news he entrusted me with.                                                                                                                                  </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">1 Tim 1:8-11</p>


### Paul’s Gratitude for Salvation


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’m grateful to Christ Jesus who has strengthened me and considered me worth trusting though I’d been a blasphemer, persecutor, insolent person. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I received mercy because I did it ignorantly in disbelief. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Lord’s grace overflowed with the faith and love that’s in Christ Jesus. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>There’s a reliable statement that deserves full acceptance: </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Christ Jesus came to the world to save sinners and I’m the worst.” </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I received mercy so Christ Jesus could use me to demonstrate </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>his
</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>extreme patience. The “worst sinner” provided an example to people that would later trust in </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>Christ</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> for eternal life. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Now to the eternal King and only God, immortal, invisible </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>be</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> honor and glory forevermore! Amen.      </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">1 Tim 1:12-17</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’m entrusting that instruction to you, Timothy </span><i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>my</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> son. I’m doing it because of the prophecies about you early on so that on the basis of them you can fight the good fight </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>with faith and a clear conscience. Some have refused to listen to </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>their conscience</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> and have shipwrecked as far as faith is concerned. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Hymeneas and Alexander are </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>two examples</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>; I’ve delivered them to Satan to teach them not to blaspheme.          </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">1 Tim 1:18-20</p>
