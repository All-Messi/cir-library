<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They should be examined first, then serve as deacons if they’re blameless. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Their</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> wives need to have good character too, not be inclined to malicious gossip, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>but</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> temperate, faithful in every respect. </span></span>
