## 2 Thessalonians Chapter 2


### Concerning Christ’s Return


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Now, about our Lord’s return and our gathering to him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t let a spirit, a message, a letter claiming to be from us disturb you if it says it’s time for the Lord to return. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He won’t return till apostasy occurs and “the lawless one” appears. That “one destined for destruction” </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>will oppose <i>God</i> and exalt himself above everything that’s called deity or is worshiped. He’ll seat himself in God’s Temple and claim to be deity. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You remember me telling you that, don’t you, while I was there? </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You know what’s holding <i>him</i> back for now—till he’s revealed in his time. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That secret lawlessness is already at work, but the one that’s holding <i>him</i> back now <i>will continue to do so</i> till he’s gone. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then “the lawless one” will appear, the one the Lord will kill with his breath and destroy at his return. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The
<i>lawless one’s</i> coming will be like Satan’s working: with power, signs, false wonders, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and every sinful deception, all aimed at people that perish, people that haven’t welcomed a love for the truth so they’d be saved. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So God will send them a deluding influence to cause them to believe a lie. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> the truth will judge disbelievers who delight in doing wrong.                     </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>2 Th 2:1-12</span></b></span></p>


### God’s Choice of the Thessalonians


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>’re always obliged to thank God for you, family of Christ the Lord loves, because God chose you to be the firstfruits of salvation by believing the truth and by the holiness the Spirit brings. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He called you to that <i>truth</i> by the good news we delivered, which is for obtaining the honor our Lord Jesus Christ has. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So, stand firm; hold onto the traditions we taught you by word and letter. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Now may our Lord Jesus Christ and God our Father—who loved us and gave <i>us</i> eternal comfort and good hope in grace—</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>comfort you and strengthen <i>you</i> in every good thing you do and say.                                                                                                                                    </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>2 Th 2:13-17</span></b></span></p>
