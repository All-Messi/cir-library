## Colossians Chapter 2


### Urging Continued Faithfulness


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So, as you received Christ Jesus the Lord, keep on living in him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Be rooted and built up in him, firm in faith, like you were taught. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Make sure nobody takes you captive with empty philosophy that deceives, after human traditions, according to the world’s underlying assumptions rather than after Christ. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He fully constitutes deity bodily. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In him you have everything you need; he’s the head of all rule and authority. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In him you were also ‘circumcised’ without hands. It’s not a circumcision that removes flesh from the body; it’s Christ’s ‘circumcision.’ </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You were buried with him in baptism, and raised with <i>him</i> by faith in God, who raised him from the dead. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You were ‘dead’ in sins and the uncircumcision of your flesh, but he ‘made you alive’ with him and pardoned your sins. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He canceled the certificate of debt made up of regulations that worked against us, took it out of the way, and nailed it to his cross. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He disarmed rulers and authorities and displayed them openly for what they were by overcoming them </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>in the cross and resurrection</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.</span></span></p>

### Concerns Irrelevant for Christians


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So don’t let anybody judge you about food, drink, or participating in festivals, new moons, or Sabbaths. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They’re just shadows of realities to come; but the body belongs to Christ. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>And don’t let anybody cheat you by promoting pious self-denial and angel worship—taking a stand on insights they claim to have, proud for no reason in their nature-dominated thinking—</span></span>

</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Don’t use, don’t taste, don’t touch”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>? </span></span>
