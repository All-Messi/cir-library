## 1 Corinthians Chapter 4


### Attitude Toward Christ’s Ministers


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>People should consider us ministers of Christ and stewards of God’s mysteries. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A steward must be reliable, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>but it’s not important to me that you or any human court would try to evaluate me; I don’t even try to evaluate myself. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>My conscience is clear, but that doesn’t make me right. The Lord’s the One that evaluates me. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So, don’t rush to judgment before the Lord comes. He’ll bring to light what darkness hides and reveal the motives in </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>people’s</span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> hearts. Then <u>God</u> will do the complimenting.                                    </span></span></p>

<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We’re fools for Christ; you’re wise in him! We’re weak; you’re strong! We’re disrespected; you’re respectable! </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>To this day we’re hungry, thirsty, poorly clothed, harassed, homeless, </span></span>
