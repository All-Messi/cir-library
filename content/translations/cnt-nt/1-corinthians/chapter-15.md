## 1 Corinthians Chapter 15

<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Christ died for our sins according to the scriptures, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>was buried, was resurrected the third day according to the scriptures, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>appeared to Cephas, then the Twelve, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>then to more than 500 disciples at the same time (most of them are still alive). </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Next he appeared to James and then to all the apostles. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Last, he appeared to me like someone born out of season. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’m the least of the apostles and don’t deserve to be called one because I persecuted God’s church. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But by God’s grace I am what I am, and his grace to me has produced results. <span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So whether it’s me or them, <i>Christ’s resurrection</i> is what we proclaimed and what you believed.                                          </span></span></p>

### The Resurrection of People


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If we proclaimed that Christ resurrected, how can some of you say there’s no such thing as resurrection? </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If that’s true, Christ <u>hasn’t</u> resurrected. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If
<u>he</u> didn’t, then our preaching is worthless and so’s your faith. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Besides, we’re false witnesses for God because we’ve claimed he resurrected Christ, which he didn’t do if there’s no such thing as resurrection. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If dead people don’t resurrect, Christ didn’t. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If he didn’t resurrect, your faith is useless, you’re still in your sins, </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If we have hope in Christ only in this life, we’re the most pitiable people alive.
                                                                                                    </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But, Christ <u>has</u> resurrected, the firstfruits from the dead. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Since death <i>came</i> by man, resurrection came by man. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As in Adam people die, so in Christ people resurrect—</span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then the end will come, when he does away with all <i>other</i> rule, authority, and power. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He needs to reign till he overcomes all his enemies. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The last enemy he’ll overcome is death. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“</span></i></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God
<b>will subject everything to him”</b></span></i><b><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></b><b><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Ps 8:6]</span></b><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>.</span></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When it says <i>“everything,”</i> obviously it doesn’t include the One who did the <i>“subjecting.”
</i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When God subjects <i>“everything”
</i>to the Son, the Son will be subject to God, so God will be ultimate in everything.       </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">1 Cor 15:20-28</p>

<span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Why do we face danger all the time? </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By the pride we take in you, <i>the pride </i>we have in Christ Jesus our Lord,<i> I tell you, </i>I die every day. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If, figuratively speaking, I fought wild animals in Ephesus, what good <i>did it do</i>? If the dead don’t resurrect, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        <b><i>“Let’s eat and drink;</i></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    we’re going to die tomorrow”</span></i></b><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></b><b><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Is 22:13; 56:12].</span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t kid yourselves,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        <i>“Bad company ruins good morals.”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Come to your senses about goodness, and don’t practice sinning. ### The Resurrected Body

What kind of body do they come with?” </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Foolish
</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>one</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>! What you plant doesn’t come to life unless it dies. </span></span>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v42" id="v42"><sup class="verse-num">42</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That’s how resurrection is. <span class="verse verse-v44" id="v44"><sup class="verse-num">44</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>what’s planted as a physical body resurrects as a spiritual one. There’s a physical body and a spiritual body. </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v51" id="v51"><sup class="verse-num">51</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll tell you a mystery: not everybody will die, but everybody will be changed—</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v52" id="v52"><sup class="verse-num">52</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>in an instant, in the blink of an eye, at the last trumpet blast. <span class="verse verse-v53" id="v53"><sup class="verse-num">53</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>This perishable <i>body </i>must put on what’s not perishable; this mortal <i>body</i> must put on immortality. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v54" id="v54"><sup class="verse-num">54</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When that happens, what’s written will take place,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        <b><i>“Victory has swallowed up death </i></b></span><b><span style='font-size:9.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Is 25:8].</span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        Death, where’s your sting?”</span></i></b><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></b><b><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Hos 13:14]</span></b></p>
