## 1 Corinthians Chapter 11

<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A man that prays or prophesies with his head covered disgraces his head. </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If a woman is bareheaded, she may as well be bald. If it’s shameful for a woman to cut her hair off, she should cover her head. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A man shouldn’t cover his head because he’s the image and delight of God. A wife is her husband’s delight. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Man didn’t come from woman, but woman from man. </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So she should wear <i>a sign of</i> authority on her head because of the angels. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But, in the Lord a husband and wife exist as a combination. </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Doesn’t what’s natural teach you that long hair dishonors a man, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>but it honors a woman? Long hair serves as a covering for her. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If someone wants to argue about it, <u>we</u> don’t have such a custom; neither do the churches of God.               </span></span></p>
