## John Chapter 18


### Agony in the Garden;



### The Arrest; Peter’s First Denial


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></b><b><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>18<sup>:</sup></span></b><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Jesus finished, he went out with his disciples past the wadi Kidron to an orchard </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Judas knew about, because Jesus often went there with his disciples. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Judas had received a cohort as well as officers from the chief priests and Pharisees. They came there with lanterns, torches, and weapons. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus knew what was coming and went out to meet them, <i>“Who are you looking for?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Jesus the Nazarene.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“I’m Jesus.” </i>(Judas was standing with them.) </span><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When he said, <i>“I’m Jesus,”</i> they drew back and fell to the ground.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He asked them again, <i>“Who are you looking for?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“Jesus the Nazarene.”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I told you I’m Jesus. If you’re looking for me, let these go”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> (</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>like he’d said, <i>“I haven’t lost any you gave me.</i></span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>).</span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Jn 18:1-9</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then Simon Peter, who had a sword, drew it and struck the high priest’s servant and cut off his right ear. His name was Malchus.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus said, <i>“Put your sword back in its sheath. Am I not going to drink the cup the Father gave me to drink?”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then the cohort, the tribune, and the officers of the Jews arrested Jesus and bound him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They led him to Annas first because he was the father-in-law of Caiaphas, the high priest that year. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’s the Caiaphas who counseled the Jews that it was expedient for one man to die for the people.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Simon Peter and another disciple followed Jesus. That disciple was acquainted with the high priest and went in with Jesus to the courtyard. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Peter stood outside at the gate. The other disciple went out and spoke to the gatekeeper and brought Peter in.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The gatekeeper, a servant woman, said</span><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>p</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> to Peter, <i>“You’re not one of this man’s disciples, are you?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“No.”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Servants and officers were standing there. They’d built a charcoal fire because it was cold, and they were warming themselves. Peter was standing with them, warming himself.                                                                                                           </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Jn 18:10-18</p>


### The Trial Before Annas


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></b><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The high priest asked Jesus about his disciples and his teaching. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He answered, <i>“I spoke openly to the world and taught constantly in synagogue and in the Temple, where the Jews all gather. I haven’t done anything secretly.
</i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Why ask me? Ask the ones that heard me. They know what I said.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>One of the officers standing by slapped him, <i>“Do you answer the high priest that way?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“If I said something wrong, what was it? If I didn’t, what are you hitting me for?”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then Annas sent him bound to Caiaphas the high priest.<b>                                  </b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Jn 18:19-24</p>


### Peter’s Final Denials


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></b><span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>While Simon Peter was standing there, warming himself, <i>the officers</i> said, <i>“You’re not one of his disciples too, are you?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“No.”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>One of the high priest’s servants, a relative of the one whose ear Peter had cut off, said</span><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>p</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>,
<i>“I saw you in the orchard with him, didn’t I?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Peter denied it again. Immediately a rooster crowed.                                         </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Jn 18:25-27</p>


### The Trials Before Pilate


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></b><span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They led</span><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>p</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> Jesus from Caiaphas to the Praetorium. It was early. They didn’t go into the Praetorium themselves. They didn’t want to defile themselves and not be able to eat the Passover. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So Pilate came out to them, <i>“What charges are you bringing against this man?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“If he wasn’t a criminal, we wouldn’t have delivered him to you.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pilate said, <i>“You take him and judge him by your Law.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“It’s illegal for us to <u>execute</u> anybody”</i> (</span><span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>fulfilling Jesus’ statement about how he would die</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>).</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pilate went back into the Praetorium and called Jesus, <i>“Are you the King of the Jews?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Are you asking that yourself, or have other people prompted you?”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I’m not a Jew, am I? Your nation and the chief priests delivered you to me. What have you done?”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“My ‘kingdom’ is not an earthly kingdom. If it was, my servants would be fighting to keep me from being delivered to the Jews.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v37" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“So you’re a ‘king’ then?”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“Yes, I’m a ‘king.’ That’s why I was born—why I came into the world—to speak about truth. Everybody that’s committed to truth listens to me.”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v38" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pilate said</span><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>p</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>,
<i>“What’s truth?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            When he said that, he went out to the Jews, <i>“He’s not guilty of anything. </i></span><span class="verse verse-v39" id="v39"><sup class="verse-num">39</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You have a custom that I release a prisoner to you at Passover. Do you want me to release ‘the King of the Jews’?”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v40" id="v40"><sup class="verse-num">40</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They shouted again, <i>“Not him! Barabbas!” </i>(</span><span style='font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>an insurgent</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>).                                </span></span></p>
