## John Chapter 21


### Appearance to Seven by Lake Galilee


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></b><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>After that, Jesus appeared again to his disciples at Lake Tiberias. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Simon Peter, Thomas, Nathanael from Cana in Galilee, Zebedee’s sons, and two other disciples were together.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Simon Peter said</span><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>p</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>,
<i>“I’m going fishing.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“We’ll go with you.”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They went out and got in the boat, and that night didn’t catch anything. </span><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When morning came, Jesus stood on the shore, but the disciples didn’t know it was him.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Fellows, you haven’t had anything to eat, have you?”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“No.”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Throw the net on the right side of the boat; you’ll find some.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So they did, and they couldn’t haul it in for the number of fish.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The disciple Jesus loved said</span><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>p</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> to Peter, <i>“It’s the Lord!”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            When Simon Peter heard that, he put on his outer garment (</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>he’d stripped down</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>) and dived into the lake. </span><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The rest of the disciples came in the small boat because they were only about 300 feet from shore, dragging the net full of fish. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When they got out of the boat, they saw</span><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>p</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> a charcoal fire with a fish and a loaf laid on it.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus told</span><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>p</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> them, <i>“Bring some of the fish you just caught.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Simon Peter got up and pulled the net up onto land full of big fish—153. Although there were so many, the net didn’t tear.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus said</span><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>p</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>,
<i>“Come eat breakfast.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            None of the disciples dared to ask him who he was;<i> </i>they knew it was the Lord.
</span><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He gave</span><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>p</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> them the bread and fish. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That was a third time Jesus appeared to his disciples after he resurrected. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When they’d finished breakfast, Jesus said</span><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>p</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>,
<i>“Simon </i>Bar<i>jonah, do you love me more than these do?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“Yes, Lord, you know I love you.”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“Tend my lambs.” </i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said</span><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>p</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> the second time, <i>“Simon </i>Bar<i>jonah, do you love me?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“Yes, Lord, you know I love you.”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“Tend my sheep.” </i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said</span><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>p</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> the third time, <i>“Simon Barjonah, do you love me?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            It pained Peter that Jesus asked a third time, <i>“Do you love me?” </i>He said</span><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>p</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>,
<i>“Lord, you know everything; you know I love you.”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus told him, <i>“Tend my sheep. When you were younger, you’d get dressed and walk wherever you wanted to. When you get old, you’ll stretch out your hands, and somebody else will dress you and carry you where you don’t want to go.” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>(</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said that to indicate by what kind of death he would honor God</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.) When he’d said that, he told him. <i>“Follow me.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Peter turned around and saw</span><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>p</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> the disciple Jesus loved, following, the one that leaned back on Jesus’ chest at the supper and asked, <i>“Lord, who’s going to betray you?” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> </span>
<span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Peter said</span><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>p</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>,
<i>“What about him?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus said</span><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>p</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>,
<i>“If I want him to live till I come back, what’s that to you? You follow me.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So the rumor got out among the believers that he wouldn’t die. But Jesus didn’t say he wouldn’t die; he said, <i>“If I want him to live till I come back, what’s that to you?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Jn 21:1-23</p>
