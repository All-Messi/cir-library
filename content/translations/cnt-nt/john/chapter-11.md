<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus said, <i>“<u>I’m</u> resurrection and life. <p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            They said</span><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>p</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>,

</i></span></span></p>
