## John Chapter 11

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> When he heard it, he said, "He's not going to die from this sickness; it's for God's praise so it can praise God's Son."</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Jesus was talking about death, but they thought he was talking about rest. 14Then he told them plainly, "Lazarus has died. 15For your sake I'm glad I wasn't there, so you can believe. Let's go to him."</span>
