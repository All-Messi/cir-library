## John Chapter 4

<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>he left Judaea and headed back to Galilee. </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He came<sup>p</sup> to a town in Samaria called Sychar, near the plot of land Jacob gave his son Joseph. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jacob’s well was there. Jesus was sitting on the well <i>wall </i>about noon, tired from the trip.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A Samaritan woman came<sup>p</sup> to draw water, and he said to her, <i>“Give me a drink</i></span><i><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>.”</span></i><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>(</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His disciples had gone into town to buy food</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.)</span><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The woman said<sup>p</sup>, <i>“How does a Jewish man ask a Samaritan woman for a drink? Jews don’t have anything to do with Samaritans.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus answered, <i>“If you knew God’s gift and who’s asking you for a drink, you’d be asking <u>him</u> to give <u>you</u> <u>living</u> water.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>             </span><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Sir, you don’t even have a bucket, and the well is deep. Where are you going to get ‘living water’? </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You aren’t greater than our ancestor Jacob, are you? <p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus answered, <i>“Everybody that drinks this water gets thirsty again. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Whoever drinks the water I give him won’t get thirsty again. <p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Give me that water so I won’t get thirsty and have to come here to draw it!”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus told her<sup>p</sup>, <i>“Go get your husband and come back.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I don’t have a husband.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“You said it well. <p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The woman said<sup>p</sup>, <i>“Sir, you must be a prophet. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Our ancestors worshiped on this mountain. You<sup>pl</sup> say people should worship in Jerusalem.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus told<sup>p</sup> her, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Believe me, the time’s coming when you<sup>pl</sup> won’t worship the Father on this mountain <u>or</u> in Jerusalem. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You<sup>pl</sup> don’t know what you’re worshiping. <span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But, the time’s coming—it’s here now—when true worshipers will worship the Father in spirit and truth. <span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God’s a spirit. and those who worship him need to worship him in spirit and truth.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><b><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> <sup>25</sup></span></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I know Messiah’s coming [</span></i><i><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>called Christ</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>]. <p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus said<sup>p</sup>, <i>“I <u>am</u> the Messiah.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>At that point his disciples got back and were surprised that he was talking to a woman. <span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She left her water pot and went into town and told<sup>p</sup> the men, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Come see a fellow that told me everything I ever did. <span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They left the town and were on the way to him.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Meanwhile the disciples kept saying, <i>“Rabbi, eat something.”     </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But he said, <i>“I have ‘food’ to eat that you don’t know about.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They said to one another, <i>“Nobody’s brought him anything to eat, have they?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“My ‘food’ is doing the will of the One that sent me and finishing it up. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Aren’t you<sup>pl</sup> saying harvest is still four months away? <span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Whoever<i> reaps earns wages and gathers grain for eternal life. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v37" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In this respect, the saying holds true, ‘One sows, another reaps.’ </span></i></span>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v39" id="v39"><sup class="verse-num">39</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>From that town many Samaritans believed in him because the woman said, <i>“He told me everything I ever did.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v40" id="v40"><sup class="verse-num">40</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So when the Samaritans got to where he was, they asked him to stay with them; he stayed two days. </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v42" id="v42"><sup class="verse-num">42</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They told the woman, <i>“We don’t believe just because of what you said. <p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></b><span class="verse verse-v46" id="v46"><sup class="verse-num">46</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then he went to Cana in Galilee, where he’d turned the water into wine. An official’s son in Capernaum was sick. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v47" id="v47"><sup class="verse-num">47</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When he heard that Jesus had come from Judaea to Galilee, he went to ask him to come down and heal his son, who was about to die. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v49" id="v49"><sup class="verse-num">49</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The official said<sup>p</sup>, <i>“Sir, come on down before my little boy dies.” </i></span></span></p>

<span class="verse verse-v53" id="v53"><sup class="verse-num">53</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So<i>
