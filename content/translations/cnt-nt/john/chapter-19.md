Crucify him!”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            The chief priests answered, <i>“We don’t have any king but Caesar.”</i></span></p>
