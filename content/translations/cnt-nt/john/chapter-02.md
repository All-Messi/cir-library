## John Chapter 2


### The First Miracle—at Cana


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></b><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Three days later there was a wedding at Cana in Galilee. Jesus’ mother was there, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and he and his disciples were invited. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When the wine ran out, his mother told<sup>p</sup> him they’d run out of wine.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus said<sup>p</sup>, <i>“What is this? It’s not my time yet.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His mother told<sup>p</sup> the help, <i>“Do whatever he tells you.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Six big stone water jars were standing there that the Jews use for purification. They held 20 or 30 gallons apiece. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus said<sup>p</sup>, <i>“Fill the jars with water.”</i> They filled them to the brim. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Dip some out and take it to the master of ceremonies.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When he tasted it, he didn’t know where it had come from—but the help that drew the water knew. He called the groom,<i> </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Everybody serves good wine first. When guests have had a lot to drink, they serve wine that’s not as good. You’ve kept the good wine till now.” </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So Cana was where Jesus performed his first sign and demonstrated his glory, and his disciples believed in him.                                                                               </span></span></p>

### The First Cleansing of the Temple


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></b><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Jews’ Passover was coming, and Jesus went up to Jerusalem. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In the Temple he found moneychangers sitting and merchants selling oxen, sheep, and doves. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He made a whip out of cords and drove the sheep and oxen out of the Temple. He poured out the moneychangers’ coins and turned their tables over. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told the ones that were selling doves, <i>“Get them out of here! Don’t make my Father’s house a marketplace.” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His disciples remembered the scripture,<i> <b>“Zeal for your house has consumed me”</b></i>
</span><b><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Ps 69:9a]</span></b><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Jews said, <i>“What sign do you do to show us you have a right to do this?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus answered, <i>“Destroy<sup>pl</sup> this Temple, and I’ll rebuild it in 3 days.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“It took 46 years to build; you’re going to rebuild it in 3 days?” </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>(</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He was talking about his body, “the Temple.” </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>When he resurrected, his disciples remembered he’d said that and believed it and the scripture</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.)</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>While he was in Jerusalem at the Passover, many believed in him because they saw the signs he did. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But he didn’t entrust himself to them because he knew everybody. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He didn’t need anybody to tell him about anyone; he knew what each person was like.
