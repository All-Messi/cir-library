<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            She turned around and said</span><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>p</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> in Aramaic,<i> “Rabboni (</i></span><i><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Teacher</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>)!”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus said</span><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>p</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>,

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Mary went</span><sup><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>p</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> and told them, “<i>I’ve seen the Lord!” </i>and what he’d told her to say.</span></span></p>
