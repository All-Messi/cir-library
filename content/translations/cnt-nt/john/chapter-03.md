## John Chapter 3

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Nicodemus responded, <i>“What <u>are</u> you talking about?”</i></span></span></p>

</span></i></span>

<i>viper in the desert, the Son of Man needs to be held up </i></span></span>
