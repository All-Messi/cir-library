<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> When John reprimanded Herod <em>Antipas </em>the tetraarch about Herodias, his half-brother’s wife, <strong><sup>20</sup></strong>and all the wicked things he’d done, <em>Herod</em> added this on top of them: he locked John up in prison.</span>

### The Baptism of Jesus


<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> While John had been baptizing the people, Jesus was baptized too.
