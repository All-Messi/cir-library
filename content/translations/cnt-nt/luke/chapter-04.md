<span class="verse" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> Jesus ordered him to be quiet and come out of him. The demon convulsed him right in front of them and came out without harming him.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> Everybody was astounded, "What is this? With authority and power, he gives orders to unclean spirits, and they come out!" 37The news about him went out everywhere in the surrounding countryside.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v40"><sup class="verse-num">40</sup> As the sun was going down, they brought him people with several kinds of illness. <span class="verse" id="v42"><sup class="verse-num">42</sup> At daybreak the next morning, Jesus went out away from people, and the crowd was looking for him.
