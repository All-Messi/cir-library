## Luke Chapter 14


### Healing in a Pharisee’s Home on a Sabbath


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>On a Sabbath, when he went into the house of a ruling Pharisee to eat, <i>the leaders</i> were watching him closely. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In front of him was a man with extreme swelling. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus said to the lawyers and Pharisees, <i>“Is it lawful to heal on a Sabbath?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They didn’t say anything. He took hold of him and healed him and let him go. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said, <i>“If a son or ox falls into a well, wouldn’t you pull<sup>f</sup> him out right away on a Sabbath day?” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They couldn’t answer him.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When he noticed how they were vying for the prominent places <i>at the table</i>, he started telling the guests a parable. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“When somebody invites you to a wedding banquet, don’t recline at table in a place of honor. Someone more distinguished than you may have been invited. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The one that invited you both will say to you<sup>sg</sup>, ‘Let this gentleman have </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>your place<i>.’ Then you’ll head for the last place embarrassed. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Instead, when you’re invited, go recline in the last place so when the one that invited you comes, he’ll say, ‘Friend, move up higher.’ Then the others reclining at table will respect you. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Everybody that exalts himself will be humbled, and the one that humbles himself will be exalted.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He went on to say to his host, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“When you<sup>sg</sup> have a luncheon or dinner, don’t call your friends, brothers, relatives, and wealthy neighbors. They can invite you in return. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Invite poor, disabled, lame, and blind people. You’ll be blessed; they can’t repay you. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You’ll get repaid when the righteous resurrect.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When one of those reclining at the table heard that, he said to Jesus, <i>“Blessed is the one that eats bread in God’s kingdom.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus said to him<i>, </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“A man prepared a banquet and invited a lot of people. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When the time for the banquet came, he sent a servant to tell them to come; everything was ready. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But they started making excuses. ‘I need to go see a field I bought. Please excuse me.’</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“‘I’m on my way to try out five yokes of oxen I bought. Please excuse me.’ </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></i><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“‘I just got married; I can’t come.’</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></i><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“When the servant came back, he told his master. The homeowner was mad, ‘Hurry out into the streets and alleys in town and bring in the poor, disabled, blind, and lame.’</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></i><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“‘Master, I did what you said, and there’s still room.’</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“‘Go out into the country lanes and paths and urge them to come in so my house will be full. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>None of the people I invited will taste my banquet.”                                                                                               </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>
         </span></span></p>

### The Cost of Discipleship


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Large crowds were traveling with him. He turned and told them, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Nobody that comes to me can be my disciple unless he loves me more than his father, mother, wife, children, brothers, sisters—even his own life. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Whoever doesn’t carry his own cross and follow me, can’t be my disciple. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Which of you that wants to build a tower, doesn’t sit down first and figure out what it’ll cost, to see if you have enough money to finish it? </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Otherwise, when you’ve laid the foundation and can’t finish it, people will make fun of you,
</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>‘This fellow started to build and couldn’t finish.’</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“What king engages another king in battle without sitting down first to figure out if with 10,000 he can meet one with 20,000? </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If he can’t, while the other is still a long way off, he’ll send ambassadors to ask for terms of peace. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In the same way, none of you can be my disciple unless you give up everything you own.</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Salt is good, but if it loses its saltiness, what can make <u>it</u> salty? </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>It’s useless for the ground or manure pile. People throw it out. Whoever has ears, listen!”</span></i><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'></span></span></p>
