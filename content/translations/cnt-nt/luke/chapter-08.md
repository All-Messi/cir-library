<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>as well as some women he healed from illnesses and evil spirits: Mary, called the Magdalene, that seven demons had come out of; </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joanna wife of Chuza, Herod’s business manager; Susanna, and quite a few that were contributing to them out of their own resources.  </span></span></p>

As he sowed, some seeds fell along the path; people walked on them, and birds ate them. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Some fell on rocky ground; they sprouted but shriveled up because they didn’t have enough moisture. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Some fell among thorns; the thorns grew up with them and choked them out. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Others fell on good ground, grew up, and produced 100 times as much sown. Whoever has ears, listen!”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His disciples asked him what the parable meant. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“You can come to know the unrevealed things about God’s kingdom, but to the rest I’m delivering the message in parables so </span></i></p>


### ‘though they see, they don’t perceive;


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            though they hear, they don’t understand’</span></i></b><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></b><b><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Is 6:9-10]</span></b><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“This is what the parable means. <span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The seeds along the road represent people that hear the message. <span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The seeds on rocky ground represent people that are happy to accept the message, but they don’t have root. <span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The seeds falling among the thorns represent people that hear the message; but the cares, wealth, and pleasures of life choke them out, and they don’t produce anything. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But the seeds in the good ground represent those who hear the word with a good and honest heart and produce by staying with it.</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Nobody lights a lamp and hides it under a jar or puts it under a bed. <span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Everything hidden will become known; everything secret will come to light. </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Be careful how you listen: whoever has will receive more, and whoever doesn’t have will lose what they seem to have.”                                                                                                                     </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

### Interrupt His Ministry

<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>While they were sailing, he went to sleep. A windstorm swept down on the lake, and they were taking on water and in danger of sinking. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They went and woke him up, <i>“Master, we’re going to die!”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He got up and rebuked the wind and crashing waves. The wind stopped, and the lake grew calm. </span><span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Where’s your faith?” </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They were afraid and amazed, and said to one another, <i>“Who is this that even the wind and waves obey?” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Lk 8:22-25</p>

<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Jesus got out of the boat, a fellow from the town met him. <span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When he saw Jesus, he cried out and ran to him and screamed. <i>“What is this, Jesus, you Son of God Most High? Please don’t torment me!” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>(</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus had commanded the unclean spirit to come out. It had seized him many times. He’d be bound under guard with chains and shackles; but he’d break the bonds, and the demon would drive him into uninhabited places.</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>)</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“Legion,”</i> because lots of demons had gone into him. </span><span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They started begging him not to send them away into the abyss.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A tremendous herd of pigs was feeding on the hillside. <span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When the demons went into the pigs, the herd stampeded down the steep place into the lake and drowned. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The herdsmen saw it and ran away and reported it in town and in the countryside. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>People came out to see what had happened and came to Jesus. <span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Witnesses told them how Jesus had delivered the demoniac.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v37" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The whole crowd from the area of the Gerasenes asked him to go away, because fear had overwhelmed them. When he got in the boat to go, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v38" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the fellow begged to go with him, but Jesus sent him back, </span></span>

### Raising Jairus’ Daughter;



### Healing Woman with Hemorrhaging

<span class="verse verse-v41" id="v41"><sup class="verse-num">41</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jairus, a ruler of the synagogue, came and fell down to Jesus, pleading with him to come to his house. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v42" id="v42"><sup class="verse-num">42</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His only daughter—about twelve—was at the point of death.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            As Jesus was making his way along, the crowd was pressing in on him. </span><span class="verse verse-v43" id="v43"><sup class="verse-num">43</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A woman that had been hemorrhaging for twelve years and had spent her whole living on doctors </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v44" id="v44"><sup class="verse-num">44</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>came up from behind and touched the fringe of his cloak. The hemorrhaging stopped at once.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v45" id="v45"><sup class="verse-num">45</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jesus said, <i>“Who touched me?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            While they were all denying it, Peter said, <i>“Master, the crowd’s pressing on you and jostling you.”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v46" id="v46"><sup class="verse-num">46</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But Jesus said, <i>“Somebody touched me. I felt power go out from me.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v47" id="v47"><sup class="verse-num">47</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When the woman saw she hadn’t escaped notice, she came trembling, fell down to him, and admitted in front of them why she’d touched him.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v48" id="v48"><sup class="verse-num">48</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said, <i>“Daughter, your faith has saved you. Have a good day.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v49" id="v49"><sup class="verse-num">49</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>While he was still talking <i>to her</i>, someone came<sup>p</sup> from the house of the synagogue ruler, <i>“Your daughter has died. You needn’t bother the Rabbi further.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v50" id="v50"><sup class="verse-num">50</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Jesus heard it, he responded, <i>“Don’t give up hope; just trust me. <p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            He said, <i>“Don’t keep crying. She’s not dead; she’s ‘asleep.’”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v53" id="v53"><sup class="verse-num">53</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They laughed at him because they knew she was dead. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v54" id="v54"><sup class="verse-num">54</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He took her hand and said, <i>“Girl, get up.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v55" id="v55"><sup class="verse-num">55</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She started breathing again, and stood up instantly. He told them to give her something to eat. </span></span>
