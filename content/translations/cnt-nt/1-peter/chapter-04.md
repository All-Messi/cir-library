## 1 Peter Chapter 4


### Good News Presented to the Dead


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Christ suffered; so arm yourselves with the attitude he took, because suffering purifies from sin. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You won’t be living the rest of your life by human desires, but by God’s will. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You spent enough time following the pattern of life the Gentiles carry on: a lifestyle of sensuality, lust, drunkenness, revelry, drunken parties, disgusting idolatry. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They’re surprised that you don’t run with them in that excessive, reckless living; and they slander you for it. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They’ll give account to the One who’s set to judge the living and the dead. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In that connection, the good news was presented even to the dead so they’d be judged like the living who live according to God in the spirit.   </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">1 Pet 4:1-6</p>


### Living with Christ’s Return in Mind


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The end is close. Be sensible and serious in prayer. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Especially, have ongoing love toward each other because love covers lots of sins. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Be hospitable toward each other without grudges. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Each of you needs to minister your gift to the others as good stewards of the grace God has shown in <i>these</i> different ways. If it’s speaking, speak as if God himself is speaking. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If it’s serving, do it wholeheartedly. That way you’ll praise God through Christ. Glory and dominion belong to him forevermore. Amen.                        </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">1 Pet 4:7-11</p>


### Nothing Surprising About Suffering


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t be surprised at the fiery trial that’s testing you, as if something strange is happening. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Instead, to the extent that you’re sharing Christ’s sufferings, be happy so you can be really happy when he appears in his glory. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If people dishonor you because of him, <i>you’re</i> blessed; God’s Spirit and honor rest on you. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t suffer as a murderer, thief, evildoer, intruder in other people’s business. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If you suffer as a Christian, don’t be ashamed; praise God in that name. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>It’s time to judge God’s family. If judgment begins with us, what’s going to happen to people that disbelieve God’s good news? </span></span></p>

</b></span><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“If good people are barely saved, </span></i></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        where will godless people come in?”</span></i></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span><b><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Pro 11:31 LXX]</span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So you that suffer according to God’s will, entrust yourselves to the faithful creator by continuing to do what’s right.                   </span></span></p>
