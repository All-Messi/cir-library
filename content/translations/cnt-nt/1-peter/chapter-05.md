## 1 Peter Chapter 5


### Urging Elders


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Elders, I’m a fellow elder, a witness of Christ’s sufferings. I share in the glory that’s going to be revealed. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Shepherd God’s flock that’s among you. Provide oversight, not because you have to, but because you want to; not to make money (</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>which is shameful</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>), but out of internal initiative. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t lord it over your charges; set an example for the flock. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When the Chief Shepherd appears, you’ll receive a glorious crown that won’t fade away.                                                                                                                                   </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">1 Pet 5:1-4</p>


### Urging Younger People


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Young men, defer to the elders and dress in humility toward each other because </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        <b><i>“God resists the proud</i></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    and gives grace to the humble”</span></i></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span><b><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Pro 3:34 LXX].</span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So “humble” yourselves under God’s strong hand, and he’ll exalt you in due time. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Place your cares on him because he cares about you. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Be serious-minded and alert. Your adversary the devil is prowling around looking for somebody to devour. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Resist him firmly with faith; you know your fellow believers in the world are experiencing the same sufferings. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>After you’ve suffered a little while, the all-gracious God that has called you into his eternal glory will confirm you, strengthen you, and establish you. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Dominion to him forever! Amen.           </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">1 Pet 5:5-11</p>


### Closing Comments


<p class="MsoNormal"><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ve written you a few words by Silvanus <i>my </i>faithful brother, as I consider him. I wanted to encourage you and confirm to you that what you’ve received is in fact God’s grace; stand firm in it. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The elect brotherhood in ‘Babylon’ greets you as does my son Mark. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Greet each other with a loving kiss. Peace to all of you in Christ.                                                                        </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">1 Pet 5:12-14</p>
