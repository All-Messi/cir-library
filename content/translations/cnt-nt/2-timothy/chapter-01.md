## 2 Timothy Chapter 1


### Salutation


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Paul, apostle of Christ<sup>Messiah</sup> Jesus by God’s will in regard to the life he promised in Christ Jesus, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>to Timothy, my dear son. Grace, mercy, <i>and</i> peace from God the Father and Christ Jesus our Lord.                                               </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">2 Tim 1:1-2</p>


### Stirring up Timothy’s Gift


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As I remember you in my prayers day and night, I thank my ancestors’ God that I serve with a good conscience. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I really want to see you again because I’ve been remembering your tears. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I remember your sincere faith, <i>a faith</i> your grandmother Lois and your mother Eunice had first. I’m sure it’s been in you, too, ever since. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That’s why I’m reminding you to stir up the spiritual gift you have from the laying on of my hands. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God hasn’t given us a timid mentality, but power, love, and self-discipline. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So don’t be ashamed of the Lord’s mystery or of me his prisoner; suffer with me for the good news as God gives you strength. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He has saved us and called us with a holy calling. What we can do ourselves doesn’t define <i>that calling</i>. His purpose and grace define it, which he gave us in Christ Jesus before time began. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The coming of our Savior Christ Jesus now has revealed that purpose and grace. He destroyed death and brought life and immortality to light through the good news. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’s appointed me as a preacher, apostle, and teacher <i>of that good news</i>. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That’s why I’m suffering what <i>I am</i>. But I’m not ashamed; I know who I’ve trusted, and I’m confident that he can keep what I entrusted to him till that day. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Hold onto the sound form of the message you’ve heard from me about the faith and love that are in Christ Jesus. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By the Holy Spirit who lives in us, keep what he’s entrusted <i>to you.            </i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">2 Tim 1:3-14</p>


### Paul Abandoned in Rome


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Be aware that everybody in Asia has deserted me, including Phygelus and Hermogenes. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>May the Lord show mercy to the family of Onesiphorus. He has cheered me up so many times and hasn’t been ashamed of my chains. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When he got to Rome, he went all out to find me. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>May the Lord show him mercy on <i>judgment </i>day. <u>You</u> know very well how much he ministered in Ephesus.                   </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">2 Tim 1:15-18</p>
