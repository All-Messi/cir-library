## Hebrews Chapter 11


### Roll Call of the Faithful


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Faith is confidence about what <i>we</i> hope for, certainty about what <i>we</i> can’t see. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By faith people of old received testimony.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By faith we understand that God spoke the universe into existence, so what we see didn’t come from something previous.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By faith Abel offered God a better sacrifice than Cain did. Through it he received testimony that he was good. (</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God testified in response to his gifts.</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>) Through it he’s still speaking though he’s dead.                                                 </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Heb 11:1-4</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By faith Enoch didn’t die. <b>“<i>God took him up, and he </i>simply<i> wasn’t there” </i></b></span><b><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Gen 5:24]</span></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> Before God took him up, <i>the ancient writings</i> say “<i>he pleased God” </i></span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[<b>Gen 5:22-24;</b> Sir 44:16; Enoch 70:1-4; Wis 4:10]</span><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.  </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Without faith a person can’t please God. Whoever comes to him has to believe he exists and rewards people who look for him.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By faith Noah built an ark that saved his family. God had warned him about things he couldn’t see yet, and he paid attention to the warning. By faith he condemned <i>his</i> world and inherited the results of the goodness that comes from faith.              </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Heb 11:5-7</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By faith Abraham obeyed when God called him, and went out to a place he’d inherit later. He went without knowing where he was going. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By faith he lived as a foreigner in a land God had promised him. He lived there as a nomad in tents with Isaac and Jacob, heirs of the same promise. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He did it because he was looking for a city that had foundations, whose architect and builder is God. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By faith Sarah, too, though she couldn’t have children, God enabled to conceive even after child-bearing years. She did so because she had faith in the One that promised. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So, from one <i>man</i> that was as good as dead, there descended as many as the stars in the sky and the grains of sand on the seashore.                                                                                                                 </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Heb 11:8-12</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>These all died in faith without receiving the promises. They saw them a long way off and greeted them from afar. They regarded themselves as foreigners and temporary residents on earth. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>People that say things like that show they’re looking for a homeland. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If they were looking to where they’d come from, they could’ve gone back. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But they longed for a better, heavenly homeland. So God’s not ashamed of being their God, as he showed by preparing a city for them.                                                                         </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Heb 11:13-16</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By faith Abraham, when God put him to the test, </span><i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>went ahead and </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>offered up Isaac. </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>The man</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> that was so happy to receive the promises offered up the only </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>son</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> he had. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He was the one God told, </span><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“Your lineage will descend through Isaac”</span></i></b><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></b><b><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Gen 21:12]</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>.</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He figured that God could even raise Isaac from the dead; in a way, that’s what happened.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By faith Isaac blessed Jacob and Esau even about future things. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By faith Jacob, as he was dying, blessed each of Joseph’s sons and “</span><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>worshiped, leaning on his staff” </span></i></b><b><span style='font-size:9.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Gen 48:15-16; 47:31 LXX]</span></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.
           </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Heb 11:17-21</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By faith Joseph, when he was about to die, remembered the </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>future
</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>exodus of Israel’s descendants and gave instructions about his bones.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By faith Moses’ parents hid him for three months. They saw he was a handsome little boy and weren’t afraid of Pharaoh’s decree.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By faith Moses, when he grew up, refused to be considered the son of Pharaoh’s daughter. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He chose to suffer mistreatment with God’s people rather than enjoy the pleasures of sin for a while. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He considered the disgrace of Christ more valuable than the wealth of Egypt because he was looking on ahead to the later reward. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By faith he left Egypt, not afraid of Pharaoh’s anger, and endured as if he saw the One that’s invisible. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By faith he kept the Passover and the smearing of blood </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>on the doorposts </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>so the one that destroyed the firstborn wouldn’t touch them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By faith he crossed the Red Sea as on dry ground. When the Egyptians tried it, they drowned.   </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Heb 11:22-29</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By faith the walls of Jericho fell down after the Israelites circled them for seven days.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By faith Rahab the harlot didn’t perish with the disobedient because she welcomed the spies in peace.  </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Heb 11:30-31</p>


### Extreme Faithfulness


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>What more can I say? There’s not enough time to talk about Gideon, Barak, Samson, Jephthah, David and Samuel, and the prophets. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By faith they conquered kingdoms, did good things, received promises, shut the mouths of lions, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>extinguished the power of fire, escaped the sword, were made strong out of weakness, became mighty in war, drove out armies of foreigners. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Women received back their dead by resurrection. Some<sup>mas</sup> were beaten to death, rather than accept deliverance so they could gain a better resurrection.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Others received public torture and scourging, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>were put in </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>chains and imprisoned. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v37" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They were stoned, sawn in two, murdered with swords. They went around in sheepskins and goatskins, destitute, oppressed, abused; </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v38" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>wandering in deserts and mountains, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>hiding in </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>caves and holes in the ground. The world didn’t deserve them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v39" id="v39"><sup class="verse-num">39</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They all received testimony through their faith, but they didn’t receive the promise. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v40" id="v40"><sup class="verse-num">40</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God provided something better for us so that </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>what they anticipated</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> wasn’t completed till we received it.</span></span></p>
