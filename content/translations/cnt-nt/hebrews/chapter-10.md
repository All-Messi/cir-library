## Hebrews Chapter 10


### Replacing Shadows with Realities


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Law has a shadow of good things to come, not the very things themselves; so, by the same sacrifices every year, it can never make the ones perfect that are drawing near. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Otherwise, wouldn’t those sacrifices have ceased to be offered? The worshipers wouldn’t feel like sinners anymore because they would’ve been cleansed once and for all.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But every year the sacrifices remind people that they’re still sinners. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The blood of bulls and goats can’t take away sins.                                                                                  </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Heb 10:1-4</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“You didn’t want sacrifice and offering;</span></i></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    you prepared a body for me.</span></i></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span></i></b><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You didn’t delight in whole burnt offerings</span></i></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    and sacrifices for sins.</span></i></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span></i></b><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then I said,</span></i></b></span></p>


### ‘God, I’ve come to do your will;


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                                it’s been written about me in the scroll’” </span></i></b><b><span style='font-size: 9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Ps 40:6-8, cp. LXX]</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When he says above, <b>“<i>You didn’t want or delight in sacrifices and offerings and whole burnt offerings</i></b> and <b><i>sacrifices for sins</i></b><i>,<b>”
</b></i>which are offered according to the Law, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>then
</span></i></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>he says, <b><i>“I’ve come to do your will</i></b><i>.<b>”</b> </i>He takes away the first to establish the second. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By that will, the one-time offering of Christ’s “body” has made us holy.                                                                               </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Heb 10:5-10</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Every day, priests stand ministering and offering the same sacrifices over and over that can never take away sins. </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and is waiting till his enemies become his “footstool.” </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By one offering, he permanently perfected the ones he sanctified.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Holy Spirit also testifies. After he said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <b>
 </b></span><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“This is the covenant I’ll make with them </span></i></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    after those days:</span></i></b></p>


### I’ll put my laws in their hearts


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    and write them on their minds’” </span></i></b><b><span style='font-size:9.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Jer 31:33]</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>;</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>then he says,</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        <b><i>“‘I won’t remember their sins </i></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:1.0in;'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and lawless deeds anymore’” </span></i></b><b><span style='font-size:9.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Jer 31:34]</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Where there’s forgiveness for sins, there’s no more sacrificing for them.                  </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Heb 10:11-18</p>


### Obtaining a Clear Conscience


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So we have confidence about entering the Holy Place by the blood of Jesus. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We go in </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>by the new and living way that he has opened for us through the curtain, that is, his flesh. </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If we keep on sinning deliberately after we know the truth, there’s no more sacrifice <i>to be offered</i>. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>There’s a fearful dread of judgment and the furious fire that will consume <i>God’s </i>opponents.

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <b><i>“The L</i></b></span><b><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i></b><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> will judge his people”</span></i></b><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></b><b><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Deut 32:36; Ps 135:14]</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.</span></p>
