## Hebrews Chapter 6


### Getting Beyond the Basics


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So let’s get past the basics about Christ and press on to maturity. Let’s not lay again a foundation about repentance from dead works, and <i>about</i> faith toward God, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>teaching about baptizings, laying on hands, resurrection, eternal judgment. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We’ll get past the basics, God willing. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You can’t renew people to repentance that have once seen the light, tasted the heavenly gift, partaken of the Holy Spirit, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>tasted God’s good word and the powers of the age to come—</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and then fall away. They crucify to themselves God’s Son all over again and hold him up to public disgrace. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God blesses the ground that soaks up frequent rains and produces crops farmers can use. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If it grows thorns and briars, it’s worthless and close to being accursed. It ends up getting burned off.              </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But we’re convinced that in matters of salvation you’re better than that, despite what we’re saying. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God is just and remembers your work and love in his name when you ministered to the saints—and still do. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We want each of you to keep showing that same diligence, which will endure in the end to a firm hope. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That way you won’t get sluggish. By faith and endurance, you’ll be imitating people that obtain promises.                                                                                                                                                           </span></span></p>

### God’s Oath to Abraham

<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By two unchangeable things, then (</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God can’t lie</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>), we can have great confidence who have fled for refuge to take hold of the hope we headed for. </span></span>
