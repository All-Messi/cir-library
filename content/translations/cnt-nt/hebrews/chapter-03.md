## Hebrews Chapter 3


### The Son’s Superiority over Moses


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So, holy <i>“children”</i> that take part in the heavenly calling, pay close attention to Jesus, the Author and Perfecter of what we profess. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He was faithful to the One that appointed him just like Moses <i>was</i> in his house. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But Jesus </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>has been considered worthy of more honor than Moses—as much more as a house builder has <i>more honor </i>than the house. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Somebody builds every house; God has built everything. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Moses was faithful as a <u>servant in his house</u> to testify about things that would be spoken later; </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Christ<sup>Messiah</sup>
<i>was faithful</i> as a <u>Son over his house</u>. <u>We</u> are that house if we stay confident and take pride in our hope.                                                                           </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    </span></i></b><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>don’t harden your hearts</span></i></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:1.5in;'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and tested me in the desert, </span></i></b></p>

### That generation wasn’t pleasing to me,

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Be careful so there won’t be an evil, disbelieving “heart” in any of you, and you fall away from the living God. </span></span>

<i>“harden”</i> you. </span></span>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        <b><i>“Today, if you hear him speaking</i></b><i> to you<b>,</b></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    don’t harden your hearts </span></i></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:1.0in;'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>like they did when they rebelled </span></i></b><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>against me<i>”</i></span></b><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></b><b><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Ps 95:7-8]</span></b><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>.</span></p>
