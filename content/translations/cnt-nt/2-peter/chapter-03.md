## 2 Peter Chapter 3


### Reminders About the Future


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>This is the second letter I’ve sent you. In both <i>of them</i>,<i> </i>I’m trying to keep your sincere minds alert </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>by reminding you about the message from the holy prophets and about your apostles’ commandments from the Lord Savior. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Get this, first of all: later, mockers will follow their lusts and say, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Where’s the promise about his coming? From the time our ancestors died, everything’s like it's been since creation.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They choose to forget that by God’s word the skies came into existence long ago and the earth consisted of water and emerged from water. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By God’s word, the flood destroyed that world. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By that same word, the earth and sky that exist now are destined for fire; <i>they’re being </i>kept for the judgment day and the destruction of ungodly people.
</span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">2 Pet 3:1-7</p>


### Assurance About the Future


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Friends, don’t miss this one point: to the Lord, a day <i>is</i> like 1,000 years, and 1,000 years <i>is</i> like a day. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’s not slow about <i>keeping </i>his promise the way people might think. He’s not being slow; he’s being patient. He doesn’t want anybody to perish; he wants everyone to repent. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The day of the Lord <u>will come</u>—like a thief: the sky will pass away with a loud noise; the elements will burn up; earth and its works will disappear. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Since they’re destined for destruction, how should you live </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>as you anticipate the time when God destroys the sky with fire and melts the elements with heat? </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>According to his promise, we look forward to a new earth and sky where goodness prevails.             </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">2 Pet 3:8-13</p>


### Living Meantime


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So, as you look forward to that, try hard to be at peace, spotless and blameless, when he finds you. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Consider our Lord’s patience <i>a chance </i>to be saved, as our dear brother Paul wrote to you with the wisdom he’s received. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In all <i>his</i> letters, he talks about those things. Some of them are hard to understand, and unlearned and unsteady people twist them—like <i>they do </i>the other scriptures—to their own destruction. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So, since you know <i>all that </i>ahead of time, make sure not to get deceived by unprincipled people and fall from your steadfastness. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Keep growing in the grace and knowledge of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Glory to him now and forever.                                                                              </span></p>

<p class="section-ref">2 Pet 3:14-1</p>
