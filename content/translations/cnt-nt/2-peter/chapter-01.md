## 2 Peter Chapter 1


### Salutation


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Simon Peter, servant and apostle of Jesus Christ<sup>Messiah</sup>, to those who’ve received a faith that’s equally precious to us—<i>faith</i> in the goodness of our God and Savior Jesus Christ. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Grace and peace many times over because you know God and Jesus our Lord.        </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">2 Pet 1:1-2</p>


### God’s Sufficient Provisions


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His divine power has given us everything related to life and godliness. He has provided it all by our knowing the One who called us by his own honor and goodness. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Through <i>that honor and goodness</i> he has provided us great, precious promises so we can partake of the divine character and escape the corruption that’s in the world from living by lust. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So do your best to add to your faith virtue, to your virtue knowledge, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>to knowledge self-control, endurance, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>godliness, brotherly kindness, love. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If these keep developing in you, they’ll make you useful and fruitful as a result of personally knowing our Lord Jesus Christ. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Those who lack them are nearsighted and blind. They’ve forgotten that they were cleansed from <i>their </i>old sins. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So be all the more diligent in confirming your calling and being chosen. As long as you live that way, you won’t stumble. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You’ll be making abundant provision for entering the eternal kingdom of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ.              </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">2 Pet 1:3-11</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So I’ll keep reminding you even though you know all this and are standing firm in the truth already. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As long as I’m in this body, I think it’s good to keep you alert by reminding you.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I know I’ll be laying aside my tabernacle soon, as our Lord Jesus Christ has shown me. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll be diligent so after I’m gone, you can remember what I’ve said.                                        </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">2 Pet 1:12-15</p>


### Events, not Myths


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We didn’t follow clever, made-up myths when we told you about the power and coming of our Lord Jesus Christ. We saw his majesty with our own eyes. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He received from God the Father honor and glory when the Majestic Glory declared, <i>“This is my dearly loved Son that I delight in.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We heard that voice from heaven ourselves when we were with him on the holy mountain. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>And so </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>we have verified the prophetic word. You do well <i>then </i>to pay attention to it as to a lamp shining in a dark place till the day dawns and the morning star rises in your hearts. </span><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>20</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>First of all, get this: no prophecy in scripture comes from somebody’s own insight <i>into things</i>. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>No prophet ever <i>just
</i>decided to make a prophecy. Instead, the Holy Spirit moved them to speak from God.                                                                            </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">2 Pet 1:16-21</p>
