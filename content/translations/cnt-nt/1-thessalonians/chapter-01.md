<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We are always grateful to God for you and include you in our prayers. We </span></span>
