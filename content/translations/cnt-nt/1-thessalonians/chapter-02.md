## 1 Thessalonians Chapter 2


### Paul’s History with the Thessalonians


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Our coming to you proved to be productive. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As you know, after we’d suffered and been treated disgracefully, we gathered up the courage in our God to present his good news to you during our struggles. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We didn’t pressure <i>you</i> with deception, impure motives, or treachery. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But, we spoke the way we did since God approved us and entrusted <i>us</i> with the good news. <i>We were</i>n’t trying to please people, but God, who was approving our intentions. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God
<i>is</i> witness: we never stooped<sup> </sup>to flattery or veiled greed, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>or tried to get honor from you or anybody else. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Though as Christ’s apostles, we could’ve expected you to support us, we were gentle among you like a nurse taking care of her kids. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Since you became so dear to us, we enjoyed sharing with you God’s good news and our very selves. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You remember our labor and hardship, how we worked day and night so we wouldn’t burden <i>you </i>while we presented his good news to you. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Along with God, you can confirm how devoutly, uprightly, blamelessly we carried ourselves among you—</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>like a father with his kids. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We urged you, encouraged you, and testified to you to live worthy of the One that was calling you to his kingdom and honor.</span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                                                                           1 Th 2:1-12</span></b></span></p>


### Their Openness to the Good News


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That’s why we’re constantly grateful to God. When you heard his message, you accepted it, not as a human message, but as it really is, God’s message that’s at work in you. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You followed the example of the churches of God in Christ: you suffered the same things from your countrymen that they did from the Jews—</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>who killed the Lord Jesus and the prophets, drove us out, and haven’t been pleasing to God. They’ve been hostile to everybody </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and tried to keep us from speaking to Gentiles about salvation, always piling up their own sins. But fury will come on them in the end.                                                                            </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We’ve been away from you for a little while—in person, not in heart. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We’ve really wanted to see you, but Satan prevented it time and again. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Who is our hope, our joy, the crown we’ll be proud of when the Lord Jesus comes? Aren’t <u>you</u> our joy and honor?</span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         1 Th 2:13-19</span></b></span></p>
