## 1 John Chapter 4


### Testing the spirits


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Friends, don’t believe every <i>prophet</i> <i>that claims to speak by the</i> spirit. Test whether <i>his</i> spirit is from God; a lot of false prophets have gone out. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You can recognize that a spirit is from God by whether it agrees that Jesus Christ has come in the flesh. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Any spirit that doesn’t, isn’t from God; it’s the antichristian <i>spirit</i> that you’ve heard is coming and is already here. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You belong to God. You’ve triumphed over the world, because the One that’s in you is greater than the one that’s in it. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>False leaders belong to the world, so they speak of it, and it listens to them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We belong to God; people that know him listen to us. People that don’t belong to him, don’t listen to us. That’s how you can tell if <i>a prophet has the </i>true spirit or a deceiving one.    </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>1 Jn 4:1-6</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                                                                                       </span></span></p>


### God is Love


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Let’s love each other because love comes from God, and everybody that loves has been begotten by him and knows him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>People that don’t love don’t know God because he is </span><i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>characterized by</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> love. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God showed that love by sending his only Son into the world so we could have life through him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Love is not so much that we love <u>God</u> as that he’s loved <u>us</u> and sent his Son as a basis for forgiving our sins. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If God loved us that much, we should love each other. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Nobody has ever seen God; </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>but</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> if we love each other, he’s in us and his love has accomplished its purpose in us.                                                                                                                                               </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">1 Jn 4:7-12</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We know he’s in us and we’re in him by the spirit he’s given us. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We’ve seen and speak to the fact that the Father has sent the Son to save the world. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If anybody agrees that Jesus is God’s Son, God’s in him and he’s in God. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We’ve known and believed that God has put his love in us. God is </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>characterized by </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>love and whoever keeps loving keeps being in God and God keeps being in him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Love ends up giving us confidence at judgment time because we’re like he was in the world. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>There’s no fear in love; perfect love eliminates fear because fear corresponds with punishment; whoever is afraid has not become perfect at loving. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Let’s love because he loved us first. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If people say they love God and hate fellow Christians, they’re not telling the truth. If they don’t love Christians they’ve seen, they can’t be loving </span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God they’ve not seen. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We have this commandment from him: whoever loves God must love God’s children too.                      </span></span></p>
