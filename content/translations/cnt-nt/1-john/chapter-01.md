## 1 John Chapter 1


### Life and Light


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>What was from the beginning, what we’ve heard and seen, what we’ve observed and touched about the living message—</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the eternal life that was with the Father and he revealed to us—that’s what </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>we’re passing along to you so we can have it in common with you as well as with the Father and his Son Jesus Christ<sup>Messiah</sup>. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We’re writing these things <i>to you </i>to fully share our joy.   </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">1 Jn 1:1-4</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The message is that God is light; there’s no darkness in him at all. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If we claim to be in fellowship with him and live in the dark, we’re lying and not practicing the truth. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If we live in the light with him, we have fellowship with each other, and the blood of his Son continues to cleanse us from sin.  </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8-10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If we claim we don’t have any sin, we deceive ourselves and make God a liar. If we admit we’ve sinned, we can trust him to be just and forgive us of everything.                                                                                                               </span></span></p>
