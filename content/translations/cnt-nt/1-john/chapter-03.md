## 1 John Chapter 3


### Children of God


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Look at what special love the Father has extended to us in calling us his children. The world doesn’t know us because it didn’t know him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We’re God’s children now. <p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Everybody that practices sin is practicing lawlessness; sin is lawlessness. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You know he came to take away sins; there’s no sin in him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Nobody that’s in him practices sin. If they do, they haven’t known him or seen him. </span></span>

<u>he</u> is. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Whoever does evil is like the devil because he’s been sinning since the beginning. The Son of God came to destroy what the devil does. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>None of the ones God begets practices sin because </span><i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God’s</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> seed stays in them. <span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That’s how you can tell God’s children from the devil’s. ### Love One Another


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You’ve heard all along that we should love each other </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t be surprised if the world hates you. </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They
</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>amount to</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> being murderers </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>like Cain</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>, and murderers don’t have eternal life. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We’ve known what love is because he showed us by laying his life down for us, and we should lay our lives down for our fellow Christians. </span></span>
