## James Chapter 1


### Salutation


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         1</span></b><sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>:</span></sup><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>James, servant of God and the Lord Jesus Christ<sup>Messiah</sup>, to the twelve tribes in the Dispersion. Greetings.  </span></span></p>


### Temptation, Wisdom, Humility


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Consider temptation something to be glad about. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>It challenges your faith to produce stamina, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and stamina gives you the ability to finish what you’re doing.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If you need wisdom, ask God. He gives it generously to everybody without blaming <i>them for asking</i>. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Trust him when you ask. If you doubt, you’re like an ocean wave blown around by the wind. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t suppose you’ll get anything from God. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If you can’t make up your mind, you’re unsettled about everything you do.                                                                                   </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>James 1:1-8</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Disciples in humble circumstances should take pride in their high position. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Rich disciples should take pride in their humble circumstances because their wealth will fade away like a flower in the grass. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sun climbs high and joins the hot wind to dry up the grass and wilt the flowers, and their beauty fades away. That’s how it is with rich people: they wither away in what they’re trying to do.      </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>James 1:9-11</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When you hold up under temptation you’re blessed. When approved, you’ll receive life as a crown. That’s what the Lord has promised people that care about him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When you face temptation, don’t say, <i>“God’s tempting me.” </i>Evil isn’t tempting to God, and he doesn’t tempt anybody. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Your own lusts are what tempt you, lure you away, entice you. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When lust conceives, it bears sin, and sin grows up to produce death.                                                                                                                                         </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>James 1:12-15</span></b></span></p>


### Listening, Doing, Controlling the Tongue


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t get misled. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Every good and perfect gift comes down from the Father that enlightens us. There’s no wavering or hint of inconsistency with him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He took the initiative in bringing us forth by the message about the truth so we’ve become a kind of firstfruits among people he created.                                                                                                                                          </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>James 1:16-18</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>
</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Know
<i>this</i>: people must be willing to listen, be careful about what they say, and be slow to get mad. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>People’s anger doesn’t produce God’s goodness. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So get rid of filthy ways of living and the excesses that evil brings; humbly yield to the implanted message that can save you.                                                                                                                                              </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>James 1:19-21</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Do the word; don’t just listen to it and kid yourselves. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If you hear <i>it</i> and don’t do <i>it</i>, you’re like a fellow that looks in a mirror </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and forgets what he looked like. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When you stare into the perfect “law,” the law of liberty, and continue as a doer, not just a hearer, you’ll be blessed in what you do.                                                                                                               </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>James 1:22-25</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If you consider yourself religious but don’t control your tongue, you’re fooling yourself; your religion is useless. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pure, undefiled religion with God the Father is this: looking after orphans and widows in their hardship and keeping yourselves from getting stained by the world.                                                                                                                                                 </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>James 1:26-27</span></b></span></p>
