## Titus Chapter 2


### Older Christian’s Decorum


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But you, encourage the kind of living that is appropriate to sound doctrine. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Older men should be serious-minded, respectable, sensible; sound in faith, love, and endurance. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The same thing applies to older women. They should be reverent in behavior, not prone to gossip or addicted to wine, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>but </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>teaching what’s good. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then they can teach young women to love their husbands and children. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They
</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>should be</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> sensible, pure, devoted to their families, kind, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> deferring to their husbands so people won’t slander God’s message.                  </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Tit 2:1-5</p>


### Young Men’s Decorum


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In the same way, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>encourage</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> younger men to be sensible. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Present yourself as an example of doing good. </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Demonstrate</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> pure doctrine, respectable conduct, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and sound speech that people can’t criticize. Then critics will be ashamed because they can’t find anything bad to say about you.                                                                                                                                         </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Tit 2:6-8</p>


### Salvation Offered to Everybody


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Urge</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> servants to submit to their masters, to be pleasing, not argumentive, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>not stealing, but showing good faith so they can adorn the doctrine of God our Savior.                                                                                                  </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Tit 2:9-10</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God’s grace has appeared and offered salvation to everyone. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His grace instructs us to set aside ungodliness and worldly pleasures and to live wisely, justly, and godly in the present age. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>All the while, we look forward to the blessed hope, the glorious appearing of our great God and Savior Jesus Christ. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He gave himself to redeem us from lawlessness and to purify a special people for himself, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>a people</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> with zeal zeal for doing good. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Speak these things, urge, and reprimand with authority. Don’t let anybody disregard you.            </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Tit 2:11-15</p>
