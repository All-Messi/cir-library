## Titus Chapter **3**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><strong><sup>1</sup></strong>Remind <em>people</em> to submit to rulers and authorities,<em> </em>to be obedient and ready to do everything that’s good. <strong><sup>2</sup></strong><em>Remind</em><em> them</em> not to insult or get pushy, <em>but</em> to be considerate and gentle toward everybody. <strong><sup>3</sup></strong>At one time we were foolish too, disobedient, deceived, addicted to various lusts and pleasures. We spent our lives in evil and envy, hated and hating one another. <strong><sup>4</sup></strong>But, <em>“the kindness of God our Savior appeared along with love for </em>other<em> </em><em>people.</em><em> </em><strong><sup>5</sup></strong><em>We</em><em> didn’t save ourselves by </em><em>doing good. I</em><em>n mercy </em><em><u>he</u></em><em> </em><em>saved us by the washing </em><em>that</em><em> regenerat</em><em>es</em><em> and </em><em>by the renewal </em><em>the Holy Spirit</em><em> </em>gives<em>.</em><em> </em><strong><sup>6</sup></strong>God<em> poured out his Spirit on us richly through Jesus </em><em>Christ</em><em> our </em><em>Savior. </em><strong><sup>7</sup></strong><em>I</em><em>n</em><em> grace</em><em> he</em><em> justified us so we could </em><em>inherit the </em><em>hope for eternal life.”</em><em>                                  </em><em>	 </em></span>
<p class="section-ref">Tit 3:1-7</p>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>That’s a reliable statement. I want you to emphasize doing these things so the ones that have believed God will concentrate on doing good. <em>Such things</em> are good and profitable. <strong><sup>9</sup></strong>But, avoid foolish controversies, genealogies, rivalries, legal disputes; they’re pointless and unprofitable. <strong><sup>10</sup></strong>After a first and second warning, quit having anything to do with people who cause division. <strong><sup>11</sup></strong>They’re perverted sinners that have condemned themselves.<strong> </strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Tit 3:8-11</p>

### Plans for Further Ministry


<span class="verse" id="v12"><strong><sup>2</sup></strong>When Artemas or Tychycus get to you—that I’m sending—do your best to meet me at Nicopolis. I’ve decided to spend the winter there. <strong><sup>13</sup></strong>Diligently help Zenas the lawyer and Apollos get on their way with what they need.<sup>  </sup><strong><sup>14</sup></strong>Have our <em>people</em> learn to practice doing good to meet pressing needs so they’ll be productive.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup><strong>15</strong></sup>The ones that are with me greet you. Greet those who love us in the faith. Grace <em>be</em> with you all.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Tit 3:12-15</p>
