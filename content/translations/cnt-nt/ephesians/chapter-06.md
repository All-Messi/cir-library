## Ephesians Chapter 6

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><b><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>6</span></b><sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>:</span></sup><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Children, obey your parents in the Lord; it’s the right thing to do. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2-3</sup> <b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Honor your father and mother so you’ll live long and well”</span></i></b><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></b><b><span style='font-size:8.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Ex 20:12; Deut. 5:16]</span></b><span style='font-size:8.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>(</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the first commandment with a promise <i>attached</i></span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>). </span></span>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Servants, obey your masters with fear and trembling, sincerely as if obeying Christ. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t just try to create impressions to please them; but as Christ’s servants, do God’s will honestly </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>with good intentions. Be serving the Lord, as it were, not people. </span></span>

### The Full Armor of God


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Finally, be strong in the Lord and his great power. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Put on God’s full armor so you can withstand the devil’s schemes. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You’re not fighting a physical war, but rulers, authorities, this world’s dark forces, spiritual evil in high places. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So put on God’s full armor so you can withstand sinful times, overcome everything, and stand firm. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So stand firm. Tie truth around your waist. Make goodness your breastplate. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>On your feet, wear the good news about peace. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Along with everything else, pick up faith as <i>your</i> shield so the devil’s flaming arrows will ricochet away from you. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Take salvation for a helmet and God’s word as a spiritual sword. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pray in the spirit on every occasion. Stay alert and persist in petitions for the saints. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pray that I’ll speak boldly the unrevealed message that the good news makes known. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>On behalf of it, I’m an ambassador in chains. <i>Pray</i> that I’ll speak boldly for him like I need to.                                                             </span></span></p>
