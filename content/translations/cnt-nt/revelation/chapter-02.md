</span>and <span style='color:#C00000'>walks among the seven gold lampstands</span>:

<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif; color:#00B050'>You’ve endured for me without wearing out.                                                                       </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif; color:#7030A0'>Whoever has an ear, listen to what the Spirit’s telling the churches. </span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif;color:#00B0F0'>Those who are victorious I’ll let eat from the tree of life in Paradise.”’”    </span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                                                                         </span></i></span></span></p>
