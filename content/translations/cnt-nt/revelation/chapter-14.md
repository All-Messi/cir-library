## Revelation Chapter 14


### Rev 13:11-18



### The Lamb on Mt. Zion


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I saw the Lamb standing on Mt. Zion with the 144,000 that had his and his Father’s name written on their foreheads. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I heard a noise from heaven like crashing waves and loud thunder—like harpists playing harps. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They sang a new song in front of the throne, the four living beings, and the elders. Nobody could learn the song except the 144,000 that had been purchased out of the earth. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They hadn’t defiled themselves with women; they were virgins, who followed the Lamb wherever he went. They’d been purchased from among people as firstfruits to God and the Lamb. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They’d never lied; they were blameless.                                                                           </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rev 14:1-5</p>


### The Messages from Three Angels


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I saw another angel flying in the sky who had the everlasting good news to present to every nation, tribe, language, and people group. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He shouted, <i>“Fear God and honor him! His time for judging has come. Worship the One that made sky, earth, sea, and springs.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A second angel followed, <i>“Fallen, fallen, ‘Babylon’ the Great that made the nations drink the wine of her immoral passion.”
                                            </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A third angel followed them, shouting, <i>“Those who have worshiped the beast and its image and received a stamp on their forehead or right hand, </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>will drink the wine of God’s displeasure full strength. They’ll be tormented with fire and sulfur in front of the holy angels and the Lamb. The smoke from their torment will go up forevermore. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>There’ll be no rest day or night for those who’ve worshiped the beast and its image and received the stamp of its name. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That’s why the saints stay confident in Christ.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I heard a voice from the sky, <i>“Write </i>this<i>, ‘People that die in the Lord from now on are fortunate.’ ‘Yes,’ </i>the Spirit says, <i>‘so they can rest from their labors, because </i>the account of <i>their deeds stays on their record.’”                </i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rev 14:6-13</p>


### Harvesting the Earth


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I saw what looked like a man sitting on a white cloud. He had a gold crown on his head and a sharp scythe in his hand. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Another angel came out of the sanctuary in heaven and called out to him, <i>“Use your scythe to reap; it’s time to harvest; earth’s harvest is ready.” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The one on the cloud swung his scythe over the earth and harvested it.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Another angel came out of the sanctuary, holding a sharp hook-blade. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Another angel came out of the altar with authority over fire and shouted to the one with the hook-blade, <i>“Use your blade to gather the clusters from the vine (</i></span><i><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the world</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>); the grapes are ripe.” </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The angel used it to harvest the vine, and he put the grapes in the large winepress of God’s anger. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The wine was pressed out outside the city, and the blood from the winepress came up to the horses’ bridles for some 180 miles.                                        </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rev 14:14-20</p>
