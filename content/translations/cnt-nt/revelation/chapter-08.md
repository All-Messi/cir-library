## Revelation Chapter 8


### God will wipe every tear out of their eyes.”                                           Rev 7:13-17



### Breaking the Seventh Seal


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When
<i>the Lamb</i> broke the seventh seal, heaven was silent for about a half hour. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I saw seven angels standing in front of God, receiving seven trumpets.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Another angel came and stood by the altar, holding a gold censer. He received a large amount incense to add to the prayers of the saints on the gold altar in front of the throne. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The smoke from the incense rose up from the angel’s hand with the saints’ prayers. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The angel filled the censer with fire from the altar and threw it onto the earth. There came peals of thunder, lightning flashes, and an earthquake.    </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rev 8:1-5</p>


### Blowing the 7 Trumpets


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The seven angels with the seven trumpets raised them to blow.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The first blew his trumpet. Hail and fire mixed with blood were thrown onto the earth. A third of the earth, a third of the trees, and all the green grass burned up.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The second angel blew his trumpet. Something like a burning mountain was thrown into the ocean and turned a third of it to blood, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>killed a third of marine life, and destroyed a third of the ships <i>that were on it</i>.
                          </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rev 8:6-9</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The third angel blew his trumpet. A big star, burning like a torch, fell on a third of the springs and rivers. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>It’s name was Wormwood. A third of the water turned bitter, and many people died from the brackish water.             </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rev 8:10-11</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The fourth angel blew his trumpet. A third of the sun, moon, and stars was struck. A third of the stars went out; the sun didn’t shine for a third of the day, and the moon didn’t shine for a third of the night.  </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I saw and heard an eagle flying in the sky, shouting, <i>“Woe, woe, woe to people on earth because of the last three trumpet blasts!”      </i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rev 8:12-13</p>
