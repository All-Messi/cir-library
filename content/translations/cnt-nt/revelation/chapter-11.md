## Revelation Chapter 11


### The Two Witnesses


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I received a measuring rod like a staff. <i>“Measure God’s sanctuary, the altar, and the worshipers. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t measure the court outside the sanctuary because the Gentiles have received it, and they’ll trample the Holy City underfoot for 42 months. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll authorize my two witnesses to prophecy for </span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>those <i>1260 days in burlap and ashes.”</i></span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They’re two olive trees, two lampstands in front of the Lord of the earth. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If anybody tries to injure them, fire will come out of their mouths and burn them up. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>During the time they prophesy, they’ll have authority to keep it from raining, to turn water into blood, to strike the earth with any plague as often as they like. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When they’ve finished speaking <i>for God</i>, the beast from the abyss will attack them, conquer, and kill them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Their corpses will lie in the street of the great city spiritually called Sodom and Egypt, where their Lord also was crucified. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>People from tribes, languages, and nations will look at their corpses for 3</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Agency FB",sans-serif'>½</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> days and won’t let them be buried. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They’ll celebrate and send gifts to each other because those two prophets had tormented them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>After 3</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Agency FB",sans-serif'>½</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> days, breath from God will return, and they’ll stand up and terrify everyone that sees them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I heard a loud voice from heaven tell them, <i>“Come up here”</i>; and they went up in a cloud while their enemies watched. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then a severe earthquake made a tenth of the city fall; 7,000 people died in the earthquake. Fear overwhelmed the rest, and they praised the God of heaven.                                                                                                                              </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>
</span><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The second woe is over. The third’s coming soon.</span></span></p>

### The Seventh Trumpet/The Third Woe


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The seventh angel blew his trumpet, and loud voices from heaven said,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        “<i>The kingdom of this world has become </i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the kingdom<i> of our L</i></span><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> and his Christ<sup>Messiah</sup>,
</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:1.0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and he’ll reign forevermore.”
                        </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The 24 elders on their thrones in front of God fell on their faces and worshiped him,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Thank you, L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> God Almighty, who is and was,</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    because you’ve assumed power and begun to reign. </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span></i><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The nations raged, and your anger came.</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                         The time came for judging the dead,</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    the time for rewarding your servants the prophets and saints— </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:1.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>those who respect you from least to greatest; </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    the time for destroying those who destroy the world.” </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God’s sanctuary in heaven opened, and the Ark of the Covenant became visible there.
