## Revelation Chapter 21


### The New Earth and Sky


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I saw a new earth and sky. The first earth and sky had passed away, and there wasn’t any ocean anymore. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I watched the Holy City, the New Jerusalem, come down out of heaven from God, ready like a bride adorned for her husband. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A loud voice came from the throne, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“God’s tabernacle is with his people; he’ll live with them, and they’ll belong to him.
</span></i><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’ll wipe away every tear, and there won’t be any pain, weeping, crying, or death anymore. Former things will pass away.”     </span></i><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Rev 21:1-4</span></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>
</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The One on the throne said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I’m making everything new. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Write this down because it’s true and reliable. It’s done. I’m Alpha and Omega, the beginning and end. I’ll let whoever’s thirsty drink from the spring of living water free of charge. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Those who overcome will inherit these things. I’ll be their God, and they’ll be my children.
</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But the unfaithful and corrupt, the cowards, murderers, sorcerers, idolaters, liars, and immoral will burn in the lake of sulfur, which is the second death.”
     </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rev 21:5-8</p>


### The New Jerusalem


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>One of the angels with the seven bowls of final plagues came to me, <i>“Come with me; I’ll show you the Lamb’s bride.” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He took me in spirit to a big, tall mountain and showed me the Holy City, Jerusalem, coming down out of heaven from God, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>shining with God’s glory. Its brilliance was like a very valuable stone, like crystal-clear jasper.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>It had a broad, high wall and twelve gates, with an angel at each gate and the names of Israel’s twelve tribes inscribed on them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>There were three gates each on the east, north, south, and west. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The wall consisted of twelve foundations with the names of the Lamb’s twelve apostles on them.                                                                                                                             </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Rev 21:9-14</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>       </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The
<i>angel</i> that was talking to me had a gold rod for measuring the city, its gates, and wall. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size: 14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> city was shaped like a cube 1380 miles long, wide, and high; </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>its wall measured 216 feet high, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>made out of jasper; the city was pure gold like clear glass. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The foundation stones were beautiful precious stones. In order, they were jasper, sapphire, agate, emerald, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>sardonyx, carnelian*, chrysolite* <i>[yellow topaz]</i>, beryl, topaz, chrysoprase* <i>[green quartz]</i>, hyacinth, and amethyst. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Each gate was a single pearl, and the streets were pure gold like transparent glass.                                                                                                        </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I didn’t see a sanctuary in it because God Almighty and the Lamb were its sanctuary. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The city didn’t need the sun or moon because God’s glory gave it light, and the Lamb was its lamp. </span><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>24, 26</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The nations lived by its light, and kings brought their glory and the honor of their nations into it. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Its gates never shut because there wasn’t any night there. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Nothing profane or anyone detestable or false could go in, only the ones written in the Lamb’s Book of Life.                                                                                                 </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rev 21:15-27</p>
