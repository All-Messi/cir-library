## Revelation Chapter 17


### Rev 16:17-21


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></b><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>One of the seven angels with the seven bowls came over to me,  </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Come here; I’ll show you the condemnation of the great prostitute that sits on a vast expanse of water. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Kings committed immoral acts with her, and people got drunk on the wine of her immorality.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He took me in spirit to a desert place, and I saw a woman on a red beast covered with blasphemous names and having seven heads and ten horns. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She wore purple and scarlet with precious stones and pearls, and held a gold cup full of detestable, unclean things related to her immorality. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>On her headband was the mysterious name, <i>“’Babylon’ the Great, Mother of Prostitutes and Detestable Things.”</i>    </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rev 17:1-5</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She was drunk from the blood of saints, martyrs for Jesus. I was amazed. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The angel said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Why be amazed? I’ll explain to you the meaning of the woman and the beast she’s riding. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The beast was, is not, and will come up from the abyss to </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>its
<i>eternal destruction. People will marvel when they see it—people whose names haven’t been in the Book of Life from the founding of the world. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Here’s the explanation. The seven heads represent seven hills the woman sits on. There are seven kings: </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>five fallen, one existing, and the other yet to come. When he comes, he has to stay a little while. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The beast that was and is not, is an eighth, is like the seven, and is destined for destruction. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The ten horns are ten kings that haven’t received a kingdom yet, but they’ll receive authority like kings for a short time with the beast. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They have a joint purpose and give their power and authority to the beast. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They’ll battle the Lamb, and the Lamb will conquer them because he’s Lord of lords and King of kings. The ones on his side are called, chosen, and faithful.                                      </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rev 17:6-14</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“The water where the woman is sitting represents masses of people, nations, and languages.
</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The ten horns and the beast will hate the harlot, strip her bare, eat her flesh, and burn her up. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That’s because God has put it in their minds to accomplish his purpose by making common cause and giving their kingdom to the beast till God’s words come to fulfillment.
</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The woman is the great city that rules the kings on earth.”                                                 </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rev 17:15-18</p>
