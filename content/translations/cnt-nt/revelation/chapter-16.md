## Revelation Chapter 16


### Pouring Out the 7 Angels’ Bowls


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I heard a loud voice from the sanctuary, telling the seven angels, <i>“Pour out the seven bowls of God’s anger on the earth.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The first poured out his bowl on the <u>land</u>. A foul, painful boil festered up on the ones that had the stamp of the beast and worshiped its image.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The second poured out his bowl on the <u>ocean</u>. It became like dead men’s blood. Everything in the ocean died.    </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rev 16:1-3</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The third poured out his bowl on <u>rivers and springs</u>. They became blood. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I heard that angel say, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:1.0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“You’re just, Holy One, who are and were, </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:1.0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>for decreeing those judgments.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Because they poured out the blood of saints and prophets,</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            you’ve given them blood to drink.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            That’s what they deserve!”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I heard the altar say,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        <i>“Yes, L</i></span><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> God Almighty;</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    your judgments are true and just.”                                                 </span></i></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rev 16:4-7</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The fourth angel poured out his bowl on the <u>sun</u>, and it scorched people </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>with searing heat. They blasphemed God, who authorized those plagues; and they didn’t repent or praise him.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The fifth angel poured out his bowl on the <u>throne of the beast</u>. Its kingdom grew dark, and people gnawed their tongues in agony. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They blasphemed God in their agony and sores, but they didn’t repent.                                   </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rev 16:8-11</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sixth angel poured out his bowl on the big <u>Euphrates River</u>. It dried up so the kings in the East could cross it. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I saw three unclean spirits <i>leap </i>like frogs out of the mouths of the dragon, beast, and false prophet. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They were demons that performed signs, and they went to gather the kings of the world for war at the great day of God Almighty. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I’m coming like a thief at night. Blessed are those who stay awake and dressed so they won’t be walking around naked, and people see their shame.” </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He gathered them to a place called in Hebrew The Mound of Megiddo <i>[Harmagedon]</i>*<i>.
                                                                                                           </i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rev 16:12-16</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The seventh <i>angel </i>poured out his bowl on the <u>air</u>. A voice shouted out of the sanctuary from the throne, <i>“It’s finished!”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>There came lightning flashes, peals of thunder, and the most severe earthquake in history. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The great city <i>‘Babylon’
</i>split into three parts, and the cities in the nations crumbled. God remembered to give ‘Babylon’ the Great his wine cup of fierce fury. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The islands sank, and the mountains collapsed. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Enormous hailstones, weighing over 90 pounds, fell on people. They blasphemed God because the hailstone plague was so severe.                     </span></span></p>
