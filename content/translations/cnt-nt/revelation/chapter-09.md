## Revelation Chapter 9


### The Fifth Trumpet/The First Woe


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The fifth angel blew his trumpet. I saw a star that had just fallen* to earth. He received a key to the deep abyss. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He opened it, and a cloud of smoke came out of <i>it </i>as from a huge furnace, and the sun and air grew dark from it. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Locusts swarmed out of the cloud onto the earth with ability like scorpions have. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They were told not to harm the grass, anything green, any tree—just people that didn’t have God’s seal on their foreheads. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They weren’t to kill them, but to torment them for five months as with scorpion stings. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The people would want to die but couldn’t; they’d long for death, but it would elude them.                                                                                             </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Rev 9:1-6</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The locusts looked like horses outfitted for war. They wore what looked like gold helmets* <i>[chanfrons + crinets?]</i> on their heads. They had faces like people, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>manes like women’s hair, teeth like lions’ teeth, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and iron chest plates* <i>[peytrals]</i>. Their wings sounded like hordes of horses with chariots charging into battle. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Their tails had stingers like scorpions that they could torment people with for five months. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Their king, the angel of the abyss, had the Hebrew name Abaddon—<i>Apollyon*</i> <i>[Destroyer]
</i>in Greek.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The first woe is over; two more are coming.                                                       </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Rev 9:7-12</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>       </span></span></p>


### The Sixth Trumpet/The Second Woe


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sixth angel blew his trumpet. I heard a voice from the four horns of the gold altar in front of God, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>telling the sixth angel to release the four angels on hold at the great River Euphrates.<i> </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He released the four angels—readied for an hour, day, month, and year—to massacre a third of all people. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The army of horsemen numbered 200,000,000 (</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I heard the number</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>). </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The horses and their riders in the vision looked like this: the riders wore chest plates the color of fire, hyacinth, and sulfur; the horses had heads like lions with fire, smoke, and sulfur coming out of their mouths. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The fire, smoke, and sulfur killed a third of all people. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The horses’ power was in their mouths and tails; their tails were like snakes with heads that could injure people.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>People that didn’t die from these plagues refused to stop worshiping demons and turn away from the idols their hands had made out of gold, silver, brass, stone, and wood—that can’t see, hear, or walk. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They didn’t repent of their murders, witchcraft, sexual immorality, or thefts.                                                                                                                                               </span></span></p>
