## Revelation Chapter 10


### The Little Open Scroll


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I watched another powerful angel come down from the sky, dressed in a cloud with a rainbow above his head. His face shined like the sun, and his legs were like pillars of fire. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He was holding a little, open scroll in his right hand. With his right foot on the sea and his left one on land, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>he shouted like a roaring lion. When he shouted, seven thunders pealed. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When they did, I was about to write, but a voice from the sky said, <i>“Seal up what the seven thunders said; don’t write it down.” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The angel that was standing on land and sea, lifted his right hand toward the sky </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and swore by the One who lives forevermore—who created earth, sky, sea, and everything in them—that there wouldn’t be any more delay. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When it’s time for the seventh angel to blow his trumpet, God’s unrevealed plan will come to completion like he’d told his servants the prophets.         </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Rev 10:1-7</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>       </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The voice from the sky spoke to me again, <i>“Go take the little, open scroll that’s in the hand of the angel that’s standing on land and sea.” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I went and asked him for it. He said, <i>“Eat it; it’ll make your stomach sour, but it’ll leave a sweet taste in your mouth.” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I took it and ate it. It tasted sweet like honey; but when I swallowed it, it made my stomach sour. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They told me, <i>“You have to prophesy some more to many people groups, nations, languages, and kings.”                                                                                        </i></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Rev 10:8-11</span></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>              </span></i></span></p>
