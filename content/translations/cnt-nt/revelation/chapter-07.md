<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>All the angels around the throne, the elders, and the living beings fell on their faces in front of the throne and worshiped God, </span></span></p>
