<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and rescuing righteous Lot oppressed by the sensual conduct of-the unprincipled-ones in ungodliness, </span><span style='font-size:16.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>(</span></span>
