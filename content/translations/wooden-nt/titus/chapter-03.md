## Titus Chapter 3

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><b><span style='font-size:16.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>3</span></b><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>:1</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Remind them to-subject-themselves to-rulers (and) to-authorities, to-be-obedient, to-be ready for every good deed, </span><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>to-slander nobody, to-be uncontentious, gentle, showing all humility to all men.  </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>For once <u>we</u>┘also└ were foolish, disobedient, deceived, enslaved to-various lusts and pleasures, spending-life in evil and envy, hated, hating one-another.  </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“when the kindness of-God the Savior of-us and the/his love-for-humanity appeared, </span><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>not from works [the] in righteousness that <u>we</u> did, but according-to the mercy of-him he-saved us through (the) washing of-regeneration and renewal of-(the) Holy Spirit, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>whom he-poured-out on us richly through Jesus Christ the Savior of-us, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>in-order-that, having-been-justified by-[the] that grace, we-might-become heirs according-to (the) hope of-eternal life.”</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Faithful (is) the saying, and concerning these-things I-want you to-be-emphatic in-order-that the-ones having-believed God may-concentrate-on proclaiming good deeds.  These-things are good and profitable for-[the] people.  </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But avoid foolish controversies and genealogies and rivalries and legal disputes, for they are unprofitable and worthless.  </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Have-nothing-to-do-with (a) divisive person after one and (a) second warning, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>knowing that [the] such-(a)-person is perverted and sins, being self-condemned.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When I send Artemas or Tychicus to you, hasten to-come to me in Nicopolis, for I-have-decided to-spend-the-winter there.  </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Send-forward diligently Zenas the lawyer and Apollos, in-order-that nothing is-lacking to-them.  </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>And let┘also [the] our (people)└learn to-produce good deeds unto the necessary needs, in-order-that they-may┘not└be unfruitful.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>All the-ones with me greet you.  Greet the ones loving us in faith.  [The] grace (be) with you all.</span></span></p>
