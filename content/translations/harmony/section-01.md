<h3 class="topic-header">Incarnation of the Messiah</h3>
<p class="topic-reference">(<span class="gospel-jn">Jn</span> 1:1-18)</p>
<p class="harmony-text"><span class="gospel-mt"><strong>	</strong></span><span class="gospel-jn"><strong><sup class="verse-num">1:1</sup>In the beginning the Word was there. The Word was with God, and the Word was deity. <sup class="verse-num">2</sup>He was in the beginning with God. <sup class="verse-num">3</sup>He made everything; nothing came into existence without him. <sup class="verse-num">4</sup>He had life in himself, life that gave light to mankind. <sup class="verse-num">5</sup>The light shined in the dark, but the darkness tended not to accept it.</strong></span></p>
<p class="harmony-text"><span class="gospel-jn"><strong>	<sup class="verse-num">6</sup>God sent a man named John <sup class="verse-num">7</sup>to tell people about the light so everybody would believe through him. <sup class="verse-num">8</sup>He wasn’t the light, but came to talk about <sup class="verse-num">9</sup>the light, the true light that enlightens every person in the world. <sup class="verse-num">10</sup>He was in the world and made the world, but it didn’t know him. <sup class="verse-num">11</sup>He came into his own things, but his own people didn’t accept him. <sup class="verse-num">12</sup>But, to as many as did accept him and trust him, he gave the right to become God’s children. <sup class="verse-num">13</sup>They weren’t born from blood, physical desire, the will of a husband, but from God. <sup class="verse-num">14</sup>The Word took on a physical nature and lived among us for a while. We saw his glory, glory like the only One the Father begot, full of grace and truth.</strong></span></p>
<p class="harmony-text"><span class="gospel-jn"><strong>	<sup class="verse-num">15</sup>John shouted, </strong></span><span class="gospel-jn"><strong><em>“This is the One I was talking about when I said, ‘There’s a man coming after me who has come to be before me, because he existed before me.’”</em></strong></span><span class="gospel-jn"><strong> <sup class="verse-num">16</sup>From his full measure we’ve all received grace on top of grace. <sup class="verse-num">17</sup>Moses gave law, but Jesus Messiah brought grace and truth. <sup class="verse-num">18</sup>Nobody has ever seen God; the only begotten deity—the One dear to the Father—has shown what he’s like.							     </strong></span></p>
</div>
