<div id="the-birth-of-jesus" class="harmony-topic">
<h3 class="topic-header">The Birth of Jesus</h3>
<p class="topic-reference">(<span class="gospel-mt">Mt</span> 2:1a; <span class="gospel-lk">Lk</span> 2:1-7)</p>
<p class="harmony-text"><span class="gospel-lk"><strong><sup>2:1</sup>At that time Caesar Augustus sent out a decree to take a census of the Roman Empire. <sup>2</sup>That was the first census, when Quirinius was governor of Syria. <sup>3</sup>Everybody went to his ancestral city to register. <sup>4</sup>Joseph went up from Nazareth in Galilee to Judaea, to Bethlehem, the city of David, because he descended from David. <sup>5</sup>He went up to register with Mary, who was engaged to him and pregnant. <sup>6</sup>While they were there, it came time for her to have </strong></span><span class="gospel-lk"><strong><em>her</em></strong></span><span class="gospel-lk"><strong> baby, <sup>7</sup>and she had her firstborn, a son. She wrapped him in baby cloths and laid him in a manger because there wasn’t any room for them in the inn. 	      </strong></span></p>
</div>

<p class="harmony-text">	<span class="gospel-lk"><strong><sup>8</sup>Not far away, shepherds were watching sheep outdoors at night. <sup>9</sup>An angel of the Lord stood beside them, and the glory of the Lord shined around them. <p class="harmony-text"><span class="gospel-lk"><strong>		<sup>14</sup>“</strong></span><span class="gospel-lk"><strong><em>Glory to God in the highest,</em></strong></span></p>
<p class="harmony-text"><span class="gospel-lk"><strong><em>		  and on earth peace to men that please </em></strong></span><span class="gospel-lk"><strong>him</strong></span><span class="gospel-lk"><strong><em>.”</em></strong></span></p>
<p class="harmony-text"><span class="gospel-lk"><strong>	<sup>15</sup>When the angels had gone back to heaven, the shepherds said, </strong></span><span class="gospel-lk"><strong><em>“Let’s go to Bethlehem and see what the Lord’s told us about.”</em></strong></span><span class="gospel-lk"><strong> <sup>16</sup>They hurried away and found Mary and Joseph, and the baby that was lying in a manger. <sup>17</sup>When they’d seen him, they told everybody what the angel said about him. <sup>18</sup>They were amazed at what the shepherds were saying. <sup>19</sup>Mary treasured these things up in her heart. <sup>20</sup>The shepherds went back, glorifying and praising God for everything they’d seen and heard, just like the angel said.		     			          	     	     	     	     </strong></span></p>

<p class="harmony-text">	<span class="gospel-lk"><strong><sup>21</sup>When the eight days had passed for circumcising him, they named him “Jesus,” as the angel had directed </strong></span><span class="gospel-lk"><strong><em>them </em></strong></span><span class="gospel-lk"><strong>before Mary became pregnant. </strong></span></p>

<p class="harmony-text"><span class="gospel-lk"><strong>		29</strong></span><span class="gospel-lk"><strong><em>“Now, Master, your servant can leave the world</em></strong></span></p>
<p class="harmony-text"><span class="gospel-lk"><strong><em>in peace as you said. </em></strong></span></p>

<p class="harmony-text"><span class="gospel-lk"><strong><sup>31</sup></strong></span><span class="gospel-lk"><strong><em>you prepared in front of all peoples, </em></strong></span></p>

<div id="the-visit-of-the-magi" class="harmony-topic">
<h3 class="topic-header">The Visit of the Magi</h3>

<p class="harmony-text"><span class="gospel-mt"><strong>2:1</strong></span><span class="gospel-mt">When Jesus was born in Bethlehem in Judaea during the reign of Herod the king, Magi from the East arrived in Jerusalem,</span><span class="gospel-mt"><strong> 2</strong></span><span class="gospel-mt"><em>“Where’s the one born king of the Jews? In the East we saw his star and came to pay homage to him.”</em></span> <span class="gospel-mt"><strong><sup>3</sup></strong></span><span class="gospel-mt">When Herod heard this, he was disturbed and Jerusalem as well. </span><span class="gospel-mt"><strong><sup>4</sup></strong></span><span class="gospel-mt">He gathered the chief priests and scribes of the people and asked them where the </span><span class="gospel-mt"><u>Messiah</u></span><span class="gospel-mt"> would be born. </span></p>
<p class="harmony-text"><span class="gospel-mt"><strong><sup>5</sup></strong></span><span class="gospel-mt">They said, </span><span class="gospel-mt"><em>“In Bethlehem in Judaea. The prophet wrote, </em></span></p>
<p class="harmony-text">	<span class="gospel-mt"><strong><sup>6</sup></strong></span><span class="gospel-mt"><strong><em>‘YOU, BETHLEHEM </em></strong></span><span class="gospel-mt"><em>in Judah,</em></span></p>
<p class="harmony-text">		<span class="gospel-mt"><strong><em>ARE </em></strong></span><span class="gospel-mt"><em>by no means </em></span><span class="gospel-mt"><strong><em>LEAST AMONG THE LEADERS OF JUDAH.</em></strong></span></p>

<p class="harmony-text">	<span class="gospel-mt"><strong><sup>7</sup></strong></span><span class="gospel-mt">Herod privately called the Magi to find out when the star had appeared. </span><span class="gospel-mt"><strong><sup>8</sup></strong></span><span class="gospel-mt">He sent them to Bethlehem, </span><span class="gospel-mt"><em>“Look for the child carefully. <div id="the-return-from-egypt-and-settlement-in-nazareth" class="harmony-topic">

The ones that were trying to kill the child have died.”</em></span> <span class="gospel-mt"><strong><sup>21</sup></strong></span><span class="gospel-mt">He got up and took them to Israel. <p class="harmony-text"><span class="gospel-lk"><strong><sup>40</sup>The child grew and became strong and increased in wisdom, and God’s grace was on him.	    								    </strong></span></p>
