<div id="the-youth-of-jesus" class="harmony-topic">
<h3 class="topic-header">The Youth of Jesus</h3>
<p class="topic-reference">(<span class="gospel-lk">Lk</span> 2:41-52)</p>
<p class="harmony-text"><span class="gospel-lk"><strong><sup>41</sup>Every year his parents would go up to Jerusalem to the Passover Festival. <sup>42</sup>When he was twelve, they went up to the festival as usual. <sup>43</sup>After it was over, they headed back; but the boy Jesus stayed in Jerusalem, and his parents didn’t know it. <sup>44</sup>They supposed he was in the caravan. They traveled for a day and started looking for him among relatives and acquaintances. <sup>45</sup>When they didn’t find him, they went back to Jerusalem. <sup>46</sup>Three days later they found him in the temple, sitting among the teachers, listening and asking them questions. <sup>47</sup>Everybody that heard him was surprised at his understanding and answers. <sup>48</sup>When </strong></span><span class="gospel-lk"><strong><em>his parents</em></strong></span><span class="gospel-lk"><strong> saw him, they were astonished. </strong></span></p>
<p class="harmony-text"><span class="gospel-lk"><strong>His mother said, </strong></span><span class="gospel-lk"><strong><em>“Son, why have you done this to us? Your father and I have been looking for you, worried sick.” </em></strong></span></p>
<p class="harmony-text"><span class="gospel-lk"><strong>	<sup>49</sup>He said, </strong></span><span class="gospel-lk"><strong><em>“Why were you looking all over for me? Didn’t you know I’d be in my Father’s house?” </em></strong></span></p>
<p class="harmony-text"><span class="gospel-lk"><strong>	<sup>50</sup>They didn’t understand what he meant.</strong></span></p>
<p class="harmony-text"><span class="gospel-lk"><strong>	<sup>51</sup>He went down with them to Nazareth and was subject to them, and his mother treasured this up in her heart. <sup>52</sup>Jesus kept growing in wisdom and stature, in favor with God and man.									    	    </strong></span></p>
</div>
