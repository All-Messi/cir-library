<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When I leave, the Spirit of the </span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> will carry you where I don’t know. <p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Elijah said, <i>“As the </i><i>L</i></span><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> of Hosts lives, I’ll present myself to </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ahab<i> today.”</i></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> 1 Kg 18:1-15</span></b><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I haven’t brought trouble on Israel. <p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Elijah told the Baal prophets, <i>“Choose an ox and prepare it first; there are lots of you.
