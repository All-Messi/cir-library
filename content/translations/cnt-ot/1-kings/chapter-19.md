## 1 Kings Chapter 19


### Elijah Flees to Sinai


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><a name="_Hlk126253361"><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ahab told Jezebel what Elijah had done, and how he’d killed the prophets with the sword. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She sent a messenger to Elijah, <i>“The gods do so to me and more, if I don’t make your life like theirs by tomorrow at this time.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He fled for his life to Beersheba in Judah and left his attendant there. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He went on a day’s journey into the open country and sat down under a juniper tree, <i>“That’s enough, </i><i>L</i></span><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>. Take my life. I’m no better than my ancestors.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He lay down and slept under the tree, and an angel touched him, <i>“Get up and eat.” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He looked and at his head was a loaf of bread baked on hot stones and a jug of water. He ate and lay back down. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>An angel of the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> came a second time and touched him, <i>“Get up and eat some more, or the trip will be too much for you.” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He ate and on the strength of that food went for 40 days to Horeb/<i>Sinai</i>, the mountain of God.<b>
</b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>1 Kg 19:1-8</span></b></span></p>


### The LORD Speaks to Elijah


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He came upon a cave and stayed there, and a message from the L</span><span style='font-size: 9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> came to him, <i>“What are you doing here, Elijah?” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I’ve been zealous for you because Israel has abandoned your covenant, torn down your altars, and killed your prophets with the sword. I’m the only one left, and they’re trying to kill me.” </span></i></b></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said, <i>“Go out and stand on the mountain in my presence.”</i> The L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> was passing by! A gale was tearing the mountains and breaking the rocks in pieces in the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>’s presence, but the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> wasn’t in the wind. After the wind an earthquake came, but the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> wasn’t in the earthquake. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>After the earthquake, fire <i>came</i>, but the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> wasn’t in the fire. After the fire<i> </i>the sound of a gentle whisper came. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Elijah heard it, he wrapped his face in his mantle, and went out and stood at the entrance to the cave. A voice came to him, <i>“What are you doing here, Elijah?”</i><b>
</b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>1 Kg 19:9-13</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><a name="_Hlk126253509"><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I’ve been zealous for you because Israel has abandoned your covenant, torn down your altars, and killed your prophets with the sword. I’m the only one left, and they’re trying to kill me.”</span></i></b></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> told him, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Go back on the road to the Wilderness of Damascus. When you get there, anoint Hazael king over Aram, </span></i><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>anoint Jehu son of Nimshi king of Israel, and anoint Elisha son of Shaphat from Abel-meholah to succeed you as prophet. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The one that escapes from Hazael’s sword, Jehu will kill, and the one that escapes from Jehu’s sword, Elisha will kill. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll preserve 7,000 in Israel, whose knees haven’t bowed to Baal, whose lips haven’t kissed him.”</span></i></span></p>

<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He left the oxen and ran after Elijah, <i>“Let me kiss my parents. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He went back, took a yoke of oxen, sacrificed them, and boiled their meat with the yokes.
