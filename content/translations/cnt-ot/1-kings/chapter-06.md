## 1 Kings Chapter 6


### Solomon Builds the Temple


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><a name="_Hlk126241154"><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> </a><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In the 480<sup>th</sup> year after the Israelites left Egypt, in the fourth year of Solomon’s reign, the second month (</span></b><b><span style='font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>Ziv</span></b><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>)</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>, he started building the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>’s Temple. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>It was 90 x 30 x 45ft high. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The vestibule across the nave was 30ft long—equal to the Temple’s width—and 15ft deep. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He made recessed frames for the windows. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He also built a three-story structure against the Temple walls, going around the nave and inner sanctuary, and made side rooms around <i>the outside</i>. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The first story was 7½ft wide, the middle one 9ft, and the third 10½ft; because on the outside he made offsets in the wall of the Temple all the way around so the beams didn’t insert into the Temple walls. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>While the Temple was being built, the stones were prepared at the quarry. There wasn’t any sound of hammer, axe, or iron tool <i>on site</i>. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The entry for the lowest side room was on the right side of the Temple. People could go up winding stairs to the middle and third stories. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He built the Temple and finished it with beams and cedar planks </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>in addition to the <i>three </i>stories, each 7½ft high, resting against the Temple wall on cedar timbers.          </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>1 Kg 6:1-10</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A message from the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> came to Solomon, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><a name="_Hlk126241271"><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> </a><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“As to this Temple you’re building, if you follow my statutes, carry out my ordinances, and keep my commandments, I’ll keep the promise with you that I made to your father. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll live among the Israelites and not abandon my people.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Solomon finished building the Temple. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He paneled the inside of the walls with cedar boards from floor to ceiling and overlaid the floor with cypress boards. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Inside the back of the Temple, he separated off the inner sanctuary 30ft deep, the Holy of Holies. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The nave in front of it was 60ft long. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The cedar all over the inside of the Temple was decorated with carved shapes of gourds and open flowers. No stone was showing. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He prepared the inner sanctuary for the Ark of the L</span><span style='font-size: 9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>’s Covenant, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>30 x 30 x 30ft high and overlaid with pure gold. He overlaid the altar with cedar. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He drew gold chains across the front of the inner sanctuary and overlaid the rest of the interior with gold. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He overlaid the whole Temple with gold as well as the altar by the inner sanctuary.<b>         </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>1 Kg 6:11-22</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><a name="_Hlk126241417"><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23-26</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In the inner sanctuary he made 2 identical cherubs out of olive wood, each 15ft tall </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>with wings 7½ft long and their tips 15ft apart. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He placed the cherubs in the middle of the inner room with their wings spread out so the wing of one touched one wall, the wing of the other touched the opposite wall, and their <i>other</i> wings touched in the center of the Temple. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He overlaid them with gold.   </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He carved cherubs, palm trees, and open flowers on the walls of the inner and outer sanctuaries, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and overlaid both floors with gold. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31-32</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The entrance to the inner sanctuary was 2 doors made out of olive wood with a lintel and 5-sided doorposts, with carved cherubs, palm trees, and open flowers on them; and overlaid them with gold. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>For the entrance to the nave, he made 4-sided doorposts out of olive wood </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and 2 doors of cypress wood. Both leaves of both doors folded in on pivots. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He carved cherubs, palm trees, and open flowers on them; and overlaid them with gold evenly applied on the carved work. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He built <i>the wall of </i>the inner court with 3 courses of cut stone capped by a layer of cedar beams.  </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v37" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In <b>the fourth year, the month Ziv,</b> the foundation of the L</span><span style='font-size: 9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>’s Temple was in place. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v38" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In
<b>the eleventh year, eighth month (</b></span><b><span style='font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>Bul</span></b><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>), </span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>it was finished as designed. It took 7 years to build.<b>                                                                  </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>1 Kg 6:23-38</span></b></span></p>
