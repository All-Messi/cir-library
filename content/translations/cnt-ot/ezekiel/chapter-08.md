But you'll see still more detestable things."</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Then he took me to the entrance to the court and showed me a hole in the wall. 8"Dig through the wall." Each one was holding a censer, and the fragrance of the incense was rising in a cloud. 12"See what Israel's elders are doing in the dark in the room of carved images?
