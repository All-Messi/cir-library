## Ezekiel Chapter 26


### A Message for Tyre


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The <b>first day of the month, eleventh year</b>, a message from the L</span><span style='font-size: 9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> came to me:</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Son of man, Tyre has said about Jerusalem, ‘Ha! its gate’s broken; it has swung open to me. I’ll be filled now that it’s laid waste.’ </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But the Lord G</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>OD</span></i><i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> says, ‘I’ll bring nations against you, Tyre, like waves in the sea. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They’ll destroy your walls and tear down your towers. I’ll scrape away the rubble and make you a bare rock, </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>a place for spreading nets by the sea. I’ve spoken: you’ll become spoil for the nations, </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and your inhabitants on the mainland will die by the sword. <u>You’ll know I’m the </u></span></i><i><u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>L</span></u></i><i><u><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></u></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.’”</span></i><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>  </span></b></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“From the north I’ll bring Nebuchadnezzar, king of Babylon, king of kings, with horses, chariots, cavalry, and an enormous army. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’ll slay your inhabitants on the mainland with the sword, build siege walls, put up a mound and a roof of shields against you. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’ll aim his battering rams at your walls and tear down your towers with his axes. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The huge number of his horses will cover you with dust; your walls will shake from his cavalry, wagons, and chariots when they pour through your gates like men overwhelming a breached city. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’ll trample your streets with his horses and slay your people with the sword. He’ll tear down your strong pillars, </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>make spoil out of your wealth, and plunder your merchandise. <span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So I’ll silence your songs and harps. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You’ll be a bare rock, a place to spread nets. You won’t rebuild; I’ve said so.”                                                                                                       </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Ezek 26:7-14</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Lord G</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>OD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> says to Tyre, ‘Won’t the whole coastland quake at the sound of your fall when the wounded groan and the slaughter is taking place in you? </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then the rulers of the sea will step down from their thrones, remove their robes, strip off their embroidered clothes, and dress themselves with cringing. <span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They’ll chant a dirge for you,</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  “</span></sup></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>How you’ve disappeared from the seas, renowned city!</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            You and your residents were powerful seafarers</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  that imposed fear on people everywhere!</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></i><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The islands trembled the day you fell.</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            Yes, the islands in the sea</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  were distressed at your passing.”’”                         </span></i></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Ezek 26:15-18</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Lord says G</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>OD</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>, “When I make you a desolate city, like towns with no residents, when I wash the deep up over you and big rivers flood you, </span></i></span>
