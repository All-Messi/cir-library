## Ezekiel Chapter 25

### A Message for Ammon

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> A message from the LORD came to me:</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <em>“Face</em><em> the Ammonites and prophesy against them, </em><strong><sup>3</sup></strong><em>‘Because</em><em> you said, “Ha!” at my sanctuary when it was profaned, and at Israel when it became a wasteland, and at Judah when they went into exile, </em><strong><sup>4</sup></strong><em>I’m</em><em> going to turn you over to </em><em>the people from the east.
