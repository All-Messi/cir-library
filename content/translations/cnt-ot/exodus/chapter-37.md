Ex 37:10-16 The Lampstand


<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup><strong>17</strong></sup>He formed <u>the lampstand</u> out of pure hammered gold with its base, shaft, cups, bulbs, and flowers as one piece. <strong><sup>18</sup></strong>There</span>
