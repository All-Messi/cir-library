## Exodus Chapter 26

### The Tabernacle

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <em>“Make the </em><em>T</em><em>abernacle with </em><em>10</em><em> curtain</em><em>s</em><em> of fine</em><em>ly twin</em><em>ed linen and blue, purple, and scarlet </em>material<em>. Make them with cherubs, a skilled weaver’s work. </em><strong><sup>2</sup></strong><em>All </em><em>the curtains will be 42 x 6ft. </em><strong><sup>3</sup></strong><em>Make 2 sets of 5 joined curtains. </em><strong><sup>4</sup></strong><em>Make blue loops on the edge of the outer curtain in the </em>first <em>set; do the same with the second set. </em><strong><sup>5</sup></strong><em>Make 50 loops in the one curtain, and 50 loops on the edge of the second set. <span class="verse" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <em>“Make </em><em>5</em><em> </em><em>brace</em><em>s </em><em>out </em><em>of acacia wood for the boards on one side of the </em><em>T</em><em>abernacle, </em><strong><sup>27</sup></strong><em>5 brace</em><em>s for the boards on the other side</em><em>, and 5 braces for the rear, </em><em>west end.</em><em> </em><strong><sup>28</sup></strong><em>The middle brace</em><em> in the center of the boards will go from one end to the other.</em><em> </em><strong><sup>29</sup></strong><em>Overlay the boards with gold and make their gold rings into holders </em><em>for the brace</em><em>s.</em><em> </em><em>Overlay the b</em><em>race</em><em>s with gold too.</em><em> </em><strong><sup>30</sup></strong><em>Build the </em><em>T</em><em>aber</em><em>nacle by the plan I showed you o</em><em>n the mountain.</em><em>”</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ex 26:26-30</p>

<span class="verse" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <em>“Make a </em><em>curtain</em><em> out of blue, purple, and scarlet </em>material<em> and fin</em><em>ely twin</em><em>ed linen.</em><em> Put cherubs on it, a skilled weaver’s work</em><em>.</em><em> </em><strong><sup>32</sup></strong><em>Hang it on four posts of acacia wood overlaid with gold, having gold hooks, and set on four silver sockets. </em><strong><sup>33</sup></strong><em>Hang the curtain under the clasps and bring the Ark of Testimony inside the curtain that separates the Holy Place from the Holy of Holies. </em><strong><sup>34</sup></strong><em>Mount the mercy seat on the Ark. </em><strong><sup>35</sup></strong><em>Outside the curtain, set the table on the north side of the Tabernacle and the lampstand on the south side. </em><strong><sup>36</sup></strong><em>For the doorway of the tent, make a screen out of </em><em>blue, purple, and scarlet material and fine linen, a weaver’s work. </em><strong><sup>37</sup></strong><em>For the screen, make five posts of acacia wood overlaid with gold, having gold hooks, and set on 5 bronze sockets.</span>
