## Exodus Chapter 18


### Jethro Visits Moses


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" ><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jethro/<i>Reuel</i>, priest in Midian, Moses’ father-in-law, heard what all God had done for Moses and his people in bringing them out of Egypt. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’d taken Moses’ wife Zipporah after <i>Moses</i> sent her away </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>with her two sons Gershom (</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>because</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>he said, <i>“I’ve been a ‘foreigner’ in a foreign land”</i></span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>)</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and Eliezer (</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>because he said, <i>“My father’s ‘God helped’ me and delivered me from Pharaoh’s sword”</i></span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>).
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5-6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jethro/<i>Reuel</i> came to Moses with his wife and two sons, where <i>Moses</i> camped at the mountain of God. He sent word that he was bringing them<i>. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Moses</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> went out to meet him, bowed and kissed him. They asked each other how they were doing and went into the tent. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Moses told him what all the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> had done to Pharaoh and the Egyptians for Israel’s sake, the hardships they’d had on the journey, and how the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> had delivered them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jethro/<i>Reuel</i> was overjoyed at the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>’s goodness to Israel. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:10.0pt;margin-left:1.0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Bless the L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> that delivered you from Pharaoh and the Egyptians’ control. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Now I know he’s<sup> </sup>greater than all other gods. He proved it when they dealt arrogantly against </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the people<i>.” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" ><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jethro/</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Reuel</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> brought a burnt offering and sacrifices for God, and Aaron came with the elders to eat a meal with him in God’s presence.                                                                                 </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" ><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The next day Moses sat judging the people. <span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jethro/Reuel</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> saw what he was doing, he asked, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:10.0pt;margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“What are you doing? Why sit </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>to judge</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> so many people by yourself from morning to evening?”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:10.0pt;margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“They come to me to ask God. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When they have a dispute, I decide between them and teach </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>them
</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God’s laws and statues.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:10.0pt;margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That’s not good. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You and the people will both wear out. Your job’s too big. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Here’s some advice and God be with you. Be the people’s representative to God and bring the disputes to him. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then teach them the laws and statutes, how they’re supposed to act and what they’re supposed to do. </span></i></span>

<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Let them judge lesser matters. <span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and chose capable men to lead thousands, hundreds, fifties, and tens. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They judged on a regular basis and dealt with smaller issues. More difficult disputes they took to Moses. </span></span>
