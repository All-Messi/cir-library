## Exodus Chapter 2


### The Birth of Moses


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><a name="_Hlk125539794"><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A descendant from Levi married a woman from Levi, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and she had a son. When she saw he was such a handsome baby, she hid him for three months. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When she couldn’t hide him anymore, she got a papyrus reed basket and covered it with tar. She put him in it and set it in the reeds by the bank of the Nile. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His sister stood a distance away to see what would happen to him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pharaoh’s daughter came down to bathe at the Nile with her attendants. As she walked along the river, she saw the basket in the reeds and had her maid bring it <i>to her</i>. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When she opened it, she saw the baby crying and felt sorry for him, <i>“This is one of the Hebrews’ babies.”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then his sister asked her, <i>“Do you want me to call a Hebrew nurse for you?”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Go ahead.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She went and called his mother. </span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><a name="_Hlk126837234"></a><a name="_Hlk125539962"></a><a name="_Hlk126837278"><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pharaoh’s daughter said, <i>“Nurse him for me, and I’ll pay you.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He grew and she took him to Pharaoh’s daughter, and he became her son. She named him Moses because she ‘pulled him out’ of the water.                                      </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Ex 2:1-10</p>


### Moses Flees from Egypt


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><a name="_Hlk126837338"><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Moses grew up, he went out to his relatives and looked at their hard labor. He saw an Egyptian beating a Hebrew. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He looked around and saw nobody was there; he struck down the Egyptian and hid him in the sand. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He went out the next day, and two Hebrews were fighting. He asked the one that started it, <i>“Why are you hitting your friend?”     </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Who made you a ruler or judge over us? Are you going to kill me like you did that Egyptian?”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Moses was afraid, <i>“Clearly, people know what I did.” </i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> 15</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Pharaoh heard about it, he tried to execute Moses, but he fled to Midian.</span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Ex 2:11-15</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><a name="_Hlk125540441"></a><a name="_Hlk126837421"></a><a name="_Hlk126837456"><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He sat down by a well. </span></a><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The priest in Midian had seven daughters that came to draw water and fill the troughs for their father’s flock. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Some other shepherds came and drove them away, but Moses got up and helped them and watered their flock. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When they went to their father Reuel/<i>Jethro</i>, he asked, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“How did you get back so soon today?”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><a name="_Hlk126837602"><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> </a><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“An Egyptian protected us from the shepherds. He even drew water for the flock.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Where is he? Why did you leave him behind? Invite him to have something to eat.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Moses was willing to live with the man, and he gave him his daughter Zipporah. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She had a son he named Gershom, <i>“Because I’ve been a ‘foreigner’ in a foreign country.”                                                                                    </i></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> Ex 2:16-22</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><a name="_Hlk125540599"><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>After a long time, that king of Egypt died. The Israelites groaned under the slavery. They cried out and their call for help went up to God. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He heard their groaning and remembered his covenant with Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He took notice of them; he knew who they were.</span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></b></span></p>
