<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" ><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10-11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pharaoh</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“Oh, no! <p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" ><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Moses</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> left Pharaoh and prayed to the LORD. </span></span>
