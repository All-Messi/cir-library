## Proverbs Chapter 8

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Wisdom calls out,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> It takes its stand on high places</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> It shouts at the city gates:</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Learn prudence, simple ones;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Listen, so I can tell you nobler things,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> I tell the truth</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Everything I say is good;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> What I say makes sense to anyone that has insight;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Take instruction rather than silver;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Respecting the LORD means hating sin,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> I offer advice about wise living</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> Kings reign by me,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> I walk in the way of goodness,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> I stood beside him like a general contractor,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> Obey instruction in order to be wise;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> Blessed are those who pay attention to what I say,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> The ones that find me find life</span>

<span class="verse" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> But those who miss out on me hurt themselves;</span>
