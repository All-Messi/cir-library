## Proverbs Chapter 9

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Wisdom has built her house; she’s carved its seven columns.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> She’s done her butchering, mixed her wine, and set her table.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> She’s sent her maids to call out from the heights of the city:</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><strong><sup>	</sup></strong><strong><sup>6</sup></strong><em>Abandon</em><em> your foolish ways</em><em> and </em><em>start having a </em><em>life</em><em>;</em> <em>	</em><em>	</em><em>	</em><em>conduct yourself with </em><em>good judgment</em><em>.”</em><strong>	</strong><strong>Prov 9:1-6</strong></span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> If you correct a scoffer, you’ll be abused; if you reprove sinners, you’ll get insulted.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><strong><sup>	</sup></strong><strong><sup>8</sup></strong>So don’t bother reproving scoffers; they’ll hate you; reprove the wise, and they’ll love you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Instruct the wise, and they’ll be wiser still; teach the good, and they’ll be better yet.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Respect for the LORD is basic to wisdom; knowing the Holy One clarifies your thinking.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> It’ll multiply your days and add years to your life.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> If you’re wise, you’re wise for yourself; if you scoff <em>at wisdom</em>, it’ll be to your detriment.<strong>	</strong><strong>Prov 9:7-12</strong></span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> A foolish woman is loud; she’s naïve and ignorant.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><strong><sup>4</sup></strong>She sits in the doorway of her house or takes a seat on the high places in town.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><strong><sup>5</sup></strong>She calls out to those who are going by, who are following straight paths:</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><strong><sup>18</sup></strong>But they don’t know that the dead are there, that her guests lie in Sheol.<strong>	</strong><strong>Prov 9:13-18</strong></span>
