## 2 Chronicles Chapter 3


### Solomon Builds the Temple


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Solomon started building the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>’s Temple on Mount Moriah, where <i>the Lord</i> had appeared to David, the place David prepared on the threshing floor of Ornan/<i>Araunah</i> the Jebusite. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He began construction on the second day, second month, fourth year of his reign</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.
</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The <u>foundation</u> for God’s Temple was 30 x 90ft. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The <u>porch</u> in front of it was 30ft wide and 180ft tall, overlaid with pure gold inside. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He lined the <u>Temple itself</u> with cypress wood overlaid with fine gold and decorated with palm trees and chains;  </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         6</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>adorned the Temple with precious stones—the gold was from Parvaim;  </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         7</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>overlaid the Temple with gold—the beams, thresholds, walls, and doors—and carved cherubs on the walls; </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         8</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>made the <u>Holy of Holies</u>—30ft square, equaling the width of the Temple—and overlaid it with 22 2/3 tons <sup>600 talents</sup> of fine gold—</span><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the spikes weighed 20 oz <sup>50 shekels </sup>of gold; </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      overlaid the
<u>upper rooms</u> with gold.<b>                                                                 
</b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>2 Ch 3:1-9</span></b></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He made 2 <u>sculpted cherubs</u> in the Holy of Holies and overlaid them with gold. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The combined wingspan of the cherubs was 30ft. The wing of one (</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>7<b>½</b>ft</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>) touched the wall of the Temple. The other wing (</span><span style='font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>7<b>½</b>ft</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>) touched the wing of the second cherub. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The wing of the second cherub (</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>7<b>½</b>ft</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>) touched the <i>opposite</i> wall. The other wing (</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>7<b>½</b>ft</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>) was attached to the wing of the first cherub. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They stood on their feet facing the nave.  </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He made <u>a curtain</u> out of violet, purple, crimson, and fine linen, and worked cherubs on it;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>two pillars</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> in front of the Temple 53ft tall, with 7<b>½</b>ft capitals on top of each one; </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.25in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                           16</span></sup></b><u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>chains</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> in the inner sanctuary and on top of the pillars; </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.25in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  <u>100 pomegranates</u> and put them on the chains; </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He set up the pillars on the right and left that he named Jachin and Boaz respectively.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                                                                                                                          2 Ch 3:10-17</span></b></p>
