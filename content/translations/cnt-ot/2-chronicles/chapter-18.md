## 2 Chronicles Chapter 18


### Jehoshaphat and Ahab


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jehoshaphat had great wealth and honor, and allied himself by marriage with Ahab. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Some years later he went down to visit Ahab in Samaria. Ahab slaughtered lots of sheep and oxen for him and the people with him, and induced him to go up against Ramoth-gilead. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He asked Jehoshaphat, <i>“Will you go with me against Ramoth-gilead?”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>He told him, <i>“I’m like you, my people like yours. We’ll go with you into the battle.”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In addition, he said, <i>“First let’s ask for a message from the </i><i>L</i></span><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He gathered the prophets, 400 men, and asked, <i>“Should we attack Ramoth-gilead?”
</i>They said, <i>“Go up. God will deliver it to you.”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But Jehoshaphat asked, <i>“Isn’t there still a prophet of the </i><i>L</i></span><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> here that we can ask?”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ahab</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> told him, <i>“There’s one, Micaiah son of Imla, but I despise the guy; he never prophesies anything good about me.” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>But Jehoshaphat said, <i>“Don’t say that.”</i> <b>                                                              </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>2 Ch 18:1-7</span></b></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The king of Israel called an officer and said, <i>“Quick, bring Micaiah, Imla’s son.”</i> </span></span></p>


### Micaiah’s Prophecy Against Ahab


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The two kings were sitting on their thrones, arrayed in their robes, at the threshing floor by the entrance to the gate of Samaria, and the prophets were prophesying in front of them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Zedekiah son of Chenaanah made iron horns and said, <i>“The </i><i>L</i></span><i><span style='font-size: 9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> says you’ll gore the Arameans with these till they’re consumed.” </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The prophets kept prophesying, <i>“Go up to Ramoth-gilead and prevail. The </i><i>L</i></span><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> will deliver it to you.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The messenger that went to summon Micaiah told him, <i>“The prophets are all speaking favorably for the king; so please say what they’re saying.” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“As sure as the </span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>L</span></i><i><span style='font-size: 9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> lives, I’ll say what my God says.” </span></i><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                 </span></b><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>2 Ch 18:8-13</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When he came, the king said, <i>“Should we attack Ramoth-gilead?”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“Go right on up there! Prevail! The </span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>’s</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> going<i> <u>deliver</u> it to you for sure!!” </i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The king said, <i>“How many times do I have to demand that you not tell me anything but the truth in the </i><i>L</i></span><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>’s name?”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So he said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“I saw Israel scattered on the mountains</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>                  like sheep without a shepherd.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            The </span></i><i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> said, </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>                  ‘They don’t have a master;</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>                  they need to go home in peace.’”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ahab
</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>said,
<i>“Didn’t I tell you he wouldn’t prophesy anything good about me?”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Micaiah said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Listen to the </span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>’s message. I saw him sitting on his throne, and the host of heaven was standing on his right and left. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said, ‘Who will lure Ahab into going up and falling at Ramoth-gilead?’ </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“One said one thing, another said another. </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Then a spirit came forward and said, ‘I’ll do it.’ </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>‘How?’
</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>‘I’ll be a deceiving spirit in his prophets.’ </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> ‘Go do it.’ </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></i><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The
</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> has put a deceiving spirit in your prophets and declared disaster on you.”</span></i><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>     </span></b><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>2 Ch 18:14-22</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then Zedekiah son of Chenaanah came and hit Micaiah on the cheek and said, <i>“How did the Spirit of the </i><i>L</i></span><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> leave me to speak to you?” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“You’ll see when you go hide in an inner room.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The king said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Take Micaiah back to Amon, governor of the city, and to my son Joash. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Tell them to put him in prison and feed him sparingly with bread and water till I come back safely.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Micaiah said, <i>“If you come back safely, the </i><i>L</i></span><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> hasn’t spoken by me. You all need to listen.”</span></i><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>  </span></b><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>2 Ch 18:23-27</span></b></span></p>


### The Death of Ahab


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The king of Israel and Jehoshaphat went on up against Ramoth-gilead. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ahab</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> said to Jehoshaphat, <i>“I’ll disguise myself and go into battle, but you put on your robes.”</i> The king disguised himself, and they went into battle. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The king of Aram had told the captains of his chariots, <i>“Don’t fight anybody great or small, just the king of Israel.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When the captains of the chariots saw Jehoshaphat, they shouted, <i>“It’s the king of Israel!”</i> They turned aside to attack him. But Jehoshaphat cried out, and the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> helped him by diverting them away. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When the captains saw it wasn’t <i>Ahab</i>, they quit chasing him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A man drew his bow at random and struck the king of Israel in a joint in the armor. He told the chariot driver, <i>“Turn around, and get me out of the fight. I’m seriously wounded.” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The battle raged that day, and the king of Israel propped himself up in his chariot in front of the Arameans till evening. He died at sundown.<b>                                               </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>2 Ch 18:28-34</span></b></span></p>
