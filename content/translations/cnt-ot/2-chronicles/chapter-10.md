## 2 Chronicles Chapter 10


### The Northern Tribes Revolt


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then <b>Rehoboam</b> went to Shechem, because Israel had come to Shechem to make him king. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Jeroboam heard about it, he came back. (</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He was in Egypt where he’d fled from Solomon.</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>) </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They sent for him. When Jeroboam and all Israel came, they said to Rehoboam, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Your father made our yoke heavy. Lighten his hard service and the heavy yoke he put on us, and we’ll serve you.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Come back in three days.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The people left.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He consulted the elders that had served his father Solomon, <i>“How do you advise me to answer the people?”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“If you’ll be kind to them, please them, and speak good words to them, they’ll serve you permanently.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But he disregarded their advice and consulted the young men he grew up with and served him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He asked them, <i>“What’s your advice?”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Tell them, ‘My little finger is thicker than my father’s thighs! </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He loaded you with a heavy yoke; I’ll make it heavier. He disciplined you with whips; I’ll discipline you with scorpions.’”</span></i><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></b><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>2 Ch 10:1-11</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jeroboam</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> and the people came to Rehoboam on the third day like he’d directed them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The king answered them harshly and didn’t take the elders’ advice<i>. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“My father made your yoke heavy; I’ll make it heavier. My father disciplined you with whips; I’ll discipline you with scorpions.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He ignored the people, a turn of events the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> allowed with the result that he would bring about his message through Ahijah the Shilonite to Jeroboam.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Israel saw that the king didn’t listen to them, they answered, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            “What part do we have with David?</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  What inheritance do we have in Jesse’s son?</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            Everybody, go home, Israel!”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  Look after your own house, David!”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Israel left and went home. </span><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But Rehoboam ruled the Israelites in the towns in Judah. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He sent Hadoram, who oversaw forced labor, and the Israelites stoned him to death. King Rehoboam hurried and got in his chariot and fled to Jerusalem. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Israel has been in rebellion against David’s dynasty ever since.<b>                                                                                                          </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>2 Ch 10:12-19</span></b></span></p>
