## Isaiah Chapter 60

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Arise, shine; your light has come;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Darkness will cover the earth;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Nations will come to your light,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Then your eyes will be radiant;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Caravans of camels will cover you,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> The flocks of Kedar will gather to you;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Who are these that fly like a cloud,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> The distant lands will learn to trust in me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Your gates will stay open day and night</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Nations that won't serve you will perish.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> The splendor of Lebanon will come on you,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> The descendants of your oppressors will come bowing to you;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> You'll nurse on the milk of nations, breasts of kings;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> I'll bring gold for bronze,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> Violence will disappear from your land,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> You won't need the sun for daylight</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> Your sun won't set anymore;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> Your people will be good;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> The smallest will become a clan;</span>
