## Isaiah Chapter 13

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Oracle About Babylon That Isaiah Son of Amoz Saw</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> I've commanded my consecrated ones</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> There's noise in the mountains like a large army,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> He and his instruments of indignation</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Cry out, because the day of the LORD has come,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Everybody's hands will fall limp;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> They'll panic;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> The day of the LORD is coming—</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> The stars and constellations won't glimmer;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> I'll make people rarer than gold,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> I'll make the sky shake and the earth quake</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> Like hunted gazelles or sheep with no shepherd,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> Anybody that's found will be thrust through;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> Their little ones will be dashed to pieces while they watch;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> Their bows will slaughter the young men;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> Babylon—the glory of the kingdoms, the Babylonians' pride—</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> Nobody will ever live there;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> Desert animals will rest there;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> Hyenas will howl in its fortified towers,</span>
