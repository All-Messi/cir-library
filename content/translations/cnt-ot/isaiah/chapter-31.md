## Isaiah Chapter 31


### Warning to Those Who Rely on Egypt


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpFirst" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><a name="_Hlk86930083"></a><a name="_Hlk123300461"><b><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  31<sup>:1</sup></span></b></a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Misfortune lies ahead for those who go down to Egypt for help,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  who rely on horses,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  that trust in chariots because they are many</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        and in horsemen because they are strong,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  instead of looking to the Holy One of Israel,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  instead of consulting the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>!</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’s wise and brings disaster</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  and doesn’t take back what he says;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  he rises up at the sinful and their helpers.  </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Egyptians are people, not God;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        their horses are flesh, not spirit.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  So the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> will hold out his fist,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        and the one that helps them will stumble,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        the one that’s helped will fall.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  They’ll meet their end together.</span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Is 31:1-3</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> says to me,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>      “Like a young lion growls over its prey</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        and shepherds’ shouts don’t scare it away,</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  I’ll come down</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        to wage war on Mount Zion.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Like a hovering bird I’ll guard Jerusalem,</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  protect and deliver it,</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  spare and rescue it.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Come back to the One you’ve rebelled against, Israel, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and throw away the silver and gold idols you’ve made. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.25in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    <a name="_Hlk123300619">8</a></span></sup></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“The Assyrians will fall by a sword that’s not human; </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.25in;'><b><i><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                             </span></sup></i></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>a sword that’s not human will devour them. </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.25in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        They won’t escape, </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.25in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              and their young soldiers will become slaves. </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.25in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                         </span></i><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Their ‘rock’ will pass away in panic; </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.25in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                             </span></sup></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>their princes will desert the standard,”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.25in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                          the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> says, whose fire is in Zion, </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.25in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                          whose furnace is Jerusalem.<i>                                 </i></span></p>
