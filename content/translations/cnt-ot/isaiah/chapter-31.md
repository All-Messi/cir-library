## Isaiah Chapter 31

### Warning to Those Who Rely on Egypt

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Misfortune lies ahead for those who go down to Egypt for help, who rely on horses, that trust in chariots because they are many and in horsemen because they are strong, instead of looking to the Holy One of Israel, instead of consulting the LORD!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> He’s wise and brings disaster and doesn’t take back what he says; he rises up at the sinful and their helpers.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Egyptians are people, not God; their horses are flesh, not spirit. So the LORD will hold out his fist, and the one that helps them will stumble, the one that’s helped will fall. They’ll meet their end together.</span>
