## Isaiah Chapter 65

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> I let people look for me who weren't asking about me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> All day long I spread out my hands to a rebellious nation</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> I've written it out in front of me:</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> I'll preserve a remnant from Jacob and Judah</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Sharon will be a pasture for flocks;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> So the Lord GOD says,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> The name you leave behind will be a curse to my chosen ones,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> Be happy and celebrate forever in what I create,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> I'll celebrate over Jerusalem</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> No longer will babies live just a few days,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> They'll build houses and live in them,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> They won't build and someone else occupy them;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> They won't work to no end</span>

<span class="verse" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> Before they call, I'll answer;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> Wolf and lamb will graze together;</span>
