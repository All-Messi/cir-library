## Isaiah Chapter 26

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> At that time, they'll sing a song in Judah:</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Open the gates so a good nation can enter,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> You keep steadfast minds in perfect peace</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Trust in the LORD forever</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> He brings down low the ones that live up high</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> The poor and helpless trample it.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> The road for the righteous is not rough;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> We conform our way to your judgments, LORD;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> At night I long for you;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Even if sinners receive grace,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> LORD, you're holding up your fist, but they don't see it;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> You'll establish peace for us,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Other lords have ruled us before,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> Those dead rulers won't live again;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> You've made this nation great</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> We looked to you in distress,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> Like a pregnant woman at delivery time,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> We've been pregnant, writhing in labor,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> But even your dead will live again; their corpses will rise;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> Go home, my people,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> The LORD is coming from heaven</span>
