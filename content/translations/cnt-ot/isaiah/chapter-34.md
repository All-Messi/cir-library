## Isaiah Chapter 34

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Come close, nations, and listen!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> The LORD is furious with the nations and their armies;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Their slain will be thrown out;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> The stars will fade out;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> My sword will be bloody,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Wild oxen will fall with them,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> The LORD will have a day of vengeance,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Edom's wadis will run with pitch;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> It won't go out day or night;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Hawks and hedgehogs will take it over;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> There won't be anyone to call king;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Thorns will grow in its fortresses;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> Desert animals will mix with hyenas;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> Owls will make nests and lay their eggs there;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> Read about them in the Book of the LORD;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> He has surveyed the land</span>
