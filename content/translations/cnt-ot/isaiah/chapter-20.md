## Isaiah Chapter 20


### A Sign Against Egypt and Nubia

<p class="MsoNormal" ><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“My servant Isaiah has gone naked and barefoot for three years as an omen to Egypt and Nubia. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The king of Assyria will lead captives away from there, young and old, naked and barefoot with their butts showing, to Egypt’s humiliation. </span></i></span>
