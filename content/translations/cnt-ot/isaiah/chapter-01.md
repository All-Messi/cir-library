## Isaiah Chapter 1

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Visions Isaiah Son of Amoz Saw</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Listen, earth and sky, the LORD says,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> An ox knows its owner,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Sinful nation,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Where else can he strike you</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> There's nothing healthy in you from head to toe,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Your country lies in ruins;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Zion is like a shed in a deserted vineyard,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> If the LORD of Hosts hadn't left us a few survivors,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Listen to the LORD's message, rulers in "Sodom";</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> When you show up to worship me,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Don't keep bringing me your worthless offerings;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> I despise your new moon celebrations and stated festivals;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> When you spread out your hands, I won't look;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> Learn how to do good, seek out justice,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> If you start obeying me,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> If you refuse and rebel,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> How the once-faithful city has become a prostitute,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> Your once-pure silver has turned to dross;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> Your rulers rebel, your friends steal;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> So the Lord GOD of Hosts,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> I'll turn against you;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> I'll make your judges like they were at first</span>

<span class="verse" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> Justice will redeem Zion;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> But the LORD will crush rebels and sinners;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> You'll be ashamed of the sacred terebinth groves you delighted in,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> The strong will become kindling,</span>
