## Numbers Chapter 30

### Men’s Vows

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Then Moses told the tribal leaders, <em>“This is </em><em>what</em><em> the </em><em>L</em><em>ORD</em><em> has commanded.</em><em> </em><strong><sup>2</sup></strong><em>If a man makes a vow to the </em><em>L</em><em>ORD</em><em> or takes an oath that obligates him to do something, he must keep his word. </em> 	<strong>Women’s Vows</strong></span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <em>If a woman takes a vow to the </em><em>L</em><em>ORD</em><em> and obligates herself to do something while in her father’s family in her youth, </em><strong><sup>4</sup></strong><em>and her father hears it and doesn’t object, then her vow or pledge stands. </em><strong><sup>5</sup></strong><em>But if her father forbids her, her vow or pledge doesn’t stand. The </em><em>L</em><em>ORD</em><em> will release her </em>from them<em> because her father forbade her.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <em>“But, if she marries while under her vow or rash statement </em><strong><sup>7</sup></strong><em>and her husband hears about it and doesn’t object, then her vow or pledge stands. </em><strong><sup>8</sup></strong><em>But, if he forbids her, he annuls her vow or rash statement, and the </em><em>L</em><em>ORD</em><em> will release her </em>from it.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <em>“The vow of a widow or divorced woman stands.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Num 30:1-9</p>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <em>“However, if she vows in her husband’s house or binds herself to an obligation with an oath </em><strong><sup>11</sup></strong><em>and her husband hears it, but doesn’t object, then her vow or pledge stands. </em><strong><sup>12</sup></strong><em>But if her husband annuls it, it won’t stand, and the </em><em>L</em><em>ORD</em><em> will release her from them.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <em>“A husband can annul any vow or binding oath </em>a wife<em> submits herself to. </em><strong><sup>14</sup></strong><em>If he doesn’t say anything to her the day he hears it, then he confirms it. </em><strong><sup>15</sup></strong><em>But if he tries to annul it later, he’ll bear her guilt.”</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> Those statutes the LORD commanded Moses about a husband and wife and a father and daughter in her youth in his home.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Num 30:10-16</p>
