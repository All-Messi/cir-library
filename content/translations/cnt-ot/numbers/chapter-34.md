## Numbers Chapter 34

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> The LORD told Moses to say to Israel, "When you go into Canaan, 3your south border will run from the Wilderness of Zin alongside Edom from the end of the Dead Sea eastward. 4Your border will turn from the south to the ascent of Akrabbim and go on along to Zin. It will end south of Kadesh-barnea, go out to Hazaraddar, and on along to Azmon. 5The border will turn from Azmon to where the River of Egypt empties into the Mediterranean Sea. 7"Your north border will stretch from the Mediterranean Sea to Mount Hor, 8Lebo-hamath, Zedad, 9Ziphron, and end at Hazar-enan. 10"Your east border will run from Hazar-enan to Shepham 11down to Riblah on the east of Ain down to the slopes east of the Sea of Chinnereth. 12It will go down to the Jordan and on down to the Dead Sea."</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Moses told Israel, "That's the land you'll inherit by lot, land the LORD told you to give to the-nine-and-a-half tribes. 14-15Reuben, Gad, and the Manasseh half-tribe have received their inheritance 15beyond the Jordan."</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> The LORD told Moses, 17"Eleazar the priest and Joshua son of Nun will divide up the land. 18Name one leader from each tribe to divide it up:</span>

<span class="verse" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> The LORD commissioned those men to divide up Canaan for Israel.</span>
