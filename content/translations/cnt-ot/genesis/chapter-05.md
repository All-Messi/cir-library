## Genesis Chapter 5

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Adam’s Descendants God created people to be like himself. <strong><sup>2</sup></strong>He made them male and female, blessed them, and called them human.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><strong><sup>3</sup></strong>Adam lived 130 years and had a son in his own image like himself. He named him Seth. <strong><sup>4</sup></strong>He lived 800 years after that and had sons and daughters. <strong><sup>5</sup></strong>Altogether he lived 930 years and died.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Seth lived 105 years and had Enosh. <strong><sup>7</sup></strong>He lived 807 years after that and had sons and daughters. <strong><sup>8</sup></strong>Altogether he lived 912 years and died.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Gen 5:1-8</p>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><strong><sup>9</sup></strong>Enosh lived 90 years and had Kenan. <strong><sup>10</sup></strong>He lived 815 years after that and had sons and daughters. <strong><sup>11</sup></strong>Altogether he lived 905 years and died.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Kenan lived 70 years and had Mahalalel. <strong><sup>13</sup></strong>He lived 840 years after that and had sons and daughters. <strong><sup>14</sup></strong>Altogether he lived 910 years and died.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> Mahalalel lived 65 years and had Jared. <strong><sup>16</sup></strong>He lived 830 years after that and had sons and daughters. <strong><sup>17</sup></strong>Altogether he lived 895 years and died.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> Jared lived 162 years and had Enoch. <strong><sup>19</sup></strong>He lived 800 years after that and had sons and daughters. <strong><sup>20</sup></strong>Altogether he lived 962 years and died.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Gen 5:9-20</p>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> Enoch lived 65 years and had Methuselah. <strong><sup>22</sup></strong>Then Enoch walked with God 300 years after that and had sons and daughters. <strong><sup>32</sup></strong>Altogether he lived 365 years. <strong><sup>24</sup></strong>He walked with God and wasn’t there because God took him.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> Methuselah lived 187 years and had Lamech. <strong><sup>26</sup></strong>He lived 782 years after that and had sons and daughters. <strong><sup>27</sup></strong>Altogether he lived 969 years and died.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> Lamech lived 182 years and had a son. <strong><sup>29</sup></strong>He named him “Noah,” <em>“He’ll ‘relieve’ us</em><em> </em><em>of </em><em>our labor from the ground the </em><em>L</em><em>ORD</em><em> cursed.”</em> <strong><sup>30</sup></strong>Lamech lived 595 years after that and had sons and daughters. <strong><sup>31</sup></strong>Altogether he lived 775 years and died.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> Noah was 500 years old and had Shem, Ham, and Japheth.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Gen 5:21-32</p>
