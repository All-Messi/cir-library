## Genesis Chapter 35


### Jacob Returns to Bethel


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><a name="_Hlk125371220"><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God told Jacob, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Go up to Bethel and live and build an altar to me. </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’m the One</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> that appeared to you when you were running from Esau.” </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jacob told his household and everybody with him, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Put away your foreign gods, purify yourselves, and change clothes. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Let’s go to Bethel. I’m going to build an altar to God there, who answered me in my distress and was with me wherever I’ve gone.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They gave Jacob their foreign gods and earrings, and he buried them under a terebinth tree near Shechem. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As they moved along, a deep dread hovered over the towns around them, and they didn’t pursue Jacob’s sons. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So Jacob and his people were able to reach Luz (</span><span style='font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>Bethel</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>) in Canaan. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He built an altar, and called the place El-Bethel, because “God” revealed himself to him there when he was running from his brother. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Deborah, Rebekah’s nurse, died, and </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jacob</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> buried her below Bethel under the oak named Allonbacuth</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[Oak of Weeping].                                                                                                                                             </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 35:1-8</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><a name="_Hlk125371383"><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God appeared to Jacob again after he came from Paddan-aram and blessed him. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“You’re Jacob, </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        but you won’t be called Jacob anymore.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  Your name will be Israel.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’m God Almighty </span></i><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[El-Shaddai].</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    Have children and multiply.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    A group of nations will come from you;</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    kings will descend from you.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span></i><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll give you and your descendants </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:1.0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the land I </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>promised </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Abraham and Isaac.”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God ascended from him, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and Jacob set up a stone marker “where God” had spoken to him. He poured out a liquid offering on it, poured olive oil on it, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and named the place Bethel.</span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> Gen 35:9-15</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>  </span></b></span></p>

<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The midwife said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Don’t be afraid; you have another son.” </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As her life was draining away, she named him Benoni; but his father called him Benjamin.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Rachel died and was buried along the road to Ephrath. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jacob set up a marker over her grave that’s still there. </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>While he was living there, Reuben went and slept with Bilhah, his father’s concubine, and Israel found out about it.                                                                                           </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>               </span><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>by Leah: Reuben, his firstborn, Simeon, Levi, Judah, Issachar, Zebulun;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>
</span><span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>by Rachel: Joseph and Benjamin;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>
</span><span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>by Bilhah, Rachel’s maid: Dan and Naphtali; and</span></span></p>

</span><span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>by Zilpah, Leah’s maid: Gad and Asher.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jacob went to see his father Isaac at Mamre near Kiriath-arba (</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Hebron</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>), where Abraham and Isaac had lived temporarily. </span></span>
