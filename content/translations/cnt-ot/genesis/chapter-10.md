## Genesis Chapter 10


### The Table of Nations


<p class="MsoNormal" align=center style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-top:0in;text-align:center'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Generations of <u>Shem</u>, <u>Ham</u>, and <u>Japheth</u>, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" align=center style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-top:0in;text-align:center'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Noah’s Sons and Their Descendants</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><a name="_Hlk125446996"><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> </a><u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Japheth</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> was the father of <u>Gomer</u>, Magog, Madai, <u>Javan</u>, Tubal, Meshech, and Tiras.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Gomer</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> was the father of Ashkenaz, Riphath, and Togarmah. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Javan</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> was the father of Elishah, Tarshish, Kittim, and Dodanim. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Those descendants spread inland from the coasts into their nations, each by language and clan.   </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 10:1-5</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:4.5pt;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>          </span><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ham</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> was the father of <u>Cush</u>, <u>Mizraim</u>, Put, and <u>Canaan</u>.  </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:.5in;'><b><u><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>7</span></sup></u></b><u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Cush</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> was the father of Seba, Havilah, Sabtah, <u>Raamah</u>, and Sabteca. </span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Raamah</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> was the father of Sheba and Dedan.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:1.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Cush was also the father of Nimrod, who became prominent in the world, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>a great hunter before the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>. It became an expression, <i>“Like Nimrod, a great hunter before the L</i></span><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.”
</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His kingdom stretched across Babel, Erech, Accad, and Calneh in Shinar. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>From that land he went to Assyria and built Nineveh, Rehoboth-Ir, Calah, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and Resen, a big city between Nineveh and Calah.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:1.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Mizraim</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> became the father of the Ludim, Anamim, Lehabim, Naphtuhim, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pathrusim, Casluhim (</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ancestors of the Philistines</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>), and Caphtorim.      </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 10:6-14</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:1.5in;'><a name="_Hlk125447092"><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> </a><u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Canaan</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> became the father of Sidon, <i>his firstborn</i>, Heth, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the Jebusites, Amorites, Girgashites, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Hivites, Arkites, Simites, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Arvadites, Zemarites, Hamathites. Afterwards the Canaanite clans spread out </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>from Sidon—on the way to Gera—as far as Gaza across to Sodom, Gomorrah, Admah, and Zeboiim as far as Lasha. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Those were Ham’s descendants by clan, language, territory, and nation.        </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 10:15-20</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:1.5in;'><a name="_Hlk125447141"><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> </a><u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Shem</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>, Japheth’s older brother, became the ancestor of Eber’s descendants. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Shem’s sons were Elam, Asshur, <u>Arpachshad</u>, Lud, and <u>Aram</u>. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Aram</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>’s sons were Uz, Hul, Gether, and Mash.  </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Arpachshad</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> became the father of <u>Shelah</u>, father of <u>Eber</u>. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Eber</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> had two sons. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:2.0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>One <i>was</i> <u>Peleg</u>; in his time the earth divided. <u>Joktan</u> was <i>his </i>brother, </span><span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and father of Almodad, Sheleph, Hazarmaveth, Jerah, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Hadoram, Uzal, Diklah, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Obal, Abimael, Sheba, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ophir, Havilah, and Jobab. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They lived from Mesha all the way to Sephar in the eastern hill country. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Those were Shem’s descendants by clan, language, territory, and nation. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Those were the clans of Noah’s descendants by genealogy, language, and nation.
