## Genesis Chapter 37

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><a name="_Hlk125368497"><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jacob lived in Canaan, where his father had lived as a nomad. </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 36:31-37:1</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" align=center style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-top:0in;text-align:center'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jacob’s Dreams</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Joseph was still a youth of 17, he was pasturing the flock with his half-brothers, including the sons of Bilhah and Zilpah, his father’s wives. He brought his father a bad report about what they were doing. </span><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Now Israel loved Joseph more than his other sons because he was born when Israel was old. He made him a tunic with many colors in it. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His brothers saw that their father loved him more than them, so they despised him and wouldn’t speak to him in a civil way. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joseph had a dream. When he told it to his brothers, they despised him more, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Let me tell you a dream I had. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We were tying up bundles in the field. My bundle stood up straight and yours gathered around and bowed to mine.” </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His brothers asked, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“You’re think you’re going to reign over us? You’re going to rule us?”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> They hated him even more for his dreams and what he said.  </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told his brothers another dream he had, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“The sun, moon, and 11 stars were bowing down to me.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told his father too, who scolded him, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“What’s a dream like that mean? Are your brothers, your mother, and I really going to bow to you?”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His brothers resented him, but his father wondered what it might mean</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.
                                                                                        </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 37:2-11</p>

<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Israel told Joseph, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Your brothers are pasturing at Shechem? Go join them.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told him to bring him word how they and the flock were doing</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.
</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He sent him from the Valley of Hebron to Shechem. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A fellow found him walking in the countryside and asked him, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“What are you looking for?”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“My brothers. Where are they pasturing </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the flock</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>?”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“They’ve moved on from here. I heard them say they were going to Dothan.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joseph trailed them to Dothan.                                                                            </span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 37:12-17</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When they saw him in the distance, they said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><a name="_Hlk125369616"><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> </a><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Here comes the dreamer! </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Let’s kill him and throw him into one of the cisterns. We can say a wild animal ate him. Then let’s see what comes of his dreams!” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But Reuben heard about their scheme and came to his rescue, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Let’s not kill him. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t shed blood. <span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Joseph got to his brothers, they stripped off his multi-colored robe </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and put him in an empty cistern.                                        </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 37:18-24</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They sat down to eat. When they looked up, a caravan of Ishmaelite </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>traders</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> was coming from Gilead with camels carrying aromatic gum, salve, and myrrh—headed for Egypt. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Judah said to his brothers, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“What’s in it for us if we kill our brother and cover up his blood? </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Let’s sell him to the Ishmaelites, not hurt him. He’s our brother, our own flesh.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> They agreed. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then some of the Midianite traders (Ishmaelites) went by; they pulled him out of the cistern and sold him to them for 20 </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>pieces</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> of silver. They took him to Egypt.           </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 37:25-28</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Reuben went back to the cistern, and Joseph wasn’t in it. He tore his clothes! </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He went to his brothers, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“The boy’s not there. What am I going to do?”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They took Joseph’s tunic and slaughtered a male goat. They dipped the robe in its blood </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and took it to their father,  </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“We found this. See if it’s your son’s robe.”</span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  33</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He examined it, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“It’s his. A wild animal has torn him to pieces and eaten him!”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jacob tore his clothes and put burlap around his waist and mourned for him many days.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His sons and daughters tried to comfort him, but he refused to be comforted, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I’ll go down to Sheol mourning for him.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> His father cried over him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Meanwhile, the Midianites sold him in Egypt to Potiphar, an officer of Pharaoh, captain of the bodyguard.
