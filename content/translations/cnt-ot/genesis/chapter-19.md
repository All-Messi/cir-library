## Genesis Chapter 19


### The Destruction of Sodom and Gomorrah

</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>in Sodom’s gate. When he saw <i>them</i>, he got up to meet them and bowed to the ground. </span></span>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“No, we’ll spend the night in the square.”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></i><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But he urged them strongly, so they went to his house. <span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They called out to Lot, <i>“Where are the men that came tonight? Bring them out so we can rape them.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But Lot went out and shut the door behind him, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Brothers, don’t be wicked. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Look, I have two daughters that haven’t slept with men. <p class="section-ref">Gen 19:1-8</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><a name="_Hlk125452216"><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> </a><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Get out of the way.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> Besides, they said, <i>“This foreigner has come in already acting like a judge. <span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But the angels reached out and pulled him in and shut the door. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They struck the men blind, big and small; so they wore themselves out <i>groping around </i>for the door.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The
<i>two</i> angels said to Lot, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Who else do you have here?                                     </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 19:9-13</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></i><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Lot went out to tell his daughters’ fiancés, <i>“Get up! <span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When it started to get daylight, the angels urged Lot, <i>“Get up! <span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He hesitated, so the angels grabbed his hand and his wife’s and daughters’ hands. The L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> had mercy on him and took him outside the city.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><a name="_Hlk125452595"><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Lot said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“No, Sir! </span></i><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’m pleasing to you, and you’ve shown me great kindness by saving me; but I can’t escape to the mountains. The disaster would overtake me, and I’d die. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That town is near enough to run to. </span></i><i><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Isn’t it small?</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> Let me escape there to save my life.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“All right, I won’t overthrow it. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Hurry and get on over there! <p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sun had climbed high by the time Lot got to Zoar. </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He overthrew those towns, the whole valley, its people, and what grew there. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But
<i>Lot’s</i> wife, behind him, looked <i>back</i>, and became a pillar of salt.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Abraham got up early <i>and went</i> to where he’d stood with the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He looked down toward Sodom, Gomorrah, and the valley, and saw smoke coming up as from a furnace. </span></span>

### Lot and His Daughters


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><a name="_Hlk125452737"><span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Lot went up from Zoar and stayed in the mountains with his two daughters. He was afraid to keep staying in Zoar. They were living in a cave. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The older daughter told the younger one, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“There’s not a man around to come into us in the normal way. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Let’s get our father drunk on wine and lie with him so we can preserve our family through him.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They got him drunk that night, and the older one went in and lay with him. <span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The next day the older one said, <i>“Last night I lay with my father. <span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So both daughters got pregnant from their father. </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v38" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The younger <i>daughter</i> had a boy too and named him Ben<i>-</i>ammi (ancestor of the Ammonites).                                                     </span></span></p>
