## Genesis Chapter 45


### Joseph Reveals Himself to His Brothers


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joseph couldn’t control himself in front of everybody that was standing there. He cried out, <i>“Everybody out!”</i> Nobody was with him when he introduced himself to his brothers. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He cried so loud the Egyptians heard it, and Pharaoh’s household heard <i>about it</i>.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told his brothers, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I’m Joseph. Is my father still alive?” </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They were so overwhelmed by his presence they couldn’t answer him. </span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then he said, <i>“Come closer.” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I’m Joseph that you sold into Egypt. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t grieve or be angry with yourselves. God sent me ahead of you to save you. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The famine has been in the land for two years. There won’t be any plowing or harvesting for five more years. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God sent me ahead to preserve you as a remnant here and keep you alive by a great rescue.
</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>It wasn’t so much you as God. He’s made me like a father to Pharaoh, manager over his household and ruler over Egypt. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Hurry back and tell my father his son Joseph says, ‘God has made me lord over Egypt. Come down to me right away. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You’ll live in the territory of Goshen near me—you, your children, grandchildren, flocks and herds, everything you own. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll take care of you there so your household and possessions won’t perish. The famine’s going to last five more years.’ </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You and my brother Benjamin can see I’m telling you this directly. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Tell my father about my splendor in Egypt and everything you’ve seen. Hurry and bring him down here.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><a name="_Hlk125361757"><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then he embraced Benjamin and cried, and Benjamin cried and embraced Joseph. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joseph kissed his brothers and cried on them. Afterward they talked.                                                                                                                                </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 45:1-15</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Pharaoh’s household heard that Joseph’s brothers had come, they were happy. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pharaoh said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Tell them to load their animals and go to Canaan, </span></i><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>get their father and households, and come to me. I’ll give them the best land in Egypt. They’ll eat off the rich land. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Have them take wagons for their wives and little ones, and bring their father. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Tell them not to worry about their personal belongings; they’ll have the best in Egypt.’” </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 45:16-20</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joseph gave them wagons and supplies for their trip. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He gave each of them a change of clothes, but he gave Benjamin 300 <i>pieces of </i>silver and 5 changes of clothes. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He sent his father 10 donkeys loaded with the best things Egypt had <i>to offer</i> and 10 female donkeys loaded with grain, bread, and provisions for his father’s trip back. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He sent them off, <i>“Don’t quarrel on the trip.” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They went up to Canaan </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and told their father, <i>“Joseph’s still alive! He’s actually the ruler of Egypt!”</i> He was stunned! He couldn’t believe it! </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When they told him what Joseph had said and he saw the wagons he sent to take him back, he revived. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Joseph must still be alive! I’ll go see him before I die.”                       </span></i></span></span></p>
