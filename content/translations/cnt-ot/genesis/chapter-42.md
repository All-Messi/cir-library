## Genesis Chapter 42

<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When he recognized them, he disguised himself and was gruff </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>with them</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>,
</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Where are you from?”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Canaan;
</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>we came </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>to buy food.”</span></i></p>

<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He remembered his dreams about them, and said, </span><i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“You’re spies. <p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></i><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“No, my lord; we’ve come to buy food. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We’re honest men, not spies.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><a name="_Hlk125364465"><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> </a><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“The truth is, you’re spies.</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They said to one another, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“We’re guilty about our brother. <p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Reuben answered, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“Didn’t I tell you not to sin against the boy? <p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When they got to Canaan, they told their father what had happened, </span></span></p>
