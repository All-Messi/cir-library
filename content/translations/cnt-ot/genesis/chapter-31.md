<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then God’s angel said in the dream, ‘Jacob,’ and I said, ‘Yes?’</span></i><b><i><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></sup></i></b></span>

<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’m the God in Bethel, where you anointed a marker and took a vow to me.
