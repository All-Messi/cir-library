## Genesis Chapter 33


### Jacob Meets Esau


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><a name="_Hlk125374643"><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jacob looked up and saw Esau coming with his 400 men. He divided the children between Leah, Rachel, and the two maids. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He put the maids and their children in front, Leah and her children next, Rachel and Joseph last. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He went on ahead of them and bowed to the ground seven times as he got closer to his brother. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then Esau ran to meet him, embraced him, hugged him, and kissed him. They cried <i>together</i>.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Esau</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> looked up and saw the women and children, <i>“Who are these?”                                                            </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Children God has graciously given me.”                                                             </span></i></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 33:1-5</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The maids came with their children and bowed. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Leah came up with her children and bowed. Last, Joseph and Rachel came and bowed.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Esau</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> asked, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“What do you mean by all this?”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            Jacob</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“To please you.”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But Esau said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“I have plenty, brother. Keep what you have.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“No, if I’m pleasing to you, accept my present; I look up to you like someone looks up to God, and you’ve received me graciously. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Take the gift I’ve brought you; God has been generous with me; I have plenty.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He kept urging him, so he took it.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></i><a name="_Hlk125374880"><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Esau said,</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> “Let’s travel on. I’ll go ahead of you.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But he told him, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“You know the children are young, and I’m concerned about the flocks and herds that are nursing. If they’re driven hard for a day, they’ll die. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Go on ahead. I’ll go at the pace of the livestock and children till I come to you in Seir.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Let me leave some of my people.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“What’s the need? Be gracious to me</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> on this</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.”         </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Esau went back that day to Seir. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jacob traveled to Succoth, built a house, and made “corrals” for his livestock; so the name of the place became Succoth.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jacob went safely to Shechem in Canaan and camped by the city. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The piece of land where he’d pitched his tent, he bought from the sons of Hamor, Shechem’s father, for 100 pieces of silver. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He built an altar and called it El</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>-</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Elohe</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>-</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Israel</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[El the God of Israel].       </span></span></p>
