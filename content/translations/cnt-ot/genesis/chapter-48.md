## Genesis Chapter 48


### Jacob Blesses Ephraim and Manasseh


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><a name="_Hlk125320371"><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>After this, they told Joseph, <i>“Your father is failing.”</i> So he took his sons Manasseh and Ephraim with him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When they told Jacob, <i>“Joseph’s here,” </i>Israel gathered his strength and sat up in bed. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told Joseph,   </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“God Almighty [</span></i><i><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>El-Shaddai</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>] appeared to me in Luz and blessed me: </span></i><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>‘I’ll make you countless, a group of nations. I’ll give your descendants this land as a permanent possession.’ </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Your two sons born in Egypt before I came will be considered mine alongside Reuben and Simeon. (</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Your later offspring will be yours</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.) They’ll inherit with their brothers. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When I was coming from Paddan, sadly, Rachel died in Canaan on the way before we got to Ephrath (</span></i><i><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Bethlehem</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>). I buried her there.”                                                                                                                          </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 48:1-7</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Israel saw Joseph’s sons, he asked, <i>“Who are these?”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“They’re the sons God gave me here.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Bring them to me so I can bless them.”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><a name="_Hlk125320527"><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Israel’s sight was so weak from age he could hardly see. Joseph brought them, and he kissed them and hugged them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told Joseph, <i>“I never expected to see you, and God has let me see your children too!” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joseph took them from his knees and bowed to the ground. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joseph took Ephraim with his right hand toward Israel’s left, and Manasseh with his left hand toward Israel’s right. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But Israel put his right hand on Ephraim’s head, crossing his hands, even though Manasseh was the firstborn. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He blessed Joseph, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“God in whose presence my forefathers Abraham and Isaac lived, </i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        God that’s been my shepherd my whole life,</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    </span></i><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the angel that redeemed me from every misfortune,</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    bless the boys.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        May I and my forefathers live on in them,</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        and they become a large number.”                                               </span></i></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 48:8-16</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><a name="_Hlk125320567"><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Joseph saw his father put his right hand on Ephraim’s head, it displeased him. He took hold of his father’s hand to move it to Manasseh’s head, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Not that way, father. This is the firstborn. Put your hand on his head.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></i><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But his father refused, <i>“I know, son; I know. He’ll become numerous too. But his younger brother will be more numerous. Many nations will descend from him.” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He blessed them that day, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        “By you Israel will pronounce blessing and say, </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        ‘May God make you like Ephraim and Manasseh!’”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He put Ephraim ahead of Manasseh.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then he told Joseph, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I’m about to die, but God will be with you and take you back to where your ancestors lived. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’m giving you one portion more than your brothers, which I took from the Amorite with my sword and bow.”  </span></i></span></span></p>
