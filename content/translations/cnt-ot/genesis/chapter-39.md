<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But he refused, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She hounded Joseph day after day, but he wouldn’t give in to her. </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then she told him, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“The Hebrew slave you brought us came in to abuse me. </span></i></span>

</span></i></span></span></p>
