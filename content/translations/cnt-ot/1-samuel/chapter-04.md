<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They fought and defeated Israel, and every man fled home. ### The Death of Eli


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A man from Benjamin ran to Shiloh the same day with his clothes torn and dust on his head.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Eli was sitting on his seat by the road watching because he was worried about the Ark. <span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Eli heard the outcry and asked what it was about</span><i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>. </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The man hurried over to tell him. </span></span>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The man told him, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I fled from the battle today.”   </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When he mentioned the Ark, <b>Eli</b> fell backward off his seat by the gate, broke his neck, and died.
