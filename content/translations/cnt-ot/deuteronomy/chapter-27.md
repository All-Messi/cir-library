## Deuteronomy Chapter 27

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Then Moses and the elders in Israel charged the people, "Keep the commandments I'm giving you. 2When you cross the Jordan, set up large stones coated with lime. 3Write this Law on them clearly so you can go to the promised land that's flowing with milk and honey. 4Set up these stones on Mount Ebal. 5Build an altar there out of uncut stones—don't use any iron tools on them. 6Offer a burnt offering to the LORD on it, 7and sacrifice peace offerings. Eat there and celebrate in his presence." [ 8]</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Then Moses and the Levitical priests told Israel, "Be still and listen. Today you've become a people for the LORD your God; 10so obey him."</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Moses charged them, 12"When you cross the Jordan, Simeon, Levi, Judah, Issachar, Joseph, and Benjamin will stand on Mount Gerizim to bless the people. 13Reuben, Gad, Asher, Zebulun, Dan, and Naphtali will stand on Mount Ebal to pronounce a curse. 14With a loud voice the Levites will answer the people of Israel, 15'Accursed is anyone that makes an idol or molten image—a thing detestable to the LORD, the work of a craftsman—and sets it up secretly.'</span>
