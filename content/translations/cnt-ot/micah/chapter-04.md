## Micah Chapter 4


### The Mountain of the LORD


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In the last days,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      the mountain the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>’s Temple’s on</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  will be the tallest mountain.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>It will loom above the hills,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and the people from many nations will stream to it.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They’ll say,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      <i>“Let’s go up to the L</i></span><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>’s mountain,</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  to the Temple of Jacob’s God,</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  so he can teach us his ways,</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  and we can follow in his paths.”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Law will go out from Zion,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>’s message from Jerusalem.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’ll mediate between many nations</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and settle disputes between powerful countries far away.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They’ll hammer their swords into plowshares</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and their spears into pruning hooks.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Nation won’t attack nation </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      or train for war anymore.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Everyone will sit under their own vine and fig tree,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and nobody will make them afraid,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> has said so.</span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Mic 4:1-4</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><a name="_Hlk122904133"><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The nations conduct themselves</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      in the name of their gods;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      <u>we</u> will follow the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> our God forever.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  and the exiles a powerful nation.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  and agony seized you as in childbirth?</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            and go to Babylon.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  and redeem you from your enemies there.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Now many nations have gathered against you, saying, </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Get up and thresh, Zion.</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      I’ll give you iron horns and bronze hoofs</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  so you can pulverize many nations</span></i></p>
