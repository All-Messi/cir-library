## Micah Chapter 2


### Judgment Against Wealthy Oppressors


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Misfortune awaits those who devise sin,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  who lie awake plotting schemes in bed!</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      In the morning they do them</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  because they can.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They covet fields and seize them;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  <i>they desire </i>horses and take them.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      They rob people </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  and confiscate the property they’ve inherited.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><a name="_Hlk122903492"><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> says,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      <i>“I’m devising a catastrophe for this family;</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  you won’t get your necks out of the noose.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      You won’t keep carrying on arrogantly;</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  it’ll be a terrible time for you.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Your enemies<i> will taunt you with a proverb </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and wail a bitter dirge </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>in mockery<i>,</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  ‘We’re ruined!</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God
<i>is reapportioning our things.</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  He’s taking them away from us</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  and giving our fields to apostates!’</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So you won’t have anyone stretching out a surveying line</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      for you by lot in the L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>’s assembly.</span></i></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Mic 2:1-5</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><a name="_Hlk122903661"><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> </a><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“‘Don’t preach about such things,’ they say;</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      ‘disgrace won’t catch up with us.’</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Are you </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>really
<i>saying such a thing, House of Jacob?     </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      Isn’t the L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>’s Spirit patient?</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      Does he do such things?</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      Don’t his words do good </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  for those who do right?</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>My people rise up against my people;</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>they strip robes off the peaceful,</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      off of people that go along innocently</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  with no thought of fighting. </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They evict my people’s women from their pleasant homes</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and forever strip away from their children </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  the splendor I would give them.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Get up and get out of here!</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      this is no longer a place to rest;</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  there’s sinfulness here </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  that brings painful destruction.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If a liar full of hot air tells you </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      he’ll preach about the fun of wine and alcohol,</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      he’ll certainly get a hearing from you!</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><a name="_Hlk122903716"><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> </a><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I’ll assemble you, Jacob, remnant of Israel,</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  and put you together like sheep in a fold;</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      like a flock in its pasture,</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  you’ll be a noisy crowd in your land.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The One that opens the gate will lead you out;</span></i></span></p>
