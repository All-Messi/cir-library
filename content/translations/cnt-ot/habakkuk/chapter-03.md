## Habakkuk Chapter 3

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> A Prayer of Habakkuk</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> LORD, I've heard about you and stand in awe.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> God is coming from Teman,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> His brightness shines like sunshine;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Disease goes ahead of him;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> He stops and looks the world over;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> I can see the tents in Nubia in distress;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Did you rage against the rivers?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> No. You took the cover off your bow</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> The mountains watched you and shook</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> The sun and moon stood still;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> In your fury you marched across the world;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> You headed out to save your people,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> You thrust through the leaders of their troops</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> But with your horses you tramped down the sea of soldiers,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> I heard it and my body trembled;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> Though the fir tree hasn't budded,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> I'll still rejoice in the LORD</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> The Lord GOD is my strength;</span>
