## Joel Chapter 2

### The Day of the LORD

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Blow a bugle in Zion; sound an alarm on my holy mountain! Let the people in the land tremble because the day of the LORD is coming. It’s near,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Fire consumes ahead of them; flames burn behind them. The land is like the Orchard of Eden in front of them, but desolation <em>lies </em>behind them; nothing escapes.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> They don’t bump into one another; each one marches straight ahead. When they break through the defenses, they just keep going.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> They rush at the city and run along its walls; they climb into houses through windows like thieves.</span>
