## Joel Chapter 1

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> A Message from the LORD to Joel Son of Pethuel</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Listen to this, elders;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Tell your descendants about it;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> What the cutting locusts left</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Wake up, you drunks, and cry;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> A nation has invaded my land,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> It's destroyed my grapevines</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Wail like a virgin wearing burlap</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> The grain offerings and liquid offerings have stopped</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> The fields are ruined, the land is bare</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Despair, farmers; wail, vinedressers,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> The grapevines have shriveled up, the fig trees have withered;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Put on burlap and chant a dirge, priests;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> Consecrate a fast, declare a solemn assembly;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> Dread the day!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> Hasn't food disappeared in front of us,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> Seeds shrivel under the clods;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> The cattle are bawling!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> I'm calling out to you, LORD,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> Even the wild animals pant for you</span>
