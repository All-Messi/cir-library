## Nehemiah Chapter 1

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpFirst" align=center style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.25in;text-align:center;'><b><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>1<sup>;1</sup></span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Memoirs of Nehemiah Son of Hacaliah</span></p>


### Report from Jerusalem


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>In the <b>month Chislev, the twentieth year</b>, I was in Susa, the capital. </span><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Hanani, one of my brothers, and some men came from Judah. I asked them about the Jews that had survived the captivity and about Jerusalem. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They told me, <i>“The remnant in the province is in dire straits and dishonor. The wall around Jerusalem has been torn down and its gates burned.”</i></span></span></p>


### Nehemiah’s Prayer


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When I heard it, I sat down and cried and mourned for days. I fasted and prayed, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> God of heaven, great awesome God that keeps covenant and enduring love for those who love you and keep your commandments, </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>let your ear be attentive and your eyes open to my praying day and night for the Israelites</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> <i>your servants, and for confessing our sins against you. Even I and my father’s family have sinned. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We’ve acted corruptly and not kept the commandments, statutes, or ordinances you gave Moses. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Remember your promise to him, ‘If you’re not faithful, I’ll scatter you among the nations. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But if you come back to me and keep my commandments—though scattered beyond the horizons—I’ll gather you up and bring you to the place where I’ve put my name.’
</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They’re the people you’ve redeemed by your power and strength. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Please listen to my prayer and the prayer of your servants who delight in honoring you. Make me successful today; grant me mercy from this man.”</span></i></span></p>
