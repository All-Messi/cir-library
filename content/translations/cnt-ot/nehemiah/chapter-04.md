## Nehemiah Chapter 4


### Opposition to the Work


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpFirst" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Sanballat heard we were rebuilding the wall, he was furious and ridiculed the Jews. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In front of his relatives and wealthy men in Samaria, he asked, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“What are these feeble Jews doing? Are they going to restore it for themselves? Can they offer sacrifices? Can they finish it in a day? Can they remake something out of the charred stones from dusty rubble?”</span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Tobiah the Ammonite was close by and said, <i>“Even a fox could jump on that stone wall they’re building and break it down!”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Listen how they despise us, God! Turn their mockery back on their heads and give them up for plunder in a land that takes them captive. </span></i><b><i><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>5</span></sup></i></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t forgive them; don’t blot out their sin. They’ve tried to demoralize the builders.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We were able to join the whole wall together to half its original height because the people were willing to work. <b>                        </b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Neh 4:1-6</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Sanballat, Tobiah, the Arabs, the Ammonites, and the Ashdodites heard that repairing the walls was continuing and that we were closing up the breaks in it. They were really mad. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They conspired to fight Jerusalem and create confusion in it, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>but we prayed to God and set up a guard day and night. </span><a name="_Hlk146038073"></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The people from Judah were saying, <i>“The laborers were getting worn out,</i> <i>but there’s still a lot of rubble to move. We can’t rebuild the wall ourselves.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  11</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Our enemies said, <i>“They won’t know it till we attack them, kill them, and stop the work.”</i> </span><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When the Jews that lived close to them came and told us ten times, <i>“They’ll come up against us from every direction,”</i> </span><a name="_Hlk146037667"></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I stationed men behind the wall in the lowest parts, the exposed places. </span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I stationed the people in families with their swords, spears, and bows. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When I saw how afraid they were, I told the nobles, the officials, and the rest of the people, <i>“You don’t have to be afraid of them. Remember our great awesome Lord. Fight for your brothers, your children, your wives and houses.”</i><b>
</b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Neh 4:7-14</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When our enemies heard that we knew about <i>their plans</i> and that God had frustrated it, each of us went back to work on the wall. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>From then on, half of my men worked while the other half held spears, shields, bows, and breastplates. The commanders were behind the people of Judah. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The ones that were rebuilding the wall and carrying burdens did their work with one hand and held a weapon in the other. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Each one attached his sword to his side as he built while the bugler stood close to me. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I told the nobles, the officials, and the rest of the people, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“The work is important and extensive, and we’re a long way apart on the wall. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Wherever you hear the bugle, rally there. Our God will fight for us.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpLast" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We carried out the work with half of them holding spears from dawn till the stars came out. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I told the people then, <i>“Each man and his servant, spend the night inside Jerusalem so you can be guards at night and workmen during the day.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I, my brothers, servants, and guards didn’t change clothes. Everybody kept a weapon on them.                                                                     </span></span></p>
