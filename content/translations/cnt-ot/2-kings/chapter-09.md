## 2 Kings Chapter 9


### Jehu Anointed King in Israel


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Elisha called a man from the school of the prophets,   </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Gird up*, take this flask of olive oil to Ramoth-gilead. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Find Jehu son of Jehoshaphat son of Nimshi. Tell him to get up from his companions and take him to a room inside. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pour the flask of olive oil on his head and say, ‘The L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> says, “I’ve anointed you king over Israel.”’ Then open the door and run.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The young man went to Ramoth-gilead. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The commanders of the army were sitting there, and he said, <i>“I have a message for you, commander.” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jehu said, <i>“Which one?”</i> </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“You, Sir.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> <b>                                                                                                              </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>2 Kg 9:1-5</span></b></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><a name="_Hlk126337352"><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He got up and went into the house. The young man poured the oil on his head, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“The L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> God of Israel says,</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> <i>‘I’ve anointed you king over his people Israel.</i> </span><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Strike Ahab’s family so I can avenge the blood of my prophets that Jezebel shed. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ahab’s family will perish, and I’ll remove from him every male, bond and free. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll destroy his family like Jeroboam’s<b><sup>I</sup></b> and Baasha’s. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Dogs will eat Jezebel in Jezreel, and nobody will bury her.’” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He opened the door and ran.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jehu came out to the other officers, and one of them asked, <i>“Is everything all right? Why did that mad fellow come to you?” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said, <i>“You know very well how a man like him can babble.”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“</span></sup></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That’s not what happened. Tell us.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said, <i>“He said such and such, ‘The L</i></span><i><span style='font-size: 9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> says, “I’ve anointed you king of Israel.”’”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Each man hurried and put his cloak under Jehu on the bare steps, blew the bugle, and said, <i>“<u>Jehu</u> is king!”</i></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>       2 Kg 9:6-13</span></b></span></p>


### Jehu Kills Joram and Ahaziah


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jehu plotted against <u>Joram</u>. With all Israel, Joram had been defending Ramoth-gilead against Hazael, king of Aram, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>but he’d gone back to Jezreel to heal from wounds he got from the Arameans. Jehu said, <i>“If you agree, don’t let anybody leave the city to tell it in Jezreel.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then Jehu rode a chariot to Jezreel because <u>Joram</u> was lying there healing. <b>Ahaziah</b>, king of Judah, had come down to see Joram. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A watchman standing on the tower in Jezreel saw Jehu’s company <i>of soldiers</i>,
<i>“A company is coming.”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>Joram said, <i>“Send a horseman to meet them and ask him if he’s coming in peace.”</i> </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A horseman went to meet him, <i>“The king asks if you’re coming in peace.”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>Jehu said, <i>“What do you have to do with peace? Defect and follow me.”</i> </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>The watchman reported, <i>“The messenger went to them, but didn’t come back.” </i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He sent out a second horseman, <i>“The king asks if you’re coming in peace.”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>Jehu answered, <i>“What do you have to do with peace? Defect and follow me.” </i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><a name="_Hlk126337629"><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The watchman reported, <i>“He went to them, but didn’t come back. His driving is furious like Jehu’s.”</i><b>    </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>2 Kg 9:14-20</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joram said, <i>“Get my chariot ready.”</i> They got it ready. <u>Joram</u> and <b>Ahaziah</b> went out in their chariots to meet Jehu and found him on Naboth’s property. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Joram saw Jehu, he said, <i>“Are you coming in peace, Jehu?”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.25in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“What peace, with so many of your mother’s prostitutions and witchcrafts?”</i> </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joram reined around and fled. He told Ahaziah, <i>“It’s treason!”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jehu drew his bow with his full strength and shot Joram between his arms. The arrow went through his heart, and he sank in his chariot. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then Jehu told Bidkar his officer, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Pick him up and throw him on Naboth’s field. Remember when we were riding behind his father Ahab, the L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> laid this oracle against him: </span></i><span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>‘I certainly saw the blood of Naboth and his sons yesterday. I’ll pay you back on that property.’ Throw him on the property like the L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> said.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Ahaziah saw that, he fled past the garden house. Jehu chased him and said, <i>“Shoot him, too, in the chariot.”</i> They shot him at the ascent of Gur, at Ibleam. He fled as far as Megiddo and died. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His servants hauled him to Jerusalem in a chariot and buried him in his own grave with his ancestors in The City of David. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ahaziah had become king in Judah in <b>the eleventh year of Joram</b>.<b>     </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>2 Kg 9:21-29</span></b></span></p>


### Jezebel Killed


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><a name="_Hlk126338392"><span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Jehu arrived at Jezreel, Jezebel heard about it, and put on eye shadow and adorned her head, and looked out the window. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Jehu came in the gate, she said, <i>“Is everything all right, Zimri, your master’s murderer?”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He looked up at the window and said, <i>“Who’s with me?” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>Two of the three officials looked down. </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Throw her down.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>    </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>They threw her down, and her blood spattered on the wall and the horses, and he tramped her under his horses hooves. </span><span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He came in and ate, and said, <i>“See to that cursed woman and bury her. She <u>is</u> a king’s daughter.” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They went to bury her, but they couldn’t find any of her except skull, feet, and palms. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They came back and told him. He said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“The L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> said by Elijah, ‘Dogs will eat her body on her property. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v37" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Her corpse will be like manure on her field; people won’t be able to tell if it’s Jezebel.’”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> <b>                                                      </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>2 Kg 9:30-37</span></b></span></p>
