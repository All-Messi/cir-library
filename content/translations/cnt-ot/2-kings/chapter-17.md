## 2 Kings Chapter 17


### Hoshea Last King of Israel


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In the <b>twelfth year of Ahaz</b>, <u>Hoshea</u> son of Elah became king of Israel in Samaria <i>and ruled</i> for 9 years. </span><a name="_Hlk126378038"></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He did evil, but not as bad as the previous kings of Israel. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Shalmaneser, king of Assyria, attacked him, and he had to pay tribute <i>to Assyria</i>. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But the king of Assyria discovered a plot he was laying. He’d sent ambassadors to So, king of Egypt, and withheld the annual tribute to Assyria; so <i>Shalmaneser</i> shut him up bound in prison. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then he invaded the whole country and went up to Samaria and laid siege to it for 3 years. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In the <b>ninth year of Hoshea</b>, the king of Assyria conquered the city and took Israel into exile in Assyria. He settled them in Halah and Habor on the Gozan River and in towns of the Medes.<b>    </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>2 Kg 17:1-6</span></b></span></p>


### Israel Exiled over Sin

They’d feared other gods, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>followed the customs of the nations the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> had <i>originally
</i>driven out, and the customs Israel’s kings had brought in. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Israel secretly did what wasn’t right: </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>they built high places in their towns from watchtower to fortified city; </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>they put sacred pillars and Asherah poles on every tall hill under every green tree; </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>they burned incense on the high places—things that provoked the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.
</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They served idols the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> told them not to serve, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>even though he warned Israel and Judah through his prophets and seers, <i>“Turn from your sins and keep my commandments, my statutes—the whole Law I’ve commanded your ancestors through the prophets.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But they ignored him. <span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They rejected the statutes and the covenant he made with their ancestors and <i>dismissed
</i>his warnings. They became vain and followed the nations around them that he commanded them not to be like.<b>           </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>2 Kg 17:7-15</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><a name="_Hlk126377788"><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They made molten images, <i>that is, </i>two calves and an Asherah pole. <span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They burned their sons and daughters as offerings, and practiced fortunetelling and sorcery. <span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He was angry with them and removed them away from him. None were left except the tribe of Judah.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Judah didn’t keep his commandments <i>either</i>, but followed the customs Israel brought in. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> rejected Israel’s descendants, oppressed them, and delivered them to plunderers till he’d thrown them out. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When he’d torn Israel away from David’s dynasty, they made Jeroboam</span><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> son of Nebat king. He drove them away from the L</span><span style='font-size: 9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> and got them to commit a great sin. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Israel</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> continued to follow in his sins </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>till the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> removed <i>Israel</i> away from him. Israel has been in exile in Assyria ever since.</span></span></p>


### Foreigners Settled in Samaria

<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When they first started living there, they didn’t respect the L</span><span style='font-size: 9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>. <p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“The nations you exiled into the towns in Samaria don’t know the Law of the god of the land.
