## 2 Kings Chapter 21


### Manasseh Rules in Judah


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><a name="_Hlk126405451"><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> </a><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Manasseh</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> was 12 and ruled for 55 years in Jerusalem. He worshiped and served the host of heaven, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>built altars in the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>’s Temple where the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> had said, <i>“I’ll identify myself with Jerusalem.”</i> </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He burned his son as an offering, practiced witchcraft, used sorcery, and dealt with mediums and spiritists. <p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Forever I’ll identify with this Temple and with Jerusalem, which I’ve chosen from the tribes of Israel. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I won’t make Israel wander anymore from the country I gave their ancestors—if only they’ll observe the whole Law that I’ve commanded them through Moses.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> <b>                                                                                                     </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>2 Kg 21:1-8</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But they didn’t listen, and Manasseh seduced them to sin more than the nations the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> <i>originally</i> destroyed in front of them.</span></span></p>

<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll stretch over Jerusalem Samaria’s measuring line and Ahab’s plumb bob. <span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They’ve done evil and made me angry ever since I brought their ancestors out of Egypt.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Besides the sin he got Judah to do, Manasseh filled Jerusalem with innocent blood from one end to the other. </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He did evil like Manasseh, </span></span>
