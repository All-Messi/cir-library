<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Four lepers were sitting at the entrance to the city gate. If we sit here, we'll die." 5As it was getting dark, they went to the Arameans' camp. When they got to the edge of it, nobody was there. 6The LORD had made the army hear the chariots and horses of a big army.
