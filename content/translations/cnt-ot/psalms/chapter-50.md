## Psalms Chapter 50

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> God, the Mighty One, the LORD, has spoken</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> He shines forth out of Zion in perfect beauty.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Our God comes; he doesn’t stay quiet;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> He calls on the sky above and the earth beneath</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Gather my people to me</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> The heavens declare his justice</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Listen, my people, and I’ll speak;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> I don’t have any complaint about your sacrifices</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> But I don’t need any bulls from your barns</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> I know every bird in the mountains;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> If I was hungry, I wouldn’t say anything to you about it,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Will I eat the meat of bulls</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> Offer to me a sacrifice of thanksgiving;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> Call on me when there’s trouble;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> But God says to the sinful,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> You hate discipline</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> Thieves are your friends;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> You give your mouth free reign;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> You sit around and gossip about your brother;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> I didn’t speak up when you did those things,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> Consider this, you that forget about me,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> Giving thanks is the sacrifice that honors me;</span>
