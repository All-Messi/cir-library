## Psalms Chapter **115**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Not to us, LORD, not to us, <strong>	</strong>but to you be honor because of your enduring love and faithfulness.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>Why should the nations say, <em>“Where’</em><em>s their God?”</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>Our God is in heaven, where he accomplishes what he intends.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>Their man-made idols are <em>just </em>silver and gold</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>with mouths that can’t talk, eyes that can’t see,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>ears that can’t hear, noses that can’t smell,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>hands that can’t feel, feet that can’t walk, throats that can’t<em> </em>make a sound!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>The ones that make them will become like them, <em>likewise </em>everybody that relies on them.<strong>	</strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 115:1-8</p>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>Israel, trust in the LORD; <strong>he’s your help</strong><strong>er</strong><strong> and shield.</strong></span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>House of Aaron, trust in the LORD; <strong>he’s your help</strong><strong>er</strong><strong> and shield.</strong></span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>All of you that respect the LORD, trust in him; <strong>he’s your help</strong><strong>er</strong><strong> and shield.</strong></span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>He cares about you; he’ll bless you; Israel and Aaron;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup><strong>13</strong></sup>he’ll bless everyone that respects him, least and greatest.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup><strong>14</strong></sup>May he increase you and your children;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup><strong>15</strong></sup>may the Maker of earth and sky bless you.<strong>	</strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 115:9-15</p>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup><strong>16</strong></sup>The sky belongs to him, but he’s given us the earth.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup><strong>17</strong></sup>The dead don’t praise the LORD; nobody does that descends into silence;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup><strong>18</strong></sup>but <u>we</u>’ll bless him from now on forever. <strong>Hallelujah!</strong><strong>	</strong></span>
