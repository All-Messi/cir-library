## Psalms Chapter **54**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Deliver me, God, by your name; vindicate me by your power.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>Listen to my prayer; hear what I have to say.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>Foreigners have attacked me; violent people have tried to kill me; they don’t care about you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>But God helps me; the LORD sustains my life.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>He pays back my adversaries and destroys them in his faithfulness.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>I’ll sacrifice to him willingly and thank him because he’s good.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>He’s delivered me from all my trouble so I could triumph over my enemies.</span>
