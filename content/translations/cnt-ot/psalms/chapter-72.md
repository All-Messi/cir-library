## Psalms Chapter 72

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Give the king your judgments, God;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> May he judge your people fairly,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Let the mountains bring prosperity to the people;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> May he defend the downtrodden,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Let them respect you as long as the sun lasts,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> May the king rule like rain on mown grass,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> May good people flourish in his time</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> May he rule from sea to sea,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Let desert nomads bow to him,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Let the kings of Tarshish and the islands bring presents,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Let all kings bow to him</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> He’ll deliver the needy when they call for help,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> He’ll have mercy on the poor and needy</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> He’ll rescue them from oppression and violence;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> May grain be abundant in the land;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> May the king’s name last forever</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> Bless the LORD, the God of Israel,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> Bless his glorious name forever;</span>
