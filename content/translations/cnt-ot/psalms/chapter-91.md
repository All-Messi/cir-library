## Psalms Chapter **91**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Those who live in the shelter of the Most High will find rest in the shadow of the Almighty.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>They’ll say about the LORD, <em>“</em><em>Our</em><em> refuge and fortress, </em><em>the</em><em> God </em><em>we</em><em> trust!”</em><em>	</em></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 91:1-2</p>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>He delivers <em>you</em> from the trapper’s snare and deadly pestilence.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>He covers you with his pinions; you can find refuge under his wings; his faithfulness shields and protects you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>You won’t be afraid of terrors at night, of arrows that fly by day,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>of pestilence that lurks in the dark, or destruction that lays waste at noon.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>A thousand may fall at your side, ten thousand by your right hand; but disaster won’t touch <u>you</u>.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>You’ll stand by and watch his recompense to the wicked,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>because you’ve made the LORD your refuge, the Most High your hiding place.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>Misfortune won’t fall on you; no plague will get near you.<strong>	</strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 91:3-10</p>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>He’ll make his angels responsible for guarding you whatever you do.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>They’ll carry you so you won’t trip over a rock.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup><strong>13</strong></sup>You’ll step on lions and cobras and crush them. <strong><sup>14</sup></strong><em>“I’ll </em><em>rescue the</em><em>m </em><em>that</em><em> love me</em> <em>	</em><em>and set up</em><em> high </em><em>those who</em><em> know me.</em></span>
