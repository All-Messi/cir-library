## Psalms Chapter 91

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Those who live in the shelter of the Most High</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> They’ll say about the LORD,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> He delivers you from the trapper’s snare</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> He covers you with his pinions;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> You won’t be afraid of terrors at night,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> A thousand may fall at your side,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Misfortune won’t fall on you;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> He’ll make his angels responsible</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> They’ll carry you</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> You’ll step on lions and cobras</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> I’ll rescue them that love me</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> They’ll call on me, and I’ll answer them;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> I’ll satisfy them with long life</span>
