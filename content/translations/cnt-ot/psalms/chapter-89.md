## Psalms Chapter 89

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> I’ll sing forever about the LORD’s enduring love and make known his faithfulness to every generation,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> because I’ve said, “You build up enduring love; in the heavens you establish your faithfulness.”</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> The LORD said, “I’ve made a covenant with my chosen one; I’ve sworn to David my servant, 89:4‘I’ll make your descendants secure forever and build up your throne to all generations.’”</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 89:1-4</p>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> The sky praises your wonders and extols your faithfulness in the assembly of your holy angels.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Who in the sky can compare with you? Who among the heavenly beings is like you,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> a God greatly respected in the council of holy angels and awesome above everyone around him?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Lord GOD of Hosts, who is like you? You’re completely faithful.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> You rule the raging sea; when its waves rise up, you quiet them.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> You’ve crushed Rahab like one that’s slain and scattered your enemies with your strong arm.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 89:5-10</p>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> The sky is yours; the earth belongs to you; you’ve founded the world and everything in it.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> You’ve created north and south; Tabor and Hermon shout for joy over you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> You have a powerful arm and a strong hand; you lift your right hand high.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> Goodness and justice make the foundation for your throne; enduring love and truth proceed ahead of you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> How blessed people are who know the sound of joy, who walk in the light of your presence, LORD.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> In your name they celebrate all day; your goodness lifts their spirits.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> They glory in your strength; your blessing honors them.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> Their shield belongs to the LORD; their king belongs to the Holy One of Israel.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 89:11-18</p>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> Long ago you said in a vision to your holy people, “I’ve crowned a great man, chosen from the common people.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> I found my servant David and anointed him with my holy oil.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> My hand will make him secure; my arm will give him strength.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> His enemies won’t outsmart him; sinful people won’t oppress him.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> I’ll crush his adversaries and strike down those who hate him.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> My faithfulness and enduring love will be with him, and I’ll make him honored.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 89:19-24</p>

<span class="verse" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> I’ll establish his rule as far as the sea and set his control over the rivers.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> He’ll call out to me, ‘My Father, my God, the Rock that keeps me safe.’</span>

<span class="verse" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> I’ll make him my firstborn, the highest among the kings on earth.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> I’ll keep my enduring love for him forever and confirm my covenant with him.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> I’ll make his descendants permanently secure, his throne as eternal as the sky.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 89:25-29</p>

<span class="verse" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> If his descendants leave my Law and don’t follow my judgments,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> if they violate my statutes and don’t keep my commandments,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> then I’ll bring a rod to bear on their sin and stripes on their iniquity.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> But I won’t break off my enduring love from him or deal falsely in my faithfulness.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> I won’t violate my covenant or alter what I’ve said.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> Once I’ve sworn by my holiness, I won’t lie to David.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> His descendants will continue permanently, his throne will last like the sun with me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> It’ll be secure as permanently as the moon, as faithful as its witness in the sky.”</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 89:30-37</p>

<span class="verse" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> But, you’ve thrown him away and rejected him; you’re angry with your anointed.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v39"><sup class="verse-num">39</sup> You’ve spurned the covenant with him and profaned his crown in the dust.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v40"><sup class="verse-num">40</sup> You’ve broken down his walls and brought his fortresses to ruin.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v41"><sup class="verse-num">41</sup> Everyone that passes by plunders him; he’s become a disgrace to his neighbors.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v42"><sup class="verse-num">42</sup> You’ve raised up his adversaries and made them celebrate.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v43"><sup class="verse-num">43</sup> You’ve turned back the edge of his sword and haven’t made him stand in battle.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v44"><sup class="verse-num">44</sup> You made his splendor cease and threw his throne on the ground.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v45"><sup class="verse-num">45</sup> You’ve shortened his youth and covered him with shame.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 89:38-45</p>

<span class="verse" id="v46"><sup class="verse-num">46</sup> How long, LORD, will you conceal yourself, will your anger burn like fire?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v47"><sup class="verse-num">47</sup> Remember how long life is. What emptiness have you created people for?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v48"><sup class="verse-num">48</sup> Who can live without dying? Can anyone avoid Sheol?</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 89:46-48</p>

<span class="verse" id="v49"><sup class="verse-num">49</sup> Where’s your former enduring love that you faithfully swore to David?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v50"><sup class="verse-num">50</sup> Remember the scorn your servants endure, how I carry many people’s insults inside me</span>

<span class="verse" id="v51"><sup class="verse-num">51</sup> that your enemies have used for taunts, that they’ve mocked your anointed one with.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v52"><sup class="verse-num">52</sup> Bless the LORD forever! Amen and Amen.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 89:49-52</p>
