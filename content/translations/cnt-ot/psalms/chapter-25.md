## Psalms Chapter 25

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> I hold my life up to you, LORD;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> I trust you; don’t let me down or let my enemies celebrate over my defeat.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Nobody that waits for you will be discredited; people who deal treacherously are the ones that will be discredited.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 25:1-3</p>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> LORD, let me know your ways; teach me your paths;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> lead me in your truth because you keep me safe; I’ll always depend on you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Remember your mercy and enduring love that have existed from ages past.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Don’t keep in mind the sins I committed when I was young; remember me in the light of your enduring love.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 25:4-7</p>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> The LORD is good and just, so he instructs sinners in his way</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> and leads the humble in what’s right.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Everything he does comes from truth and enduring love for those who keep his covenant and testimonies.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> For your own honor, pardon my sin because it’s great.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 25:8-11</p>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Who of you respect the LORD? He’ll instruct you in what to choose</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> so you’ll keep succeeding and your offspring will inherit the land.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> People that respect the LORD are his friends; he’ll make his covenant known to them.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> I keep looking to him because he untangles my feet from my enemies’ net.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 25:12-15</p>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> Turn to me and be gracious because I’m lonely and downcast.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> Relieve me of my pain and anguish, and lift me out of my doldrums.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> Look at my troubles and forgive my sin.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 25:16-18</p>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> Look at all the enemies I have that hate me so much!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> Guard my life and rescue me; don’t let me regret taking refuge in you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> Oh that integrity and godliness would protect me because I depend on you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> Redeem Israel, God, from all its troubles.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 25:19-22</p>
