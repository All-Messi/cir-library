## Psalms Chapter 59

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Deliver me from my enemies, God;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Save me from sinners;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> They lie in ambush to kill me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> For no fault of mine, they run to attack me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> LORD God of Hosts, God of Israel,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Don’t kill them; otherwise, my people will forget;</span>
