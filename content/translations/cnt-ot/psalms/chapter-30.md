<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> I’ll praise you, LORD, because you’ve rescued me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> I appealed to you for help, my God,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Sing praise to the LORD, his holy people;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Your blessing, LORD, made me feel as stable as a mountain;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> I called out to you in a petition:</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> What does my death accomplish if you let me go down to the Pit?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Hear me, LORD; be gracious and help me.”</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> You turned my mourning into dancing;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Now I can sing praise to you unrestrained;</span>
