## Psalms Chapter 109

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> God that I praise, speak up for me!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Deceitful people speak against me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> They surround me with hateful words</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> In return for my love, they accuse me</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> They repay me evil for good,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Appoint someone corrupt over them,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> When they’re judged, pronounce them guilty;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Shorten their lives</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Make their children fatherless</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Have their children go begging,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Have their creditors seize everything they own</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> May no one extend enduring love to them</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Cut off their posterity;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> LORD, remember their fathers’ sins</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> They loved to curse; put curses on them!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> They cover themselves with cursing like clothes;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> Put it on them like a robe they have to wear all the time,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> Make such things the LORD’s reward to accusers</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> But LORD God, deal graciously with me</span>

<span class="verse" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> I’m passing on like a lengthening shadow;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> My knees are weak from fasting;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> I’m someone people scorn;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> Help me, LORD my God;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> Show them this is your doing.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> Let them curse, but you bless;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> Dress those accusers in dishonor;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> I’ll praise the LORD repeatedly;</span>
