## Psalms Chapter 104

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Bless the LORD, everything that I am!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> You cover yourself with light as with a robe;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> You laid the beams for your rooms on the water;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> You make the winds your messengers</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> You built the earth on foundations</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> You covered it with the ocean like a robe</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> At your rebuke it receded;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> The mountains rose up; the valleys sank down</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> You set a boundary the water couldn’t cross</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> You make springs in the valleys</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> They water the animals in the countryside</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Birds live beside them</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> You water the mountains from your home above;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> You make grass grow for the livestock</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> The LORD’s trees drink their fill,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> High mountains are for wild goats,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> You made the moon to mark the seasons;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> You send darkness, and it becomes night;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> Young lions roar after their prey</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> When the sun comes up,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> LORD, how many things you’ve done!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> Yonder is the ocean, big and wide;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> Ships move along on it,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> They all depend on you</span>

<span class="verse" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> You give them food, and they gather it;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> If you hide, they’re scared;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> You send out your breath, and they’re created;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> Let the LORD’s honor endure forever;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> The world trembles at his look;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> I’ll sing to the LORD as long as I live;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> May my meditations please him;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> Let sinners vanish from the earth;</span>
