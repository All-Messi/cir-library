## Psalms Chapter 83

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> God, don’t keep quiet; don’t be silent.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Your enemies are making an uproar;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> They’re plotting against your people,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Together they’re signing a treaty against you:</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Edom and the Ishmaelites, Moab and the Hagrites;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Gebal, Ammon, and Amalek;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Assyria has joined them too;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Deal with them like you dealt with Midian,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Make their nobles like Oreb and Zeeb</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Make them like whirling dust,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> Like a wildfire that burns a whole forest,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> Write shame all over their faces</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> Frighten them forever;</span>
