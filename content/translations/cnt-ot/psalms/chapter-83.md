## Psalms Chapter 83

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> God, don’t keep quiet; don’t be silent.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Your enemies are making an uproar; those who hate you are exalting themselves.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> They’re plotting against your people, conspiring against them 4and saying, “Let’s wipe out their nation so no one will remember them anymore.”</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Together they’re signing a treaty against you:</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Edom and the Ishmaelites, Moab and the Hagrites;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Gebal, Ammon, and Amalek; Philistia and the people in Tyre;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Assyria has joined them too; they’re allied with Lot’s descendants.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 83:1-8</p>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Deal with them like you dealt with Midian, with Sisera and Jabin at the Kishon River,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> that you destroyed at En-dor, that became like manure on the ground.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Make their nobles like Oreb and Zeeb and their officials like Zebah and Zalmunna,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> who said, “Let’s take over God’s pastures.”</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 83:9-12</p>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Make them like whirling dust, like chaff in the wind.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> Like a wildfire that burns a whole forest, like a flame that sets mountains on fire;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> chase them with your storm; terrify them with your tempest.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> Write shame all over their faces till they submit to you, LORD.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> Frighten them forever; let them die in disgrace</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> so they’ll know that you are supreme over the whole world.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 83:13-18</p>
