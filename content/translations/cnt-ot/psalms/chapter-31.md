## Psalms Chapter **31**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>I take refuge in you, LORD; never let me be disgraced; in your goodness protect me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>Listen to me, rescue me quickly; be a rock for me, a strong fortress to save me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>You’re my rock and fortress; for your honor, lead me and guide me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>Free me out of the trap people secretly laid for me because you’re my protection.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>I’m entrusting my spirit to you; rescue me, LORD, true God.<strong> </strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 31:1-5</p>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>I detest those who respect worthless idols;<strong>	</strong> I trust in you, LORD.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>I’ll be happy in your enduring love, because you see my difficulties and know the trouble I’m in,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>because you haven’t handed me over to my enemies; you’ve set me on a spacious place.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 31:6-8</p>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>Be gracious to me in my distress, LORD; my eyes are melting away with grief, my soul and body are getting weak as well</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>because I live in constant sorrow; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong><strong><sup>	</sup></strong><strong><sup>	</sup></strong>I spend my years in sighing. My strength is failing because of sin; my body is wasting away.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>Because I have so many enemies, <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>my neighbors despise me, my acquaintances abhor me, and people shun me in the streets.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>I’m forgotten like someone <em>long </em>dead, <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>out of mind like a broken pot.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup><strong>13</strong></sup>I hear people’s slander; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong><strong><sup>	</sup></strong>there’s terror on every side; my enemies conspire against me and lay plots to kill me.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 31:9-13</p>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup><strong>14</strong></sup>But I trust you, LORD; I swear, <em>“You’re my God.”</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup><strong>15</strong></sup>My future’s in your hands; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>rescue me from my enemies, from those who persecute me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup><strong>16</strong></sup>Smile at me; save me with your enduring love.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup><strong>17</strong></sup>Don’t let my enemies disgrace me, LORD, <strong><sup>	</sup></strong><strong><sup>	</sup></strong>because I’m calling on <u>you</u>. Put <u>sinners</u> to disgrace; let <u>them</u> be silent in Sheol.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup><strong>18</strong></sup>Make lying lips be mute that insult the good with pride and contempt.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 31:14-18</p>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup><strong>19</strong></sup>How great your goodness is that you’ve stored up for those who respect you, that you’ve shown to people that take refuge in you so others can see.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup><strong>20</strong></sup>You secretly hide them with you away from people’s plots, <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>and shelter them from the conflict that tongues can cause.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup><strong>21</strong></sup>Bless the LORD because he showed me his enduring love when I was surrounded like a city under siege.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup><strong>22</strong></sup>I despaired in my alarm, <em>“I</em><em> can’t see you!</em><em>”</em> <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>but you heard my appeal.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 31:19-22</p>

<span class="verse" id="v23"><sup><strong>23</strong></sup>Love the LORD, all you holy people! He preserves the faithful and repays those who act arrogantly.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v24"><sup><strong>24</strong></sup>Be strong and courageous, you that rest your hopes in him!</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 31:23-24</p>
