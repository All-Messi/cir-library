## Psalms Chapter **66**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Make a joyful sound to God, all the earth.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>Sing about his honor; make his praise glorious.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>Say to him, <em>“</em><em>What you do is</em><em> so awesome!</em> <em>	Your enemies feign obedience to </em><em>you</em> <em>	</em><em>	out of fear for your</em><em> power.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup><em>All the earth worships you and praises</em><em> you;</em> <em>	they sing praises to you.”</em></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 66:1-4</p>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>Come and see what God has done; it’s awesome what he’s done for people.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>He turned the <em>Red </em>Sea into dry ground; the people crossed through the <em>Jordan</em> on foot; they celebrated over him there.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>He rules by his power forever; he keeps watch over the nations; the rebellious don’t make an uprising.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 66:5-7</p>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>Bless our God, you nations; sound his praise abroad.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>He keeps us alive and doesn’t let our feet slip.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>You’ve tried us, God, and refined us like silver.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>You caught us in your net and laid a heavy load in our lap.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>You put a ruler over us and passed us through fire and water; yet you brought us out into a large place.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup><strong>13</strong></sup>I’ll come to your Temple with burnt offerings and keep the vows <strong><sup>14</sup></strong>I made when I was in trouble.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup><strong>15</strong></sup>I’ll offer you burnt offerings of healthy animals with the smoke of rams; I’ll offer bulls and male goats.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 66:8-15</p>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup><strong>16</strong></sup>Come and listen, you that respect God, and I’ll tell you what he’s done for me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup><strong>17</strong></sup>I appealed to him with my mouth and extolled him with my tongue.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup><strong>18</strong></sup>If I cherish sinfulness in my heart, the LORD won’t hear me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup><strong>19</strong></sup>But he’s certainly heard me; he’s listened to my prayer.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup><strong>20</strong></sup>Bless God, who hasn’t ignored my prayer or <em>withdrawn</em><em> his</em> enduring love.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 66:16-20</p>
