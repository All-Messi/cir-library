## Psalms Chapter **139**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>LORD, you’ve known me and examined me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>You know when I sit down and when I get up; from a long way off you know what I’m thinking.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>You search out my traveling and my resting; you’re well acquainted with my ways.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>Before I speak, you know what I’m going to say.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>You’ve protected me front and back <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>and laid your hand over me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>Knowledge like that is too amazing for me; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>it’s beyond me; I can’t imagine it.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 139:1-6</p>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>Where can I go from your Spirit? Where can I go to get away from you?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>If I go up to the sky, you’re there; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>if I make my bed in Sheol, you’re there.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>If I take the wings of morning and live by the most distant part of the ocean,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>even there you’ll lead me and support me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>If I say, <em>“</em><em>Let darkness </em><em>cover me,</em> <strong><em><sup>	</sup></em></strong><strong><em><sup>	</sup></em></strong><em>and</em><em> the light around me </em><em>become night,</em><em>”</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>the dark’s not dark to you; the night’s as bright as day.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 139:7-12</p>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup><strong>13</strong></sup>You formed my inner parts and knit me together in my mother’s womb.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup><strong>14</strong></sup>Thank you; the way you’ve made me <strong><sup>	</sup></strong><strong><sup>	</sup></strong>is awesome and wonderful. <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>What you<strong><sup> </sup></strong>do is marvelous; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong><strong><sup>	</sup></strong>I know that for sure.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup><strong>15</strong></sup>My frame wasn’t hidden from you when I was being made in secret, intricately put together deep in the earth.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup><strong>16</strong></sup>You saw my unshaped substance; you wrote in your scroll the length of life you appointed <em>for me</em> before there was any of it.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 139:13-16</p>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup><strong>17</strong></sup>How precious your thoughts are to me! How vast their number is!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup><strong>18</strong></sup>If I could count them, they’d outnumber the grains of sand. When I wake up, I’m still with you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup><strong>19</strong></sup>Oh that you’d slay the sinful, God. <strong><sup>	</sup></strong><strong><sup>	</sup></strong>(Go away from me, you murderers!)</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup><strong>20</strong></sup>Your enemies say awful things about you; they use your name as a byword.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup><strong>21</strong></sup>Don’t I hate those who hate you? Don’t I despise the ones that oppose you?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup><strong>22</strong></sup>I have total disdain for them; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong><strong><sup>	</sup></strong>they’re my enemies.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v23"><sup><strong>23</strong></sup>Examine me, God, and know my heart; test me and see how anxious my thoughts are.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v24"><sup><strong>24</strong></sup>See if there’s anything offensive in me and lead me in the everlasting way.<strong>	</strong></span>
