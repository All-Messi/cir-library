## Psalms Chapter 36

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Sin speaks to sinful people’s hearts; they don’t respect God at all.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> They flatter themselves that he can’t discover their sin.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Their words deceive; they’ve stopped being wise and doing good.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> They devise schemes in bed and carry them out in the morning with no qualms</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 36:1-4</p>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> LORD, your enduring love reaches the stars; your faithfulness is as high as the sky;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> your goodness as mighty as the mountains; your justice as deep as the ocean; your provision sufficient for everything that breathes.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> How precious that enduring love is! All humanity finds protection under your wings.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> They drink their fill from the abundance in your house, from your river of delights.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> You’re their fountain of life; in your light they see light.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Maintain your enduring love to all who know you, your goodness to the godly in heart.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 36:5-10</p>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Don’t let the proud tramp on me or the sinful shove me away.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Let sinners fall where they sin, thrown down so they can’t get up.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 36:10-12</p>
