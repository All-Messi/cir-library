## Psalms Chapter **48**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>The LORD is great and deserves great praise in the city of our God on his holy mountain.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>Mount Zion, the heights of Zaphon, is high and beautiful— <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>the joy of the whole earth, the city of the great King.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>In its towers God reveals himself as a strong defense.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>Kings joined forces and advanced <em>against that strong defense</em>.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup><em>But</em><em> </em>as soon as it came into view, <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>they panicked and fled.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>Panic seized them like the anguish of a woman in childbirth.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup><em>The </em><em>L</em><em>ORD</em> destroyed them <strong><sup>	</sup></strong> like ships of Tarshish capsized by an east wind.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>As we’ve heard, so we’ve seen in the city of the LORD of Hosts, our God; he’ll make it safe forever.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 48:1-8</p>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>In your Temple we think about your enduring love.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>Like your name, God, your praise is everywhere; your right hand is filled with goodness.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>Let Mount Zion be happy, and the residents of Judah celebrate your goodness.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>Walk around Zion and count its towers;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup><strong>13</strong></sup>inspect its ramparts and tour its fortresses so you can tell the next generation;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup><strong>14</strong></sup>because <em>God</em> is God forever; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>he’ll guide us till we die.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 48:9-14</p>
