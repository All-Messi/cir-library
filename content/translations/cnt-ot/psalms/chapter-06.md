## Psalms Chapter **6**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Don’t lash out at me, LORD, <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>or take me to task in your anger.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>Be gracious to me because I’m weak; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>heal me because I’m troubled deep inside.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>I’m really scared; how long, LORD, before you save me?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>Come back and rescue me; because of your enduring love, save <em>me</em>;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>because nobody talks about you when they’re dead; who gives you gratitude in Sheol?</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 6:1-5</p>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>I’m worn out from moaning; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>I flood my bed with tears every night and drench my couch with weeping.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>My eyes melt from grief, worn out over my adversaries.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>Go away, all you sinners, because the LORD has heard me call.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>He’s heard my plea and accepts my prayer.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup><em>He’ll</em> disgrace my enemies and greatly distress them; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>they’ll suddenly retreat, humiliated.</span>
