## Psalms Chapter **27**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>The LORD is my light and salvation; who am I going to be afraid of? He defends me; who am I going to dread?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>When bad people come to devour me, they stumble and fall.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>Though an army attacks me, it won’t frighten me; though war breaks out against me, I’ll stay confident.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 27:1-3</p>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>I ask the LORD for one thing—what I look for most: to live in his house my whole life, to see his beauty and meditate in his Temple.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>When trouble comes, he’ll shelter conceal me; he’ll hide me in a secret place and station me on a <em>high </em>rock.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>I’ll hold up my head above my enemies; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>in his Tabernacle I’ll offer sacrifices with shouts of joy and sing praises to the LORD.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 27:4-6</p>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>Listen when I call out, LORD; be gracious and answer me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>You said, <em>“Seek me,”</em> <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>my heart and soul responded, <em>“I’ll seek you.”</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>Don’t ignore me or turn away angry. You’ve been my help; don’t abandon me, God my Savior.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>Even if my parents exposed me I <em>as an infant</em> <em>to die</em>, <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>you would pick me up.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 27:7-10</p>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>Teach me your way, LORD; lead me on a level path away from my enemies.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>Don’t turn me over to them, because lying witnesses have taken the stand against me, the kind that threatens violence with every breath.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup><strong>13</strong></sup>I’d have despaired if I hadn’t believed <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>I’d see the LORD’s goodness while I was alive.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup><strong>14</strong></sup><em>So </em>wait for him; be strong and courageous. Yes, wait for him.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 27:11-14</p>
