## Psalms Chapter 38

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> LORD, don’t rebuke me in your wrath</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Your arrows have pierced deep into me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Because of your indignation, my body’s not sound;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> My iniquities have piled up higher than my head</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> My wounds have festered and grown foul.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> I’m burning up with fever;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> I’m numb and crushed,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Lord, you know what I long for;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> My heart’s pounding, my strength’s failing;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> My friends and loved ones stand back from my sickness;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Those who want to kill me lay snares for me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> But like someone deaf, I don’t hear;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> Yes, I’m like somebody that can’t hear,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> I’m depending on you, LORD;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> Don’t let them celebrate over me</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> I’m ready to fall;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> I’m confessing my iniquity;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> But my enemies are vigorous and strong;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> Don’t leave me, LORD;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> Hurry and help me, Lord my savior!	Ps 38:13-22</span>
