## Psalms Chapter 145

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> I’ll praise you highly, my God and king,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> I’ll bless you every day</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> You’re great and deserve great praise;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> One generation will praise your works to the next</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> They’ll think about your majestic glory and splendor</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> People will talk about the power evident in your awesome deeds</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> They’ll overflow with the fame of your abundant goodness</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> The LORD is gracious and merciful,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> He’s good to everyone;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Everything he’s made will thank him;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> They’ll speak of the honor his kingdom has</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> His kingdom lasts forever;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> The LORD catches the ones that fall down</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> Everyone looks to him for help,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> He meets the needs of every living thing.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> The LORD shows his goodness in everything he does,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> He’s near to those who call on him,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> He provides for the people who respect him;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> He protects those who love him,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> I’ll speak praise to the LORD;</span>
