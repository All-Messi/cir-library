## Psalms Chapter 112

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Hallelujah! How blessed people are that respect the LORD and delight in what he calls on them to do.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Their descendants will be successful and a blessing to their own generation.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> They’ll have wealth and riches in their homes, and their goodness will last forever.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Godly people have light in the dark; they’re gracious, merciful, and good.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Goodness comes to those who graciously lend and conduct their affairs with justice.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Misfortune won’t overcome them, and people will remember the good they’ve done.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 112:1-6</p>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Bad news doesn’t scare people like that because they’ve committed themselves to the LORD they trust;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> they’re confident and courageous and face their adversaries with assurance.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> They give freely to the poor, so their goodness lasts forever in ongoing influence and honor.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Sinners will see that and get mad; they’ll grit their teeth—and leave; what they wanted won’t happen.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 112:7-10</p>
