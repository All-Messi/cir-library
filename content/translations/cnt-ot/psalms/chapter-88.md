## Psalms Chapter 88

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> LORD God who keeps me safe,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Let my prayer come to you;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> I’ve had so much trouble;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> I’m as good as dead,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Abandoned among the dead</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> You’ve put me in the Pit,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Your anger overwhelms me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> You’ve made my friends avoid me</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Do you perform wonders for the dead?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Do they declare your enduring love in the grave</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Are your wonders known in the dark</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> Why are you rejecting me?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> I’ve been attacked and close to death from my youth up;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> Your furious anger has swept over me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> They surround me like water all the time</span>
