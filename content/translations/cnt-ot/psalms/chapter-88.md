## Psalms Chapter 88

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> LORD God who keeps me safe, I call out to you day and night.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Let my prayer come to you; listen to my call!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> I’ve had so much trouble; I’m close to Sheol.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> I’m as good as dead, like someone with no strength left.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Abandoned among the dead like someone slain in the grave, I’m forgotten and cut off from you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> You’ve put me in the Pit, in regions dark and deep.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Your anger overwhelms me; wave after wave weighs heavily on me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> You’ve made my friends avoid me by making me someone they detest; I’m trapped and can’t escape.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> My sight is blurry from tears; I call on you every day, LORD, and reach out to you for help.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 88:1-9</p>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Do you perform wonders for the dead? Do departed spirits rise and praise you?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Do they declare your enduring love in the grave or your faithfulness in the place of destruction?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Are your wonders known in the dark or your goodness in the land of forgetfulness?</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 88:10-12</p>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> LORD, I call out to you for help; in the mornings my prayers go up to you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> Why are you rejecting me? Why are you hiding from me?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> I’ve been attacked and close to death from my youth up; I suffer from the terrors you bring; I’m overwhelmed.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> Your furious anger has swept over me; your dreaded assaults have destroyed me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> They surround me like water all the time and engulf me completely.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> You’ve removed lover and friend far from me; my companions lie in darkness.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 88:13-18</p>
