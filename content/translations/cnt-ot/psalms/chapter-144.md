## Psalms Chapter 144

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Bless the LORD, my Rock,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> He’s my enduring love and fortress,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> LORD, what are we that you give us any thought</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> We’re like a breath;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Lower the sky, LORD, and come down;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Throw your lightning bolts and scatter my enemies;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Reach down from on high</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> You deliver kings</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Deliver me from aliens that lie,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Make our sons in their youth like grown-up plants</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Fill our granaries with every kind of crop;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> Have our cattle deliver without miscarriage or failure to bear;</span>
