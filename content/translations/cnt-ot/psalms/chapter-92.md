## Psalms Chapter **92**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>It’s good to thank you, LORD, to sing praises to you, Most High;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>to declare your enduring love in the morning and your faithfulness at night</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>with a lute and a ten-stringed harp, with melody on a lyre.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>Because you’ve made me happy by what you’ve done, I’ll sing for joy over what you do.<strong>	</strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 92:1-4</p>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>Your deeds are so great, LORD, your thoughts so deep!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>Senseless people don’t realize, ignorant mortals don’t understand</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>that when sinners sprout up like grass and the wicked flourish, it’s only that they’ll be destroyed forever.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>But you’re on high forever.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>Your enemies will perish; everybody that sins will scatter.<strong>	</strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 92:5-9</p>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>You’ve made me as strong as a wild ox and anointed me with fresh oil.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>I’ve looked in triumph at my enemies and heard about <em>the defeat of</em> my attackers.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>Good people flourish like palm trees; they grow like cedars in Lebanon.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup><strong>13</strong></sup>Planted in the LORD’s Temple, they flourish in the courts of our God.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup><strong>14</strong></sup>They bear fruit even when they’re old; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>they’re still green and full of energy.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup><strong>15</strong></sup>They declare that the LORD is good, that he’s our rock; there’s nothing bad about him.<strong>	</strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 92:10-15</p>
