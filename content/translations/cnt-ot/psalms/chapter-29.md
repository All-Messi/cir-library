## Psalms Chapter **29**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Children of the mighty, attribute to the LORD honor and strength.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>Honor him like he deserves; worship him in his holy splendor.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 29:1-2</p>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>The sound from the LORD carries out over the water; our glorious God thunders across the ocean expanse.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>His voice is loud and majestic</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>and blows down cedars in Lebanon.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>He makes Lebanon skip like a calf and Sirion [<em>Mt. Hermon</em>] jump around like a young wild ox.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>The sound from the LORD flashes out lightning bolts</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>and shakes the Wilderness of Kadesh;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>it makes deer go into labor <strong><sup>	</sup></strong><strong><sup>	</sup></strong>and strips the trees bare. In his Temple everyone shouts, <em>“Glory!”</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>The LORD sits like a king above the flood, <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>enthroned forever.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>He gives his people courage and blesses them with peace.</span>
