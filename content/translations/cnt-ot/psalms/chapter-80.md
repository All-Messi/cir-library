## Psalms Chapter 80

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Listen, Shepherd of Israel,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Stir up your power against Ephraim, Benjamin, and Manasseh;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Restore us, God;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> LORD God of Hosts,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> You’ve fed us tears</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> You made us a taunt to our neighbors;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> God of Hosts, restore us;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> You took a grapevine out of Egypt,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> You cleared the ground for it;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> It covered the mountains with its shadow</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> It grew its branches all the way out to the Mediterranean Sea,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Why did you tear down its walls</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> A boar from the woods roots it up;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> God of Hosts, please come back now;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> Our enemies have cut it down and burned it;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> Encourage the man at your right hand,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> Then we won’t turn away from you anymore;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> LORD God of Hosts, restore us;</span>
