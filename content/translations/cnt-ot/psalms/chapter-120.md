## Psalms Chapter **120**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>In my troubles I appealed to the LORD, and he answered me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>LORD, rescue me from lying lips and treacherous tongues.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>What will God do to you, treacherous tongues? How will he intensify your punishment?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup><em>He’ll pierce you with a</em> soldier’s sharp arrows and burn you with coals from a broom tree.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>Misfortune awaits me because I’m a foreigner in Meshech, <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>because I live among the tents of Kedar!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>For too long I’ve lived where people hate peace.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>I’m for peace; but when I speak up, they’re for war.</span>
