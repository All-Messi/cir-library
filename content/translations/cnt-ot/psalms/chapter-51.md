## Psalms Chapter **51**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Be gracious to me, God, in proportion to your enduring love; according to your great mercy, blot out my transgressions.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>Wash me thoroughly from my sin; cleanse me from my iniquity.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>I know my failings; my sin always weighs on my mind.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>I’ve sinned against you and you only; I did what you consider evil; so you’re justified in what you say to me, blameless when you condemn me.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 51:1-4</p>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>I was born in sin; in iniquity my mother conceived me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>You want truth in the inward self; you’ll make me know wisdom in myself.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>Purify me with hyssop, and I’ll be clean; wash me, and I’ll be whiter than snow.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>Fill me with joy and happiness again, <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>and<strong><sup> </sup></strong>let the bones you broke mend up.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>Don’t look at my sins; blot out my iniquities.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 51:5-9</p>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>Create a clean heart in me, God; renew a steadfast spirit within me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>Don’t banish me from your presence or take your Holy Spirit away from me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>Restore to me the joy your salvation brings; grant me a willing attitude to obey.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup><strong>13</strong></sup>Then I’ll teach transgressors your ways; sinners will be converted to you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup><strong>14</strong></sup>Deliver me from being guilty of murder so I can joyfully sing about your goodness.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup><strong>15</strong></sup>LORD, open my lips so I can express your praises.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup><strong>16</strong></sup>You don’t delight in sacrifice; otherwise, I’d give it; you’re not pleased with burnt offerings.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup><strong>17</strong></sup>The sacrifice you want is a broken spirit; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong><strong><sup>	</sup></strong>you appreciate a repentant heart.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup><strong>18</strong></sup>In your good favor, do good to Zion; build up the walls around Jerusalem.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup><strong>19</strong></sup>Then you’ll delight in godly sacrifices, in burnt offerings and whole burnt offerings; then young bulls will again be offered on your altar.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 51:10-19</p>
