## Psalms Chapter **17**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>LORD; listen to my call for justice; pay attention to my sincere prayer.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>Declare me innocent <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>because you see that I’m righteous.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>You’ve visited me at night and tested my heart; you’ve put me on trial and found nothing wrong; I’m determined not to say anything sinful.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>Your word has kept me from following <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>the example of<strong><sup> </sup></strong>cruel people.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>My steps have stayed on your path; my feet haven’t slipped.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>I’m calling on you, God, <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>because you answer me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>Demonstrate your amazing, enduring love for the ones that take refuge in you from people that rebel against you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>Keep me as the pupil of <em>your </em>eye; hide me under your wings</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>from sinners who plunder, from deadly enemies that surround me,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>that have no mercy in their hearts, <strong><sup>	</sup></strong><strong><sup>	</sup></strong><strong><sup>	</sup></strong>and brag arrogantly.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>They’ve tracked me down and surrounded me, and threaten to knock me to the ground.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>They’re like lions eager to maul me, like young lions lurking in ambush.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 17:6-12</p>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup><strong>13</strong></sup>Confront them, LORD; knock <u>them</u> down; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>deliver me from sinners with the sword</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup><strong>14</strong></sup>that’s in your hand, from people who’ve cast their lot in this life, whose stomach you fill with your treasure. They’re satisfied with having children and leaving their wealth to their babies.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup><strong>15</strong></sup><em>But</em> because I’m righteous, <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>I’ll be satisfied by seeing you when I wake up.</span>
