## Psalms Chapter 22

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> My God, my God, why have you forsaken me?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> I call out in the daytime, but you don’t answer;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Yet you’re holy,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Our ancestors trusted in you, and you delivered them.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> They called out to you, and you saved them;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> But I’m a worm, not a man;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Everybody that sees me makes fun of me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Entrust yourself to the LORD; let him deliver you;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Yet you’re the One that delivered me from the womb</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> I’ve depended on you from birth;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Don’t be distant because trouble’s near;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Many bulls have surrounded me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> They bellow at me</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> I’m being poured out like water;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> My strength has dried up like a potsherd,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> Dogs have surrounded me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> I can count all my bones;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> Don’t be distant, LORD;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> Deliver me from the sword;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> I’ll tell my brothers about you</span>

<span class="verse" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> You that respect the LORD, praise him;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> The theme of my praise in the great assembly comes from you;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> The poverty-stricken will eat and be satisfied;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> The most distant places will remember and turn to the LORD;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> Prosperous people will eat and worship;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> Posterity will serve him;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> To people not yet born,</span>
