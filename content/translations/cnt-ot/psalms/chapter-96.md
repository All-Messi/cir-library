## Psalms Chapter 96

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Sing a new song to the LORD; sing to him, all the earth.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Bless him in song; proclaim every day the good news that he saves.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Tell about his glory the world over, his marvelous deeds among the nations.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> The LORD is great and deserves great praise; he’s to be respected more than all gods</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> because the nations’ gods are idols, but the LORD created the sky.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Honor and majesty lie in him; strength and beauty fill his sanctuary.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 96:1-6</p>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Families of the nations, attribute to the LORD honor and strength.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Attribute to the LORD the glory he deserves; take an offering to his courts.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Worship him in holy attire; tremble at him, all the earth.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Say among the nations, “The LORD reigns; yes, the world is firmly set; it won’t move; he’ll judge the nations fairly.”</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Let the heavens be happy and the earth rejoice; let the ocean roar and everything that’s in it;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> let the fields celebrate and all that grows there; then the trees in the forest will sing 13to the LORD because he’ll judge the world with justice and its people with truth.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 96:7-13</p>
