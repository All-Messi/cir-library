## Psalms Chapter 118

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Be grateful to the LORD because he’s good; his love lasts forever.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Let Israel say, “His love lasts forever.”</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Let the house of Aaron say, “His love lasts forever.”</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Let those who respect the LORD say, “His love lasts forever.”</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 118:1-4</p>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> I called on the LORD in my distress, and he answered me and set me free.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> He’s on my side; so I won’t be afraid; what can mortals do to me?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> He’s beside me to help me; so I look in triumph at those who hate me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> It’s better to take refuge in the LORD than trust in people,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> to take refuge in him than put trust in rulers.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 118:5-9</p>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> All the nations surrounded me; in the LORD’s name I destroyed them.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> They surrounded me on every side; in the LORD’s name I destroyed them.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> They swarmed around me like bees; they stung like burning briers; in the LORD’s name I destroyed them.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> They shoved me back so hard I started to fall, but the LORD came to my aid.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> He’s my strength and song; he’s my salvation.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 118:10-14</p>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> Joyful shouting over deliverance goes on in the tents of good people; the LORD’s right hand has done valiantly.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> He has lifted high his right hand; His right hand has done mighty things.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> I’ll live to tell what he’s done.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> He disciplined me severely, but he didn’t let me die.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 118:15-18</p>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> Open the gate to goodness for me so I can go in and thank the LORD.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> This is his gate; good people enter through it.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> Thank you for answering me; you’ve become my salvation.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 118:19-21</p>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> The stone the builders rejected has become the cornerstone.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> That’s the LORD’s doing; it’s amazing.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> Today is a day the LORD has made; let’s celebrate and be happy in it.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> LORD, save us and send us prosperity.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> Blessed is the one that comes in the LORD’s name; we bless you from his Temple.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> The LORD is God; he gives us light; tie the festival sacrifice to the horns of the altar.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> You’re my God; I thank you; you’re my God; I praise you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> Give thanks to the LORD because he’s good; his love lasts forever.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 118:22-29</p>
