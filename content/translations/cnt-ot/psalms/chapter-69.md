## Psalms Chapter 69

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Save me, God;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> I’ve sunk in deep mud, where there’s no foothold;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> I’m worn out from crying; my throat’s dry;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> The people that hate me for no reason</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> God, you know about my foolish choices;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> May those who put their hope in you</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> I’m alienated from my brothers;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> I’m consumed with zeal for your Temple;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> When I humble myself with fasting, they mock me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Those who sit in the city gate talk about me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> But, I pray to you, LORD;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> Save me from sinking in the mud;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> May the flood not overwhelm me</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> Answer me, LORD, because your enduring love is good;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> Don’t abandon me,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> Come close to my soul and redeem it;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> You know my disgrace, shame, and dishonor;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> Insults have broken my heart; I’m heartsick.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> Instead, they gave me gall to eat and vinegar to drink.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> May their table become a snare</span>

<span class="verse" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> May their eyes go blind</span>

<span class="verse" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> Pour out indignation on them;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> Make their camp desolate</span>

<span class="verse" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> They persecute the one you yourself have struck;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> Pile their sins high;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> Erase them from the Book of Life;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> I’m miserable with pain;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> I’ll praise God with song</span>

<span class="verse" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> That will please the LORD more</span>

<span class="verse" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> The humble will see it and be happy;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> The LORD listens to the needy;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> Let earth and sky praise him,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> God will save Zion and rebuild the towns in Judah</span>

<span class="verse" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> The descendants of his servants will inherit it;</span>
