## Psalms Chapter **40**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>I waited patiently for the LORD; he listened to my call <em>for help</em>.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>He brought me up out of the pit of despair, out of the muddy clay, set my feet on solid rock, and made my footsteps secure.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>He put a new song in me, a song about praising God; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>other people will hear it, see what he’s done, and trust in him.<strong>	</strong></span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>How blessed people are that trust in the LORD <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>instead of turning to proud or deceitful people.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>LORD my God, you’ve done so many amazing things; your good intentions for us are too many to tell. Nobody compares with you; if I tried to tell about <em>your wonderful acts</em>, I couldn’t recount them all.<strong> </strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 40:1-5</p>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>Your don’t delight in sacrifices and meal offerings; you haven’t required burnt offerings and sin offerings; you’ve pierced my ears. [you gave <u>me</u> a body<sup>LXX</sup>.]</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>Then I said, <em>“I</em><em>’m coming</em><em>;</em> <em>	</em><em>	</em><em>it’s written about me</em><em> </em><em>in the scroll</em><em>;</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup><em>I delight </em><em>in</em><em> do</em><em>ing</em><em> your will, my God;</em> <em>	</em><em>	I have your Law at</em><em> heart.”</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>In the great assembly <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>I’ve proclaimed the good news about goodness I didn’t hold back</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>or keep to myself the truth about your goodness; I spoke openly about your saving power, about your enduring love and truth.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 40:6-10</p>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>LORD, don’t withhold your mercy from me; let your enduring love and truth keep protecting me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>Troubles beyond number surround me, and my own sins overtake me till I can’t<em> </em>see <em>any way to escape them</em>.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup><strong>13</strong></sup>LORD, please hurry and help me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup><strong>14</strong></sup>Embarrass and humiliate those who keep trying to destroy me; disgrace the ones that delight in hurting me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup><strong>15</strong></sup>Make them appalled at their disgrace who say to me, <em>“Aha, aha!”</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup><strong>16</strong></sup>Make them happy who look to you, who love your salvation and chant, <em>“</em><em>T</em><em>he </em><em>L</em><em>ORD</em><em> </em><em>is</em><em> </em><em>great</em><em>.”</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup><strong>17</strong></sup>Keep me in your thoughts <strong><sup>	</sup></strong><strong><sup>	</sup></strong>because I’m in misery and need you, my helper, my deliverer. Don’t delay.<strong>	</strong><strong>	</strong></span>
