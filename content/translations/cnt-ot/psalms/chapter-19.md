## Psalms Chapter **19**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>The heavens show God’s glory; the sky displays his workmanship.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>Day in and day out <em>they</em> speak; night after night they display intelligence.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>There’s no speech or words; there’s nothing to hear.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>It’s like a groom coming out of his room, excited like a man in shape to run a race.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>It rises at one side of the sky and makes its circuit to the other; nothing escapes its warmth.<strong>	</strong><strong>	</strong></span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>The Law of the LORD is perfect for reviving the soul; his Testimony is reliable for making simple people wise.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>The precepts of the LORD are right, bringing happiness; his commandments are clear, enlightening the mind.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup><strong>14</strong></sup>May what I think and say <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>be acceptable to you, LORD, my Rock, my Redeemer.</span>
