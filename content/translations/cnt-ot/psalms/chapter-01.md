## Psalms Chapter **1**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>People are blessed that don’t take advice from the wicked or stand among sinners or sit with scoffers,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>but delight in the Law of the LORD and meditate on it day and night.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>They’re like trees planted beside a river that bear fruit in season, whose leaves don’t wither. They’re successful at what they do.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>Sinners aren’t like that: they’re like chaff that wind blows away;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>they won’t withstand judgment or stand among the godly.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>The LORD watches over the godly, but the wicked perish.</span>
