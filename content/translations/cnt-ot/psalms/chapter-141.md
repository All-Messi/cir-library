## Psalms Chapter **141**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>LORD, I’m calling on you; come quick; hear me out.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>Consider my prayer as incense and the raising of my hands as an evening offering.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>Guard my mouth; watch over my lips.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>Incline my heart away from anything evil, from practicing sinful things with sinners.<strong>	</strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 141:1-4</p>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>Let a godly person strike me or rebuke me graciously; that would be ointment for my head; Don’t let me refuse it. I still pray constantly against the deeds of sinners.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>When their leaders are thrown over the cliffs; they’ll learn that the LORD’s word is true. <strong><sup>7</sup></strong><em>“Like someone plowing up rocks,</em> <em>	our bones have been scattered at the mouth of Sheol.”</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>I look to you, Lord GOD, <strong>	</strong>and take refuge in you; don’t leave me with no defense<strong>. </strong></span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>Keep me from the jaws of the trap they’ve set for me, from sinners’ snares;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>may they stumble into their own nets while I go on by safely.<strong>	</strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 141:5-10</p>
