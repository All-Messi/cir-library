## Psalms Chapter **63**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>God, you’re my God; I look to you in earnest; my soul thirsts for you; my flesh yearns for you in this dry, weary land.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>So I’ve looked at you in the sanctuary and seen your power and glory. <strong><sup>3</sup></strong> I’ll praise you because your enduring love is better than life;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>I’ll bless you as long as I live and lift up my hands in your name.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>You satisfy my soul like the finest food; I offer praises gladly.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 63:1-5</p>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>I remember you when I’m in bed; I think about you during the night.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>You’ve been my help; I sing for joy in the shadow of your wings.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>My soul clings to you; your right hand upholds me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>But those who are trying to destroy me <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>will end up in the ground.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>The sword will overwhelm them; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>they’ll become prey for foxes.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>But the king will celebrate in God; everybody that swears by him will glory <em>while </em>liars’ mouths will be stopped.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 63:6-11</p>
