## Psalms Chapter 81

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Sing to God your Strength;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Sing, beat the tambourine,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> At the new moons blow your ram’s horn,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> It’s a statute for Israel,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> He established it as a Testimony in Joseph</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> I’ll take the burden off your shoulders</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> But my people ignored me;</span>
