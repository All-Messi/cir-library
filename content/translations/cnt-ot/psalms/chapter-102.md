## Psalms Chapter 102

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Listen to my prayer, LORD!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Don’t turn away from me in my distress;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> My days dissipate like smoke;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> My heart is weak and withers away like grass;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> I’m like a pelican in the desert,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> I lie awake</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> My enemies taunt me all day;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> I eat ashes for food</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> My days are like a shadow;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> But you live forever, LORD;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Have mercy on Zion;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> Your servants delight in its very rocks</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> So the nations will respect the LORD;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> The LORD will build up Zion;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> He’ll have regard for the destitute;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> Write this for the next generation,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> He looked down from his holy height;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> He weakened my strength</span>

<span class="verse" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> I say to him,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> You founded the earth long ago;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> Even they will perish, but you’ll remain;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> But you’ll be the same; your years won’t end.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> Your people’s children will continue in security;</span>
