## Psalms Chapter **61**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Listen to my prayer, God; hear my prayer.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>With a faint heart I call to you from the end of the earth; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>lead me to the rock that’s higher than me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>You’re my refuge, <em>my</em> strong tower against the enemy.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>Let me live in your tent forever and take shelter under your wings.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>You’ve heard my vows, God, and given <em>me</em> the inheritance that comes to those who respect you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>Lengthen the king’s life; make his lifetime like many generations.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>Have him sit enthroned in your presence forever; appoint enduring love and truth to preserve him.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>I’ll sing praise to you forever as I pay my vows each day.</span>
