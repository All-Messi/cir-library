## Leviticus Chapter 9


### The Priests Begin Their Service

<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told Aaron, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:10.0pt;margin-left:1.0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Take a bull calf for a sin offering and a ram for a burnt offering—both without defect—and offer them to the L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.
</span></i><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then tell the Israelites,
</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:10.0pt;margin-left:.25in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            ‘Take a male goat for a sin offering, </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:10.0pt;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>a year-old calf and lamb without defect for a burnt offering, </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:10.0pt;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>an ox and ram for peace offerings, and</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:10.0pt;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>a grain offering mixed with olive oil </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:10.0pt;margin-left:.25in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    because the L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> is going to appear to you today.’”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" ><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They took what Moses commanded to the front of the Tent of Meeting. The whole community came and stood in front of the </span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Moses said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“The L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> has told you to do that so his glory can appear to you.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told Aaron, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Come to the altar and offer your sin offering and burnt offering so you can atone for yourself. <p class="section-ref">Lev 9:1-7</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" ><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Aaron came to the altar and slaughtered the calf for the <u>sin offering for himself</u>.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His sons handed him the blood. <span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then he offered up in smoke the fat, kidneys, and lobe of the liver; </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>but he burned the meat and skin outside the camp.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" ><a name="_Hlk126818088"><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He slaughtered the <u>burnt offering</u>. <span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They handed him the burnt offering in pieces with the head, and he offered </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>them</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> up in smoke. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He washed the entrails and legs and offered </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>them</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> up in smoke with the burnt offering.
</span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Lev 9:8-14</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" ><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  </span><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He presented the <u>people’s offering</u>. <span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He also presented the burnt offering as required by the Law. </span></span>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" ><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then he slaughtered the ox and the ram, <u>peace offerings</u> for the people. <span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19-20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>On the breasts they placed the fat from the ox and the ram—the fat tail, fat covering, kidneys, and lobe of the liver. He offered them up in smoke. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But Aaron presented the breasts and right thigh as a <u>wave offering</u> to the <i>L</i></span><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>—like Moses had commanded.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" ><a name="_Hlk126818178"><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Aaron lifted up his hands toward the people and blessed them. <span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He and Moses went into the Tent of Meeting. <span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Fire came out from the </span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> and consumed the burnt offering and the portions of fat on the altar.
