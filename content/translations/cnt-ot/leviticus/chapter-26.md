## Leviticus Chapter 26

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <em>“Don’t make idols, set up images or </em>sacred<em> pillars, or put stone figurines in your country to bow to. </em><strong><sup>2</sup></strong><em>Keep my Sabbaths and reverence my sanctuary. </em><strong><sup>3</sup></strong><em>If you follow my statutes and keep my commandments, </em><strong><sup>4</sup></strong><em>I’ll give you rain in its season so the ground will grow crops and the trees will bear fruit. </em><strong><sup>5</sup></strong><em>Threshing time will last till grape harvest, and grape harvest will last till planting season. You’ll have plenty to eat, and you’ll live securely in your country. </em><strong><sup>6</sup></strong><em>I’ll grant peace so you can lie down without feeling afraid.
