### 10“If your burnt offering is a sheep or goat from the flock, it’ll be without defect. 11Slaughter it on the north side of the altar in front of the LORD, and the sons of Aaron will sprinkle its blood around on the altar. 12Then butcher it with its head and fat, and the priests will arrange the pieces on the wood on the altar. 13You’ll wash the entrails and legs, and the priest will offer it up in smoke for a burnt offering on the altar, an aroma pleasing to the LORD.	 Lev 1:10-13 Burnt Offering of Birds 14“If it’s a burnt offering of birds, bring an offering of a turtledove or young pigeon. 15The priest will take it to the altar, wring it neck, and offer it up in smoke. He’ll drain out its blood on the side of the altar, 16take away its crop and feathers, and cast it on the east side of the altar toward the place for the ashes. 17Then he’ll tear it by its wings—but not cut it in two—and offer it up in smoke on the altar.
