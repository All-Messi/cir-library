## Leviticus Chapter 13


### Tests for Serious Skin Diseases

<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But if the discoloration doesn’t appear to go that deep and the hair on it hasn’t turned white, the priest will quarantine you for a week </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and examine it on the seventh day. If the spot has changed or spread, he’ll quarantine you for another week </span></i></span>

<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>it’s chronic leprosy. He won’t quarantine because you’re </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>definitely</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> unclean. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If the leprosy spreads from head to toe, </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>he’ll pronounce you clean if it has all turned to normal skin color. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But if open sores appear, </span></i></span>

<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If the open sores turn to normal skin color again, </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the priest will pronounce you clean.                                                                                                   </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:10.0pt;margin-left:.5in;'><a name="_Hlk126819770"><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> </a><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“When a boil heals </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and a discoloration or reddish-white swelling comes up in its place, show it to the priest. </span></i></span>

<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But if there aren’t any white hairs in it, it’s not deeper than the skin, and it’s faded, you’ll quarantine for a week. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If it spreads, the priest will pronounce you unclean; it’s an infection. </span></i></span>

</span></i></span></span></p>

<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If there’s no white hair in the bright spot and it’s no deeper than the skin, but has faded, you’ll quarantine for a week. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The priest will examine you on the seventh day. If it spreads on the skin, he’ll declare you unclean; it’s leprosy. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But if the bright spot doesn’t spread, but has faded, it’s the swelling from the burn. </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:10.0pt;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“If any of you, man or woman, has an infection on the head—or in the beard, </span></i></span>

<span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But if it appears to be no deeper than the skin and there’s no black hair in it, quarantine for a week. </span></i></span>

<span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>then shave except for the spot and quarantine for another week. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>On the seventh day, the priest will examine it. <span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If the spot spreads after that cleansing, </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the priest need not look for yellowish hair; you’re </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>definitely<i> unclean. </i></span></span>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:10.0pt;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v38" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“If any of you, man or woman, has a white bright spot on the skin, </span></i></span>

<span class="verse verse-v48" id="v48"><sup class="verse-num">48</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>as well as on any leather article, </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v49" id="v49"><sup class="verse-num">49</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>if the mark is greenish or reddish, show it to the priest. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v50" id="v50"><sup class="verse-num">50</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’ll examine it and quarantine it for a week. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v51" id="v51"><sup class="verse-num">51</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then he’ll examine it on the seventh day. If it has spread, it’s infectious and unclean. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v52" id="v52"><sup class="verse-num">52</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Burn it.                                         </span></i><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Lev 13:45-52</span></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>
</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:10.0pt;margin-left:.5in;'><a name="_Hlk126819925"><span class="verse verse-v53" id="v53"><sup class="verse-num">53</sup> </a><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“But if the spot hasn’t spread, </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v54" id="v54"><sup class="verse-num">54</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the priest will have them wash it. He’ll quarantine it for another week </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v55" id="v55"><sup class="verse-num">55</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and examine it again.
