## Leviticus Chapter 23

<span class="verse" id="v1"><strong><sup>-2</sup></strong>The LORD told Moses to tell Israel, <strong>Sabbaths</strong> <em>“The </em><em>L</em><em>ORD</em><em> has appointed these times: </em><strong><sup>3</sup></strong><em>six days you can work, but the seventh day is a Sabbath for complete rest, a holy convocation. It’s a Sabbath to the </em><em>L</em><em>ORD</em><em> wherever you live. </em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <em>Along with the bread, present 7 year-old male lambs without defect, a bull from the herd, and 2 rams. They’ll be a burnt offering along with their grain offering and liquid offerings. </em>It’s <em>an offering by fire of an aroma pleasing to the </em><em>L</em><em>ORD</em><em>. </em><strong><sup>19</sup></strong><em>Also offer 1 male goat for a sin offering and 2 yearling male lambs for a peace offering. </em><strong><sup>20</sup></strong><em>The priest will wave them with the bread from the first part of the harvest for a wave offering with 2 lambs in front of the </em><em>L</em><em>ORD</em><em>. They’re holy to the </em><em>L</em><em>ORD</em><em> for the priest. </em><strong><sup>21</sup></strong><em>On that same day, make a proclamation. Hold a holy convocation. Don’t work. It’s an ongoing permanent statute wherever you live. </em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> The LORD told Moses, <strong><sup>27</sup></strong><strong><sup>-28</sup></strong><em>“T</em><em>he tenth day</em><em>, seventh month, is The Day of A</em><em>tonement</em><em>, </em><em>a holy convocation.</em><em> Fast and</em><em> </em><em>don’t work; </em><em>present an offering by fire to the </em><em>L</em><em>ORD</em><em> to atone for yourselves to the </em><em>L</em><em>ORD</em><em> your God. </em><strong><sup>29</sup></strong><em>Any of you that won’t fast </em><strong><sup>30</sup></strong><em>and refrain </em><em>from working that day, I’ll destroy from among the people. </em><strong><sup>31</sup></strong><em>It’s a permanent statute wherever you live. </em><strong><sup>32</sup></strong><em>On the ninth of the month at evening, rest till the next evening.”</em><em> </em><em>	 </em><strong>Lev 23:26-32</strong> <strong>Feast of Tabernacle</strong><em>	</em><em>	     </em> <strong><sup>33</sup></strong><strong><sup>-34</sup></strong>Again the LORD told Moses to tell Israel, <em>“The fifteenth day, seventh month is the Festival of Booths to the </em><em>L</em><em>ORD</em><em> </em><em>for a week. </em><strong><sup>35</sup></strong><em>The first day is a holy convocation. Don’t do any kind of work. </em><strong><sup>36</sup></strong><em>For a week, present an offering by fire to the </em><em>L</em><em>ORD</em><em>. On the eighth day, hold a holy convocation and present an offering by fire to the </em><em>L</em><em>ORD</em><em>. </em></span>
