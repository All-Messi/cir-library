## Leviticus Chapter 7


### Guilt Offering


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:10.0pt;margin-left:.5in;'><a name="_Hlk126790868"><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> </a><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“This is the law for the <u>guilt offering</u>; it’s most holy<b>. </b></span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They’re to kill the guilt offering where they kill the burnt offering. </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The priest </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>will sprinkle its blood around on the altar. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then he’ll offer its fat: the fat tail, the fat that covers the entrails, </span></i></span>

<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The priest will offer them up in smoke on the altar as an offering by fire to the L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.
</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Any male among the priests can eat it in a holy place. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The same law applies to the guilt offering and sin offering. The priest that makes atonement with it will have it. </span></i></span>

<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Every grain offering that’s baked in an oven or prepared in a pan or on a griddle is for the priest that presents it. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Every grain offering, dry or mixed with olive oil, will be for any of Aaron’s descendants.    </span></i></span></span></p>

<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If you offer it as a thanksgiving, then along with it, offer cakes without yeast mixed with olive oil, wafers without yeast anointed with olive oil, and cakes of stirred fine flour mixed with olive oil. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Present your offering with cakes of bread without yeast. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Present one of each offering as a contribution to the L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>. <span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If it’s an offering for fulfilling a vow or a freewill offering, eat it that day or the next. </span></i></span>

<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If you eat meat from it on the third day, you will not be accepted. It’s an offense that makes you guilty.</span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Lev 7:15-18</p>

</span></i></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Lev 7:19-21</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:10.0pt;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22-23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The
</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> told Moses to tell Israel,</span></span></p>

<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Whoever eats the fat of an animal offered by fire to the L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> will be excluded from the community. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t eat the blood of any bird or animal at home. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Whoever does, will be excluded from the community.”    </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Lev 7:22-27</p>

</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> told Moses to say to Israel,</span></span></p>

<span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The priest will offer up the fat in smoke on the altar, but the breast will be for Aaron and his sons. </span></i></span>
