## Lamentations Chapter 5

<span class="verse" id="v1"><strong><sup>1</sup></strong>Yahveh, remember what’s happened to us; look at our disgrace!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><strong><sup>2</sup></strong>Our inheritance has been turned over to foreigners, and our houses <em>belong </em>to aliens.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><strong><sup>3</sup></strong>We’ve become orphans without fathers; our mothers are like widows.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><strong><sup>4</sup></strong>We have to pay for drinking water and buy wood.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><strong><sup>5</sup></strong>Our drivers <em>put yokes</em> on our necks; we’re worn out; we can’t get any rest.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Lam 5:1-5</p>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><strong><sup>6</sup></strong>We’ve submitted to Egypt and Assyria to get enough bread.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><strong><sup>7</sup></strong>Our fathers sinned, and they’re gone; <em>but </em>we have to suffer for their sins.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><strong><sup>8</sup></strong>Slaves rule us; nobody can deliver us from them.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><strong><sup>9</sup></strong>We risk our lives <em>just</em> getting bread because of the sword in the open country.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><strong><sup>0</sup></strong>Our skin is hot like an oven, because of the burning heat of famine.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><strong><sup>1</sup></strong>They rape the women in Zion, and the virgins in the towns in Judah.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Lam 5:6-11</p>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><strong><sup>2</sup></strong>They hang up officials by their hands, and show no respect for old men.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><strong><sup>3</sup></strong>Young men work at the grinding mill; and stagger under loads of wood.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><strong><sup>4</sup></strong>Elders are gone from the city gate, young men <em>have quit</em> their music.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><strong><sup>5</sup></strong>The joy in our hearts is gone; our dancing has turned into mourning.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><strong><sup>6</sup></strong>The crown has fallen from our head; misfortune awaits us because we’ve sinned!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><strong><sup>7</sup></strong>Our heart is faint because of it; our eyes are weak.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><strong><sup>8</sup></strong>Jackals prowl in desolate Zion.<strong> </strong><strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Lam 5:12-18</p>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><strong><sup>9</sup></strong>Yahveh, you rule forever; your throne goes on for endless generations.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><strong><sup>0</sup></strong>Why don’t you keep us in mind forever? Why abandon us so long?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><strong><sup>1</sup></strong>Restore us to yourself, Yahveh, so we can be restored; renew our days as of old,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><strong><sup>22</sup></strong>unless you’ve completely rejected us, and are really angry with us.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Lam 5:19-22</p>
