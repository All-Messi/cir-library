## Lamentations Chapter 5

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> LORD, remember what's happened to us;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Our inheritance has been turned over to foreigners,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> We've become orphans without fathers;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> We have to pay for drinking water and buy wood.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Our drivers put yokes on our necks;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> We've submitted to Egypt and Assyria to get enough bread.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Our fathers sinned, and they're gone,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Slaves rule us; nobody can deliver us from them.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> We risk our lives just getting bread</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Our skin is hot like an oven,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> They rape the women in Zion</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> They hang up officials by their hands</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Young men work at the grinding mill</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> Elders are gone from the city gate,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> The joy in our hearts is gone;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> The crown has fallen from our head;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> Our heart is faint because of it;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> Jackals prowl in desolate Zion.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> LORD, you rule forever;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> Why don't you keep us in mind forever?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> Restore us to yourself, LORD, so we can be restored;</span>
