## Lamentations Chapter 2

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> How the Lord in his anger has covered Zion under a cloud!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Without mercy the Lord has swallowed up the residents in Jacob.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> In his fury he has cut down Israel's power</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> He has bent his bow like an enemy;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> The Lord has become like an enemy;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> He's violently treated his Tabernacle like a garden shed</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> The Lord has rejected his altar, disowned his sanctuary,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> The LORD decided to destroy the wall around Zion.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Its gates have sunk into the ground;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> The elders of Zion sit silent on the ground;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> My eyes are worn out from crying;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> They ask their mothers, "Where is the bread and wine?"</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> What can I say for you?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> Your prophets have deceived you with false visions;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> Everybody that goes by claps their hands at you;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> Your enemies mock you;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> The LORD has accomplished his purpose</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> Their heart appealed to the Lord,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> Get up, cry out loud at the beginning of the night watches;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> See, LORD, look! Who else have you ever dealt with this way?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> Young and old lie on the ground in the streets;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> You invited terrors on every side of me as to an appointed festival.</span>
