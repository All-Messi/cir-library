## 1 Chronicles Chapter 23


### Duties of Levites


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When David got old, he appointed Solomon king over Israel. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He gathered the leaders with the priests and Levites. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Levites 30 years old and up numbered 38,000. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Of these, 24,000 oversaw the work, 6,000 were officers and judges, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>4,000 were gatekeepers, and 4,000 praised the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> with instruments David made for that purpose. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He divided them according to the sons of Levi: Gershon, Kohath, and Merari. <b>    </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>1 Ch 23:1-6</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sons of <u>Gershon</u> were Ladan and Shimei. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>      </span><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Three sons of Ladan were Jehiel the oldest, Jetham, and Joel. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>      </span><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Three sons of Shimei were Shelomoth, Haziel, and Haran. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            These were the heads of families in Ladan/<i>Libni</i>.
</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10-11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The other sons of Shimei were Jahath, first; Zina/<i>Zizah</i>, second. Jeush and Beriah didn’t have very many sons, so they were all counted as one family.</span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> 1 Ch 23:7-11</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sons of <u>Kohath</u> were Amram, Izhar, Hebron, and Uzziel.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         13</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sons of Amram were Aaron and Moses. </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  </span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Aaron and his sons were set apart permanently to sanctify the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> as most </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        holy, to burn incense to him, to perform service to him, and to bless in his </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.25in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    name.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  14</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sons of Moses, the man of God, were included in the tribe of Levi.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  15</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sons of Moses were Gershom and Eliezer.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                           16</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The son of Gershom was Shebuel the chief.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                           17</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The only son of Eliezer was Rehabiah.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    </span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Rehabiah had lots of sons.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      </span><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The son of Izhar was Shelomith the chief.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      </span><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sons of Hebron were Jeriah, first; then Amariah, then Jahaziel, then Jekameam.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         20</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sons of Uzziel were Micah, first, then Isshiah.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The descendants of <u>Merari</u> were Mahli and Mushi.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         </span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sons of Mahli were Eleazar and Kish.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Eleazar died and had only daughters, so their cousins, Kish’s sons, married them.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      </span><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sons of Mushi were Mahli, Eder, and Jeremoth.<b>                                </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>1 Ch 23:12-23</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Those were Levi’s descendants by heads of families as counted in their census. They served in the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>’s Temple from 20 years old and up. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>David said, <i>“The </i><i>L</i></span><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> God of Israel has given his people rest, and he lives in Jerusalem permanently.
</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Levites won’t need to carry the Tabernacle and the utensils for its service.” </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By David’s last words, Levi’s descendants were counted from 20 years old and up. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They were to assist Aaron’s descendants with the service in the courts and rooms in the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>’s Temple. They were to purify the holy things </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and help with the showbread, the fine flour for grain offerings, wafers without yeast, what’s baked in the pan, or what’s well-mixed, and all measures of volume and size. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They were to stand each morning and evening to thank and praise the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They offered burnt offerings on Sabbaths, new moons, and scheduled festivals in the number set by the Law. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They maintained the Tent of Meeting and the Holy Place and helped Aaron’s descendants with the service in the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>’s house.</span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>                                                                                                         </span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>                                                                                                                            1 Ch 23:24-32</span></b></p>
