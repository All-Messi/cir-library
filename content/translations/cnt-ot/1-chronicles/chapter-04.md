<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Naarah bore him Ahuzzam, Hepher, Temeni, and Haahashtari. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sons of Helah <i>were</i> Zereth, Izhar, Ethnan,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         </span><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and Koz, ancestor of Anub and Zobebah, and the families of Aharhel son of Harum.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         </span><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A man named </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>Jabez had more honor than his brothers, and his mother named him Jabez because she bore him “in pain.” </span></span>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         </span><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Chelub brother of Shuhah was father of Mehir father of <u>Eshton</u>.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  </span><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Eshton was father of Beth-rapha, Paseah, and Tehinnah father of Irnahash. These </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         </span><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sons of Kenaz <i>were</i> <u>Othniel</u> and <u>Seraiah</u>.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  The son of Othniel was Hathath.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         </span><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>14b</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Seraiah was father of Joab, founder of Ge-harashim, so-called because they were craftsmen.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         </span><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>14a</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Meonothai was father of Ophrah.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         </span><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sons of Caleb son of Jephunneh <i>were</i> Iru, <u>Elah</u>, and Naam.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  The son of Elah <i>was</i> Kenaz.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         </span><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sons of Jehallelel <i>were</i> Ziph and Ziphah, Tiria and Asarel.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         </span><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sons of Ezrah <i>were </i>Jether, <u>Mered</u>, Epher, and Jalon. <span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His Jewish wife bore Jered father of Gedor, Heber father of Soco, and Jekuthiel father of Zanoah. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sons of the wife of Hodiah sister of Naham were the fathers of Keilah the Garmite and Eshtemoa the Maacathite. <b>                                                                                  </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>1 Ch 4:11-19</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         </span><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sons of Shimon <i>were</i> Amnon and Rinnah, Benhanan and Tilon. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         The sons of Ishi <i>were </i>Zoheth and Benzoheth. </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         </span><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sons of Shelah son of Judah <i>were</i> Er father of Lecah, Laadah father of Mareshah, the families of the linen workers at Beth-ashbea, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jokim the men of Cozeba, Joash Saraph, who ruled in Moab; and Jashubi-lehem. The records are ancient. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>These were the potters and people of Netaim and Gederah. They lived there with the king for his work.</span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                   1 Ch 4:20-23</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Shaul was </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>father of Shallum father of Mibsam</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>father of <u>Mishma</u>.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Mishma was father of Hammuel, Zaccur, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> Shimei.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                      </span><span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Shimei had 16 sons and 6 daughters, but his brothers didn’t have </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                                  many sons. Their whole family didn’t multiply like the </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                                  descendants of Judah. </span><span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They lived in Beersheba, Moladah, and Hazar-shual, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>at Bilhah, Ezem, Tolad, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Bethuel, Hormah, Ziklag, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Beth-Marcaboth, Hazar-susim, Beth-Birei, and Shaaraim. Those were their towns till David’s reign. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Their villages </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>were</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> Etam, Ain, Rimmon, Tochen, and Ashan—5 towns, </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Meshobab, Jamlech, Joshah son of Amaziah; </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joel, Jehu son of Joshibiah son of Seraiah son of Asiel; </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Elioenai, Jaakobah, Jeshohaiah, Asaiah, Adiel, Jesimiel, Benaiah, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v37" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ziza son of Shiphi son of Allon son of Jedaiah son of Shimri son of Shemaiah: </span></span>
