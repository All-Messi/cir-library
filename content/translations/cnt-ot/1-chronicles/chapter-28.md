## 1 Chronicles Chapter 28

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>David gathered to Jerusalem the officials in Israel, the leaders of the tribes, the commanders of the divisions that served the king, the commanders over thousands and hundreds, the overseers of the property and livestock that belong to the king and his sons, the officials and elite soldiers, all the brave warriors. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He stood up and said, </span></span></p>

<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>From my sons he’s chosen Solomon to sit on the throne. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told me, ‘Solomon will build my temple and courts. I’ve chosen him to be my son, and I’ll be his father. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll make his kingdom permanently secure if he’s resolute in keeping my commandments and ordinances, like he is today.’ </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In front of Israel, in the assembly of the </span></i><i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>, and in the hearing of our God, endeavor to follow all his commandments so you can keep the good land and leave it for your descendants.</span></i></span></p>
