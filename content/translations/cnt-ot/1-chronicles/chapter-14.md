### Philistines Defeated

He heard about it and went out against them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They’d made a raid in the Valley of Rephaim. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>David asked God, <i>“Should I attack the Philistines? <span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They went up to Baal-perazim, and David defeated them. <span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Philistines </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>abandoned their gods there; David had them burned.</span></span></p>
