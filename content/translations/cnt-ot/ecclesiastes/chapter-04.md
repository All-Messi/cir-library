## Ecclesiastes Chapter 4

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:9.0pt; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><a name="_Hlk122424564"><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then I looked at the oppression that’s under the sun and saw the tears of the oppressed that have no comforters because their oppressors have the power. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So I decided the dead are better off than the living, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and the ones that never existed are better off yet. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:9.0pt; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I saw that competition between people drives work and achievement. That's pointless too, like chasing the wind. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Fools fold their hands, so their bodies atrophy. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>One handful with quietness is better than two with toil—and chasing the wind.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:9.0pt; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I saw something else that’s pointless under the sun: </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>people with nobody that needs them—offspring or sibling, yet they work as hard as they can to get wealth, but to no end because wealth doesn’t satisfy them: <i>“Who am I working for? </i>Who am I<i> depriving myself of enjoyment for?” </i>It’s all meaningless and depressing.</span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Eccl 4:1-8</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:9.0pt; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Two are better than one because they can get more done. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If either falls, the other can help; misfortune awaits whoever doesn’t have anybody that can help. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Besides, if two lie down together, they can keep warm; how can one keep warm alone? </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A bully can overpower someone that’s alone, but two can resist. A cord with three strands doesn’t break easily. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:9.0pt; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A poor, wise youth is better off than a foolish old king who doesn’t know how to accept instruction anymore. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A youth </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>can come out of prison to become king, though he was born in poverty. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But I’ve seen people throng to the next young man to replace him, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and there’s no end to his successors. But people won’t be happy with those successors either. That whole scenario is pointless, too, like chasing the wind.</span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Eccl 4:9-16</p>
