## Ecclesiastes Chapter 1

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpFirst" align=center style='margin-bottom:0in; text-align:center'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Words of the Preacher, Son of David, King in Jerusalem</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Completely pointless,” </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the Preacher says;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;line-height:normal'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      “Completely pointless! It’s all pointless.”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;line-height:normal'><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>What do people get out of their work under the sun?</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>One generation passes away and another comes along,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      but the earth just goes on and on.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sun comes up, the sun goes down,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and hurries back to where it comes up again.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The wind blows south, the wind blows north;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      it just keeps circulating around and around.<b>                     </b></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Eccl 1:1-6</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The rivers flow to the ocean; yet the ocean doesn’t run over;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      they flow again to where they flowed before.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Everything is too tiresome to talk about;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      eyes aren’t satisfied by seeing;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      ears aren’t fulfilled by hearing.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>What has been is what will be;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      what’s been done is what’ll be done;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      there’s nothing new under the sun. </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Is there anything you can say,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      <i>“Look, that’s new”</i>?</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> 11</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>People don’t remember the past;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            later, they won’t remember the future either.</span></p>


### The ones that come after you won’t remember you.        Eccl 1:7-11

<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I decided to examine with wisdom everything people do under the sky. It’s a grievous job God has stricken them with. </span></span>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:9.0pt; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  <i>“I’m wiser than anybody that’s ruled Jerusalem before; </i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:9.0pt; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  I’ve gained a wealth of knowledge.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></p>
