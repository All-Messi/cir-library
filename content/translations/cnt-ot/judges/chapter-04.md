## Judges Chapter 4


### Deborah Judges Israel


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> </span><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>After Ehud died, the people sinned again, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> sold them to Jabin, king of Canaan in Hazor. Sisera commanded his army from Harosheth-hagoyim. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The people appealed to the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> because <i>Sisera</i> had 900 iron chariots and oppressed Israel cruelly for <b>20 years</b>.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Deborah</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>, a prophetess, wife of Lappidoth, was judging Israel. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She sat under the Palm of Deborah between Ramah and Bethel in the hill country of Ephraim. The people came up to her for judgment. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She sent for <b>Barak</b> son of Abinoam from Kedesh in Naphtali, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“The
</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> God of Israel commands you, ‘Gather to Mount Tabor 10,000 troops from Naphtali and Zebulun. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll draw out Sisera, Jabin’s general, to meet you by the Kishon River with his chariots and troops. I’ll give him to you.’”</span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Barak said, <i>“If you’ll go, I’ll go. If you won’t, I won’t.”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I’ll go with you, but that won’t give </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>you any <i>honor. The </i><i>L</i></span><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> will be selling Sisera to a woman.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She went with him to Kedesh, </span><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and Barak called Zebulun and Naphtali to Kedesh; 10,000 men went up with him.         <b>
</b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jud 4:1-10</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Heber the Kenite had moved away from the Kenites, descendants of Hobab, Moses’ <i>brother</i>-in-law*. He had pitched his tent as far away as the oak in Zaanannim near Kedesh.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Sisera heard that Barak had gone up to Mount Tabor, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>he called out his 900 iron chariots and all his army from Harosheth-hagoyim to the Kishon River. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Deborah told Barak, <i>“Get up! The </i><i>L</i></span><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> is going to deliver Sisera to you today. Isn’t it the </span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> that’s leading you out?”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> So Barak went down from Mount Tabor with 10,000 men. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> routed Sisera, his chariots, and army. Sisera jumped down from his chariot and fled on foot. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Barak chased the chariots and the army <i>back home </i>to Harosheth-hagoyim, and Sisera’s whole army fell by the sword. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Sisera fled to the tent of Jael, wife of Heber the Kenite, because he and Heber’s family had been on good terms. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jael came out to meet him, <i>“Turn aside, Sir. You don’t have to be afraid.”</i> He turned aside into her tent, and she covered him with a rug. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said, <i>“Please give me a little water. I’m thirsty.”</i> She opened a skin of milk and gave him a drink and covered him up. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said, <i>“Stand at the door. If anybody comes and asks if anybody’s here, say, ‘No.’”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But she took a tent peg and a hammer, and went quietly to him and drove the peg into his temple till it stuck into the ground while he was asleep, exhausted. That’s how he died. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As Barak pursued him, Jael went out to meet him, <i>“Come here, I’ll show you the man you’re looking for.” </i>He went into her tent and found Sisera lying there dead with the tent peg through his temple.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God subdued Jabin that day in front of Israel. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They kept pressing harder and harder on him till they’d destroyed him.<b>
</b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jud 4:11-24</span></b></span></p>
