## Judges Chapter 9


### Abimelech’s Conspiracy


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Abimelech went to his mother’s relatives in Shechem and said to her clan, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Ask the leaders in Shechem which is better: for Jerubbaal’s 70 sons to rule you or for one </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>man <i>to rule you. Remember, I’m your flesh and bone.” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His mother’s relatives circulated that around for him, and the people were inclined to follow him <i>because</i> he was their<i> kin. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They gave him 70 pieces of silver from Baal-berith’s temple. He used them to hire worthless, reckless fellows </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>to go with him to his father’s family at Ophrah and kill his 70 brothers on one stone. But Jotham, Jerubbaal’s youngest son, hid. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The people in Shechem and Beth-millo gathered and made Abimelech king by the Oak of the Pillar at Shechem. <b>       </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jud 9:1-6</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Jotham heard about it, he went and stood on top of Mount Gerizim and shouted, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Listen, people of Shechem, so God will listen to you. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The trees went out to anoint a king. They asked the olive tree to rule them. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But the olive tree said, ‘Should I leave my richness that honors gods and mortals, and go sway over the trees?’ </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The trees said to the fig tree, ‘<u>You</u> come and rule us.’ </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But the fig tree said, ‘Should I leave my sweetness and my good fruit, and go sway over the trees?’ </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The trees said to the grapevine, ‘<u>You</u> come and rule us.’ </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But the grapevine said, ‘Should I leave my wine that cheers gods and mortals, and go to sway over the trees?’ </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then the trees said to the bramble, ‘<u>You</u> come and rule us.’ </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The bramble said, ‘If you’re anointing me king in good faith, then come and take refuge in my shade. If not, let fire come out of the bramble and consume the cedars of Lebanon.</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“[</span></i><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>]
</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>My father fought for you and risked his life to rescue you from Midian. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You’ve risen up against his family and killed his 70 sons on one rock. You’ve made Abimelech, son of his maidservant, king of Shechem because he’s your relative. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If you’ve acted honorably in good faith with Jerubbaal and his family as his deeds deserved, then be happy with one another. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If not, let fire come out from Abimelech and consume the people in Shechem and Beth-millo.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jotham fled to Beer and lived there for fear of his half-brother. <b>                                   </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jud 9:7-21</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Abimelech ruled Israel for <b>3 years</b>. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God put animosity between him and the people in Shechem, and they dealt treacherously with him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That was </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>so the violence done to Jerubbaal’s 70 sons might come on Abimelech who murdered them and on the men who helped him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The men in Shechem put men in ambush against him on the mountaintops and robbed everybody that went by. Abimelech heard about it.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Gaal son of Ebed moved to Shechem with his relatives and gained the confidence of the people there. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They went out and gathered grapes from their vineyards, trod them, and held a feast. They went to the temple of their god and ate and drank, and ranted about Abimelech. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Gaal said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Who is Abimelech? Why should we serve him? Didn’t the son of Jerubbaal and Zebul his officer serve the men of Hamor the father of Shechem? Why then should we serve <u>him</u>? </span></i><span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If these people were under my command, I’d remove Abimelech! I’d tell him to muster an army and come out and fight.”</span></i><b><span style='font-size: 10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         Jud 9:22-29  </span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Zebul the ruler of the city heard what Gaal said, he was mad </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and sent ambassadors to Abimelech at Arumah, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Gaal and his relatives have come to Shechem; they’re stirring up the city against you. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So you and your men go during the night and lie in ambush in the fields. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In the morning at sunup, rush on the city. When he and his men come out against you, do to them what you can.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Abimelech and his men got up during the night and laid in ambush against Shechem in four companies. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Gaal went out and stood in the entrance of the city gate, and Abimelech and his men rose up from the ambush. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Gaal saw them, he told Zebul, <i>“Look, men are coming down from the mountains!”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>Zebul said, <i>“You’re just seeing the mountains’ shadows.”
</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v37" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Gaal said again, <i>“Look, there are men are coming down from the center of the land, and another company is coming from the direction of the Diviners’ Oak.” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v38" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then Zebul said, <i>“Where’s your bragging now, you that said, ‘Who is Abimelech that we should serve him?’ Aren’t these the men you despised? Go fight them.” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v39" id="v39"><sup class="verse-num">39</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Gaal led the men out to fight him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v40" id="v40"><sup class="verse-num">40</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Abimelech chased him and he fled. Many fell wounded all the way back to the entrance of the gate. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v41" id="v41"><sup class="verse-num">41</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Abimelech retreated to Arumah, and Zebul drove Gaal and his relatives out of Shechem.<b>
</b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jud 9:30-41</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v42" id="v42"><sup class="verse-num">42</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The next day the men went out to the fields. Abimelech heard about it. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v43" id="v43"><sup class="verse-num">43</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He divided his men into three companies and laid in wait there. When the men come out of the city, he attacked them and killed them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v44" id="v44"><sup class="verse-num">44</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Abimelech and his company rushed up and stood at the entrance to the city gate while the two companies attacked and killed the ones in the fields. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v45" id="v45"><sup class="verse-num">45</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Abimelech fought the city all day and took it. He killed the people in it, tore it down, and scattered salt all over it.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v46" id="v46"><sup class="verse-num">46</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When people in the tower at Shechem heard about it, they withdrew into the fortress of El-berith’s temple. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v47" id="v47"><sup class="verse-num">47</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Abimelech found out they were in the tower, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v48" id="v48"><sup class="verse-num">48</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>he and his men went up to Mount Zalmon. He cut a bundle of brush with an axe and put it on his shoulder. He told his men to hurry<i> </i>and do the same thing<i>.</i>
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v49" id="v49"><sup class="verse-num">49</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Each one cut a bundle and followed him. He piled the bundles against the fortress and set it on fire over the ones inside. Everybody in the tower died, about 1,000 men and women.<b>                                 </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jud 9:42-49</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v50" id="v50"><sup class="verse-num">50</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then Abimelech went to Thebez, camped against it, and took it. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v51" id="v51"><sup class="verse-num">51</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But the city had a strong tower. The men and women fled to it, shut themselves in, and went up to the roof. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v52" id="v52"><sup class="verse-num">52</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He attacked it and came to burn down the tower door. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v53" id="v53"><sup class="verse-num">53</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A woman threw an upper millstone down on him and cracked his skull. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v54" id="v54"><sup class="verse-num">54</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Right away he called to his young armor bearer, <i>“Draw your sword and kill me so people won’t say a woman killed me.” </i>His young man thrust him through, and he died. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v55" id="v55"><sup class="verse-num">55</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Israel saw Abimelech was dead, they went home. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v56" id="v56"><sup class="verse-num">56</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So God repaid him for his crime against his father in killing his 70 brothers. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v57" id="v57"><sup class="verse-num">57</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God also made the sinfulness of the men in Shechem come back on their heads and made Jotham’s curse fall on them.</span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                                                                                                             Jud 9:50-57</span></b></span></p>
