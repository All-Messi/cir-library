<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> A Message from the LORD to Jeremiah 2"Inquire from the LORD for us; Nebuchadnezzar is attacking us. Maybe the LORD will show us one of his wonderful acts so our enemy will withdraw."</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Jeremiah told them, "Tell Zedekiah 4that the LORD God of Israel says, 'I'm going to make your weapons useless for fighting those who are laying siege outside the wall. I'll let them come to the center of the city. 5I'll fight you myself with outstretched hand and strong arm in anger, wrath, and fury. 6I'll strike down people and animals with a severe plague. 7After that, I'll turn over to Nebuchadnezzar, Zedekiah, his officials, and people that survive the sword, famine, and disease. He won't spare, he won't pity, he won't have mercy.' 8"Tell the people the LORD says, 'I'm giving you a choice, a way to live and a way to die. 9If you stay in the city, you'll die by sword, famine, and disease. If you surrender, you'll have your life as the reward of war. 10I've decided to give this city to the king of Babylon to burn.' 11"Tell the king of Judah's household, 'Listen to the LORD's message, 12dynasty of David:</span>
