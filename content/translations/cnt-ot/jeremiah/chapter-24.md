## Jeremiah Chapter 24


### Two Baskets of Figs


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><a name="_Hlk123652304"><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Nebuchadrezzar/<i>Nebuchadnezzar</i> took to Babylon Jeconiah/<i>Jehoiachin</i> son of Jehoiakim, king of Judah, and the officials in Judah along with the craftsmen and artisans. Then the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> showed me two baskets of figs sitting in front of the Temple. </span></span>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said, <i>“What do you see, </i>Jeremiah<i>?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I said, <i>“Figs, really good ones and really bad ones you can’t eat because they’re rotten.”</i><b> </b></span></p>

<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll tend to them and bring them back. I’ll build them up, not tear them down; plant them, not pull them up. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll give them a heart to know me because I’m the L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.
