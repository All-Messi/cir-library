## Daniel Chapter 10


### Daniel’s Vision of a Messenger


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The <b>third year of Cyrus, king of Persia</b>, a message came to Daniel (</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Belteshazzar</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>) about great warfare. He understood that the vision was about future events. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>At that time, I, Daniel, had been in mourning for three weeks. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I didn’t eat any tasty food or meat, drink any wine, or use any lotions that whole time. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The <b>twenty-fourth of the first</b> <b>month</b>, I was on the bank of the Tigris River. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I looked up and saw a man dressed in linen<i> with</i> a <i>belt </i>of pure gold from Uphaz around his waist. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His body was like beryl, his face flashed like lightning, his eyes flamed like torches, his arms and feet shined<i> </i>like polished bronze, and his voice sounded like<i> </i>an enormous crowd. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I was the only one that saw the vision, but such an overwhelming sense of dread came over the men that were with me that they ran and hid. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So I was alone. My strength was gone, and my healthy appearance grew deathly pale.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When I heard him start to speak, I passed out facedown on the ground.<b>
            </b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Dan 10:1-9</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A hand took hold of me and helped me up shaking onto my hands and knees. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said, <i>“Daniel, man highly esteemed, understand what I’m about to tell you. Stand up; I’ve been sent to you.”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><a name="_Hlk124494777"><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> I stood up shaking. </span></a></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Don’t be afraid. You’ve been heard from the first day you determined to understand this and humble yourself before your God. I’ve come in answer to what you’ve said. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The prince of Persia resisted me for two days. Then Michael, one of the chief princes, came to help me because I’d been left there with the kings of Persia. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Now I’ve come to let you understand what will happen to your people later. The vision deals with the future.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> <b>                   </b></span></span></p>
