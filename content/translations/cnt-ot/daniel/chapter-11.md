## Daniel Chapter 11


### Kings of the North and South


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Three more kings will come to power in Persia. A fourth will gain far more wealth than the others. As soon as he gains power by his wealth, he’ll stir up the </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>empire<i> against Greece. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A strong king will come to power </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>there <i>and rule as he pleases. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But not long after he ascends to power, his kingdom will break up into the four points of the compass. It won’t pass on to heirs or continue with the authority he exercised. His sovereignty will cease and pass on to others than </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>descendants<i>.</i></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>
</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Then the king of the South will grow strong along with one of his commanders. But his commander will gain more power than he has and obtain a large kingdom of his own. </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>After some years they’ll form an alliance, and the daughter of the king of the South will be given to the king of the North to make a </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>marriage<i> alliance. But she won’t retain her influence </i>there<i>. She and her father won’t last, and they’ll be given up along with her attendants and supporters.</i><b>                                                                          </b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Dan 11:2-6</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><a name="_Hlk124498206"><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> </a><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“In those times, one of her relatives in the South will come to power in her place, attack the king of the North, enter his fortress, and defeat him. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’ll carry off their idols to Egypt, their molten images, their precious gold and silver items. For some years he’ll refrain from attacking the king of the North. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then the latter will invade the realm of the king of the South, but he’ll soon go back to his own land.</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“But the sons of the king of the North will gather a group of large forces and wage war. They’ll keep on coming, overrun, pass through, and carry the war as far as his fortress. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then the king of the South will be angry and go to fight the king of the North. The latter will raise a large army, but he’ll fall to the other. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When the enemy army is defeated, the king of the South will get proud and execute thousands, but he won’t prevail for long. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The king of the North will raise a bigger army than before; and after some years, he’ll press on with that large army and lots of equipment.</span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Dan 11:7-13</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><a name="_Hlk124498380"><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> </a><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“In those times, there’ll be an uprising against the king of the South. Violent men among your people will also lift themselves up to fulfill the vision, but they’ll fall. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then the king of the North will come, throw up a siege mound, and capture a well-fortified city. The forces of the South won’t have the strength to stand their ground, not even their best troops. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The one that comes against him will do as he pleases, and nobody will be able to withstand him. He’ll stay for a time in the Glorious Land and control it. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’ll be determined to come with the power of his whole kingdom and to bring a proposal for peace that he’ll put into effect. He’ll give him a daughter in marriage to overthrow the kingdom from within, but she won’t take a stand for him. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then he’ll turn his attention to the coastland and capture many cities. But a commander will put a stop to his insolence and repay him for it. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So he’ll take refuge in the fortresses in his own land, but he’ll stumble and fall and fall out of the picture.</span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Dan 11:14-19</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><a name="_Hlk124498540"><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> </a><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Then in his place one will rise up and send a tax collector to maintain the royal splendor. Yet within a short time he’ll die, though not in anger or in battle. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In his place a despicable man will come to power who isn’t in line for royal succession. He’ll come in peace time and seize the kingdom by intrigue. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’ll sweep away armies, including the prince of the covenant. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>After he makes various alliances, he’ll act deceitfully and seize power with a small force. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Without warning he’ll enter the richest parts of the realm and accomplish what his predecessors had never done. He’ll distribute plunder, booty, and possessions among them. He’ll plot against fortresses, but only for a while. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’ll stir up his courage against the king of the South with a large army. So the king of the South will mobilize a very big army, but he won’t stand because there’ll be plots against him. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The<i> members of his own household will cause his downfall. His army will be swept away, and many will be slain. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Both kings will be intent on evil and lie to each other at the conference table; but to no avail because the end will come at the appointed time. </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><a name="_Hlk147750887"><span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> </a><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The king of the South will head for home with considerable substance. On the way, he’ll set himself against </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the<i> </i>people of <i>the holy covenant, and do a lot of damage before going on back to his own land.</i><b> </b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Dan 11:20-28</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><a name="_Hlk124498837"><span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> </a><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“At the appointed time, he’ll invade the South again, but this time it won’t turn out like before. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ships from Kittim will attack him. He’ll retreat and take his out anger on</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> the people of<i> the holy covenant. So he’ll come and listen to the ones that forsake the holy covenant. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His army will defile the Temple fortress, forbid the daily burnt offering, and set up the abomination that makes desolate. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>By flattery he’ll turn to godlessness those who’ve violated the covenant, but the people that know their God will stay strong and resist him. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Wise leaders among the people will instruct many, though they’ll fall by flame and sword, be captured and robbed. </span></i><a name="_Hlk147751821"></span>
<span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> </a><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They’ll receive little help, and many who do join them won’t be sincere. </span></i><a name="_Hlk147752098"></a></span>
<span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Some of the wise will fall victim to persecution as a means of refining and cleansing them, making them pure till the time of the end, because the appointed time is still to come.                   </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Dan 11:29-35</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“The king will do as he pleases, put himself above every god, and blaspheme the God of gods. He’ll succeed till the time of wrath is over because what’s been decreed will happen. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v37" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He won’t show any regard for the gods of his ancestors, for any </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>god<i> women favor, or for any other god. He’ll magnify himself above them all. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v38" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Instead, he’ll honor the god of fortresses, a god his ancestors never knew. He’ll honor him with gold, silver, costly gems, and costly gifts. </span></i><a name="_Hlk124498941"></a></span>
<span class="verse verse-v39" id="v39"><sup class="verse-num">39</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’ll attack the strongest fortresses with the help of a foreign god, and greatly honor those who acknowledge him. He’ll make them rulers over many people and parcel out land among them as their reward.</span></i><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                                                               </span></b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Dan 11:36-39</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v40" id="v40"><sup class="verse-num">40</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Toward the end of </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Israel’s <i>time, the king of the South will attack him, and the king of the North will storm out against him like a tornado with chariots, cavalry, and a large navy. He’ll invade various lands and overrun them. </i></span><a name="_Hlk147758169"></span>
<span class="verse verse-v41" id="v41"><sup class="verse-num">41</sup> </a><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’ll enter the Glorious Land, and many countries will fall, but Edom, Moab, and most of Ammon will escape. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v42" id="v42"><sup class="verse-num">42</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then he’ll attack still other countries, including Egypt. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v43" id="v43"><sup class="verse-num">43</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’ll gain control of Egypt’s precious treasures, its gold and silver, Libyans and Nubians will become his subjects. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v44" id="v44"><sup class="verse-num">44</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But alarming news will come from the east and north, and he’ll go out with great fury to exterminate many. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v45" id="v45"><sup class="verse-num">45</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’ll pitch his royal pavilion between the sea and the beautiful Holy Mountain. Yet he’ll come to his end, and nobody will help him.</span></i><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>
