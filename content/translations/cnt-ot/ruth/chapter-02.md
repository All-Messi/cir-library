## Ruth Chapter 2


### Ruth Meets Boaz


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><a name="_Hlk124505528"><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Naomi’s husband Elimelech had a wealthy relative named Boaz. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ruth said to Naomi, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Let me go glean among the heads of grain after one that might take an interest in me.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Go ahead, my daughter.”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She went and gleaned behind the harvest crew. She happened to come to the part of the field that belonged to Boaz. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He came from Bethlehem and said to the crew, </span><i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“The L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> be with you.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“The L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> bless you.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He asked the crew chief, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Whose young woman is this?”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“She’s the Moabite that came back with Naomi. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She asked to glean behind the harvest crew among the bundles.  She came and stayed since morning. She’s been sitting in the house for a little while.”                                     </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Ruth 2:1-7</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Boaz told Ruth, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Listen carefully, my daughter. Don’t glean anywhere else. Stay here with my maids. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Watch which field they harvest and follow them. I’ve warned the young men not to bother you. When you’re thirsty, go to the water jars and drink what the servants draw.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><a name="_Hlk124505814"><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She fell on her face, bowing to the ground, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Why have I been pleasing enough to you that you’ve paid attention to me? I’m a foreigner.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Boaz said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I’ve heard what you’ve done for your mother-in-law after her husband died: you left your parents and the land where you were born and came to people you didn’t know. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>May the L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> God of Israel reward your work and pay your wages in full under whose wings you’ve taken refuge. </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“You’ve been gracious to me, my lord. You’ve comforted me and talked nice to me, though I’m not one of your workers.”</span></i><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>   </span></b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Ruth 2:8-13</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>At mealtime Boaz told her, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Come and eat some bread and dip your piece in the wine.” </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She sat beside the harvest crew, and he served her roasted grain. She ate till she was satisfied and had some left over. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When she got up to glean, Boaz told his men, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Let her glean even among the sheaves, and don’t get after her for it. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pull some out from the bundles and leave it for her. Don’t fuss at her.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She gleaned in the field till evening. When she beat out what she’d gleaned, it was about 1.1 bushels <sup>1 </sup></span><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ephah</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> of barley. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She picked it up and went into town, and her mother-in-law saw it. She gave Naomi what she had left after she’d had enough.      </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Ruth 2:14-18</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Her mother-in-law said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“Where’d you glean today? Where’d you work? May he who noticed you be fortunate.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“I worked with a man named Boaz.”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“The L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> bless him. He hasn’t withdrawn his kindness to the living and the dead. He’s one of our closest relatives.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ruth said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Besides, he told me to stay close to his workmen till they’ve finished his harvest.’”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><a name="_Hlk124507052"><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Naomi said </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“It’s good for you to go out with his maids so others won’t harm you in another field.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She gleaned close by Boaz’s maids till the end of barley harvest and wheat harvest. She was living with her mother-in-law.<b>    </b></span></span></p>
