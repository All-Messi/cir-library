## 2 Samuel Chapter 4


### Ish-Bosheth Murdered


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Ish-bosheth heard that Abner had died in Hebron, his courage failed, and Israel lost courage too.
</span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The people of Beeroth fled to Gittaim and still live there as foreigners. </span></span></p>
