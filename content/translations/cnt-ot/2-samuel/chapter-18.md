## 2 Samuel Chapter 18


### The Death of Absalom


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then David counted his people and set over them commanders of thousands and hundreds. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He sent out the troops, a third each under Joab, Abishai, and Ittai. He told them,
<i>“I’ll go out with you too.” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> </span></i><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“You shouldn’t go out. If we flee, they won’t care even if half of us die. You’re worth 10,000 of us. It’s better for you to be ready to help us from town.” </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said, <i>“I’ll do what you think’s best.” </i>He stood beside the gate, and the troops went out by hundreds and thousands. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He charged Joab, Abishai, and Ittai, <i>“For my sake, deal gently with young Absalom.” </i>The troops heard it.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The troops went out, and the battle took place in the Forest of Ephraim. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>David’s men defeated the people of Israel with a great slaughter—20,000 men. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The battle spread out over the whole countryside, and more people died because of the forest than died from the sword.<b>        </b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">2 Sam 18:1-8</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><a name="_Hlk126066436"><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Absalom happened to meet <i>some of</i> David’s men. He was trying to escape on his mule, and it went under the thick branches of a big terebinth tree. His hair got tangled up in them and left him hanging as the mule kept going. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>One fellow told Joab, <i>“I saw Absalom hanging from a terebinth tree.”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joab said, <i>“You saw him! Why didn’t you strike him to the ground? I would’ve given you 10 pieces of silver and a belt.”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The man said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Even if you’d given me 1,000, I wouldn’t have attacked the king’s son. He charged you, Abishai, and Ittai for his sake to protect young Absalom! </span></i><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Otherwise, if I’d have dealt deviously with him (</span></i><i><span style='font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>and there’s nothing hidden from the king</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>), you wouldn’t have gotten involved.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joab said, <i>“I’m not wasting time here with you.”</i> He took three spears and thrust them through Absalom’s heart while he was still alive in the tree. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ten young men that carried Joab’s armor gathered around Absalom and killed him.<b>
</b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">2 Sam 18:9-15</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joab blew the ram’s horn, and his troops came back from chasing Israel. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They threw Absalom in a deep pit in the forest and piled a big mound of rocks over him. Israel fled home. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Absalom had set up a marker in the King’s Valley. He said, <i>“I don’t have a son to carry on my name.” </i>So he named the marker after himself; it’s still called Absalom’s Monument. </span></span></p>


### David Mourns Absalom’s Death


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then Ahimaaz said,
<i>“Let me run to the king with the news that the </i><i>L</i></span><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> has freed him from his enemies.”     </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joab told him, <i>“You’re not the one to carry news today. You’ll carry it another day. The king’s son is dead.”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joab told the Nubian, <i>“Go tell him what you’ve seen.”</i> He bowed to Joab and ran. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ahimaaz said again, <i>“Whatever happens, let me run too.”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            Joab said, <i>“Why would you run, son, since you won’t get anything for it?”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Whatever happens, I’ll run.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“Go ahead.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">2 Sam 18:16-23a</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ahimaaz ran by way of the plain and outran the Nubian. </span><span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>David was sitting between the inner and outer gates <i>of the town</i>. The watchman went up to the roof of the gate by the wall and saw a man running by himself. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He called it out to the king. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            He said, <i>“If he’s by himself, he has good news.” </i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            He came closer and closer. </span><span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then the watchman saw another man running. He called to the gatekeeper,<i> “There’s another man running by himself.” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The king said,<i> “That one is bringing good news too.” </i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  </span></sup></b><span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The watchman said, <i>“The first runner looks like Ahimaaz.”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            The king said, <i>“He’s a good man, bringing good news.”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ahimaaz called out to the king, <i>“All’s well.”</i> He prostrated himself to the king.
<i>“Blessed is the </i><i>L</i></span><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> your God, who has delivered up the rebels.”</span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Is Absalom all right?”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>Ahimaaz answered, <i>“When Joab sent the king’s servant and me, I saw a big great disturbance, but I didn’t know what was going on.”                                                                                                                                     </i></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">2 Sam 18:23b-29</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The king said, <i>“Come over and stand here.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Nubian arrived and said, </span><i><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Receive good news. The </span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>L</span></i><i><span style='font-size: 9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> has freed you today from your attackers.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Is Absalom all right?”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            The Nubian answered, <i>“Let everyone who opposes you be like that young man!”</i> </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The king was deeply moved and went up to the room above the gate and cried. He said as he walked, <i>“Absalom! My son! My son Absalom! Oh that I’d have died instead of you, Absalom! My son! My son!”</i><b>                             </b></span></span></p>
