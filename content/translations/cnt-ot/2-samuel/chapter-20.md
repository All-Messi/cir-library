## 2 Samuel Chapter 20

<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>David told Abishai, <i>“Sheba will do us more damage than Absalom. <span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So Joab’s men, along with the king’s bodyguard and the strong warriors, left to pursue Sheba. </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joab said to Amasa, <i>“Are you doing well, brother?”</i> Joab took Amasa by the beard with his right hand to kiss him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Amasa wasn’t on guard against the dagger in Joab’s <i>left </i>hand. <span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>One of Joab’s young men stood by him and said, <i>“Whoever is for Joab and David, follow Joab.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But Amasa lay wallowing in his blood on the road. <span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then the men went on behind Joab to pursue <i>Sheba</i>.</span></span></p>
