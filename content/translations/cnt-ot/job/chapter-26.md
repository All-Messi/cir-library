## Job Chapter 26

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <strong>Job answered:</strong></span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <em>“What a help you are to the weak!</em> <em>	</em><em>	How you’ve saved arms with no strength!</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <em>What counsel you’ve given me in my stupidity!</em> <em>	</em><em>	What brilliant insight you’ve provided!</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <em>Who’ve you been talking to?</em> <em>	</em><em>	Whose spirit has been speaking through you?!</span>
<p class="section-ref">Job 26:1-4</p>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <em>“The departed spirits tremble</em> <em>	</em><em>	below the water and its inhabitants.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <em>Sheol is naked in front of him;</em> <em>	</em><em>	Abaddon has no covering </em>before him<em>.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <em>He stretches out the north over empty space</em> <em>	</em><em>	and hangs the earth on nothing.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <em>He wraps up water in his clouds,</em> <em>	</em><em>	and they don’t burst under its weight.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <em>He shrouds the full moon in clouds.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <em>He has set the horizon between the water above and below      </em> <em>	</em><em>	and put a boundary between light and darkness.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <em>The </em>mountains,<em> pillars of the sky, tremble</em> <em>	</em><em>	and shudder at his rebuke.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <em>He calms the sea monster with his power</em> <em>	</em><em>	and breaks Rahab in pieces with his intelligence.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <em>He clears </em>clouds from <em>the sky with his breath</em> <em>	</em><em>	and wounds the </em>celestial <em>fleeing Serpent with his hand.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <em>These are just fringes of what he does:</em> <em>	</em><em>	his faint whisper!</em> <em>	</em><em>	his loud thunder!</em> <em>	</em><em>	</em><em>	Who can comprehend his power?”</span>
