## Job Chapter 41

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Can you pull Leviathan in on a fishhook</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Can you tie it up with a rope in its nose</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Will it beg you for mercy</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Will it agree to work for you</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Will you make a pet out of it like a bird</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Will traders bid on it</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Can you hurt its hide with harpoons</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Put your hands on it, and you'll remember the fight!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Anything you try to do with it is useless;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Nobody is fierce enough to dare to disturb it;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Who has given to me that I should repay?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Who can strip off its outer armor</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> Who can pry its jaws open?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> It's proud of its hard scales</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> They interlock with one another</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> When it sneezes, it flashes out light;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> Burning torches blaze out of its mouth;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> Smoke pours out of its nostrils</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> It has a strong neck</span>

<span class="verse" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> It makes deep water roil like a boiling pot;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> The water in its wake foams behind it;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> There's nothing like it on earth;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> It stares down at everything tall;</span>
