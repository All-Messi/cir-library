## Job Chapter 19

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Job answered:</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> You've insulted me ten times over;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Even if I did make mistakes,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> If you're acting superior to me</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> He has blocked my way so I can't go on;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> He has stripped me of my honor</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> He has destroyed me on every side; I'm finished;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> He has flashed his anger at me</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> His troops have come at me in force;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> My relatives are gone;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> The ones who live in my house</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> When I call to my servant, he doesn't answer me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> My breath is repulsive to my wife;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> Even kids despise me</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> My associates abhor me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> I'm skin and bones;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> Show me some mercy, friends;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> Do you have to harass me like he does?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> Oh that they were engraved permanently on a rock</span>

<span class="verse" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> I know my Redeemer's alive,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> Even after my skin decays,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> I'll see him myself;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> If you say, 'Can we find a way to harass him?'</span>
