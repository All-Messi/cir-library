## Job Chapter 37

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> My heart pounds when I think about this;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Listen closely to his thundering,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> He unleashes his lightning under the whole sky;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> God thunders wondrously</span>
