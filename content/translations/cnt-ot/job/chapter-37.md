## Job Chapter 37

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif; color:#00B0F0'>            </span></i><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif; color:#00B0F0'>After it his roars;</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif; color:#00B0F0'>                  and doesn’t hold anything back.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif; color:#00B0F0'>            </span></i><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif; color:#00B0F0'>God thunders wondrously</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif; color:#00B0F0'>            </span></i><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif; color:#00B0F0'>He tells the snow to fall on the ground</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif; color:#00B0F0'>            </span></i><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif; color:#00B0F0'>He stops everyone’s labor </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif; color:#00B0F0'>                  so they can watch his work.</span></i><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                  </span></b></span></p>
