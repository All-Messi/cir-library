## Job Chapter 39

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Do you know when mountain goats give birth?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Can you count the months of their gestation</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> They crouch down to deliver their young.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Their offspring grow strong out in the open;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> I gave it the open country for a home,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> It scorns city turmoil</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> It roams the mountains for pasture,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Can you harness it to plow in a furrow?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Will you depend on it because it's strong</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Will you trust it to bring your grain home</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> She abandons her eggs in the ground</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> She forgets that a foot may crush them</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> She's harsh toward her young as if they weren't hers;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> When she jumps up to run,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> Can you make it leap like a locust?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> It paws in the valley and frolics in its strength</span>

<span class="verse" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> As often as the bugle blows, it says, 'Aha!'</span>

<span class="verse" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> Does an eagle mount up at your command</span>

<span class="verse" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> It lives and lodges on the cliffs,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> From there it spies out prey</span>
