## Job Chapter 6

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Job answered:</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> It would outweigh the sand by the seashore;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> The arrows of the Almighty are piercing me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Does a wild donkey bray when it has grass?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Does anyone eat tasteless food without salting it?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> My mouth won't touch them;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Oh that he'd be willing to crush me,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> This is my consolation:</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> I don't have enough strength to endure.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Am I as hard as rock?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> I can't get any help from inside me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> My brothers have been unreliable,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> Now they're turbulent with melting ice and snow;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> Their course winds into wasteland and disappears.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> Caravans from Tema look for them;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> They're disappointed;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> You've been like that to me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> Have I said, 'Give me something';</span>

<span class="verse" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> Honest words carry force,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> Do you think you can rebuke me</span>

<span class="verse" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> You'd cast lots for orphans</span>

<span class="verse" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> Look me in the eye;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> Just stop supposing I've done something wrong;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> Has there been anything false about what I've said?</span>
