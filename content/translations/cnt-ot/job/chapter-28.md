## Job Chapter 28

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> There's a mine for silver</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Iron comes out of the ground,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> They use a torch in the darkness</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> They sink mine shafts far from where people live</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> People grow food on top of the ground;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Its rocks yield lapis lazuli,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Birds of prey can't get to it;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Proud animals don't set foot in it;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Miners find flint</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> They chisel out tunnels in the rock</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> They dam up trickling streams</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> People don't know what it's worth;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> The deep says, 'It's not here';</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> Pure gold doesn't exchange for it;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> Its value can't be calculated in the gold from Ophir,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> Gold or crystal can't match it;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> Coral and crystal can't be mentioned with it;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> The topaz in Nubia can't compare to it;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> Where then does wisdom come from?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> Nothing alive can see it;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> Abaddon and Death say,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> He can see all the way to the end of the earth</span>
