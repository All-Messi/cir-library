## Job Chapter 29

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Job took up his discourse again:</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Young men saw me and stepped aside,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> When people heard me, they praised me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> People about to die blessed me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> I dressed in goodness;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> I served as eyes for the blind and feet for the lame.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> I became a father to the needy</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> My honor is always fresh;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> When I spoke, they had nothing to add;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> They waited for me like spring rain</span>
