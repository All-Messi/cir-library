## Job Chapter 16

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <strong>Job answered:</strong></span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <em>“I’ve heard a lot of things like that;</em> <em>	</em><em>	you’re miserable comforters.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <em>Is there no end to windy words?</em> <em>	</em><em>	What’s your problem that you answer </em>me like that<em>?</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <em>I could talk to you like you’re talking if I was in your place.</em> <em>	</em><em>	I could string out words and shake my head at you.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Or, <em>I could encourage you </em> <em>	</em><em>	so my comfort could lessen your pain.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Job 16:1-5</p>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <em>“If I speak, it doesn’t take away my pain;</em> <em>	</em><em>	if I don’t speak, how much of it goes away?</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <em>God has worn me out;</em> <em>	</em><em>	he’s taken away my family.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <em>He has made me skin and bones</em> <em>	</em><em>	and used it as a witness against me.</em> <em>	My haggard body shows in my gaunt face. </em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <em>His anger hunts me down and tears me up;</em> <em>	</em><em>	he gnashes his teeth at me;</em> <em>	</em><em>	my adversary glares at me.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <em>People gape at me with their mouth</em> <em>	</em><em>	and slap me on the cheek in their contempt;</em> <em>	</em><em>	they gang up on me.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Job 16:6-10</p>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <em>God has handed me over to ungodly people</em> <em>	</em><em>	and thrown me away to sinners.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <em>I was at ease, but he tore me apart;</em> <em>	</em><em>	he grabbed me by the neck and dashed me to pieces.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <em>His arrows have pierced me all over;</em> <em>	</em><em>	he’s slashed open my kidneys without mercy</em> <em>	</em><em>	and poured out my gall on the ground.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <em>He’s broken me down time after time</em> <em>	</em><em>	and charged at me like a soldier.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <em>I sewed burlap on my skin</em> <em>	</em><em>	and laid my strength in the dust.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <em>My face is flushed from crying;</em> <em>	</em><em>	I have dark circles around my eyes</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <em>although I haven’t been violent,</em> <em>	</em><em>	</em><em>	and my prayer is pure.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Job 16:11-17</p>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <em>“Earth, don’t cover up my blood;</em> <em>	</em><em>	don’t let my outcry cease.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <em>Even now my witness is in heaven;</em> <em>	</em><em>	my Advocate is on high.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <em>My friends make fun of me;</em> <em>	</em><em>	I shed my tears to God.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <em>Oh that I could plead with him </em> <em>	</em><em>	like a person does with his friend!</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <em>In a few years, I’ll go the way of no return.</em></span>
