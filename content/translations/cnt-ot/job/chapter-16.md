<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <strong>Job answered:</strong></span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><strong><sup>7</sup></strong><em>God has worn me out;</em> <em>	</em><em>	he’s taken away my family.</em></span>
