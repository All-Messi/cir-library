## Job Chapter 16

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Job answered:</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Is there no end to windy words?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> I could talk to you like you're talking if I was in your place.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Or, I could encourage you</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> God has worn me out;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> He has made me skin and bones</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> His anger hunts me down and tears me up;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> People gape at me with their mouth</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> God has handed me over to ungodly people</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> I was at ease, but he tore me apart;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> His arrows have pierced me all over;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> He's broken me down time after time</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> I sewed burlap on my skin</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> My face is flushed from crying;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> Even now my witness is in heaven;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> My friends make fun of me;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> Oh that I could plead with him</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> In a few years, I'll go the way of no return.</span>
