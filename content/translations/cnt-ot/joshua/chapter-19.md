## Joshua Chapter 19

### Allotment to Simeon

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> The second lot came out for <strong>Simeon</strong> by clans. Its inheritance was inside Judah. <strong><sup>2</sup></strong>It had Beer-sheba, Sheba, Moladah, <strong><sup>3</sup></strong>Hazarshual, Balah, Ezem, <strong><sup>4</sup></strong>Eltolad, Bethul, Hormah, <strong><sup>5</sup></strong>Ziklag, Beth-marcaboth, Hazar-susah, <strong><sup>6</sup></strong>Beth-lebaoth, Sharuhen—13 towns with their villages.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Ain, Rimmon, Ether, Ashan—4 towns with their villages,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> and the villages around those towns as far as Baalath-beer, Ramah in the Negev. That was Simeon’s inheritance by clans. <strong><sup>9</sup></strong>They took his territory out of Judah’s because Judah’s share was too big.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Josh 19:1-9</p>

### Allotment to Zebulun


<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> The third lot came out for <strong>Zebulun</strong> by clans. The territory went as far as Sarid. <strong><sup>11</sup></strong>Then their border passed up to the west to Maralah, touched Dabbesheth, and reached the stream east of Jokneam. <strong><sup>12</sup></strong>It turned from Sarid east as far as the border of Chisloth-tabor, proceeded to Daberath and up to Japhia. <strong><sup>13</sup></strong>It continued east to Gath-hepher, to Eth-kazin, and on to Rimmon that stretches to Neah. <strong><sup>14</sup></strong>The border on the north turned to Hannathon and ended at the Valley of Iphtahel. <strong><sup>15</sup></strong>It included Kattah, Nahalal, Shimron, Idalah, Bethlehem—12 towns with their villages. <strong><sup>16</sup></strong>That was Zebulun’s inheritance by clans. 	<strong>Allotment to I</strong><strong>ssachar</strong></span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> The fourth lot fell to <strong>Issachar</strong> by clans. <strong><sup>18</sup></strong>Their territory included Jezreel, Chesulloth, Shunem, <strong><sup>19</sup></strong>Hapharaim, Shion, Anaharath, <strong><sup>20</sup></strong>Rabbith, Kishion, Ebez, <strong><sup>21</sup></strong>Remeth, En-gannim, En-haddah, Beth-pazzez. <strong><sup>22</sup></strong>The border reached Tabor, Shahazumah, Beth-shemesh, and ended at the Jordan—16 towns with their villages. <strong><sup>23</sup></strong>That was Issachar’s inheritance by clans.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Josh 19:10-23</p>

<span class="verse" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> The fifth lot fell to <strong>Asher</strong> by clans. <strong><sup>25</sup></strong>Their territory included Helkath, Hali, Beten, Achshaph, <strong><sup>26</sup></strong>Allammelech, Amad, and Mishal. On the west it reached Carmel and Shihor-libnath. <strong><sup>27</sup></strong>It turned east to Beth-dagon and reached Zebulun and the Valley of Iphtahel north to Beth-emek and Neiel. Then it proceeded north to Cabul, <strong><sup>28</sup></strong>Ebron, Rehob, Hammon, Kanah, as far as Great Sihon. <strong><sup>29</sup></strong>The border turned to Ramah and the fortified city of Tyre, then turned to Hosah, and ended at the sea; Mehebel, Achzib, <strong><sup>30</sup></strong> Ummah, Aphek, Rehob—22 towns with their villages. <strong><sup>31</sup></strong>That was Asher’s inheritance by clans.<strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Josh 19:24-31</p>

### Allotment to Naphtali


<span class="verse" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> The sixth lot fell to <strong>Naphtali</strong> by clans. <strong><sup>33</sup></strong>Their border was from Heleph, from the oak tree in Zaanannim, Adami-nekeb, and Jabneel as far as Lakkum, and ended at the Jordan. <strong><sup>34</sup></strong>The border turned west to Aznoth-tabor, proceeded to Hukkok, and reached Zebulun on the south, Asher on the west, and Judah at the Jordan on the east. <strong><sup>35</sup></strong>The fortified towns were Ziddim, Zer, Hammath, Rakkath, Chinnereth, <strong><sup>36</sup></strong>Adamah, Ramah, Hazor, <strong><sup>37</sup></strong>Kedesh, Edrei, En-hazor, <strong><sup>38</sup></strong>Yiron, Migdal-el, Horem, Beth-anath, and Beth-shemesh—19 towns with their villages. <strong><sup>39</sup></strong>That was Naphtali’s inheritance by clans.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Josh 19:32-39</p>

### Allotment to Dan


<span class="verse" id="v40"><sup class="verse-num">40</sup> The seventh lot fell to <strong>Dan</strong> by clans. <strong><sup>41</sup></strong>Their territory included Zorah, Eshtaol, Irshemesh, <strong><sup>42</sup></strong>Sha-alabbin, Aijalon, Ithlah, <strong><sup>43</sup></strong>Elon, Timnah, Ekron, <strong><sup>44</sup></strong>Eltekeh, Gibbethon, Baalath, <strong><sup>45</sup></strong>Jehud, Bene-berak, Gath-rimmon, <strong><sup>46</sup></strong>Me-jarkon, Rakkon, with the territory against Joppa. <strong><sup>47</sup></strong>When Dan’s territory was lost to them, they went up and fought Leshem and captured it. They struck it with the sword, possessed it, and settled in it. They called it Dan after their ancestor. <strong><sup>48</sup></strong>That was Dan’s inheritance by clans.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Josh 19:40-48</p>

### Allotment to Joshua


<span class="verse" id="v49"><sup class="verse-num">49</sup> When they finished dividing up the land, the Israelites gave Joshua an inheritance. <strong><sup>50</sup></strong>In accordance with the LORD’s command, they gave him the city he asked for, Timnath-serah in the hill country of Ephraim. He rebuilt the city and settled in it.</span>
