## Joshua Chapter 12

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> These are the kings the Israelites defeated and whose land they took east of the Jordan—from the Wadi Arnon to Mount Hermon with all the eastern Arabah:</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Sihon, king of the Amorites. He lived in Heshbon, and ruled from Aroer, on the edge of the valley of Arnon—from the middle of the valley to the river Jabbok, which is the boundary with the Ammonites, that is, half of Gilead 3and the Arabah to the Sea of Chinneroth east, and in the direction of Beth-jeshimoth to the Sea of Arabah (the Dead Sea), south to the foot of Pisgah.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Og, king of Bashan, one of the remaining Rephaim that lived at Ashtaroth and Edrei 5and ruled Mount Hermon, Salecah, all Bashan to the boundary of the Geshurites and the Maacathites, and half of Gilead to the boundary of Sihon, king of Heshbon.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Moses and Israel gave their land to Reuben, Gad, and the Manasseh half-tribe.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> These are the kings Joshua and Israel defeated west of the Jordan, from Baal-gad in the Valley of Lebanon to Mount Halak, which rises toward Seir: (Joshua gave their land to the tribes by allotments, 8in the hill country, the lowland, the Arabah, the mountain slopes, the open country, the Negev, the land of the Hittites, Amorites, Canaanites, Perizzites, Hivites, and Jebusites.) 9one king each in Jericho; Ai, next to Bethel; 10Jerusalem, Hebron, 11Jarmuth, Lachish, 12Eglon, Gezer, 13Debir, Geder, 14Hormah, Arad, 15Libnah, Adullam, 16Makkedah, Bethel, 17Tappuah, Hepher, 18Aphek, Lasharon, 19Madon, Hazor, 20Shimron-meron, Achshaph, 21Taanach, Megiddo, 22Kedesh, Jokneam in Carmel, 23Dor in Naphath-dor, Goiim in Galilee, 24and Tirzah (31 kings).</span>
