## Joshua Chapter 10


### The Sun Stands Still


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Adonizedek, king of Jerusalem, heard how Joshua had taken Ai and destroyed it like Jericho and its king. <i>He heard</i> how Gibeon had made peace with Israel and was among them.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He was really afraid because Gibeon was a big city, like a royal city. It was bigger than Ai and its men were strong. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So he sent to Hoham king of Hebron, Piram king of Jarmuth, Japhia king of Lachish, and Debir king of Eglon, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Help me attack Gibeon; it has made peace with Israel.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Those five allied forces camped against Gibeon and attacked it. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The men in Gibeon sent to Joshua at camp in Gilgal, <i>“Don’t relax your grip on us. Come quick and save us! The Amorite kings from the hill country have formed an alliance against us.” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> </span></i><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So Joshua and the fighting men went up from Gilgal. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> told Joshua, <i>“Don’t be afraid of them. I’ll deliver them to you. Not a man will stand against you.” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He marched up all night and attacked them by surprise. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> threw them into panic, and Israel killed them with a great slaughter. They chased them by the way of the ascent of Beth-horon and struck them all the way to Azekah and Makkedah. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As they fled down the ascent of Beth-horon, the L</span><span style='font-size: 9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> sent big hailstones on them all the way to Azekah. More died from hailstones than Israel killed with the sword. <b> </b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Josh 10:1-11</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then in front of Israel, Joshua spoke to the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> the day he gave the Amorites to Israel,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        <i>“Have the sun stand still at Gibeon</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    and the moon over the Valley of Aijalon.”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So the sun stood still, and the moon stayed till the nation took revenge on its enemies. It’s recorded in <i>The Book of Jashar. </i>The sun stood still at midday and didn’t go down for about a day. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>There wasn’t a day like it before or since when the L</span><span style='font-size: 9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> listened to <i>such an extreme request by </i>someone, because he fought for Israel.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joshua and Israel came back to camp at Gilgal.</span></span></p>


### Five Amorite Kings Executed


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The five kings fled and hid in the cave at Makkedah. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>People</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> told Joshua where they were hiding. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Roll large rocks against the mouth of the cave and post a guard. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t stay there. Chase your enemies and fall on them. Don’t let them get into their towns. The </span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>L</span></i><i><span style='font-size: 9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> your God will deliver them to you.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joshua and the Israelites killed them with a great slaughter till they wiped them out, but some stragglers got back into the fortified towns. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The people came back safe to Joshua at Makkedah. Nobody said a word against Israel.<b>     </b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Josh 10:12-21</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then Joshua told them to open the cave and bring out the five kings<i>.</i> [</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>]</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told the commanders and fighting men, <i>“Put your feet on their necks.</i> </span><a name="_Hlk77243974"></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> </a><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t be afraid or intimidated; be strong and courageous. This is what the </span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>L</span></i><i><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> will do to all the enemies you fight.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He struck them and put them to death and hung them on five trees. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>At sundown he had <i>the men</i> take them down, throw them back into the cave, and put large rocks against its mouth. They’re still there.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That day Joshua struck down everybody in <u>Makkedah</u> with the sword and did to its king what he’d done to Jericho’s king.</span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Josh 10:22-28</p>


### Southern Towns Conquered


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He and Israel passed on from Makkedah and attacked <u>Libnah</u>. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> gave it and its king to Israel. He struck everybody in it with the sword.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Next, Joshua lay siege to <u>Lachish</u>, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> gave it to Israel. He took it on the second day and struck everybody in it with the sword.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then Horam, king of <u>Gezer</u>, came to help Lachish, and Joshua struck him and his people till there weren’t any left.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He passed on from Lachish, laid siege to <u>Eglon</u>, and attacked it. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They took it that day, struck it with the sword, and destroyed everyone in it.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joshua went up from Eglon to attack <u>Hebron</u>. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v37" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He struck it, its king, and surrounding villages with the sword and destroyed everybody in them.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v38" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then he turned back and attacked <u>Debir</u>. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v39" id="v39"><sup class="verse-num">39</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He struck it, its king, and surrounding villages with the sword and destroyed everybody in them. So he did to Debir what he’d done to Hebron, Libnah, and their kings.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v40" id="v40"><sup class="verse-num">40</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joshua defeated the hill country, the Negev, the western foothills, the slopes, and their kings. He destroyed everybody that breathed—like the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> commanded. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v41" id="v41"><sup class="verse-num">41</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He defeated them from Kadesh-barnea to Gaza, the whole country of Goshen as far as Gibeon. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v42" id="v42"><sup class="verse-num">42</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He took those kings and their land in one campaign because the L</span><span style='font-size:9.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>ORD</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> God of Israel fought for them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v43" id="v43"><sup class="verse-num">43</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then he and Israel went back to camp at Gilgal.<b>  </b></span></span></p>
