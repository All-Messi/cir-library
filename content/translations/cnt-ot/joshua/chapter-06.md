## Joshua Chapter 6


### The Fall of Jericho

<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As soon as you hear a long blast on the rams’ horns, raise the battle cry as loud as you can, and the city wall will fall down flat. <p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told the people to start marching around the city with the armed men ahead of the Ark. <b>         </b></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joshua got up early, and the priests took the Ark, </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The seventh time, when the priests blew the rams’ horns, Joshua told the people, </span></span></p>

<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Make sure you keep away from the things devoted to destruction. <p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The priests</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> blew the rams’ horns.
