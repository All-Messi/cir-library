## Ezra Chapter 1

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> In the first year of Cyrus, king of Persia, to fulfill his message through Jeremiah, the LORD stirred up Cyrus. He sent a written proclamation everywhere in his kingdom: 2"Cyrus, king of Persia, says, 'The LORD God of heaven has given me the kingdoms of the world and appointed me to build him a temple in Jerusalem, in Judah. 3Whoever is from his people, your God be with you! Go up and rebuild the Temple of the LORD God of Israel, the God in Jerusalem. 4Wherever there's a survivor, support him with silver and gold, supplies and livestock, plus freewill offerings.'" 	       Ezra 1:1-4</span>
