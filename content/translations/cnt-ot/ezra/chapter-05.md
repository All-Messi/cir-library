## Ezra Chapter 5


### Tattenai’s Letter to Darius


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The prophets Haggai and Zechariah son of Iddo served as prophets to the Jews in Judah and Jerusalem in the name of the God of Israel. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then Zerubbabel and Jeshua started again to rebuild God’s Temple. His prophets supported them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>At that time, Tattenai, governor of the province Beyond the River, and Shethar-bozenai, and their colleagues came and asked, <i>“Who issued a decree for you to rebuild this Temple and restore this structure?” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We told them the names of the men that were reconstructing it. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But God kept an eye on the elders of the Jews. They didn’t stop them till a report could go to Darius and a written reply could come back.     </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ezra 5:1-6</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>                 </span><b><span style='font-size: 10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>          </span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Here’s a copy of Tattenai’s letter to King Darius: </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><a name="_Hlk124869825"><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> </a><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“To King Darius, all peace.</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We have gone to the province Judah, to the Temple of the great God, which is being built with huge stones and with beams that are being laid in the walls. This work is progressing with great care and is getting done. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We asked those elders who issued them a decree to rebuild that Temple. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We asked for their names so we could write down who was in charge and inform you. </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“They answered, ‘We serve the God of earth and sky. We’re rebuilding the Temple that was built many years ago by a great king in Israel. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But because our ancestors provoked the God of earth and sky, he delivered them to Nebuchadnezzar the Babylonian. He destroyed that Temple and deported the people to Babylon. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But
<b>in the first year of Cyrus</b>, king of Babylon, Cyrus issued a decree to rebuild this Temple. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He also took out the gold and silver utensils from God’s Temple that Nebuchadnezzar had taken to the temple in Babylon. He gave them to a man named Sheshbazzar, who’d been appointed governor. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told him to take those items and deposit them in the Temple in Jerusalem and let God’s Temple be rebuilt on its site. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Sheshbazzar came and laid the foundations. From then till now, it’s been under construction and isn’t finished.’ </span></i><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      </span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><a name="_Hlk124869932"><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> </a><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“If it pleases you, conduct a search in the royal treasure house there in Babylon to see if King Cyrus did issue a decree to rebuild that Temple, and send us your decision on the matter.”</span></i><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>
