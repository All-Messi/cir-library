## Jeremiah Chapter 43


### Jeremiah Taken to Egypt


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As soon as Jeremiah finished, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Azariah Ben-Hoshaiah, Johanan Ben-Kareah, and the arrogant men told him, <i>“You’re lying. Yahveh didn’t say not to go live in Egypt. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Baruch Ben-Neriah is prompting you to turn us over to the Babylonians so they can execute us or exile us to Babylon.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>  </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4-5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So Johanan and the commanders took to Egypt the entire remnant that had come back from the countries where they’d been driven: </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>men, women, children, the king’s daughters, everyone Nebuzaradan had left with Gedaliah, together with Jeremiah and Baruch. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They got as far as Tahpanhes.</span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Jer 43:1-7</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Bury some big rocks in the mortar in the brick </span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>terrace<i> at the entrance to Pharaoh’s palace in Tahpanhes so some of the Jews can see it. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Tell them that Yahveh of Hosts says, ‘I’m going to send for Nebuchadnezzar my servant and set his throne on those rocks. He’ll spread his canopy over them </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>when he comes to strike Egypt. The ones destined to die will die; the ones destined for captivity will go captive, the ones destined for the sword will die by the sword. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12-13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’ll burn the temples of Egypt’s gods and shatter the obelisks in Heliopolis.
