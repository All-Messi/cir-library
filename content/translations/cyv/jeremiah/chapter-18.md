### The Potter’s Shop


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Go down to the potter’s shop. <p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then Yahveh’s message came to me: </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Israel, can’t I deal with you like this potter did? <span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If it turns from its sin, I’ll relent. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>At another time I might talk about planting it or building it up. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If it doesn’t obey, I’ll change my mind about the good I was going to do for it. </span></i></span>

<p class="section-ref">Jer 18:5-12</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So Yahveh says,</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  <i>‘Ask around the nations;</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        who ever heard of anything like that?</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        The virgin Israel has done something terrible.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  </span></i><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Does the snow in Lebanon ever leave </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        the rocky mountaintops out in the open country?</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  Does the cold, flowing mountain water ever dry up?</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  </span></i><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>My people have forgotten me</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        and burn incense to foreign gods;</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  they stumble off their ancient ways</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        to walk on footpaths instead of a good road.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  </span></i><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They end up making their land desolate,</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        something for </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>other
<i>people to mock;</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  everybody that goes by just shake their heads at.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  </span></i><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll scatter them ahead of their enemies like an east wind</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        and turn my back on them when disaster comes.’”</span></i><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>    </span></b></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Jer 18:13-17</p>


### A Plot Against Jeremiah


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then they said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Let’s lay a plot to stop Jeremiah. </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We’ll still have the Law through our priests; </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      we can get our advice from </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>other <i>advisors; </i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      the message can come from </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>other<i> prophets! </i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Let’s spread rumors about him</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and ignore what he says.”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  19</span></sup></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Listen, Yahveh;</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  do you hear what my opponents are saying!?</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  20</span></sup></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Should people repay good with bad?</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  They’ve dug a pit to put me in.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            Remember how I stood in your presence</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  to speak good on their behalf</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  and turn your anger away from them.</span></i><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              </span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  21</span></sup></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So let their children starve;</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  deliver them to the sword.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            Have their wives become childless widows;</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  let their husbands be struck down</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  and their young men die in battle.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  22</span></sup></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>May they scream in their houses</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        when raiders suddenly break into them,</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  because they’ve dug a pit to capture me</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        and hidden snares for me to step in.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  23</span></sup></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh, you know about their deadly designs against me.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  Don’t forgive their iniquity;</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        don’t blot out their sin;</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  overthrow them in anger!”</span></i></span></p>
