## Jeremiah Chapter 13

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Yahveh told me, "Go buy a linen waistcloth and put it on. Don't wash it."</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> So I bought one and put it on.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Then he said, 4"Take it to the Euphrates and hide it in a crevice in a rock." 5So I went and hid it there. 6A long time later, Yahveh told me to go get it. 7I went and dug it out of where I'd put it. It was ruined.</span>
