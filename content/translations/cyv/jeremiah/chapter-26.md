### Jeremiah Escapes Death


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh says, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Stand in the court of Yahveh’s Temple and speak to the towns in Judah that have come to worship here. Don’t hold back a word! </span></i><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Maybe they’ll listen so I can change my mind about the catastrophe I’m planning for them. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Tell them, ‘If you won’t follow the Law I’ve given you </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>or listen to the prophets I’ve sent you time and again, </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>then I’ll make this Temple like Shiloh and this city accursed to every nation.’”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The priests, the prophets, and the people were listening to Jeremiah saying this. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When he finished, they grabbed him<i>, “You have to die! </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Why did you prophesy in Yahveh’s name that this Temple will be like Shiloh and this city without residents?”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> The people gathered around him.<b>                        </b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Jer 26:1-9</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When the officials of Judah heard about that, they came up from the king’s palace to Yahveh’s Temple and sat in the entrance of the New Gate. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The priests and prophets told the officials and the people, <i>“This man has to die! You’ve heard him prophesy against this city.” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then Jeremiah answered, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Yahveh sent me to prophesy the message you heard. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If you mend your ways and deeds and obey him, he’ll change his mind about this misfortune. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Do to me what you like. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Just know that if you put me to death, you’ll bring innocent blood on yourselves, this city, and its people.”          </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Jer 26:10-15</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The officials and the people told the priests and prophets, <i>“This man doesn’t deserve to die. He’s spoken to us in the name of Yahveh our God.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Some elders of the land stood up and told the assembly, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Micah of Moresheth prophesied at the time of Hezekiah, king of Judah. He told the people in Judah, </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            ‘Yahveh of Hosts has said, </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  “Zion will be plowed like a field;</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  Jerusalem will become a ruins;</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  the Temple mount </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>will be<i> like a thicket.”’</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Did Hezekiah and Judah put him to death? Didn’t they respect Yahveh and ask for his blessing? Yahveh changed his mind about the misfortune he‘d pronounced on them. We’re about to do ourselves great harm.”</span></i><b><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>          </span></b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Jer 26:16-19</p>

Like Jeremiah, he was prophesying against this city and the land. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When King Jehoiakim, his officials, and soldiers heard it, the king tried to put him to death, but Uriah found out and fled to Egypt. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The king sent Elnathan Ben-Achbor and some others to Egypt, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and they brought him back. Jehoiakim killed him with a sword and threw his body into the graves for commoners.<b> </b></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But Ahikam Ben-Shaphan defended Jeremiah; so <u>he</u> wasn’t executed.<i>     </i></span></span></p>
