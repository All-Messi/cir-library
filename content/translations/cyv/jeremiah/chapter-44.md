## Jeremiah Chapter 44

<p class="MsoNormal" align=center style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-top:0in;text-align:center;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A Message to Jeremiah for the Jews Living in Egypt</span></span></p>

### Disaster Because of Idolatry


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Yahveh of Hosts, the God of Israel, says, ‘You’ve seen the catastrophe I brought on Jerusalem and the towns in Judah; they’re in ruins. Nobody’s living in them </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>because their sinfulness made me angry.  They kept serving and burning sacrifices to gods they and their ancestors hadn’t known. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yet I sent them my prophets time and again, saying not to do that detestable thing.
</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>‘But they didn’t listen, turn from their sin, or quit burning sacrifices to other gods. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So my anger boiled over and burned in the towns of Judah and the streets of Jerusalem.’ </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Now Yahveh says, ‘Why harm yourselves and slay man and woman, child and infant, from Judah and leave yourselves without </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>even<i> a remnant? </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You’ve made me angry with the things you’ve made with your hands and the sacrifices you’ve burned to other gods in Egypt. You’ll be destroyed and become a curse and disgrace among the nations. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Have you forgotten your ancestors’ sinfulness, the sinfulness of Judah’s kings and their wives, the sinfulness you and your wives committed in Judah and the streets of Jerusalem? </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You haven’t humbled yourselves or respected </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>me <i>to this day or followed the Law and statutes I set before you and your ancestors.’</i><b>                                                                           </b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Jer 44:1-10</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“So Yahveh of Hosts says, ‘I’ll destroy everybody that’s from Judah </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and remove the remnant that’s determined to go live in Egypt. <span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Like I’ve punished Jerusalem, I’ll punish Egypt with sword, famine, and disease. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Only a few refugees will ever make it back to the land they long to see.’”                                                                       </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“We don’t have to listen to you! </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We’ll keep burning sacrifices to the Queen of Heaven and pouring out liquid offerings to her like we and our ancestors, our kings and officials did in Judah and Jerusalem. We were well off then with plenty of food and no misfortune. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Since we stopped doing that, we’ve lacked everything and met our end by sword and famine.” </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The women<i> </i>said,<i> “When we were burning sacrifices to her, pouring out liquid offerings to her, and making sacrificial cakes in her image, didn’t our husbands know it?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Jer 44:15-19</p>

<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Didn’t Yahveh know about the smoking sacrifices you and your ancestors, kings and official, and common people burned? </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He wouldn’t put up with your sinful deeds and detestable acts anymore. <span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>This catastrophe has fallen on you because you’ve burned sacrifices and sinned against him, and didn’t obey him or follow his Law, statutes, or testimonies.</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> <i>[</i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>]<b>
</b></span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You and your wives have said, ‘We’ll keep our vows to burn sacrifices to the Queen of Heaven and pour out liquid offerings to her.’ Go ahead and keep your vows! </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But listen to what Yahveh says, ‘I’ve sworn by my great name that nobody from Judah that’s in Egypt will ever invoke my name again, “As the Lord Yahveh lives . . .” </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll see to it that they’re harmed. They’ll meet their end by sword and famine. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Only a few will ever make it back to Judah. Then the remnant in Egypt will know whose message will stand, theirs or mine. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29-30</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>This is how you’ll know: I’ll punish you here </span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>too<i> and deliver Pharaoh Hophra </i>[Apries] <i>to his enemies like I turned Zedekiah over to Nebuchadnezzar.’”</i></span></span></p>
