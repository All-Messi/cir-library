### Obligations of the Covenant


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The rest of the people</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>—the priests, Levites, gatekeepers, singers, Temple servants, everybody that separated from the people in the land, their wives, their sons and daughters, everybody that had knowledge and understanding—</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>are joining their kinsmen and nobles in taking a curse and an oath. <i>They swear</i> to follow God’s Law through Moses, to observe his commandments, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and not to intermarry with the people in the territory. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If any people in the land bring wares or grain to sell on the Sabbath or a holy day, we won’t buy them. We won’t plant crops in the seventh year or exact any debt <i>that year</i>. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We’ve obligated ourselves to contribute yearly <a name="_Hlk145162123">.13 oz <sup>⅓ of a shekel</sup> </a>of silver for the service in God’s Temple: </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>for the showbread, the continual grain offering and burnt offering; the Sabbaths, new moon, and appointed times; for the holy things, the sin offerings to atone for Israel, and the work on God’s Temple.<b>                                                                                                   </b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Neh 10:28-33</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Likewise, we cast lots for supplying wood to the priests, Levites, and the people so they could bring it to God’s Temple by families at set times each year. It was for burning on the altar as the Law requires. </span><a name="_Hlk125804671"></span>
<span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> </a><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We committed to</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> bringing the first part of the harvest from our ground and trees to Yahveh’s Temple every year; </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>to bringing our male firstborns, cattle, herds, and flocks as the Law requires, especially in written form, for the priests that minister in God’s Temple. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v37" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We’ll also bring the first of our dough, our contributions, the fruit of every tree, the new wine and the olive oil to the priests at the rooms of God’s Temple. <i>We’ll bring</i> a tenth from our ground to the Levites, because they’re the ones that receive the tithes in the rural towns. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v38" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The priest, the descendant of Aaron, will be with the Levites when they receive the tithes.
