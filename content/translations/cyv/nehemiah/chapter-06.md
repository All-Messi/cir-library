## Nehemiah Chapter 6


### Conspiracy Against Nehemiah

<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Sanballat and Geshem sent me a message<i>, “Meet us at Chephirim in the Plain of Ono.” </i>They were planning to harm me. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I sent messengers, <i>“I’m in an extensive project. Why should the work stop while I’m with you?” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They sent me four messages like that; I answered them the same way. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then Sanballat sent his servant a fifth time with an open letter in hand. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’d written, </span></span></p>

Those reports say you’ll be their king. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You’ve also appointed prophets to make a declaration in Jerusalem about you, ‘There’s a king in Judah!’ Those reports will be passed on to the king. Come; let’s talk about it.” </span></i><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                </span></b></span></span></p>

<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I said, <i>“Should a man like me run to the Temple to save my life? I won’t do that.”</i> </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Many in Judah were bound to him under oath because he was the son-in-law of Shecaniah son of Arah.
