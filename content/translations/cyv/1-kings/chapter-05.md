## 1 Kings Chapter 5


### Preparations for Building the Temple


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>Hiram, king of Tyre, sent ambassadors to Solomon when he heard they’d anointed him king in place of his father. He’d always been a friend to David. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Solomon sent him a message,</span><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%'> </span></sup></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>“You know my father couldn’t build a temple for Yahveh his God because he carried on warfare till Yahveh put down his enemies. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>But now Yahveh has given me peace in every direction. There’s no adversary or misfortune. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>I intend to build a temple for Yahveh like he said, ‘Your son that I’ll set on your throne will build me a temple.’ </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Have </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>your woodsmen<i> cut cedars from Lebanon, and my workmen will work with yours. I’ll pay them whatever wages you set. You know there’s nobody among us that knows how to cut timber like Sidonians.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>When</span><b><i><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%'> </span></sup></i></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Hiram heard what Solomon said,<i> </i>he was happy, <i>“Bless Yahveh that’s given David a wise son over this great people.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>He sent word to Solomon,<i> </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>“I’ve heard your message. I’ll do what you want about the cedar and cypress timber. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>My workmen will bring it down from Lebanon to the sea, and tie it together in rafts to go by sea to where you designate. I’ll have them taken apart there for you to take away. Then you’ll satisfy me by feeding my household.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Hiram gave him as much cedar and cypress as he wanted. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Each year Solomon gave him <a name="_Hlk77367124">220,000 bushels </a></span><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%'>20,000 kors</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'> of wheat and 1800 gallons </span><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%'>20 kors</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'> of pressed olive oil </span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>for his household. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Yahveh gave Solomon wisdom like he’d promised. Hiram and Solomon signed a peace treaty.     </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>                                                                                                                                            </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%'>1 Kg 5:1-12</span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><a name="_Hlk126068569"><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> </a><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>King Solomon conscripted 30,000 laborers from all over Israel, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>and sent them to Lebanon in shifts of 10,000 a month. They worked in Lebanon a month and were home two months. Adoniram oversaw them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Solomon had 70,000 transporters and 80,000 stone cutters in the mountains, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>besides the 3,300 foremen that oversaw the project and managed the workmen. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>He commissioned them to quarry huge, high-quality stones for the foundation of the Temple. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>His and Hiram’s builders and workmen from the town of Gebal cut them and prepared the timbers and stones for the Temple.<b>
</b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%'>1 Kg 5:13-18</span></b></span></p>
