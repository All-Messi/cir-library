## 1 Kings Chapter 20


### Ahab’s War with Syria


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>Benhadad, king of Aram, gathered his army, 32 kings with horses and chariots. He went up and laid siege to Samaria and attacked it. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>He sent ambassadors to Ahab in the city, <i>“Benhadad says, </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>‘<i>Your silver and gold are mine. Your most beautiful wives and children are mine too.’”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>The king of Israel said, <i>“It’s according to your message, my lord king. I and everything I have are yours.”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>The ambassadors came back, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>“This is what Benhadad said, ‘I sent to you saying, “You’ll give me your silver and gold, your wives and your children,”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'> </span><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>but about this time tomorrow I’ll send my officials to search your palace and your officials’ houses. They’ll carry off whatever you consider valuable.’”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>The king of Israel called the elders of the land, <i>“See how this man is looking to make trouble; he sent for my wives, my children, my silver and gold. I didn’t tell him no.” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>The elders and the people advised, <i>“Don’t agree </i>to anything<i>.”</i>
<b>                         </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt; line-height:115%'>1 Kg 20:1-8</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>He said to Benhadad’s ambassadors, <i>“Tell my lord the king, ‘Everything you sent for the first time I’ll do, but I can’t do this.”</i> The ambassadors took him that message. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>Benhadad sent to him, <i>“May the gods do so to me and more also, if there’s enough dust left in Samaria for each of my soldiers to have a handful.”</i>
</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>The king of Israel answered, <i>“The one that puts on his armor shouldn’t brag like the one that takes it off.” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>When Benhadad heard it, he was drinking with the kings in temporary shelters and told his officers, <i>“Take</i> <i>your positions.”</i> <b>                                      </b></span></span></p>


### Ahab’s Victory over Ben-Hadad



### They took their positions against the city.
1 Kg 20:9-12


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>A prophet approached Ahab, <i>“Yahveh says: ‘I’ll deliver this big army to you today, and <u>you’ll know I’m Yahveh</u>.’”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>Ahab said, <i>“Who’ll do it?”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'> <i>“Yahveh says, ‘The young men of the rulers in the province.’” </i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>“Who’ll begin the battle?”</span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%'> </span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'> <i>“You.” </i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>He mustered the troops of the 232 provinces, 7,000 Israelites. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>They went out at noon while Benhadad was getting drunk in temporary shelters with his 32 allied kings. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>Israel’s troops went out first. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>Benhadad sent out <i>an inquiry</i>, and they told him, <i>“Troops have come out of Samaria.”</i> </span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>“If they’ve come out either for peace or war, take them alive.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>The troops came out of the city along with the entire army. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>They each killed their man, the Arameans fled, and Israel chased them. Benhadad escaped on a horse with the cavalry. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>The king of Israel struck the horses and chariots, and killed the Arameans with a great slaughter.<b>                                                                                                                                        </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%'>1 Kg 20:13-21</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>The prophet came to the king, <i>“Make plans for another attack. At the first of the year, the king of Aram will come back.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>Now the officers of Aram’s king told him, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>“Their gods are mountain gods; so they were stronger than we were. Let’s fight them on the plains. We’ll surely be stronger than they are. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>Put a commander in place of each king. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Muster an army like the one you’ve lost, horse for horse, chariot for chariot. Then fight them in the plain; we’ll surely be stronger than they are.” </span></i><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt; line-height:115%'>1 Kg 20:22-25</span></b><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>     </span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>At the first of the year, Benhadad mustered the Arameans and went up to Aphek to fight Israel. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>The Israelites mustered, gathered provisions, and went to meet them. They camped there like two little flocks of goats, but the Arameans filled the countryside.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Then a man of God came up and told the king of Israel<i>, </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>“Yahveh says, ‘Because the Arameans have said I’m a mountain god, not a god of the valleys, I’ll give this great army to you; <u>you’ll</u> <u>know I’m Yahveh</u>.’”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'> </span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>They camped against one another for a week. The seventh day, the battle began, and the Israelites killed 100,000 foot soldiers in one day. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>The rest fled into Aphek, and the wall fell on 27,000 more. Benhadad fled to the city into an inner room.<b>
</b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%'>1 Kg 20:26-30</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>His officers told him, <i>“We’ve heard that the kings of Israel are merciful. Let’s put burlap around our waists and ropes on our heads, and go out to the king of Israel. Maybe he’ll let you live.” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>So they put burlap around their waists and ropes on their heads, and went to the king of Israel, <i>“Benhadad says, ‘Please don’t kill me.’” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>He said, <i>“Is my brother still alive.”</i></span></p>


### A Prophet Condemns Ahab’s Release


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>The officers quickly picked up on his word and said, <i>“Your brother Benhadad</i>.<i>”</i> He said, <i>“Go bring him here.” </i>Benhadad came out, and he took him up into the chariot.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>Benhadad</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'> told him, <i>“I’ll restore the towns my father took from your father, and you’ll make streets in Damascus like my father did in Samaria.” Ahab</i> said, <i>“And I’ll let you go with this treaty.”</i> He made a treaty with him and let him go.     </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%'>1 Kg 20:31-34</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>One man from the company of prophets said to another by a message from Yahveh, <i>“Strike me,”</i> but he wouldn’t do it. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>“Because you’ve ignored Yahveh, as soon as you leave me, a lion will kill you.” </span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%'>That’s what happened. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v37" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>He found another man, <i>“Strike me.”</i> He struck him and wounded him. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v38" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>The prophet left and waited for the king by the road. He disguised himself with a bandage over his eyes. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v39" id="v39"><sup class="verse-num">39</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>As the king went by, he shouted, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>“I went into the battle, and a man brought a fellow over and said, ‘Guard this man. If he’s missing for any reason, it’s your life for his, or you’ll pay <a name="_Hlk145078894">75½ lbs <sup>a talent</sup> of silver</a>.’ </span></i><span class="verse verse-v40" id="v40"><sup class="verse-num">40</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>While I was busy here and there, he got away.”  </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>The king told him, <i>“That’s what your judgment will be. You’ve decided it yourself.”</i> </span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v41" id="v41"><sup class="verse-num">41</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>Right away he took the bandage off his eyes, and the king recognized he was one of the prophets. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v42" id="v42"><sup class="verse-num">42</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>He told him, <i>“Yahveh says, since you let the man go that he’d devoted to destruction, it’s your life for his and your people for his.’”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v43" id="v43"><sup class="verse-num">43</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>The king of Israel went home to Samaria sullen and resentful.<b>                      </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%'>1 Kg 20:35-43</span></b></span></p>
