## Nahum Chapter **2**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Attackers are advancing to crush you, <em>Nineveh</em>. Man the ramparts, guard the road, muster your forces, get ready.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>The shields of the valiant flash red; the soldiers wear scarlet uniforms; the chariots glitter like steel as they muster into formation; they rattle their cypress spears.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>The chariots rush madly down the streets and race wildly around the squares. They shine like torches, dashing here and there like lightning.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup><em>The</em><em> king</em> orders his officers; they scramble around in their haste and hurry to the wall to set up defenses.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>They strip it and loot it. Its young women moan like pigeons and beat their chests.</span>
