<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Take me with you; let's hurry off together! The king will take me to his rooms. <span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Don't stare at me because I'm dark, because the sun has darkened my skin. My mother's sons were mad at me; they made me take care of vineyards, I've neglected my own vineyard.</span>
