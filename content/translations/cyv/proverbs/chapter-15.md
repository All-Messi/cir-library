<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup><strong>15</strong></sup>Negative people have nothing but bad days, but with a happy heart they enjoy a continual feast.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup><strong>19</strong></sup>Briers overgrow lazy people’s paths, but the upright travel on an open road.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup><strong>21</strong></sup>Foolishness is fun for the foolish, but the wise walk on a straight path.</span>
