<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      pay attention, everybody in the land.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Tell your descendants about it;</span></span></p>

### Joel 1:1-3


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>What the cutting locusts left</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      the swarming locusts have eaten.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>What the swarming locusts left</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      the hopping locusts have eaten.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>What the hopping locusts left</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      the stripping locusts have eaten. </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Wake up, you drunks, and cry;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      wail, wine drinkers,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      for the sweet wine you can’t find anymore.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A nation has invaded my land, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      powerful and without number.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Its teeth are like a lion’s teeth;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      it has fangs like a lioness.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>It’s destroyed my grapevines</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and ruined my fig trees.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>It’s stripped off their bark and thrown it away,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      so their branches are bare and white.                   </span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Joel 1:4-7</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Wail like a virgin wearing burlap</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      for her young groom.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The grain offering and liquid offering have stopped</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      in Yahveh’s Temple.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The priests, Yahveh’s ministers, mourn.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The fields are ruined, the land is bare</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      because the grain has been destroyed,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            the new wine has dried up,  </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            the fresh oil has failed.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Despair, farmers; wail, vinedressers,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      for the wheat and barley;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      the harvest is ruined.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The grapevines have shriveled up, the fig trees have withered;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      the pomegranate, palm, and apple trees as well.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The fruit trees in the orchard have dried up,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      the joy among the people too.                              </span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Joel 1:8-12</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Put on burlap and chant a dirge, priests;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      wail, ministers at the altar!</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Come, spend the night in burlap, ministers of God,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      because the grain offerings and liquid offerings</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            have stopped in his Temple.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Consecrate a fast, declare a solemn assembly;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      gather the elders and the people to Yahveh’s Temple,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and cry out to him.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Dread the day!</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      The day of Yahveh is upon us,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      a day of destruction that’s coming from the Almighty.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Hasn’t food disappeared in front of us,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and happiness left the Temple?</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Seeds shrivel under the clods;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      barns stand empty;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      granaries are abandoned</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            because the crops have failed.                        </span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Joel 1:13-17</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The cattle are bawling!</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      Herds are wandering aimlessly</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            because there’s no pasture;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      flocks of sheep are bleating in misery.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’m calling out to you, Yahveh,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      because fire has burned off the open range;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      flames have burned up the trees in the fields.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Even the wild animals pant for you</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      because the streams have dried up</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and fire has scorched the pastures.                       </span></p>
