## Joshua Chapter 7


### Achan’s Sin


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But the people violated the instructions about the things set apart <i>to Yahveh</i>. Achan Ben-Carmi (</span><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ben-Zabdi Ben- Zerah</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>) from the tribe of Judah, took some devoted things, and Yahveh’s anger burned against Israel.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joshua sent men from Jericho to Ai near Beth-aven, east of Bethel to spy out the area.<i> </i>They spied it out, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>came back and said, <i>“Just have 2,000 or 3,000 go up to attack Ai. There aren’t many of them.</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>About 3,000 went up but fled. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The men from Ai killed about 36. They chased them from the gate as far as Shebarim and killed them at the descent. The people’s hearts melted and became like water.        </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Josh 7:1-5</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joshua tore his clothes. He and the elders fell on their faces by the Ark of Yahveh till evening and put dust on their heads. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joshua said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Lord Yahveh, why bring us across the Jordan and turn us over to the Amorites? If only we’d been content to live beyond the Jordan! </span></i><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>What can I say when Israel has turned its back on its enemies? </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Canaanites and the people in the land will hear about it and surround us. They’ll remove our name from the earth, and what will you do for your great name?”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh told Joshua, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Why fall on your face? Get up.  </span></i><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Israel has broken my covenant and taken some devoted things. They’ve stolen and lied about it and put them among their own things. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So Israel can’t stand up to its enemies. They turn their backs on them because now they’re something to be destroyed. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Get up. Sanctify yourselves for tomorrow. You can’t stand up to your enemies till you get rid of the devoted things. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In the morning, come forward by tribes. The tribe Yahveh</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> <i>takes will come forward by families. The family he takes will come by households. The household he takes will come one by one. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The one that took the devoted things and everything he has will be burned. He’s sinned against Yahveh’s covenant by doing something horrible.”       </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Josh 7:6-15</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joshua got up early and brought Israel forward tribe by tribe; <i>Yahveh</i> took Judah. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He brought the clans of Judah forward; <i>Yahveh </i>took the family of Zerah. He brought the family of Zerah forward one household at a time; <i>Yahveh </i>took the household of Zabdi/Zimri<sup>mss</sup>. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He brought <i>Zabdi’s</i> household forward one man at a time; <i>Yahveh</i> took Achan Ben-(</span><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>Carmi Ben-Zabdi/Zimri Ben-</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>) Zerah. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joshua told Achan,
<i>“Son, honor Yahveh God of Israel and praise him. What have you done?”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Achan answered, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Truthfully, I did sin against Yahveh</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> <i>God of Israel. </i></span><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I saw among the spoils a beautiful mantle from Shinar, <a name="_Hlk145108379">5 lbs <sup>200 shekels</sup> of silver, and a gold bar weighing 20 oz <sup>50 shekels</sup></a>. I wanted them and took them and hid them in the ground inside my tent with the silver underneath.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joshua sent messengers to the tent. <i>The devoted things</i> were there with the silver under them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They brought them out to Joshua and put them down in front of Yahveh. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joshua and all Israel took Achan, the silver, the mantle, the gold bar, his children, cattle, donkeys, and sheep, his tent and everything he had, and went up to the Valley of Achor. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joshua said, <i>“Why trouble on us? Yahveh</i> <i>is bringing trouble on you now.” </i>Israel stoned
<i>them</i> and burned them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They piled a big mound of rocks over him that’s still there. Then Yahveh turned away his burning anger.        </span></span></p>
