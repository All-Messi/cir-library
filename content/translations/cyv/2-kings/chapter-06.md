## 2 Kings Chapter 6


### The Floating Ax head


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>The school of the prophets told Elisha, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>“There’s not enough room where we’re living. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Let us go to the Jordan, and each take a log to build a place there to live.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>“Go ahead.”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>One of them asked him to come along.                               </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>He went along to the Jordan, and they started cutting down trees.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>But as one of them was cutting down a
<i>tree for a </i>beam, the axe head fell in the water. He cried out, <i>“Master! I borrowed it!”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>            </span></i><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>The man of God said, <i>“Where did it fall?”</i> When he showed him, he cut off a stick and threw it in there and made the iron float.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>He said, <i>“Pick it up.”</i> He reached out and took it.<b>                                                </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%'>2 Kg 6:1-7</span></b></span></p>


### Elisha Traps Blinded Arameans


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>The king of Aram was at war with Israel. He advised his officers, <i>“I’m going to camp in such and such a place.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>The man of God sent a message to the king of Israel, <i>“Don’t go near that place; the Arameans are coming down there.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>The king of Israel sent to where the man of God told him. He warned him more than once, so he guarded himself there.
</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>The king of Aram was mad and called his officers, <i>“Who’s been an informant for the king of Israel?”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>One of the officers said, <i>“No, my lord the king! Elisha the prophet in Israel tells him what you say in your bedroom.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>“Go see where he is so I can send and capture him.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>            He found out he was<i> </i>in Dothan.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>The king of Aram </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>sent horses, chariots, and a big army and surrounded the city at night.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>                                                                                                                                            </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%'>2 Kg 6:8-14</span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>When the man of God’s attendant got up early and went out, an army was circling the city with horses and chariots. He said, <i>“Master! What are we going to do?”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>“Don’t be afraid. There are more with us than with them. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Show him, LORD.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>            Yahveh let him see that the mountains all around were full of horses and flaming chariots. </span><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>When they came down to him, Elisha prayed, <i>“Strike them with blindness.”</i> He did. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Elisha told them, <i>“This is not the way, and this is not the city. Follow me and I’ll take you to the man you’re looking for.”</i> He took them to Samaria. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>When they got there, Elisha said, <i>“Yahveh, let them see.” </i>He let them see again that they were in the middle of Samaria. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>When the king of Israel saw them, he said to Elisha, <i>“Father! Should I kill them?”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>“No. Would you kill the ones you’ve taken captive with your sword and bow? Give them bread and water so they can eat and drink and go home to their master.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>He prepared a big banquet for them. When they’d eaten, he sent them away. Marauding bands from Aram never came into Israel again.        </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%'> 2 Kg 6:15-23</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>“This woman said to me, ‘Give your son so we can eat him today, and we’ll eat my son tomorrow.’ </span></i><span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>We boiled my son and ate him, and I told her the next day, ‘Give your son so we can eat him’; but she has hidden her son.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'> </span></span></p>

<span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Then he said, <i>“God do so to me and more if Elisha’s head remains on him today.”</i><b>   </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%'>2 Kg 6:24-31</span></b></span></p>
