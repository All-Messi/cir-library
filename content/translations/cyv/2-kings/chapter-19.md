## 2 Kings Chapter 19

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>“Tell your master, ‘Yahveh says, “You don’t have to be afraid of what the Assyrian king’s messengers have blasphemed.

<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>When he heard them say that Tirhakah [Taharqa], king of Nubia,<i> </i>had set out to fight him<i>,</i> he sent messengers back again to Hezekiah, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>Hezekiah read the letter, went up to Yahveh’s Temple, and spread it out to him. </span></span>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>                  You’ve said, “With my many chariots</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>                              I went up to the highest mountains,</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>                                    to the most remote parts of Lebanon.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>                              I cut down its tallest cedars and best cypress.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>                              I entered its most distant lodging place, its thickest forest.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>                        I planned it from ancient times.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>                  Now I’ve made it happen:</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>                        that you should turn fortified towns into piles of rubble,</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>                        when you come and go,  </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>                  </span></i><span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Because you’ve raged against me,</span></i></span></p>
