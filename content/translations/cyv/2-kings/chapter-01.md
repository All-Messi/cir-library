## 2 Kings Chapter 1


### Conversational Yahveh Covenant



### Yahveh’s Judgment on Ahaziah


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>After Ahab died, Moab revolted against Israel.   </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <u><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>Ahaziah</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'> fell through the lattice in his upper room in Samaria and seriously injured himself. He told messengers to go ask Baal-zebub, Ekron’s god, if he’d recover<i>. </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>But the angel of Yahveh told Elijah the Tishbite, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>“Go meet the king’s messengers and say, ‘Are you asking Baal-zebub because there’s no God in Israel?</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>’ </span><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>So Yahveh says you won’t get out of bed. You’ll die.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:.25in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Then Elijah left.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>When the messengers came back, he said, <i>“Why’d you come back?”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>“A man came up to us and said to go back and tell you that Yahveh says, ‘Are you asking Baal-zebub because there’s no God in Israel? You won’t get out of bed.’”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>“What did he look like?”</span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>“He was wearing a leather belt and clothes made out of hair.”
</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>“That was Elijah the Tishbite.” </span></i><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>                                                                              </span></b><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%'>2 Kg 1:1-8</span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>The king sent him a captain with his 50 men <i>to arrest him</i>. He went up to <i>Elijah</i>, who was sitting on a hill, <i>“Man of God, the king says to come down!”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>“If I’m a ‘man of God,’ fire from the sky will consume you and your 50 men.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'> That’s what happened.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>He sent another captain with his 50 men. <i>“Man of God, the king says to come down now!” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>“If I’m a ‘man of God,’ fire from the sky will consume you and your 50 men.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'> That’s what happened. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>He sent a third captain with his 50 men. When the captain came up, he knelt to Elijah, begging him, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>“Man of God, please value my life and the lives of these 50 servants of yours. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Fire from the sky consumed the first two captains with their men. Regard my life as worth something to you.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>The angel of Yahveh told Elijah, <i>“Go with him; don’t be afraid of him.” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Elijah</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'> told <i>Ahaziah</i>, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>“Yahveh says, ‘Is there no God in Israel? Since you’ve asked Baal-zebub, you won’t get out of bed.’”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'> </span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>He died, and <u>Jehoram</u> became king in the <b>second year of Jehoram</b> Ben-Jehoshaphat, king of Judah, because <i>Ahaziah</i> didn’t have a son. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>The rest of his acts are recorded in <i>The Chronicles of the Kings of Israel</i>.<b>                    </b></span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%'>2 Kg 1:9-18</span></b></span></p>
