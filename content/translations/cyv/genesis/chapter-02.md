## Genesis Chapter 2

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1-2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The seventh day God had finished creating the earth and sky and everything in them, and he rested from his work. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He blessed the seventh day and set it aside because he’d finished working that day.                             </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 1:24-2:3</p>


### The Creation of Mankind


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>This is what Yahveh God did when he created the earth and sky. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Shrubs and plants hadn’t sprouted yet, because he<sup>Y</sup> hadn’t made it rain and there wasn’t anybody to cultivate the ground. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A mist was coming up from the ground to water it. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then he<sup>Y</sup> formed a man out of the “ground,” breathed into him, and made him alive. The man became a living, breathing being.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh God planted an orchard in the East in Eden and put the man there. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>From the ground he<sup>Y</sup> made trees grow that looked nice and were good for producing food. In the orchard <i>he planted</i> the tree of life and the tree for knowing good and bad. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A river ran out of Eden to water the orchard. Then it separated into four rivers:
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11-12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the Pishon, that flows around the land of Havilah, where there’s fine gold, bdellium, and onyx; </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the Gihon, that flows around Cush; </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the Tigris, that flows east of Assyria; and the Euphrates.  </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 2:4-14</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh God put the man in the orchard to trim it and take care of it. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He<sup>YG</sup></span><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>told him, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“You’re free to eat </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the fruit<i> from any tree in the orchard </i></span><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>except the one that gives the knowledge of good and bad. If you eat its fruit, you’ll die.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh God said<i>, “It’s not good for the man to be by himself. I’ll make him a helper that corresponds to him.” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Out of the ground he’d<sup>Y</sup> formed every land animal and bird. He brought <i>them</i> to the man to see what he’d call them. Whatever he called them became their name. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He named the cattle, birds, and wild animals; but he didn’t find a helper that corresponded to him. </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 2:15-20</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So Yahveh God put him into a deep sleep, took one of his ribs, and closed up the flesh in place of it. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He</span><sup><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Y</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> formed a woman from the rib and brought her to him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The man said,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        <i>“This is bone from my bones,</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    flesh from my flesh.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        She’ll be called ‘woman,’</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    because she was taken out of ‘man.’” </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That’s why a man is to leave his parents and unite with his wife, and they’ll become one physical identity. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The man and his wife were both naked, but they weren’t self-conscious. </span></span></p>
