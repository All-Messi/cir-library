## Genesis Chapter 25


### The Death of Abraham


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Abraham married another wife, Keturah. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She had Zimran, <u>Jokshan</u>, Medan, <u>Midian</u>, Ishbak, and Shuah.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:1.0in;'><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jokshan</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> had Sheba and <u>Dedan</u>. The sons of <u>Dedan</u> were Asshurim, Letushim, and Leummim.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Midian</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>’s sons were Ephah, Epher, Hanoch, Abida, and Eldaah. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>Those descended from Keturah.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Abraham willed everything to Isaac. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He gave gifts to his concubines’ sons before he died and sent them east away from Isaac. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Abraham lived 175 years </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and died at a good old age, satisfied with <i>life</i>; and passed on to his people. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9-10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Isaac and Ishmael buried him with Sarah in the Cave of Machpelah, in the field Abraham had bought from Ephron, east of Mamre. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>After Abraham died, God blessed Isaac, who continued to live in Beer-lahai-roi.                                                                                                                   </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 25:1-11</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" align=center style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-top:0in;text-align:center'><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ishmael’s Generations </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" align=center style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-top:0in;text-align:center'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>(</span><span style='font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>Abraham’s Son by Hagar the Egyptian, Sarah’s Maid</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>)
</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>13, 16</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ishmael’s sons in the order of birth, by villages and camps, 12 princes by tribes: Nebaioth, Kedar, Adbeel, Mibsam, </span><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Mishma, Dumah, Massa, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Hadad, Tema, Jetur, Naphish, and Kedemah. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ishmael lived 137 years and passed on to his relatives. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His
<i>descendants </i>settled from Havilah to Shur, east of Egypt on the road to Assyria, away from his relatives.            </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 25:12-18</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" align=center style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-top:0in;text-align:center'><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Isaac’s Generations</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Isaac was 40 when he married Rebekah. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He prayed for her because she wasn’t having children. Yahveh answered him, and she became pregnant, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>but the children struggled with each other inside her. She said, <i>“Why am I this way?” </i>She went to ask Yahveh. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>                        <i>“You have two nations in your womb;</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              two nations will come from your body.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        One will be stronger than the other;</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              the older will serve the younger.”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When it came time to deliver, she had twins. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The first was born red all over like a “hairy” piece of clothes. They named him Esau.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His brother was born “holding” onto Esau’s “heel”; so they named him Jacob. Isaac was 60 when she had them.                                                 </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 25:19-26</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When the boys grew up, Esau became a skilled hunter, an outdoorsman. Jacob stayed around home, living in tents. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Isaac liked Esau because he had a taste for game; Rebekah liked Jacob. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>One day when Jacob was cooking stew, Esau came in from the countryside famished. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told Jacob, <i>“Let me have some of that ‘red’ </i>stew<i>. I’m starving.” </i>(</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So he was called Edom</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.)
</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Sell me your birthright first.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I’m about to die. What good’s a birthright?”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Swear to me first;” </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>so he swore to him and gave him his birthright. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then Jacob gave him bread and lentil stew. He ate it, got up, and left.                                               </span></span></p>
