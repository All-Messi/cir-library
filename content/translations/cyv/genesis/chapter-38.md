## Genesis Chapter 38


### Judah and Tamar


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>About that time, Judah left his brothers and went down to visit an Adullamite named Hirah. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He saw the daughter of a Canaanite named Shua<sup>mas</sup>. He married her and slept with her. </span></span>

</span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and one he named Shelah. That happened at Chezib. </span></span></p>

<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But Er did what Yahveh considered evil, so he took Er’s life. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Judah told Onan, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“Sleep with your brother’s wife and perform your duty as a brother-in-law to her. <span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Onan knew the offspring wouldn’t be his; so when he slept with her, he ejected his semen on the ground so he wouldn’t provide offspring for his brother. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That displeased Yahveh; so he took his life too. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Judah told Tamar, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Remain a widow in your father’s family till my son Shelah grows up.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> (</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told</span><i><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> himself</span></i><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>, </span><i><span style='font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“I’m afraid he’ll die like his brothers.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>)</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Tamar went and lived in her father’s family.                                                        </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 38:1-11</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>After quite a while, Shua’s daughter, Judah’s wife, died. <span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Tamar found out that her father-in-law was going there to shear his sheep. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She took off her widow’s clothes, put on a veil, wrapped herself, and sat in the gate of Enaim, which is on the road to Timnah. <span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Judah saw her, he thought she was a </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>shrine</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> prostitute, because she’d covered her face. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He turned aside to her by the road, and said, </span><i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Let me sleep with you.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“What will you give me?”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I’ll send you a kid from the flock.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Will you give me a pledge till you send it?”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“What pledge do you want?”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Your seal and cord, and the staff that’s in your hand.”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He gave </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>them</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> to her and slept with her, and she got pregnant. </span><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She got up and left, took off her veil, and put her widow’s clothes </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>back</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> on.</span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 38:12-19</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Judah sent the kid by his friend the Adullamite to get back the pledge, he couldn’t find her. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He asked the men there, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Where’s the </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>shrine
</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>prostitute that was by the road at Enaim?” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“There hasn’t been any </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>shrine</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> prostitute here.”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So he went back, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I couldn’t find her. </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The men said</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>there hasn’t been any </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>shrine</span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> prostitute there.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Judah said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“Let her keep them so they don’t ridicule us. I did send this kid, but you couldn’t find her.”                                            </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Judah said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Bring her out and burn her!”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>While she was being brought out, she sent to her father-in-law, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I’m pregnant by the man that owns these things. <p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Judah recognized them, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“She’s better than I am, because I didn’t give her to my son Shelah.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> He never slept with her again. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When it was time for her to deliver, she was carrying twins. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As she was giving birth, one put out a hand, and the midwife tied a red </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>thread</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> on it, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“This one came out first.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But he pulled his hand back and his brother came out. </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The midwife</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“What a ‘breach’ you’ve made for yourself”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>; so he was named Perez. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Afterward his brother was born with the “red” </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>thread</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> on his hand.
