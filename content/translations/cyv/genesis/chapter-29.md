## Genesis Chapter 29


### Jacob Marries Leah and Rachel


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jacob continued on his trip to the country in the east. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Up ahead he saw a well in a pasture where they watered the flocks. Three flocks of sheep were lying by it, and it had a big stone over it. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When all the flocks got there, they’d roll the stone off the well, water the sheep, and put it back. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jacob asked them, “</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Brothers, where are you from?”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            “Haran.”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Do you know Laban Ben-Nahor.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Yes.”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“How is he?”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“He’s good. That’s his daughter Rachel coming with the sheep.”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jacob</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“It’s still the middle of the day, not time to herd the livestock. </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Why aren’t you</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> watering the sheep and taking them to pasture</span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>?</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“We can’t water them till all the flocks get here and they roll the stone off the well.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>While he was still talking to them, Rachel came with her father’s sheep</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.</span><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Jacob saw her and Laban’s sheep, he went and rolled the stone off the well and watered Laban’s flock. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He kissed Rachel and cried out loud. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told her he was Rebekah’s son, a relative of her father. She ran to tell her father.                                                                                                   </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 29:1-12</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Laban heard it, he rushed to meet Jacob, hugged him, kissed him, and took him to his house. Jacob told him what all had happened. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Laban said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“For sure, you’re my flesh and bone.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> Jacob stayed with him a month. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Laban said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“Because you’re my relative doesn’t mean you have to work for nothing. What do you want for pay?” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Laban had two daughters, Leah, the older one, and Rachel. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Leah had less beautiful eyes, but Rachel was beautiful in form and appearance. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jacob loved Rachel, so he said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I’ll work for you seven years for your younger daughter Rachel.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Laban said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“It’s better to give her to you than to anybody else. Stay on with me.” </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jacob worked seven years for Rachel, but they seemed like just a few days because he loved her so much.                                                        </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Gen 29:13-20</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then Jacob told Laban, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Give me my wife so I can sleep with her. I’ve completed my time.” </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Laban gathered the men in the place and made a feast. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In the evening he brought <u>Leah</u> to him, and Jacob slept with her. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Laban also gave his maid Zilpah to Leah as a servant. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In the morning, it was Leah! Jacob said to Laban, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“What’ve you done to me? Didn’t I work for you for Rachel? Why have you deceived me?” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But Laban said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“It’s not the custom here to marry off the younger before the firstborn. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Complete the bridal week for this one, and we’ll give you the other one too for another seven years’ work.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jacob completed her week, and Laban gave him Rachel as a wife. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He also gave his servant Bilhah to Rachel as her maid. </span><a name="_Hlk124590268"></span>
<span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> </a><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jacob</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> slept with Rachel too. He loved her more than Leah and worked for Laban for seven more years.      </span></span></p>

### Jacob’s Children


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh saw that Leah wasn’t loved, and opened her womb, but Rachel was barren. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Leah had a son she named Reuben, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Because Yahveh has ‘seen’ my misery. Surely my husband will love me now.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She had another son, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Because Yahveh has ‘heard’ that I’m not loved, he gave me this son too”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>;</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>so she named him Simeon. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She had another son, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“This time my husband will be ‘attached’ to me, because I’ve borne him three sons”</span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>;</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>so she named him Levi. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She became pregnant again and bore Jacob a son and said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“This time I’ll ‘praise’ Yahveh”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>; so she named him Judah. Then she stopped having children.                                                                       </span></span></p>
