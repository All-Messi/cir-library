## Genesis Chapter 41


### Pharaoh’s Dreams


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Two years later, Pharaoh dreamed he was standing by the Nile. </span></span>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He went back to sleep and had another dream. <span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and swallowed the filled-out heads. Pharaoh woke up and realized it was a dream. </span></span>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The cupbearer said to Pharaoh, </span></span></p>

</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>both</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> had a dream the same night. </span></i></span>

<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pharaoh told him, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“I’ve had a dream that no one can interpret. I’ve heard that you can interpret dreams.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></i><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“It’s not me. God will give you a sensible answer</span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>           </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pharaoh told Joseph,</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I dreamed I was standing on the bank of the Nile. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Seven healthy, well-fed cows came up from the river and started grazing on the marsh grass. </span></i></span>

<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>came up and ate the first seven cows. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yet when they’d eaten them, you couldn’t tell it. They were as scrawny as before. Then I woke up.</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I also dreamed about seven filled-out, good grain heads that grew on a single stalk. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Seven withered, thin, wind-scorched grain heads sprouted up, </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and swallowed the seven good heads. <p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span></i><span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Joseph told him,</span></span></p>

<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The seven bony, scrawny cows that came up after them and the seven thin heads scorched by the east wind represent seven years of famine. [</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>]</span></i><b><i><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></sup></i></b></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Seven abundant years are coming on Egypt. [</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>]
</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The seven-year famine that follows will ruin the land. It’ll be severe enough to erase the memory of previous abundance. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Repeating the dream emphasizes that God is determined to do it. He’ll make it happen soon.
</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Now you can look for a discerning man to appoint over Egypt. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He can appoint land managers and exact a fifth of the crop during the seven abundant years. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They can gather the food during those years, store it up in cities under your authority, and guard it. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>It can remain in reserve for the seven famine years so the land won’t perish </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>from the famine</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>.    </span></i></span></span></p>

<span class="verse verse-v38" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He asked, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“Can we find anyone else like this who has a divine spirit in him?”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v41" id="v41"><sup class="verse-num">41</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ve set you over Egypt.”</span></i></span></p>
