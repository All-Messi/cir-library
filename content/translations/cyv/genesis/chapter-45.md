## Genesis Chapter 45

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Joseph couldn't control himself in front of everybody that was standing there. He cried out, "Everybody out!" Is my father still alive?" They were so overwhelmed by his presence they couldn't answer him.</span>
