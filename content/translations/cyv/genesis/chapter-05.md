## Genesis Chapter 5

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Adam's Descendants God created people to be like himself. 2He made them male and female, blessed them, and called them human.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Adam lived 130 years and had a son in his own image like himself. He named him Seth. 4He lived 800 years after that and had sons and daughters. 5Altogether he lived 930 years and died.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Seth lived 105 years and had Enosh. 7He lived 807 years after that and had sons and daughters. 8Altogether he lived 912 years and died.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Enosh lived 90 years and had Kenan. 10He lived 815 years after that and had sons and daughters. 11Altogether he lived 905 years and died.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Kenan lived 70 years and had Mahalalel. 13He lived 840 years after that and had sons and daughters. 14Altogether he lived 910 years and died.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> Mahalalel lived 65 years and had Jared. 16He lived 830 years after that and had sons and daughters. 17Altogether he lived 895 years and died.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> Jared lived 162 years and had Enoch. 19He lived 800 years after that and had sons and daughters. 20Altogether he lived 962 years and died.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> Enoch lived 65 years and had Methuselah. 22Then Enoch walked with God 300 years after that and had sons and daughters. 32Altogether he lived 365 years. 24He walked with God and wasn't there because God took him.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> Methuselah lived 187 years and had Lamech. 26He lived 782 years after that and had sons and daughters. 27Altogether he lived 969 years and died.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> Lamech lived 182 years and had a son. 29He named him "Noah," "He'll 'relieve' us of our labor from the ground Yahveh cursed." 30Lamech lived 595 years after that and had sons and daughters. 31Altogether he lived 775 years and died.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> Noah was 500 years old and had Shem, Ham, and Japheth. Evil Multiplies</span>
