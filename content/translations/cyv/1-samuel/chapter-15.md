## 1 Samuel Chapter 15


### The LORD Rejects Saul


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then Samuel told Saul, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Yahveh of Hosts sent me to anoint you king over his people. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He says, ‘I’m going to settle up with Amalek for opposing Israel on their way up from Egypt. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Strike him and destroy everything he has. Don’t spare him. Put to death man, woman, child, infant, ox, sheep, camel, and donkey.’”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Saul called in the people and counted them at Telaim—200,000 foot soldiers and 10,000 men of Judah. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He came to Amalek’s city and set up an ambush in the valley. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told the Kenites, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Get away from the Amalekites so I won’t be destroying you with them. You were kind to Israel when they came up from Egypt.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>The Kenites left. <b>                                                                                                  </b></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">1 Sam 15:1-6</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Saul defeated the Amalekites from Havilah all the way to Shur, east of Egypt. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He captured Agag the king and destroyed the people with the sword. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But he and the people spared Agag and the best sheep, cattle, grain-fed animals, lambs, and everything that was good. <p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A message from Yahveh came to Samuel, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I’ve changed my mind about having Saul as king. He’s not been doing what I tell him.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> Samuel was distressed and cried out to Yahveh all night. </span><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He got up early to meet Saul. <p class="section-ref">1 Sam 15:7-12</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Samuel came and Saul said to him, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Yahveh’s blessed you! <p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But Samuel asked, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“What’s this bleating and bawling?”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“They’ve brought them from the Amalekites. The people spared the best sheep and cattle to sacrifice to Yahveh your God. The rest we’ve destroyed.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Wait, let me tell you what Yahveh told me last night.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“Tell me.” </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Isn’t it true, though you felt inadequate, Yahveh made you the head over Israel? He anointed you king </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and sent you to kill off the Amalekites completely. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Why didn’t you obey him, but rushed on the spoil and did what he considered bad.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I did obey him; I went on his mission and brought back Agag the king and destroyed the Amalekites. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But the people took some of the spoil—sheep and cattle, the best things devoted to destruction—to sacrifice to Yahveh at Gilgal.” </span></i><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>    </span></b></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">1 Sam 15:13-21</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Samuel said,                         </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Does Yahveh delight as much in burnt offerings and sacrifices</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        as
</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>he does </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>in obedience?</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            Obedience is better than sacrifice,</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        listening than the fat of rams. </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Rebellion is like fortunetelling;</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        insubordination is like iniquity and idolatry.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            Because you’ve refused to do what Yahveh said,</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        he’s rejected you from being king.”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Saul said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I’ve sinned against his command and what you’ve said. I was afraid of the people and listened to them. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pardon my sin and go back with me so I can worship Yahveh.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I won’t go back with you. You’ve rejected his<sup>Y</sup> message, and he’s rejected you from being king.”</span></i><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>        </span></b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">1 Sam 15:22-26</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As Samuel turned to go, Saul grabbed the hem of his robe, and it tore. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Samuel said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Yahveh has torn the kingdom from you today and given it someone better than you. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Glory of Israel doesn’t lie or change his mind. He’s not a mortal that he’d do that.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I’ve sinned, but please honor me in front of the elders and my people. Go back with me so I can worship Yahveh your God.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Samuel followed him back, and Saul worshiped Yahveh. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then Samuel said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Bring me Agag.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> Agag came to him relieved, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Bitter death must be past.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But Samuel said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“As your sword has made women childless, so your mother will be childless.” </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He cut Agag to pieces in Yahveh’s presence at Gilgal.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then he went to Ramah, but Saul went home to Gibeah. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Samuel never saw him again; he grieved over him. Yahveh changed his mind about having him be king.</span></span></p>
