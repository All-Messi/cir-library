## 1 Samuel Chapter 1


### Conversational Yahveh Covenant



### The Birth of Samuel


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Elkanah was from Ramathaim-zophim in the hill country of Ephraim. <p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He went up to Shiloh every year to worship and sacrifice to </span><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh of hosts. <span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>but he’d give Hannah a double portion because he loved her. But </span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh had closed her womb, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and her rival would bully her mercilessly about it </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>year after year. <span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Elkanah asked her, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“Hannah, why are you crying and not eating? Why are you sad? Am I not better to you than ten sons?”                                                                                    </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">1 Sam 1:1-8</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Hannah got up after the meal, while Eli the priest was sitting on the seat by the doorpost of Yahveh’s Tabernacle. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She was distressed, and prayed and cried bitterly. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She took a vow, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Yahveh of hosts, if you’ll look on my grief and give me a son, I’ll give him to you<sup>Y</sup> all his life. He’ll never shave his head.”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Eli was watching her mouth as she prayed. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Her lips were moving, but she wasn’t making any sound. Eli thought she was drunk. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“How long will you be drunk? Put your wine away.”</span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>  </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“No, my lord, I’m depressed. I haven’t drunk wine or alcohol. I’m pouring out my heart to Yahveh. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t consider me some worthless woman. I’ve been speaking out of my great anguish and distress.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“May God grant your petition.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Be gracious to me.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> So she went away and ate and wasn’t sad anymore.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They got up early and worshiped in </span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh’s presence and went back to Ramah. Elkanah had relations with Hannah, and Yahveh remembered her. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In due time, she had a son and named him Samuel, </span><i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Because I’ve ‘asked’ Yahveh</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> <i>for him.”  </i></span></span></p>

<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But Hannah wouldn’t go, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I won’t go till I’ve weaned him. <p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Elkanah said</span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>, “Do what you think’s best. She took him to </span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh’s Tabernacle in Shiloh even though the child was so young. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They butchered the bull and took the boy to Eli. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>She said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“As sure as you’re alive, Sir, I’m the woman that stood here beside you, praying to Yahveh. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I prayed for this boy, and Yahveh</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> <i>gave me what I asked for.
</i></span></span>
