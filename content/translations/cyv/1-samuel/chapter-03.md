<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Samuel didn’t know Yahveh yet, and Yahveh hadn’t revealed a message to him </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>before</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh called out to him the third time.
