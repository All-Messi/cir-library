## Isaiah Chapter 26


### A Song of Praise to the LORD


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>At that time,</span><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'> </span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>they’ll sing a song in Judah:</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        We have an impregnable city;            </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              it’s set up with secure walls and ramparts.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Open the gates so a good nation can enter,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              one that stays faithful.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You keep steadfast minds in perfect peace</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              because they trust in you.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Trust in Yahveh forever</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              because in him we have an everlasting Rock.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He brings down low the ones that live up high</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    in their “safe city”;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              </span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>he throws it to the ground.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The poor and helpless trample it.<b>                                          </b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Is 26:1-6</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The road for the righteous is not rough;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              Righteous One, level out our path.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We conform our way to your judgments, Yahveh;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              with all our heart we want to honor you.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>At night I long for you;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    my spirit looks for you earnestly;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              when the earth experiences your judgments,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    its people learn goodness.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Even if sinners receive grace,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    they don’t learn goodness;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              they keep dealing unjustly in an unjust land</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    and don’t sense Yahveh’s majesty. <b>                           </b></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Is 26:7-10</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh, you’re holding up your fist, but they don’t see it;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    show them your zeal for your people; </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    humble your adversaries;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    consume them with fire.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You’ll establish peace for us,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    since you’re the One that <i>actually </i>does for us </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                          everything we <i>try to </i>do.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Other lords have ruled us before,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    but you’re the only one we acknowledge.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Those dead <i>rulers</i> won’t live <i>again</i>; </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        their departed spirits won’t rise.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    You’ve punished them with destruction</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    and wiped out any memory of them.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You’ve made this nation great</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    and extended its borders; we honor you.<b>                    </b></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Is 26:11-15</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We looked to you in distress,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    able only to whisper a prayer,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    because you were disciplining us.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Like a pregnant woman at delivery time,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    she writhes and cries out in labor pains;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    that’s how we were before you, Yahveh.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              </span><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We’ve been pregnant, writhing in labor,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                          but nothing came of it;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    we couldn’t save the world</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                          or bring life into it. </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But
<i>even </i>your dead will live again; their corpses will rise;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    those who lie in the dust will wake up and sing for joy.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              Your light will fall on them like dew, </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    and the ground will give birth to their departed spirits. </span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Is 26:16-19</p>


### Deliverance for Israel


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Go home, my people,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    and lock the doors behind you;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              hide for a while</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    till
<i>Yahveh’s</i> anger has run its course.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh is coming from heaven</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    to punish people for their sin;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              the ground will show those whose blood’s been shed;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    it won’t any longer <i>be able to </i>hide its slain.<b>              </b></span></p>
