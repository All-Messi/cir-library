## Isaiah Chapter 27

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>At that time, Yahveh will punish Leviathan with his fierce, big strong sword, Leviathan the swift, slithering snake. He’ll kill the dragon that’s in the sea.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        <i>“</i>There’ll be <i>a delightful vineyard to sing about! </i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I, Yahveh, will be its keeper; </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              I’ll water it constantly </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              and guard it day and night so no one harms it.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span></i><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I won’t be angry anymore. </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              If anybody gives me briars and thorns in battle, </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              I’ll trample and burn them completely</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    </span></i><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>unless they rely on me for protection.</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              Let them make peace with me;</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              let them make peace with <u>me</u>.”</span></i><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                         </span></b></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Is 27:1-5</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In time to come, Jacob will take root;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              Israel will sprout and blossom,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              and fill the world with fruit.                                             </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    7</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Has
<i>Yahveh</i> struck <i>Israel</i> </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    the way he struck <i>Israel’s</i> strikers?</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              Has he slain Israel </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    the way he slew <i>Israel’s</i> slayers?</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>No, he contended with <i>Israel</i> by banishing them,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              expelling them with his fierce east wind.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So that’s how he’ll purge Jacob’s iniquity;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              that’s what pardoning its sin will cost,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    when
<i>God</i> makes <i>pagan </i>altar stones like pulverized chalk,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    when he leaves no Asherah pole or incense altar standing.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The fortified towns will be deserted,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    their houses empty as in open country;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              calves will graze there and bed down;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    they’ll chew on brush and branches there.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When those branches dry up, they’ll break off</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    and women will gather them for kindling;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              this nation doesn’t use any sense;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    so their Maker won’t show them any mercy;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    their Creator won’t be gracious to them.<b>                    </b></span></p>
