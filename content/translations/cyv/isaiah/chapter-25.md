## Isaiah Chapter 25


### Praise for Judgment and Salvation


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh, you’re my God.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        I’ll honor and thank you</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              for doing the wonderful things you planned long ago.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You’ve reduced fortified towns to ruble;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              the foreigners’ palaces will never be rebuilt.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        </span><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So strong people will honor you;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    towns in ruthless nations will come to revere you,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              </span><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>because you’ve defended the helpless</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    and needy in their despair;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              you’re a shelter from the storm and a shade from heat,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    because the breath of ruthless people</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                          is like a storm beating on a wall </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                          </span><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>or stifling heat in drought. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              You weaken the threats of foreigner powers</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    like a cloud’s shadow lessens heat; </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              the raucous war songs of the ruthless fall silent.              </span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Is 25:1-5</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>On this mountain Yahveh of Hosts will hold a lavish banquet for everybody in the world, a banquet of aged wine and choice meat. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Here he’ll remove the shadow that lies over every country, the veil that covers the nations. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’ll swallow up death for all time, wipe away everyone’s tears, and remove the abuse of his people everywhere. Yahveh has said so.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>At that time, people will say, <i>“This is the God we’ve waited for to save us. He’s Yahveh; let’s celebrate and be glad he’s done it.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>His hand will rest on this mountain, and Moab will be trodden down like straw in the urine of a manure pit. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’ll subdue Moab like a swimmer pushes down water. He’ll end its pride and slight-of-hand. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’ll bring down its impregnable fortifications and throw them on the ground.     </span></span></p>
