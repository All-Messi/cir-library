## Isaiah Chapter 62

### Isaiah’s Prayer for Jerusalem

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> For Zion’s sake I’ll speak <em>to </em><em>God</em>; for Jerusalem’s sake I won’t keep still, until your goodness glows brightly, and your salvation radiates like a burning torch;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> so the nations can witness your goodness and kings <em>can gaze at</em> your glory. Yahveh himself will give you a new name.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> You’ll be a beautiful crown in his hand, a royal diadem he holds up high.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> You won’t be called Abandoned<em> </em>[<em>Azubah</em>] anymore or your land be named Desolate<em> </em>[<em>Shemamah</em>]; <em>	</em><em>	</em><em>	</em>you’ll be My Delight Is in It<em> </em>[<em>Hephzibah</em>], <em>	</em><em>	</em>		and your land will be Married<em> </em>[<em>Beulah</em>], <em>	</em><em>	</em><em>	</em>because he delights in you, and your land will be married <em>to him</em>.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Like a young man marries a virgin, your sons will marry you, <em>Jerusalem</em>; like a groom is happy over a bride, your God will be happy over you.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Is 62:1-5</p>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> I’ve stationed watchmen on your walls, Jerusalem, not to be silent day or night, but to remember you to Yahveh</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> till he makes you something worth praising in the world.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> He has sworn by his right hand and strong arm, <em>“I’ll never </em><em>again let</em><em> your </em><em>enemies eat </em><em>your </em><em>grain</em> <em>	</em><em>	 </em><em>	</em><em>or </em><em>foreigners drink the new wine you </em><em>labor</em><em>ed for.</em></span>

<p class="section-ref">Is 62:6-9</p>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Go out through the gates; clear the way for my people. Build a highway so they can return; get the <em>loose</em> rocks off the road. Hoist a flag the nations <em>can see</em>.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Yahveh has declared all over the world, <em>“Tell Zion</em><em> its</em><em> salvation</em><em>’</em><em>s </em><em>coming;</em> <em>	</em><em>	</em><em>h</em><em>e</em><em>’</em><em>s </em><em>bringing </em><em>his reward</em><em> </em><em>with him.”</em></span>
