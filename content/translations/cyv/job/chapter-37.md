## Job Chapter **37**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup><em>“My</em><em> heart pounds when I think about </em><em>this;</em> <em>it feels like it’s out of place.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup><em>He</em><em> unleashes his lightning under the whole </em><em>sky;</em> <em>it travels to the horizon in every direction.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup><em>After</em><em> it his </em><em>roars;</em> <em>he thunders with his majestic voice</em> <em>and doesn’t hold anything back.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup><em>He</em><em> stops everyone’s labor </em> <em>so</em><em> they can watch his work.</em><strong>	</strong></span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup><em>Animals</em><em> head for their dens</em> <em>and stay in their lairs.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup><em>The</em><em> stormy wind comes from its rooms,</em> <em>and the driving winds bring the cold.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup><em>God’s</em><em> breath makes ice,</em> <em>and the wide expanse of water freezes. </em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup><em>They</em><em> change direction by </em><em>his</em><em> </em><em>guidance;</em> <em>they do whatever he commands across the world.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup><strong>13</strong></sup><em>He</em><em> makes these things happen either to punish his people</em> <em>or to water the earth and show his love.</em><em>	</em></span>

### 14“Listen, Job,  stop and think about God’s wondrous works.


<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup><strong>15</strong></sup><em>Do</em><em> you know how he controls the clouds</em> <em>or makes lightning flash in them?</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup><strong>16</strong></sup><em>Can</em><em> you explain the layers of dark clouds,</em> <em>the wonders of One that knows everything,</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup><strong>17</strong></sup><em>you</em><em> whose clothes are hot</em> <em>because the south wind isn’t blowing?</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup><strong>18</strong></sup><em>Can</em><em> you spread out the sky like he does,</em> <em>as hard as a bronze mirror?</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup><strong>22</strong></sup><em>Golden</em><em> splendor comes from the </em><em>north;</em> <em>awesome majesty surrounds God.</em></span>
