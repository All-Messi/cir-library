## Hosea Chapter 13


### Yahveh’s Anger at Israel


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Ephraim spoke, people cringed;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      They say, <i>“Sacrifice to them, </i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So they’ll be like morning mist,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      like chaff blown from a threshing floor,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      like smoke from a chimney.<b>                                             </b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I took care of you in the desert,</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      you got independent and forgot about me.</span></i></p>
