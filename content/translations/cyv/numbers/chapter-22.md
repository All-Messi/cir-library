## Numbers Chapter 22


### Balak Summons Balaam


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-left:4.5pt;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>          </span><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Israel traveled and camped in the plains of Moab across the Jordan from Jericho. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Balak Ben-Zippor saw what Israel did to the Amorites. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The people of Moab were afraid of them because there were so many of them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The king of Moab told the elders in Midian, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“This horde will eat up everything around us like an ox eats up grass in a pasture.” </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Balak, king of Moab at the time, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>sent messengers to Balaam Ben-Beor at Pethor, near the River </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Euphrates</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> in the land of his people. He called him and said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:10.0pt;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“A people has come out of Egypt. They cover the ground; they’re living across from me. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Come and put a curse on them; there are too many of them for me to fight. Maybe I can defeat them and drive them out. I know that whoever you bless is blessed, and whoever you curse is cursed.”                                                            </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Num 22:1-6</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-left:4.5pt;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>          </span><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The elders in Moab and Midian left with a </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>fee</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> for fortunetelling in hand and went to Balaam with Balak’s message. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-left:4.5pt;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>          </span><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“Spend the night here, and I’ll bring word back to you as Yahveh tells me.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> So they stayed with him. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-left:4.5pt;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>          </span><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God came to Balaam, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Who are these men?”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:10.0pt;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Balak Ben-Zippor sent me a </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>message</span><i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>, </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>‘A people has come out of Egypt and covered the land. Come put a curse on them for me. Maybe I can fight them and drive them out.’”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-left:4.5pt;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>          </span></i><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Don’t go with them. Don’t curse the people because they’re blessed.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-left:4.5pt;'><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Balaam got up in the morning and told Balak’s leaders, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Go back. Yahveh has refused to let me go with you.”           </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Num 22:7-13</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-left:4.5pt;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>          </span><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They went back to Balak, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Balaam wouldn’t come with us.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-left:4.5pt;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>          </span><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Balak sent a bigger group of more distinguished officials, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:10.0pt;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Balak says, ‘Please don’t let anything keep you from coming to me. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll honor you richly and do whatever you tell me. Come put a curse on this nation for me.’”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-right:.5in; '><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Balaam said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:10.0pt;margin-left:.5in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  </span></sup></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Though Balak gives me his house full of silver and gold, I couldn’t do anything great or small that’s contrary to what Yahveh my God may say. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Stay here tonight, and I’ll find out what else Yahveh may say.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-left:4.5pt;'><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>God came to him at night, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“If the men have come to call you, go ahead with them; but only do what I tell you.”         </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Num 22:14-20</p>


### Balaam and His Donkey


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-left:4.5pt; '><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Balaam got up in the morning, saddled his donkey, and went with them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But God was angry because he was going, and the angel of Yahveh stood in the road to hinder him. He was riding his donkey, and his two servants were with him. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When the donkey saw the angel standing there with his sword drawn, she went off the road into a field, but Balaam hit her to get her back on the road. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then the angel stood in a narrow path between vineyards with a wall on both sides. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When the donkey saw the angel, she pressed herself to the wall and mashed Balaam’s foot; so he hit her again. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The angel went on ahead and stood in a narrow place where there was no room to turn either way. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When the donkey saw the angel, she lay down under Balaam. He was mad and hit her with his stick.                </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Num 22:21-27</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-left:4.5pt;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                28</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh opened her mouth, and she asked, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“What have I done to you to hit me three times?” </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-left:4.5pt;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                29</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Balaam said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“You’ve mocked me! If I’d had a sword, I would’ve killed you!” </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-left:4.5pt; '><span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The donkey said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Am I not the donkey you’ve ridden all your life? Have I ever acted like this before?”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-left:4.5pt; '><span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then Yahveh opened Balaam’s eyes, and he saw the angel standing on the road with his sword drawn, and he bowed to the ground. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-left:4.5pt; '><span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The angel said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:10.0pt;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Why did you hit your donkey three times? If she hadn’t, I would’ve killed you and let her live.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-left:4.5pt;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                34</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Balaam said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“I’ve sinned. I didn’t know you were standing on the road. If you want me to, I’ll turn around.” </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-left:4.5pt; '><span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But the angel said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“Go on with the men, but only say what I tell you.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-left:4.5pt;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So Balaam went along with them.
</span></p>

<span class="verse verse-v37" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“Didn’t I urgently call you? <span class="verse verse-v38" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Balaam said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“I’m here!
