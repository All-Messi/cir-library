## Psalms Chapter 98

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Sing a new song to Yahveh because he's done outstanding things; his right hand and holy arm have given him victory.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> He has made his salvation known and revealed his goodness to the nations.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> He has remembered his enduring love and faithfulness to Israel; people the earth over have seen the deliverance our God gives.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Make a joyful sound to Yahveh, all the earth; break out in joyful song.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Sing praises to him with lyre and melody;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> make a joyful sound with trumpets and rams' horns to the King, Yahveh.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Let the ocean roar and everything in it, the world and those who live here.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Let the rivers clap their hands; let the mountains sing 9to Yahveh because he's coming to judge the world righteously and its people fairly.</span>
