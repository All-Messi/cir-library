<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Sing a new song to Yahveh because he’s done outstanding things; his right hand and holy arm have given him victory.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>He has made his salvation known and revealed his goodness to the nations.</span>
