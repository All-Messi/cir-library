## Psalms Chapter 128

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> How blessed you are that respect Yahveh and follow his ways.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> When you eat what you earn, you'll be happy, and life will be good.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Your wife will be like a fruitful vine in your house; your children will be like olive plants around your table.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> That's how you're blessed who respect Yahveh.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Yahveh bless you from Zion; may you see Jerusalem prosper all your whole life.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> May you live to see your grandchildren. Peace to Israel!</span>
