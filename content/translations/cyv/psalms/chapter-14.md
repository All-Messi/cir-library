## Psalms Chapter **14**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Fools keep telling themselves there’s no God, corrupt people that do corrupt things; none <em>of them</em> does what’s right.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>Yahveh looks down from heaven <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>to see if there’s anybody that lives wisely, if anybody seeks him.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>But they’ve all veered off course and become corrupted; not a one of them does what’s right, not a one.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>Don’t sinners realize what they’re doing <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>when they eat up my people like bread and refuse to call on me?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>Terror will overwhelm them later <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>because I’m taking the good people’s side.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>Oppressors try to frustrate the endeavors of the oppressed, but I’m their refuge.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>Oh that deliverance for Israel would come out of Zion! When Yahveh restores his captive people, Jacob will celebrate; Israel will be happy.</span>
