## Psalms Chapter 64

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" align=center style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;text-align:center'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>For the Choir Director. A Psalm of David</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Listen to my complaint, God;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      keep me from
<i>living in</i> dread of my enemies.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Hide me from the secret plots of bad people,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      from the schemes of sinners</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  3</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>that sharpen their tongue like a sword,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            that aim bitter words like arrows,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  4</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>that shoot at innocent people from hiding places,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            and do it suddenly without qualms.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They latch onto sinful purposes</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and talk secretly about setting traps;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      they say, <i>“Who will notice?”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They look for ways to be unjust, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      <i>“We’re ready with a well-conceived plot,”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      because the human mind and heart are cunning.</span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Ps 64:1-6</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But God will wound them with an arrow                             </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  8</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and make them trip over their tongues!</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      Everybody that sees them will<i> </i>shake their heads.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then everybody will be respectful</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and talk about what God’s done</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and ponder what he’s made.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Good people will be happy in Yahveh</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            and take refuge in him;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      the godly in heart will honor him.<b>                                    </b></span></p>
