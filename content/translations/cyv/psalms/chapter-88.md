## Psalms Chapter **88**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Yahveh God who keeps me safe, I call out to you day and night.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>Let my prayer come to you; listen to my call!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>I’ve had so much trouble; I’m close to Sheol.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>I’m as good as dead, like someone with no strength left<em>.</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>Abandoned among the dead like someone slain in the grave, I’m forgotten and cut off from you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>You’ve put me in the Pit, in regions dark and deep.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>Your anger overwhelms me; wave after wave weighs heavily on me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>You’ve made my friends avoid me by making me someone they detest; I’m trapped and can’t escape.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>My sight is blurry from tears; I call on you every day, Yahveh, and reach out to you for help.<strong>	</strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 88:1-9</p>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>Do you perform wonders for the dead? Do departed spirits rise and praise you?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>Do they declare your enduring love in the grave or your faithfulness in the place of destruction?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>Are your wonders known in the dark or your goodness in the land of forgetfulness?</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 88:10-12</p>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup><strong>13</strong></sup>Yahveh, I call out to you for help; in the mornings my prayers go up to you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup><strong>14</strong></sup>Why are you rejecting me? Why are you hiding from me?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup><strong>15</strong></sup>I’ve been attacked and close to death from my youth up; I suffer from the terrors you bring; I’m overwhelmed.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup><strong>16</strong></sup>Your furious anger has swept over me; your dreaded assaults have destroyed me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup><strong>17</strong></sup>They surround me like water all the time and engulf me completely.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup><strong>18</strong></sup>You’ve removed lover and friend far from me; my companions lie in darkness.<strong>	</strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 88:13-18</p>
