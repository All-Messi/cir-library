## Psalms Chapter 39

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> I said, “I’ll watch what I do so I won’t sin with my tongue; I’ll put a muzzle on my mouth while sinful people come around.”</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> But when I kept silent even about what was good, it was of no avail; my agitation grew worse.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> The more I thought about it the more the fire burned within me; so I spoke up:</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Yahveh, show me my end, how long I’ll live; let me realize how fleeting my life is.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> You’ve made my days like handbreadths; my lifetime is only a moment to you. We all stand like a mere breath.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> We walk around like shadows in turmoil for nothing; we accumulate wealth without knowing who will inherit it.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Now Lord, what is it I’m waiting for? You’re my only hope.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Save me from my sins; don’t let fools humiliate me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> I can’t open my mouth; I’m unable to speak because you’re the one that disabled me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Stop striking me; I’m exhausted from your blows.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> You discipline us for our sins and consume like a moth what’s precious to us. Clearly, we’re all nothing more than a breath.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 39:7-11</p>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> Hear my prayer, Yahveh; don’t ignore my tears because I’m a passing guest, a nomad like my ancestors.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Leave me alone so I can be happy again before I’m gone and don’t exist anymore.”</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 39:12-13</p>
