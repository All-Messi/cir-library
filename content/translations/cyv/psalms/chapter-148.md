## Psalms Chapter **148**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup><strong>Hallelujah! </strong> <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>Praise Yahveh <u>from heaven</u>; praise him in the sky.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>Praise him, all his hosts of angels.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>Praise him, sun and moon and shining stars.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>Praise him, highest heaven, and water vapor in the sky.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>Let them praise Yahveh because he commanded and they came to be.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>He established them forever by a decree that won’t expire.<strong>	</strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 148:1-6</p>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>Praise Yahveh <u>from earth</u>, you sea monsters and all the great deep,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>fire and hail, snow and clouds, stormy wind fulfilling his command,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>mountains and hills, fruit trees and cedars,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>wild animals and livestock, crawling things and flying birds,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>kings of the earth and all the nations, officials and judges</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>young men and virgins, old men and children.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup><strong>13</strong></sup>Praise Yahveh because he alone is to be esteemed; his honor towers higher than earth and sky.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup><strong>14</strong></sup>He makes his people strong and establishes praise for his holy nation, for Israel that’s near him.										<strong>Hallelujah!</strong><strong>	</strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 148:7-14</p>
