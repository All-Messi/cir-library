## Psalms Chapter 148

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Hallelujah! Praise Yahveh from heaven; praise him in the sky.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Praise him, all his hosts of angels.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Praise him, sun and moon and shining stars.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Praise him, highest heaven, and water vapor in the sky.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Let them praise Yahveh because he commanded and they came to be.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> He established them forever by a decree that won't expire.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Praise Yahveh from earth, you sea monsters and all the great deep,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> fire and hail, snow and clouds, stormy wind fulfilling his command,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> mountains and hills, fruit trees and cedars,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> wild animals and livestock, crawling things and flying birds,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> kings of the earth and all the nations, officials and judges</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> young men and virgins, old men and children.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Praise Yahveh because he alone is to be esteemed; his honor towers higher than earth and sky.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> He makes his people strong and establishes praise for his holy nation, for Israel that's near him.										Hallelujah!</span>
