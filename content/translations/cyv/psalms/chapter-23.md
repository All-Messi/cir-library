## Psalms Chapter **23**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Yahveh is my shepherd; I’ll have everything I need.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>He beds me down in green pasture and leads me beside still water.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>He refreshes me and guides me along good paths for his honor.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>Even though I walk through the darkest valley, <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>I won’t be afraid, because he’s with me; his rod and staff reassure me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>You set a table for me in front of my enemies; you anoint my head with oil; my cup runs over.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>Goodness and mercy will follow me all my life, and I’ll live in Yahveh’s house forever.</span>
