## Psalms Chapter **96**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Sing a new song to Yahveh; sing to him, all the earth.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>Bless him in song; proclaim every day the good news that he saves.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>Tell about his glory the world over, his marvelous deeds among the nations.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>Yahveh is great and deserves great praise; he’s to be respected more than all gods</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>because the nations’ gods are idols, but Yahveh created the sky.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>Honor and majesty lie in him; strength and beauty fill his sanctuary.<strong>	</strong></span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>Worship him in holy attire; tremble at him, all the earth.</span>
