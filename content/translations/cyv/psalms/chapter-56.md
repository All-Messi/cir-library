## Psalms Chapter **56**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Be gracious, God; people are walking all over me, oppressing me all the time,<strong><sup> 2</sup></strong>attacking me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>When I’m afraid, I put my trust in you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>In you, whose word I praise, I’ve put my trust; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>what can mortals do to me?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>All day long they try to frustrate my purposes; they think up ways to harm me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>They stir up conflict, they lurk, they watch my every step, looking <em>for </em><em>a chance</em><em> </em>to kill me.</span>
