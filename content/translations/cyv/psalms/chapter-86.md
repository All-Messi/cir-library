## Psalms Chapter **86**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Listen, Yahveh, and answer me because I’m troubled and in need.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>Preserve my life because I’m a godly man; save me because I trust in you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>Be gracious to me because I call out to you all the time.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>Make me happy because I hold up my life to you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>You’re good, ready to forgive, and abundant in enduring love to everyone that calls on you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>Hear my prayer, Yahveh; listen to my petition.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>When I’m in trouble, I call on you because you answer me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>There’s no one like you among the gods, no other deeds like yours.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>All the nations you’ve made will come <strong><sup>	</sup></strong><strong><sup>	</sup></strong>to worship you and honor you,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>because you’re great and do great things; only you are God.<strong>	</strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 86:1-10</p>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>Teach me your way; I’ll follow your truth; make me consistently respect you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>I’ll thank you, Yahveh my God, with all my heart and honor you forever</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup><strong>13</strong></sup>because you show me such enduring love; you’ve rescued me from the depths of Sheol.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 86:11-13</p>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup><strong>14</strong></sup>Arrogant people are attacking me, God; a gang of violent men wants to kill me; they don’t care about you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup><strong>15</strong></sup>But you’re a merciful, gracious God, slow to get angry and abundant in enduring love and faithfulness.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup><strong>16</strong></sup>Turn to me and be gracious; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>give me your strength; save your servant’s son.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup><strong>17</strong></sup>Show me a sign of your favor so the ones that hate me can see it and be put to shame, because you’ve helped me and reassured me.<strong>	</strong></span>
