## Psalms Chapter **77**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>I raise my voice up to God; I call out loud; it rises to him so he’ll hear me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>When trouble comes, I turn to Yahveh for help. All night I reach out to him, but I get no reassurance.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>When I think about him, I’m shaken; I sigh and feel faint.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>He won’t let me sleep; I’m in such distress I can’t pray.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>I think about days gone by, years ago.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>I remember singing during the night, meditating and pondering in my mind.<strong>	</strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 77:1-6</p>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>Will Yahveh reject me forever now? Won’t he ever be kind to me again?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>Has his enduring love stopped permanently? Have his promises come to an end?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>Has he forgotten to be gracious? In his anger has he withdrawn his mercy?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>I said, <em>“It’s my destiny</em> <em>	that </em><em>Yahveh </em><em>has </em><em>turned against me</em><em>.”</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>Then I reconsider what all you’ve done, Yahveh, and recall your wonders from of old.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>I think about everything you do.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup><strong>13</strong></sup>Your way is holy; What god is as great as you?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup><strong>14</strong></sup>You work wonders and demonstrate your power among the nations.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup><strong>15</strong></sup>You’ve rescued your people by your strength, descendants of Jacob and Joseph.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 77:7-15</p>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup><strong>16</strong></sup>When the water faced you, God, it tremored to its depths.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup><strong>17</strong></sup>Clouds poured down water; the sky thundered; your arrows flashed.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup><strong>18</strong></sup>You thundered in the whirlwind; lightning flashes lit up the landscape; the ground shook.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup><strong>19</strong></sup>You went through the sea; your path led into deep water, but your footprints didn’t show.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup><strong>20</strong></sup>You led your people like a flock by the hand of Moses and Aaron.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 77:16-20</p>
