## Psalms Chapter **85**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Yahveh, you blessed your land; you restored the fortunes of Jacob;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>you forgave your people’s iniquity and pardoned their sins;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>you withdrew your fury and turned away from your hot anger.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>Restore us, our Deliverer; end your displeasure with us.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>Will you be angry with us forever? Will you extend your wrath to every generation?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>Won’t you revive us so we can celebrate over you?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>Show us your enduring love, Yahveh; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>grant us your salvation.<strong>	</strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 85:1-7</p>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>I’ll listen to what our God Yahveh says, <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>because he offers peace to his holy people, those who avoid foolish behavior.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>His salvation is nearby for those who respect him, so there can be honor in our land.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>Truth and enduring love combine; goodness and peace kiss each other.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>Faithfulness grows up from the ground, and goodness looks down from the sky.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>Yahveh gives what’s good so our land can yield its produce.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup><strong>13</strong></sup>Goodness goes ahead of him and makes his footprints into a path.</span>
