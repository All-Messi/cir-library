## Psalms Chapter **112**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup><strong>Hallelujah!</strong> How blessed people are that respect Yahveh and delight in what he calls on them to do.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>Their descendants will be successful and a blessing to their own generation.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>They’ll have wealth and riches in their homes, and <strong>their</strong><strong> goodness </strong><strong>will </strong><strong>last forever.</strong></span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>Godly people have light in the dark; they’re gracious, merciful, and good.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>Goodness comes to those who graciously lend and conduct their affairs with justice.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>Misfortune won’t overcome them, and people will remember the good they’ve done.<strong> </strong><strong>	</strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 112:1-6</p>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>Bad news doesn’t scare people like that <em>because </em>they’ve committed themselves to Yahveh they trust;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>they’re confident and courageous and face their adversaries with assurance.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>They give freely to the poor, so <strong>their</strong><strong> goodness last</strong><strong>s</strong><strong> forever</strong> in ongoing influence and honor.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>Sinners will see that and get mad; they’ll grit their teeth—and leave; what they wanted won’t happen.<strong>	</strong></span>
