## Psalms Chapter 136

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Give thanks to Yahveh because he's good. His love is everlasting.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Give thanks to the God of gods. His love is everlasting.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Give thanks to the Lord of lords, His love is everlasting.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> to him alone that does great things, His love is everlasting.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> to him that made the sky in his wisdom, His love is everlasting.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> to him that spread out the dry land higher than the water, His love is everlasting.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> to him that made the great lights, His love is everlasting;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> the sun to rule the day, His love is everlasting;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> the moon and stars to rule the night, His love is everlasting.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> to him that struck the Egyptians and their firstborn His love is everlasting;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> and brought Israel out of there His love is everlasting,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> with a strong hand and outstretched arm; His love is everlasting,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> to him that separated the Red Sea His love is everlasting.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> and passed Israel through it, His love is everlasting;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> but overthrew Pharaoh and his army in it; His love is everlasting;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> to him that led his people through the desert, His love is everlasting;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> to him that struck great kings His love is everlasting;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> and slew them: His love is everlasting.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> Sihon, king of the Amorites, His love is everlasting,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> and Og, king of Bashan; His love is everlasting;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> and gave their land as an inheritance, His love is everlasting,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> an inheritance for Israel his servant, His love is everlasting.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> who remembered us in our low estate His love is everlasting;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> and rescued us from our enemies, His love is everlasting,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> who gives good to every person. His love is everlasting.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> Thank God in heaven. His love is everlasting.</span>
