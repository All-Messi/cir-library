## Psalms Chapter 99

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Yahveh reigns; let the people tremble; he sits on his throne above the cherubs; let the earth quake!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Yahveh is great in Zion; he's exalted above all nations.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Let them praise his great, awesome name; he is holy.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> The great King who loves justice has established what's fair for all.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Exalt Yahveh our God and worship at his feet;	he is holy.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Moses and Aaron were among his priests; Samuel too was one who called on him; they called on Yahveh and he answered them.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> He spoke to them in the pillar of cloud; they kept the testimonies and statutes he gave them.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> He was a forgiving God; yet he disciplined them for their sins.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Exalt Yahveh our God and worship on his holy mountain; because he is holy.</span>
