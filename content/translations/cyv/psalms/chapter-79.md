## Psalms Chapter **79**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Other nations have invaded your inheritance, God; they’ve defiled your holy Temple and laid Jerusalem in ruins.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>They’ve given the buzzards your servants’ corpses, and the wild animals the flesh of your holy people.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>They’ve poured out their blood like water around Jerusalem; there wasn’t anybody to bury them.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>We’ve become a disgrace to our neighbors, mocked and derided by the people around us.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>Yahveh, will you be angry forever? Will your jealousy keep burning like fire?</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 79:1-5</p>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>Pour out your wrath on nations that don’t know you, that don’t call on you;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>they’ve devastated Jacob and laid waste his realm.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>Don’t hold our ancestors’ sins against <u>us</u>; bring us mercy quickly because we’re in despair.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>For your honor, help us, God who delivers; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>forgive our sins for your name’s sake.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>Why should the nations say, <em>“Where’</em><em>s their</em><em> God?”</em> Show those nations your vengeance for your servants’ blood that they shed.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>Listen to the moaning prisoner; with your great power save those doomed to die.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>Repay our neighbors seven times over for the taunts they’ve leveled at you, Yahveh,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup><strong>13</strong></sup>so we, your people, the sheep in your pasture, can be eternally grateful to you and recount your praise to all generations.<strong>	</strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 79:6-13</p>
