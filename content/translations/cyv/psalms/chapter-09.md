## Psalms Chapter **9**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>I’ll thank you, Yahveh, with all my heart and call attention to the amazing things you’ve done.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>I’ll gladly celebrate over you and sing your praise, Most High.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>When my enemies retreated, they staggered and died in front of you</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>because you judged in my favor; you sat on your throne and judged justly.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>You’ve rebuked the nations, <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>destroyed the wicked, <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>and blotted out their names forever.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>But Yahveh reigns as king forever, rendering judgment from his throne.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>He judges the world justly, delivers verdicts evenhandedly,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>a stronghold for oppressed people, a fortress in troubled times,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>trusted by those who know him <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>because he doesn’t abandon the ones who depend on him.<strong> </strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 9:7-10</p>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>Sing praises to Yahveh that lives in Zion; tell what all he’s done.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>He that avenges murder remembers the helpless; he doesn’t forget the cries of those who suffer.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup><strong>13</strong></sup>Be gracious to me, Yahveh; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong><strong><sup>	</sup></strong>look at how those who hate me treat me. Lift me up from death</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup><strong>15</strong></sup>The nations fall into the pit they dig and catch their feet in the net they hide.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup><strong>16</strong></sup>Yahveh makes himself known <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>for carrying out judgment. Sinners snare themselves in their own traps.<strong> </strong></span>
