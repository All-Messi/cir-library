## Psalms Chapter **143**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Hear my prayer, Yahveh; listen to my petition; in your faithfulness and goodness, come to my relief.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>Don’t put me on trial because, compared to you, nobody’s good.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>My enemy has chased me, crushed me on the ground, and made me sit in the dark like people long dead.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>So I feel overwhelmed, and my heart is empty.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>I remember the old days; I ponder everything you’ve done and think about what you’ve made.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>I reach out for you, thirsty for you like parched ground.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 143:1-6</p>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>Answer me right away, Yahveh; my spirit is failing; don’t hide from me, or I’ll get like those destined for the Pit.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>Let me hear about your enduring love every morning because I put my trust in you; show me where to walk because I’m lifting myself up to you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>Deliver me from my enemies; you’re my refuge.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>Teach me how to do your will because you’re my God; let your good Spirit lead me on level ground.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>In defense of your honor, Yahveh, protect me; in your goodness, take me away from trouble;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>in your enduring love, slay my enemies; destroy the ones that abuse me, because I’m your servant.<strong>	</strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 143:7-12</p>
