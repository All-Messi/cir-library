## Psalms Chapter 20

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> When trouble comes, may Yahveh answer you! May Jacob's God set you safely on high!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> May he send help from the sanctuary and support you from Zion!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> May he remember your many meal offerings and find your burnt offerings acceptable.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> May he grant you your heart's desire and make your plans successful.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> You'll sing for joy over his deliverance and hoist your banners in his name. May Yahveh give you your petitions.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> We know Yahveh saves his anointed ones; he answers them from his holy heaven with the saving strength of his right hand.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Some nations boast about chariots and horses, but we'll boast about Yahveh our God.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Those nations will collapse and fall, but we'll rise up and stand tall.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> Give us victory, Yahveh, when we call.</span>
