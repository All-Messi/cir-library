## Psalms Chapter **145**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>I’ll praise you highly, my God and king, and bless you forever.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>I’ll bless you every day <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>and praise you from now on.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>You’re great and deserve great praise; you have a greatness we have no way to measure.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>One generation will praise your works to the next and tell about the powerful things you’ve done.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>They’ll think about your majestic glory and splendor and about your amazing acts.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>People will talk about the power evident in your awesome deeds and tell how great you are.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>They’ll overflow with the fame of your abundant goodness and loudly sing about your godliness.<strong>	</strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 145:1-7</p>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>Yahveh is gracious and merciful, slow to get angry and great in enduring love.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>He’s good to everyone; his mercy spreads out across his whole creation.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>Everything he’s made will thank him; his holy people will bless him.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>They’ll speak of the honor his kingdom has and talk about his power</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>to make known his powerful deeds and the glorious majesty of his kingdom.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup><strong>13</strong></sup>His kingdom lasts forever; his dominion continues across al generations.<strong>	</strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 145:8-13</p>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup><strong>14</strong></sup>Yahveh catches the ones that fall down and straightens up those who are bent over.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup><strong>15</strong></sup>Everyone looks to him for help, and he gives them their food as needed.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup><strong>16</strong></sup>He meets the needs of every living thing.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup><strong>17</strong></sup>Yahveh shows his goodness in everything he does, his kindness in every deed.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup><strong>18</strong></sup>He’s near to those who call on him, that call on him sincerely.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup><strong>19</strong></sup>He provides for the people who respect him; he hears their call and saves them.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup><strong>20</strong></sup>He protects those who love him, but destroys the sinful.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup><strong>21</strong></sup>I’ll speak praise to Yahveh; everyone will bless him forever. <strong>	</strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 145:14-21</p>
