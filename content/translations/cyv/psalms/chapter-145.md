## Psalms Chapter 145

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> I'll praise you highly, my God and king, and bless you forever.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> I'll bless you every day and praise you from now on.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> You're great and deserve great praise; you have a greatness we have no way to measure.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> One generation will praise your works to the next and tell about the powerful things you've done.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> They'll think about your majestic glory and splendor and about your amazing acts.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> People will talk about the power evident in your awesome deeds and tell how great you are.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> They'll overflow with the fame of your abundant goodness and loudly sing about your godliness.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Yahveh is gracious and merciful, slow to get angry and great in enduring love.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> He's good to everyone; his mercy spreads out across his whole creation.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Everything he's made will thank him; his holy people will bless him.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> They'll speak of the honor his kingdom has and talk about his power</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> to make known his powerful deeds and the glorious majesty of his kingdom.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> His kingdom lasts forever; his dominion continues across al generations.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> Yahveh catches the ones that fall down and straightens up those who are bent over.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> Everyone looks to him for help, and he gives them their food as needed.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> He meets the needs of every living thing.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> Yahveh shows his goodness in everything he does, his kindness in every deed.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> He's near to those who call on him, that call on him sincerely.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> He provides for the people who respect him; he hears their call and saves them.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> He protects those who love him, but destroys the sinful.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> I'll speak praise to Yahveh; everyone will bless him forever.</span>
