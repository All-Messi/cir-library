## Psalms Chapter 138

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> I thank you with all my heart and sing your praises in front of the mighty.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> I bow toward your holy Temple and thank you for your enduring love and faithfulness, because you’ve guaranteed your promises with the honor your name has.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> You answered me when I called out to you and made me bold with strength.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 138:1-3</p>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Kings will thank you, Yahveh, when they hear what you say.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> They’ll sing about your ways because your honor is so great.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Though you’re high above us, you care about the lowly; but you recognize the proud from a long way off.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Though I’m in trouble, you’ll protect me and reach out against my enemies to save me.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> You’ll accomplish the purposes you have for me; your love endures forever, Yahveh. Don’t abandon the ones you’ve created.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 138:4-8</p>
