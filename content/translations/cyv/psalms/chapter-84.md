## Psalms Chapter **84**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Your Temple is so beautiful, Yahveh of Hosts!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>I long, yes, even faint with longing for your courts; my body and soul sing for joy to the living God.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>Even a sparrow finds a home for herself, a swallow builds a nest to raise her young near your altars, Yahveh of Hosts, my God and King.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>How blessed they are who live in your Temple and continually sing your praises.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>How blessed people are whose strength comes from you, who’ve resolved to make pilgrimages to Zion!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>As they pass through the Valley of Weeping, <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>they turn it into a spring; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>autumn rains cover it with blessings.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>They grow from strength to more strength till each one appears before God in Zion.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 84:1-7</p>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>Yahveh God of hosts, listen to my prayer; listen to me, God of Jacob!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>Extend your favor to the shield you anointed over us,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>because one day in your courts is better than a thousand <em>elsewhere</em>. I’d rather stand at the threshold of your Temple than live <em>at ease </em>in the tents of sinners,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>because Yahveh is sun and shield, <strong><sup>	</sup></strong><strong><sup>	</sup></strong><strong><sup>	</sup></strong>who gives grace and honor and doesn’t withhold anything good from those who do what’s right.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>Yahveh of hosts, how blessed they are who trust in you!<strong> </strong></span>
