## Psalms Chapter 111

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Hallelujah! I thank Yahveh with all my heart in the presence of his godly people.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> His works are so outstanding no one that delights in them can quit thinking about them;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> their splendor and majesty reflect his eternal goodness;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> he makes his amazing acts memorable because they show his grace and mercy.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> He feeds those who respect him and remembers his covenant with them;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> he demonstrated his power to his people by giving them other nations' land.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> His works are just and true; so his people can trust his commandments</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> to bring eternal security when they practice them sincerely.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> He has provided redemption for his people and ordained his covenant with them forever; he's holy and awesome.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Respecting Yahveh is the most important thing about being wise; doing what he commands brings understanding. Praise him forever.</span>
