## Psalms Chapter **111**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup><strong>Hallelujah!</strong> I thank Yahveh with all my heart in the presence of his godly people.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>His works are so outstanding no one that delights in them can quit thinking about them;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>their splendor and majesty reflect his eternal goodness;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>he makes his amazing acts memorable because they show his grace and mercy.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>He feeds those who respect him and remembers his covenant <em>with them</em>;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>he demonstrated his power to his people <strong><sup>	</sup></strong><strong><sup>	</sup></strong>by giving them other nations’ land.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 111:1-6</p>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>His works are just and true; so his people can trust his commandments</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>to bring eternal security when they practice them sincerely.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>He has provided redemption for his people and ordained his covenant with them forever; he’s holy and awesome.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>Respecting Yahveh is the most important thing about being wise; doing what he commands brings understanding. Praise him forever.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 111:7-10</p>
