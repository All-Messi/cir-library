## Psalms Chapter **21**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Yahveh, the king delights in your strength; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>he’s happy because you’ve helped him.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>You’ve given him his heart’s desire; you didn’t withhold his request.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>You met him with blessings and crowned him with fine gold.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>He asked you for life, <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>and you gave him life everlasting.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>He has great honor because you delivered him and endowed him with splendor and majesty.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>You make him blessed forever and happy because you’re with him.<strong>	</strong></span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 21:1-6</p>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>The king trusts in Yahveh; because of the enduring love of the Most High, he won’t be deposed.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>You’ll discover all your enemies; you’ll find out who hates you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>You’ll throw them into a blazing furnace in your anger; you’ll burn them up in your wrath</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>and destroy their offspring.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>Though they’ve devised a coup to harm you, they won’t succeed.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>You’ll put them to flight when you snap your bowstring at their faces.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup><strong>13</strong></sup>Be honored in your strength, Yahveh; we’ll sing praises about your power.<strong>	</strong><strong>	</strong></span>
