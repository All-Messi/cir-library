## Psalms Chapter 21

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Yahveh, the king delights in your strength; he's happy because you've helped him.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> You've given him his heart's desire; you didn't withhold his request.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> You met him with blessings and crowned him with fine gold.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> He asked you for life, and you gave him life everlasting.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> He has great honor because you delivered him and endowed him with splendor and majesty.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> You make him blessed forever and happy because you're with him.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> The king trusts in Yahveh; because of the enduring love of the Most High, he won't be deposed.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> You'll discover all your enemies; you'll find out who hates you.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> You'll throw them into a blazing furnace in your anger; you'll burn them up in your wrath</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> and destroy their offspring.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> Though they've devised a coup to harm you, they won't succeed.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> You'll put them to flight when you snap your bowstring at their faces.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> Be honored in your strength, Yahveh; we'll sing praises about your power.</span>
