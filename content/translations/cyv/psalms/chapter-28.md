## Psalms Chapter **28**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>I’m calling out to you, Yahveh; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>don’t turn a deaf ear, my Rock; otherwise, I’ll be like someone going down to the Pit.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>Help me when I plead for help, when I lift up my hands toward your holy sanctuary.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>Don’t drag me away with the wicked, with those who sin, who offer their neighbors peace while selfish motives lurk in their hearts.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>Pay them back according to their deeds, in proportion to the sin they practice.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>Since they don’t appreciate <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>what you’ve made and done, tear them down and don’t rebuild them.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 28:1-5</p>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>Bless Yahveh for listening to my petition.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>He’s my strength and shield; I trust him and he helps me; so I celebrate and thank him with songs.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>He’s his people’s strength, the saving defense for his anointed ones.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>Save your people and bless your inheritance; be their shepherd and carry them forever.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 28:6-9</p>
