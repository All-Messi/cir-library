## Psalms Chapter **47**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Clap your hands, everybody; shout to God with joy;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>because you are to revere Yahveh Most High, the great King over the whole world.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>He subdued people groups under us, <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>put nations beneath our feet,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>and chose our inheritance for us, the pride of Jacob that he loves.</span>
