## Psalms Chapter 29

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Children of the mighty, attribute to Yahveh honor and strength.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Honor him like he deserves; worship him in his holy splendor.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 29:1-2</p>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> The sound from Yahveh carries out over the water; our glorious God thunders across the ocean expanse.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> His voice is loud and majestic</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> and blows down cedars in Lebanon.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> He makes Lebanon skip like a calf and Sirion [Mt. Hermon] jump around like a young wild ox.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> The sound from Yahveh flashes out lightning bolts</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> and shakes the Wilderness of Kadesh;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> it makes deer go into labor and strips the trees bare. In his Temple everyone shouts, “Glory!”</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Yahveh sits like a king above the flood, enthroned forever.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> He gives his people courage and blesses them with peace.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 29:3-11</p>
