## Psalms Chapter 150

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Hallelujah! Praise God in his sanctuary; praise him in his heavenly expanse.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Praise him for his powerful deeds in proportion to his greatness.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Praise him with trumpet, harp, and lyre.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> Praise him with tambourines and dancing,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> Praise him with cymbals, with loud crashing cymbals.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> Everything that breathes, praise Yahveh. Hallelujah!</span>
