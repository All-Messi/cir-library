## Psalms Chapter 59

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" align=center style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;text-align:center'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>For the Choir Director. A Mikhtam of David,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Save me from sinners;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      rescue me from bloodthirsty people.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They lie in ambush to kill me;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      fierce enemies stir up conflict against me, Yahveh, </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      but not for my transgression.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>For no fault of mine, they run to attack me;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      get up to see and help me!                                                </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh God of Hosts, God of Israel,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      don’t extend grace to any that are treacherous.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They come back in the evening </span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            and roam the city, howling like dogs.</span></b></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            they have swords in their lips;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      they say, <i>“Who’s going to hear us?”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But you laugh at them, Yahveh;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      you scoff at the nations.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In your anger, destroy them so they’ll be out of the way,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      so people will know that you rule in Jacob</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            and everywhere.                                                          </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They come back in the evening </span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and roam the city, howling like dogs.</span></b></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:1.0in;'><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They wander around looking for food</span></span></p>
