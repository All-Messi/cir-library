## Psalms Chapter **59**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>Deliver me from my enemies, God; set me on high away from my attackers.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>Save me from sinners; rescue me from bloodthirsty people.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>They lie in ambush to kill me; fierce enemies stir up conflict against me, Yahveh, but not for my transgression.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>For no fault of mine, they run to attack me; get up to see and help me!</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 59:1-4</p>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>Yahveh God of Hosts, God of Israel, wake up and punish the nations; don’t extend grace to any that are treacherous.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup><strong>They come back in the evening </strong> <strong>	</strong><strong>	and </strong><strong>roam</strong><strong> the city, howling like dogs.</strong></span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>They growl with their mouth; they have swords in their lips; they say, <em>“Who’s going to hear us?”</em></span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>But you laugh at them, Yahveh; you scoff at the nations.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 59:5-8</p>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup>I’ll watch for you, my Strength, my fortress.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup><strong>10</strong></sup>You’ll meet me in your enduring love; and let me look down in triumph at my enemies.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v11"><sup><strong>11</strong></sup>Don’t kill them; otherwise, my people will forget; scatter them with your power and bring them <em>to their knees</em>, Yahveh our shield.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup><strong>12</strong></sup>For the sin of their mouth and the words of their lips, catch them in their pride, their curses, and the lies they tell.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup><strong>13</strong></sup>In your anger, destroy them so they’ll be out of the way, so people will know that you rule in Jacob and everywhere.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup><strong>14</strong></sup><strong>They come back in the evening </strong> <strong>	and </strong><strong>roam</strong><strong> the city, howling like dogs.</strong></span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup><strong>15</strong></sup>They wander around looking for food and growl if they’re not satisfied.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 59:9-15</p>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup><strong>16</strong></sup>But I’ll sing about your strength, every morning about your enduring love. You’ve been my fortress, my refuge in trouble.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup><strong>17</strong></sup>I’ll sing praise to you, my Strength, the God who shows me enduring love.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 59:16-17</p>
