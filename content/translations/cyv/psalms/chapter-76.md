## Psalms Chapter **76**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup>God is known in Judah; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>his name is great in Israel.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>His home is in Salem; <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>he lives on Zion.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>He has broken flaming arrows there, shields, swords, and weapons of war.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>You’re glorious, more majestic <strong><sup>	</sup></strong>than mountains <em>full of </em>beasts of prey.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>Our stouthearted enemies have been stripped of their spoil; they’ve sunk into deep sleep; none of the soldiers could raise a hand against us.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup><strong>6</strong></sup>At your rebuke, God of Jacob, horse and rider were thrown down into dead sleep.</span>
