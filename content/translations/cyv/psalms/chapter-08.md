## Psalms Chapter **8**

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup><strong>1</strong></sup><strong>Yahveh</strong><strong>, our Lord, you’re so majestic all over the world</strong>! You display your splendor higher than the sky!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup><strong>2</strong></sup>Out of the mouth of infants and nursing babies you’ve established a defensive wall against your adversaries, to silence enemies that want revenge.</span>
<p class="section-ref">Ps 8:1-2</p>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup><strong>3</strong></sup>When I look at the sky, the work of your fingers, the moon and stars you’ve set in place,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>what are we that you give us any thought or care about us?</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup><strong>5</strong></sup>You’ve made us a little lower than the angels and crowned us with glory and honor!</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup><strong>7</strong></sup>sheep and oxen, wild animals in the countryside,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup><strong>8</strong></sup>birds in the sky, fish in the sea, whatever swims through the ocean.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup><strong>9</strong></sup><strong>Yahveh</strong><strong>, our Lord, you’re so majestic all over the world</strong>!</span>
