## Leviticus Chapter 11

<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>little owls, cormorants, ibises, </span></i></span>
