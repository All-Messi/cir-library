## Leviticus Chapter 13

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Yahveh told Moses and Aaron,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v47"><sup class="verse-num">47</sup> <em>When</em><em> a piece of wool or linen cloth or clothes has mildew on it </em><strong><sup>48</sup></strong><em>as</em><em> well as on any leather article, </em><strong><sup>49</sup></strong><em>if</em><em> the mark is greenish or reddish, show it to the priest. </em><strong><sup>50</sup></strong><em>He’ll</em><em> examine it and quarantine it for a week. </em><strong><sup>51</sup></strong><em>Then</em><em> he’ll examine it on the seventh day.
