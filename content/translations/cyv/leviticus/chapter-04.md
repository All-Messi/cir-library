## Leviticus Chapter 4

<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He is to take it to the entrance to the Tent of Meeting, put his hand on its head, and slaughter it. </span></i></span>
