## 2 Chronicles Chapter 27


### Jotham Rules Judah


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <b><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jotham</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> was 25 and ruled for 16 years in Jerusalem. His mother was Jerushah Bat-Zadok. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He did what was good like his father, but he didn’t go into the Temple. The people kept acting corruptly. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He built the upper gate of </span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh’s Temple and built extensively the wall of Ophel. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He built towns in the hill country of Judah, and fortresses and towers on the wooded hills. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He fought the Ammonites and prevailed; for three years they paid him <a name="_Hlk145088452">7550 lbs <sup>100 talents </sup>of silver</a>, 110,000 bushels </span><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>10,000 kors</span></sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> of wheat, and 110,000 bushels </span><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>10,000 </span></sup><i><sup><span style='font-size: 14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>kors</span></sup></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> of barley a year. The Ammonites also paid that amount in the second and third years. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He became powerful because he ordered his ways in </span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh his God. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The rest of the acts of Jotham, including his wars, are recorded in </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Book of the Kings of Israel and Judah</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>. [</span></span>

</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He died, and they buried him in The City of David. His son Ahaz became king.                                                                                                                      </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>2 Ch 27:1-9</span></b></span></p>
