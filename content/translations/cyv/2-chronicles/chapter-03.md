## 2 Chronicles Chapter 3

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Solomon started building Yahveh's Temple on Mount Moriah, where Yahveh had appeared to David, the place David prepared on the threshing floor of Ornan/Araunah the Jebusite. 2He began construction on the second day, second month, fourth year of his reign.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> Ch 3:10-17 Temple Furnishings</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> The foundation for God's Temple was 30 x 90ft.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> The porch in front of it was 30ft wide and 180ft tall, overlaid with pure gold inside.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> He lined the Temple itself with cypress wood overlaid with fine gold and decorated with palm trees and chains;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> adorned the Temple with precious stones—the gold was from Parvaim;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> overlaid the Temple with gold—the beams, thresholds, walls, and doors—and carved cherubs on the walls;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> made the Holy of Holies—30ft square, equaling the width of the Temple—and overlaid it with 22 2/3 tons 600 talents of fine gold—9the spikes weighed 20 oz 50 shekels of gold; overlaid the upper rooms with gold.	 2 Ch 3:1-9</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> He made 2 sculpted cherubs in the Holy of Holies and overlaid them with gold. 11The combined wingspan of the cherubs was 30ft. The wing of one (7½ft) touched the wall of the Temple. The other wing (7½ft) touched the wing of the second cherub. 12The wing of the second cherub (7½ft) touched the opposite wall. The other wing (7½ft) was attached to the wing of the first cherub. 13They stood on their feet facing the nave.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> He made a curtain out of violet, purple, crimson, and fine linen, and worked cherubs on it;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> two pillars in front of the Temple 53ft tall, with 7½ft capitals on top of each one;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> chains in the inner sanctuary and on top of the pillars; 100 pomegranates and put them on the chains;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> He set up the pillars on the right and left that he named Jachin and Boaz respectively.</span>
