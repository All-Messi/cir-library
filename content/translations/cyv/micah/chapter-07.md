## Micah Chapter 7


### Israel’s Misery Turned to Hope


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Misfortune is waiting for me!</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      I feel like people that pick fruit and gather grapes</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  but have no clusters to eat </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  or first-ripe figs to satisfy their hunger.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Godly people have disappeared;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  there aren’t any good ones left.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      They all premeditate murder;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  each hunts the other with a net.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Both hands do evil well,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      officials and judges both demand bribes,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  influential people vie for preferential treatment;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      together they weave injustice.</span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Mic 7:1-3</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The best of them are like briars;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      the most godly <i>are</i> like a thorn hedge.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They can post a watchman,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      but their punishment will still come;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      their confusion is at hand.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t trust your neighbor,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      don’t confide in a friend,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      be careful what you tell your wise </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Son treats father with contempt;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      daughter defies mother,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  daughter-in-law defies mother-in-law;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      people have enemies in their own families.</span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Mic 7:4-6</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But
<u>I</u> will look to Yahveh;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      I’ll wait for God, who keeps me safe.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      He’ll listen to me.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t gloat over me, adversary.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      Though I fall, I’ll get up;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      though I live in the dark, Yahveh’s my light.             </span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Mic 7:7-8</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll bear Yahveh’s indignation</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      because
<i>admittedly </i>I’ve sinned against him;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      then he’ll give me justice <i>against my enemy</i>.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’ll bring me out to the light,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and I’ll <i>be able to</i> see his goodness.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As a result, my enemy will see <i>it too</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and be put to shame who said,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  <i>“Where’s Yahveh your God?”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      I’ll watch while he’s tramped on</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  like dirt on a road.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>It’ll be a time, <i>Israel</i>, for rebuilding your walls</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  and extending your boundaries,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         12</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>a day when people come to you</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      from Assyria and the cities of Egypt—</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  from Egypt to the Euphrates,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  from sea to sea and mountain to mountain.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But
<i>first </i>the land will become desolate </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      because of what its people have done.</span><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Mic 7:9-13</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Shepherd your people with your staff,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      your flock that lives alone in a woods</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  surrounded by productive fields.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      Let them pasture in Bashan and Gilead</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  like they did in the old days.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“I’ll show you amazing miracles </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      like I did when you came out of Egypt.”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Nations will see <i>them</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and be embarrassed at their own weakness.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They’ll put their hand over their mouth <i>in awe</i>,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and their ears will pop.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They’ll lick dust like a snake,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      like a reptile in the dirt.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They’ll come cringing out of their fortresses</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and turn to Yahveh our God.                                      </span></p>

### Yahveh’s Compassion for Israel


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>What God is like you, that pardons sin</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and passes over the rebellious acts </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  the remnant of his permanent possession has done?</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You’ll show us mercy again,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      tread our iniquities underfoot,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      throw our sins in the sea.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You’ll show <i>covenant </i>faithfulness to Jacob,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>enduring love to Abraham</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      like you swore to our ancestors so long ago.              </span></p>
