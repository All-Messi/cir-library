## Micah Chapter 1


### Conversational Yahveh Version


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" align=center style='margin:0in;text-align: center;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A Message from Yahveh to Micah from Moresheth</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" align=center style='margin:0in;text-align: center;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>at <b>the Time of Jotham, Ahaz, <i>and</i> Hezekiah, Kings of Judah.</b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" align=center style='margin:0in;text-align: center;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>What He Saw About Samaria and Jerusalem</span></p>


### Judgment Against Samaria and Jerusalem


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Listen, every nation;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      listen, world and everybody in it.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>Let the Lord Yahveh testify against you from his holy Temple.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’s coming down from his place</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      to walk on the mountaintops.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The mountains will mash down under his feet</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      and cascade into the valleys—</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  like wax in fire or</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> water on a slope.</span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Mic 1:1-4</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That’s because Jacob rebelled</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  and Israel sinned.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      Where’s Jacob’s rebellion?</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  Isn’t it <i>in </i>Samaria?</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      Where’s Judah’s <i>main </i>high place?</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  Isn’t it <i>in </i>Jerusalem?</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll make Samaria a pile of ruins in the countryside,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  a place to plant vineyards.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      I’ll drag the stones <i>of its walls</i> down to the valley</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  and expose its foundations.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         7</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll smash its idols </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  and burn everything in it.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      I’ll destroy its images </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  because they came from a prostitute’s pay;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  that’s all they’ll be.</span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Mic 1:5-7</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Because of this, I’ll mourn and wail,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  and walk around barefoot and naked.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      I’ll howl like a jackal</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  and moan like an owl.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>My people’s wound is too deep to heal;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>it’s penetrated into Judah</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  all the way up to Jerusalem’s gates.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t “tell” it in Gath;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      don’t weep at all;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      roll in the “dust” at Beth-leaphrah.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Just move on <i>into captivity </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>naked and embarrassed, residents of Shaphir;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      the people in Zaaman don’t dare “come outside” its wall;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      there’s mourning in Beth-ezel </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            because its people have no back up. </span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Mic 1:8-11</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Those living in Maroth grow weak waiting for relief,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      because “bitter” calamity from Yahveh</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  is reaching the gates of Jerusalem.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Harness a “team of horses” to a chariot and flee,</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      people in Lachish, where Zion’s sin came from,</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      where Israel’s rebellious acts spread from <i>into Judah</i>.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Send farewell gifts to Moresheth-gath;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      the houses in Achzib have “deceived” the kings in Israel.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll bring a “conqueror” on you, people of Mareshah;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      Israel’s leaders will flee to <i>the cave of </i>Adullam.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Shave off your hair <i>in despair</i> for the children you delight in;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      make yourself as bald as an eagle </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.75in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      because they’re going into exile.                                </span></p>
