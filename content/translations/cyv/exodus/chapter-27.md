### The Court of the Tabernacle 9“Make a court for the Tabernacle. Its south side will have curtains made out of finely twined linen 150ft long for one side. 10It’ll have 20 posts with 20 bronze bases. The hooks and bands on the posts will be silver. 11Make the north side of the court the same way. 12The west end of the court will have 75ft of curtains with 10 posts with their 10 bases. 13The east end of the court will be 75ft wide. 14The curtains for one side of the entrance will be 22½ft long with 3 posts and their 3 bases. 15Make the other side of the entrance the same way. 16The entrance to the court will be a screen 30ft wide woven out of blue, purple, and scarlet material with finely twined linen; with 4 posts and their 4 bases. 17The posts around the court will have silver bands with silver hooks and bronze bases. 18The court will be 150 x 75ft with curtains 7½ft high and bronze bases under its posts. 19Make out of bronze the Tabernacle’s utensils of service, its pegs, and the pegs in the court.
