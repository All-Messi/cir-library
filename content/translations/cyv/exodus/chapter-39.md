## Exodus Chapter 39


### The Priestly Garments


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>From the <u>blue, purple, and scarlet </u></span><i><u><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>material</span></u></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>, they made finely woven vestments for Aaron to perform service in the Holy Place—like Yahveh commanded Moses.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He made the <u>ephod</u> out of gold; blue, purple, and scarlet </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>material</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>; and finely twined linen. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They hammered out gold sheets and cut them into threads that were woven in with the blue, purple, and scarlet </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>material</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>; and the fine linen—an artisan’s work. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They made attaching shoulder pieces for the ephod—attached at the two </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>upper</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> ends. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The skillfully woven band on it had the same workmanship: gold; blue, purple, and scarlet </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>material</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>; and finely twined linen.  </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They made the onyx stones, set in delicate gold ornamentation. They engraved the onyx stones like the engravings on a signet, showing the names of Israel’s sons. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He placed them on the shoulder pieces of the ephod as memorial stones for them.  </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Ex 39:1-7</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He made the <u>pouch</u> to match the ephod, an artisan’s work. </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>It too was made</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> of gold; blue, purple, and scarlet </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>material</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>; and finely twined linen. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The pouch was square—9ins on a side when folded double. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They mounted 4 rows of stones on it: </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> row 1 had ruby, topaz, and emerald; </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> 11</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>row 2 had turquoise, sapphire, and diamond; </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> 12</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>row 3 had jacinth, agate, amethyst;</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> 13</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>row 4 had beryl, onyx, and jasper— </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>mounted in delicate gold settings. </span><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The stones corresponded to the names of Israel’s sons. The 12 names corresponded to the engravings of a signet, each the name of one tribe. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They made chains like cords on the pouch, twined cordage in pure gold. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They made 2 delicate gold </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>settings</span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> and 2 gold rings and put the 2 rings on the ends of the pouch. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They put the 2 gold cords in the 2 rings. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They put the </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>other</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> 2 ends of the cords on the 2 delicate </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>settings</span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> and put them on the shoulder pieces at the front of the ephod. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They made 2 gold rings and placed </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>them</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> on the inner edge of the pouch ends next to the ephod.                                   </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They made 2 gold rings for the bottom of the 2 shoulder pieces of the ephod, on the front of it, close to where it joined, above the woven band of the ephod. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They bound the pouch to the rings of the ephod with a blue cord so it could be on the woven band of the ephod, and the pouch wouldn’t come loose from the ephod.</span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Ex 39:8-21</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then he made the <u>robe of the ephod</u> out of blue woven work. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The opening of the robe was in the center at the top, like the opening of a coat of mail with a binding around its opening so it wouldn’t tear. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They made pomegranates out of blue, purple, and scarlet <i>material</i>; and twined <i>linen</i>, [</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>] </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and alternated them with pure gold bells and pomegranates around the hem.                                </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Ex 39:22-26</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They prepared <u>tunics</u> out of finely twined linen for Aaron and his sons. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They made </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>the <u>turban</u>, decorated <u>caps</u>, and the <u>pants</u> out of finely twined linen. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They wove the <u>sash</u> out of finely twined linen and blue, purple, and scarlet <i>material</i>.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            </span><span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They shaped the plate for the holy <u>crown</u> out of pure gold and inscribed it like the engravings of a signet: <i>Holy to Yahveh</i>. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They fastened a blue cord to it and attached it to the top of the turban.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That completed the work on the Tabernacle of the Tent of Meeting.
