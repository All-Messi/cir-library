## Exodus Chapter 12


### The Passover Instituted


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh told Moses and Aaron, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:9.0pt; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“This month will be the first month of your year. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Tell the assembly of Israel that on the tenth of this month, each family is to take a lamb according to their families. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If a family is too small for a lamb, that family and a nearby neighbor can take a lamb the size the number of people can eat. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>It needs to be an unblemished male a year old. Take it from the sheep or goats. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Keep it till the fourteenth of the month. </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:9.0pt; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“The whole community is to kill it in the evening. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Put some of its blood on the doorposts and lintel of the house where you eat it. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Eat the meat that same night, roasted with fire. Eat it with bitter herbs and bread without yeast. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Don’t eat any of it raw or boiled. Roast the head, legs, and entrails. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In the morning, burn what’s left over. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Eat I in a hurry, dressed, with sandals on your feet and staff in hand. It’s Yahveh’s Passover. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll go all over Egypt that night and strike down the firstborn people and animals. I’ll execute judgment on Egypt’s gods. <u>I’m Yahveh</u>. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The blood on your houses will be a sign. When I see it, I’ll ‘pass over’ you. No plague will fall on you when I strike Egypt. </span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Ex 12:1-13</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:9.0pt; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Today will serve as a memorial day for you. Celebrate it as a festival to Yahveh, a permanent ordinance. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Eat bread without yeast for a week. On the first day, take the yeast out of your houses. Whoever eats anything with yeast in it from the first day till the seventh will be excluded from Israel. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>On the first day, hold a holy assembly. On the seventh day, hold another one. Don’t work on those days except what has to be eaten by each person that only you can fix. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Observe the Festival of Unleavened Bread throughout your generations; that’s the day I brought your hosts out of Egypt. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>In the first month, eat bread without yeast from the fourteenth in the evening till the twenty-first in the evening. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>For a week, no yeast can be in your houses. Whoever eats what has yeast in it will be excluded from the community of Israel, foreign or native. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Eat bread without yeast in all your houses.”</span></i><span style='font-size:10.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Ex 12:14-20</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Moses called Israel’s elders, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:9.0pt; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Butcher a Passover lamb for each family. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Dip a bunch of hyssop in the blood that’s in the basin and apply it to the lintel and doorposts. Don’t anyone go outside till morning. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh will pass through to strike down the Egyptians. When he sees the blood on the lintel and doorposts, he’ll pass over and not let the destroyer come in to strike you. [</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <i><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>]</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When you go into the land Yahveh gives you, observe this rite. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When your children ask you what it means, </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>tell them it’s a Passover sacrifice to Yahveh, who “passed over” the Israelites’ houses when he struck the Egyptians.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The people bowed low in worship </span><span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and did what Yahveh commanded Moses and Aaron.            </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Ex 12:21-28</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>At midnight Yahveh struck the firstborn in Egypt—the firstborn of Pharaoh on the throne, the firstborn of captives in the dungeon, the firstborn livestock. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Pharaoh, his officers, and all the Egyptians got up during the night. There was a great outcry in Egypt; every home had someone die. </span></span></p>


### The Exodus


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He called for Moses and Aaron during the night<i>, </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Get up! Get away from my people, <u>you and the Israelites! Go worship Yahveh like you’ve said. </u></span></i><b><u><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>32</span></sup></u></b><i><u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Take your flocks and herds</span></u></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>. Go and bless me too.” </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Egyptians urged the people to leave right away, <i>“We’ll all be dead!”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The people took their dough before it had yeast put in it, together with their kneading bowls tied up in the cloaks on their shoulders. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Israelites had done what Moses said and asked the Egyptians for silver and gold articles, and for clothes. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh had given the Egyptians a favorable attitude toward them, so they let them have what they asked for. That’s how they “plundered” the Egyptians.      </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Ex 12:29-36</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v37" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Israelites traveled from Rameses to Succoth, about 600,000 men on foot plus women and children. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v38" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A mixed crowd <i>of non-Israelites </i>went with them along with flocks and herds, a very large number of livestock. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v39" id="v39"><sup class="verse-num">39</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The dough they brought out of Egypt they baked into flat cakes of bread without yeast. It had no yeast in it because they were driven out and couldn’t delay. They hadn’t prepared supplies.   </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v40" id="v40"><sup class="verse-num">40</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Israelites lived in Egypt 430 years. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v41" id="v41"><sup class="verse-num">41</sup> <b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>At the end of 430 years </span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>to the day, the hosts of Yahveh left Egypt. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v42" id="v42"><sup class="verse-num">42</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>It’s a night Israelites observe to him throughout their generations for bringing them out of Egypt.                                   </span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Ex 12:37-42</p>


### Instructions for Passover


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v43" id="v43"><sup class="verse-num">43</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh told Moses and Aaron, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:9.0pt; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“This is the ordinance about the Passover: no foreigner can eat it, </span></i><span class="verse verse-v44" id="v44"><sup class="verse-num">44</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>but every man’s slave purchased with money can eat it after you’ve circumcised him.
</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v45" id="v45"><sup class="verse-num">45</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>No hired hand can eat it.
</span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v46" id="v46"><sup class="verse-num">46</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Eat it in a single house. Don’t take any of the meat out of the house or break any of its bones. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v47" id="v47"><sup class="verse-num">47</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The whole community of Israel is to celebrate it. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v48" id="v48"><sup class="verse-num">48</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>If a foreigner that lives with you wants to celebrate it, all the males that belong to him need to be circumcised. Then he can come to celebrate it. He’ll become a citizen of the country, but no uncircumcised person can eat it. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v49" id="v49"><sup class="verse-num">49</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The same law applies to a native and foreigner among you.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v50" id="v50"><sup class="verse-num">50</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Israelites did what Yahveh commanded Moses and Aaron. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v51" id="v51"><sup class="verse-num">51</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That same day, he brought them out of Egypt.            </span></span></p>
