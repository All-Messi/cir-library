### Laws About Sabbaths and Festivals


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:9.0pt; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Plant and harvest your ground for six years. </span></i></span>
