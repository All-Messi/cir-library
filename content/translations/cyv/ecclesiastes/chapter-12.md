## Ecclesiastes Chapter 12

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      before the years close in and you say, </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“I don’t enjoy them </i>anymore<i>”</i>; </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         3</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>when the watchmen </span><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>[arms and hands?] </span><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>of the house </span><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>[body]</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> are palsied </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      when grinders </span><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>[teeth?]</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> stand idle because they’re few </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      </span><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>when the doors to the street</span><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> [lips?] </span><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>are shut </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            and the sound of grinding [</span><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>chewing?</span><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>] is muffled, </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            and young women sing faintly; <b>                                 </b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>      when your appetite’s gone* </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            the golden bowl breaks, </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            the pitcher by the well shatters,  </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            the pulley at the cistern doesn’t work anymore; </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         7</span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>when the dust returns to the earth where it was </span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            and the spirit returns to the God who gave it.</span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         8</span></sup></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Completely pointless,”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> the Preacher says; <i>“it’s all pointless.”</i></span></p>
