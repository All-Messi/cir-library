## Ezekiel Chapter 43

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Then he led me to the east gate, 2and the glory of Israel's God was shining through it. His voice sounded like crashing water, and the earth radiated with his glory. 3The vision I saw was like the one when he came to destroy the city, like the one by the Chebar River. I fell on my face. 4The glory of Yahveh came into the Temple through the east gate. 5The Spirit lifted me up and took me to the inner court, and the glory of Yahveh filled the Temple.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> While a man stood beside me, I heard someone talking to me from the Temple: 7"Son of man, this is the place for my throne and the soles of my feet, where I'll live among the people of Israel permanently. Israel and its kings won't defile my holy name anymore, either by their prostitution, by the dead bodies of their kings, 8or by setting their threshold by my threshold or their doorposts by my doorposts with only a wall between them and me. Show them its ordinances and laws. Write them down in their presence so they can observe them. 12This is the rule for the Temple: the whole area on top of the mountain will be most holy.
