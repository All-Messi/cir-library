<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The side rooms surrounding the Temple were wider at each successive story. <span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I saw also that the Temple had a raised platform all around. <span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The outer wall of the side rooms was 8' 7&quot; <sup>5 cubits</sup> thick. <span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and the outer rooms was 34' 6&quot; <sup>20 cubits</sup> wide all around the Temple. </span></span>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>He also measured the inner nave and the porches of the court. </span><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The nave of the Temple, the inner room, and the outer vestibule were paneled.
