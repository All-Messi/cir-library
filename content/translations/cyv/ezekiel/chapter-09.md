## Ezekiel Chapter 9


### Idolaters Killed


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He shouted, <i>“Executioners, come here with your weapons.” </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Six men holding a shattering weapon, came from the direction of the upper gate, which faces north. One of them was wearing linen with a scribe’s inkhorn on his hip. They went in and stood beside the bronze altar.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The glory of God rose up from the cherub it rested on and went to the threshold of the Temple. He called out to the man with the inkhorn,</span><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                                                                                                                                                                                                    </span></sup></b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Ezek 9:1-3</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Go all over the city and mark the foreheads of people who sigh and groan over the detestable things going on here.” </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5-6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told the others, <i>“Follow him and kill old men, young men, girls, little children, and women. Don’t pity or spare, but don’t touch anybody with the mark. Start at my sanctuary.”</i> So they started with the elders that were in front of the Temple. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told them, <i>“Defile the Temple and fill the courts with the slain. Go!”</i> So they went out and struck down the people in the city. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>While they were striking <i>them down</i> and I was by myself, I fell on my face and shouted, <i>“Lord Yahveh! Are you going to destroy the whole remnant of Israel by pouring out your anger on Jerusalem?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told me, <i>“The sinfulness of Israel and Judah is extreme. The land is filled with blood; the city is full of perversion. They’re saying, ‘Yahveh has abandoned the land; he’s not watching us!’ </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I won’t pity or spare. I’ll bring </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the consequences of<i> their actions on their own heads.”</i></span></span></p>
