<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <em>“Son</em><em> of man,</em><em> wail for Egypt’s army. <span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> 'Who are you more beautiful than? Go make your bed with the uncircumcised.'</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <em>They’ll</em><em> fall among those slain by the sword.
