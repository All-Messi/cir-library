## Ezekiel Chapter 37

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1-2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh’s hand was on me. He took me out in spirit and put me down in a valley full of dry bones, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and had me walk around among them. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:49.5pt; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Son of man, can these bones come alive?”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:49.5pt; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Lord Yahveh, you know.”</span></i><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></sup></b></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:49.5pt; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Prophesy over them, ‘Dry bones, listen to Yahveh. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll put breath in you so you can come to life. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll put sinews on you, make flesh grow back, and cover you with skin. <u>You’ll know I’m Yahveh</u>.’” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>As I prophesied, there was a rattling sound, and the bones came together, bone to bone. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I saw sinews on them, flesh grew, and skin covered them; but they weren’t breathing.                                                                                      </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ezek 37:1-8</span></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>             </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He told me to prophesy to their breath,<i> “The Lord Yahveh says to come from the four winds and breathe on these slain so they can come to life.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So I prophesied. Breath came into them, they came to life, and stood up, a big army.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:49.5pt; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then he said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Son of man, these bones are the whole House of Israel. They’re saying, ‘Our bones are dried up. Our hope is gone. We’re completely cut off.’</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So prophesy that the Lord Yahveh says, ‘I’ll open your graves and resurrect you, and take you to Israel. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You’ll know I’m Yahveh</span></u></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> when </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ve put my breath in you, you’ve come to life, and I’ve put you in your own land.’”</span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Ezek 37:9-14</p>


### Reunion of Israel and Judah


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:49.5pt; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A message from Yahveh came to me again:</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Son of man, take a stick and write on it, ‘For Judah and the people of Israel, his companions.’ Take another stick and write on it, ‘For Joseph, the stick of Ephraim and the House of Israel, his companions.’ </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then put them together in your hand to become one stick. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When your people ask you what these </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>sticks mean<i>, </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>say that the Lord Yahveh says, ‘I’ll take Joseph’s stick—which is in Ephraim’s hand—and the tribes of Israel, his companions, and put them with Judah’s stick to make one stick in my hand.’ </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The sticks you write on they’ll see in your hand. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Tell them the Lord Yahveh says he’ll gather the people of Israel from among the nations where they’ve gone and bring them to their own land. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’ll make them one nation under one king. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They won’t defile themselves with idols, detestable things, or any of their sins. He’ll deliver them from the places where they live, where they’ve sinned, and cleanse them. They’ll be his people, and he’ll be their God.”</span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Ezek 37:15-23</p>

<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ll make an everlasting peace treaty with them, bless and multiply them, set my sanctuary among them, </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>live with them, and be their God; and they’ll be my people. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <i><u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The nations will know I’m Yahveh</span></u></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> who sanctifies Israel when my sanctuary is with them forever.”’”                                                      </span></i></span></span></p>
