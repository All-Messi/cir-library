## Ezekiel Chapter 6


### A Prophecy Against the Mountains of Israel


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A message from Yahveh came to me:</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Son of man, face Israel’s mountains and prophesy, </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>‘Listen to the Lord Yahveh’s message to the mountains and hills, ravines and valleys: “I’m going to bring a sword against you and destroy your high places. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Your altars will be demolished and your incense altars smashed. You’ll fall slain in front of your idols, </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and I’ll scatter your bones around your altars. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Everywhere you live, towns will be waste, high places desolate, idols broken, incense altars cut down, and the religious objects you’ve made, destroyed. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You’ll know I’m Yahveh</span></u></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>. </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“But I’ll leave a few of you. Some will escape the sword in the nations where you scatter. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You’ll think back to me where they take you captive—how you’ve hurt me with your adulterous hearts and with eyes that carried on prostitution with idols. You’ll abhor yourselves for those detestable deeds.</span></i><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> 10</span></sup></b><i><u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You’ll know I’m Yahveh</span></u></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>, that I meant it when I said I’d inflict you with this disaster.”’</span></i></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Ezek 6:1-10</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“The Lord Yahveh says,</span></i><i><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></sup></i><i><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>‘</span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Clap your hands, stomp your feet, say, “</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>This has happened <i>because of Israel’s abominations. It will fall by sword, famine, and disease! </i></span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The one that’s far off will die by the disease. The one that’s nearby will fall by the sword. The one that’s left and besieged will starve to death.” That’s how I’ll vent my fury on you. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Your slain will lie among your idols, around your altars, on every tall hill and mountaintop, under every green tree and leafy terebinth tree—where you offered a soothing aroma to your idols. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The land will be more desolate than the desert toward Diblah. <u>That’s how you’ll know I’m Yahveh</u>.’”         </span></i></span></span></p>
