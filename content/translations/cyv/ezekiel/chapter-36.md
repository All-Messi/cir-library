## Ezekiel Chapter 36


### Prophecy to the Mountains of Israel

<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>‘So, mountains of Israel, listen to the message from the Lord Yahveh to the mountains and hills, ravines and valleys, desolate wastes and abandoned towns that have become prey and objects of derision. </span></i></span>
