## 1 Chronicles Chapter 27


### Military Divisions


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>These were heads of families, commanders of thousands and hundreds, and their officers that served the king in regard to the divisions that came in and went out month by month all year. Each division numbered 24,000.  </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>Jashobeam Ben-Zabdiel was responsible for the first division for the first month. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>He descended from Perez and was chief of the commanders of the army for the first month. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>Dodai the Ahohite and his division were responsible for the second month with Mikloth as chief officer. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>The third commander of the army for the third month was Benaiah Ben-Jehoiada the priest, as chief. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>This Benaiah was the most elite soldier of the 30 and responsible for 30. His son Ammizabad was responsible for his division.  </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%'>1 Ch 27:1-6</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>The fourth for the fourth month was Joab’s brother Asahel and his son Zebadiah.</span><b><sup><span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%'>  </span></sup></b><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>The fifth for the fifth month was commander Shamhuth the Izrahite. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>The six for the sixth month was Ira Ben-Ikkesh the Tekoite. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>The seventh for the seventh month was Helez the Pelonite, descendants of Ephraim. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>The eighth for the eighth month was Sibbecai the Hushathite from the Zerahites. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>The ninth for the ninth month was Abiezer the Anathothite from the Benjamites. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>The tenth for the tenth month was Maharai the Netophathite from the Zerahites. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>The eleventh for the eleventh month was Benaiah the Pirathonite, descendant of Ephraim. </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>The twelfth for the twelfth month was Heldai the Netophathite of Othniel.    </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt; line-height:115%'>1 Ch 27:7-15</span></b></span></p>


### Tribal Leaders


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:.25in;'><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>In charge of the tribes of Israel</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>The chief officer for the Reubenites was Eliezer Ben-Zichri;</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:.25in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>for the Simeonites, Shephatiah Ben-Maacah;</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:.25in;'><b><sup><span lang=DA style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%'>17</span></sup></b><span lang=DA style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>for Levi, Hashabiah Ben-Kemuel;</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:.25in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>for Aaron, Zadok;</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:.25in;'><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>for Judah, Elihu, David’s brother;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:.25in;'><span lang=DA style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>for Issachar, Omri Ben-Michael;</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:.25in;'><b><sup><span lang=DA style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%'>19</span></sup></b><span lang=DA style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>for Zebulun, Ishmaiah Ben-Obadiah;</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:.25in;'><span lang=DA style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>for Naphtali, Jeremoth Ben-Azriel;</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:.25in;'><span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>for Ephraim, Hoshea Ben-Azaziah;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:.25in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>for the Manasseh<i> </i>half-tribe, Joel Ben-Pedaiah;</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:.25in;'><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>for the Manasseh<i> </i>half-tribe in Gilead, Iddo Ben-Zechariah;</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:.25in;'><span lang=DA style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%'>for Benjamin, Jaasiel Ben-Abner;</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:.25in;'><b><sup><span lang=DA style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%'>22</span></sup></b><span lang=DA style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>for Dan, Azarel Ben-Jeroham.</span></p>


### Those were the officers of the tribes of Israel.
1 Ch 27:16-22


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>But David didn’t count the ones under 20 because Yahveh had said he would multiply Israel like the stars in the sky. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Joab Ben-Zeruiah had started to count
<i>them</i> but didn’t finish. Wrath came on Israel because of it, and the number wasn’t included in <i>The Chronicles of King David</i>.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Azmaveth Ben-Adiel oversaw the king’s storehouses.  </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Jonathan Ben-Uzziah oversaw the storehouses in the country, towns, villages, and  </span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>towers. </span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Ezri Ben-Chelub oversaw the farmers that plowed the ground.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Shimei the Ramathite oversaw vineyards.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Zabdi the Shiphmite oversaw what the vineyards produced <i>and what was stored</i> in </span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>cellars.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Baal-hanan the Gederite oversaw the olive and sycamore trees in the western foothills.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Joash oversaw the stores of oil.                                                                          </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%'>1 Ch 27:23-28</span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Shitrai the Sharonite oversaw the cattle that grazed in Sharon.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Shaphat Ben-Adlai oversaw the cattle in the valleys.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Obil the Ishmaelite oversaw the camels.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Jehdeiah the Meronothite oversaw the donkeys.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Jaziz the Hagrite oversaw the flocks.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Those oversaw David’s property</span></u><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>.                                                                       </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%'>1 Ch 27:29-31</span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Jonathan, David’s uncle, <i>was</i> an intelligent advisor and secretary.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Jehiel Ben-Hachmoni tutored David’s sons.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Ahithophel was advisor to the king.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-bottom: 0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Hushai the Archite was the king’s friend.</span></p>
