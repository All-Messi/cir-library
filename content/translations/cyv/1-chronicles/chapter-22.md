## 1 Chronicles Chapter 22


### David’s Preparations for the Temple


<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:0in;margin-top:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>David issued an order to gather the foreigners in Israel and set stonecutters to dress stones for God’s Temple. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>He provided large stores of iron for making nails for the doors in the gates and for clamps, <a name="_Hlk145344029">more bronze than could be weighed</a>, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>and cedar logs without number. The Sidonians and Tyrians brought him large supplies of cedar timber. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%'>He said,  </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>“My son Solomon is young and inexperienced, and Yahveh’s Temple will be magnificent, famous, and glorious all over the world. I’ll stockpile materials for it.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'> </span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in; margin-left:.25in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>David gathered lots of material <i>for it</i> before he died.                                                        </span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%'>1 Ch 22:2-5</span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:10.0pt;'><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%'>Then he called Solomon and charged him to build a temple for Yahveh God of Israel,     </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpFirst" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Son, I intended to build a temple for Yahveh, </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>but his message came to me, ‘You won’t build me a temple; you’ve shed so much blood and fought great wars. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You’ll have a son that’s not a military figure. I’ll give him rest from his enemies everywhere. His name will be Solomon because I’ll give Israel ‘peace’ and quiet during his life. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He’ll build a temple for me. He’ll be my son, and I’ll be his father. I’ll make his kingdom permanently secure.’ </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh be with you so you can succeed at building it. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>May he give you with discretion and intelligence, and give you charge over Israel so you can keep his Law. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You’ll prosper if you </span></i><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>observe carefully the statutes and ordinances he commanded Moses. Be strong and courageous; don’t be afraid or intimidated. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I’ve taken great pains to stockpile materials: <a name="_Hlk145085151">3775 tons <sup>100,000 talents </sup>of gold, 37,750 tons <sup>1,000,000 talents</sup> of silver</a>, bronze and iron past weighing, timber, and stone. You can add to that. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>You have lots of stonecutters, stone masons, and carpenters skilled in all kinds of work. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>There’s no limit to the gold, silver, bronze, and iron. Get started. Yahveh be with you.”             </span></i><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>1 Ch 22:6-16</span></b><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>
</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He directed Israel’s leaders to help Solomon, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Isn’t Yahveh with you? Hasn’t he given you rest in every direction? He’s delivered to you the nations in the land, and it’s under control. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Set your heart and soul to look to him. Build his sanctuary so you can bring the Ark and God’s holy articles into it.”                                                                                                                </span></i><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>1 Ch 22:17-19</span></b></span></p>
