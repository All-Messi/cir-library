### ½ lbs a talent of gold, and was set with precious stones. They placed it on David’s head, and he brought out a really large amount of spoil from the city. 3He brought the people out, and cut them with saws, sharp instruments, and axes. He did that to all the Ammonite towns. Then he and the people came back to Jerusalem.


<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup><strong>4</strong></sup>About this time, war broke out with the Philistines at Gezer. He had descended from the giants too. <strong><sup>7</sup></strong>When he taunted Israel, Jonathan, son of David’s brother Shimea, killed him. <strong><sup>8</sup></strong>Those <em>three </em>descended from the giants in Gath that David and his warriors killed.</span>
