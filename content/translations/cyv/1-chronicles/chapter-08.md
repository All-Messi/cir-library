## 1 Chronicles Chapter 8

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Ch 8:1-11</span>

<span class="verse" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> Bela was father of Addar, Gera, Abihud, 4Abishua, Naaman, Ahoah, 5Gera, Shephuphan, and Huram.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> These are the sons of Ehud, heads of families among the people in Geba. They were exiled to Manahath: 7Naaman, Ahijah, and Gera Avi-Uzza and Ahihud.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> Shaharaim was the father of children in Moab after he'd sent away his wives Hushim and Baara. 9By his wife Hodesh he became father of Jobab, Zibia, Mesha, Malcam, 10Jeuz, Sachia, Mirmah, sons and heads of families. 11By Hushim he'd become father of Abitub and Elpaal.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> The sons of Elpaal were Eber, Misham, Shemed—who built Ono and Lod with their villages—13Beriah and Shema (heads of families among the people in Aijalon, who routed the people in Gath); 14Ahio, Shashak, and Jeremoth.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> Zebadiah, Arad, Eder, 16Michael, Ishpah, and Joha were sons of Beriah.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> Zebadiah, Meshullam, Hizki, Heber, 18Ishmerai, Izliah, and Jobab were sons of Elpaal.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> Jakim, Zichri, Zabdi, 20Elienai, Zillethai, Eliel, 21Adaiah, Beraiah, and Shimrath were sons of Shimei.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> Ishpan, Eber, Eliel, 23Abdon, Zichri, Hanan, 24Hananiah, Elam, Anthothijah, 25Iphdeiah, and Penuel were the sons of Shashak.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> Shamsherai, Shehariah, Athaliah, 27Jaareshiah, Elijah, and Zichri were sons of Jeroham.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> These were heads of families in their generations, chief men that lived in Jerusalem. 	1 Ch 8:12-28</span>

<span class="verse" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> Jeiel Avi-Gibeon lived in Gibeon; his wife was Maacah. 30His firstborn son was Abdon, then Zur, Kish, Baal, Nadab, 31Gedor, Ahio, Zecher, and 32Mikloth Avi-Shimeah/Shimeam. These families lived near each other in Jerusalem.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> Ner was father of Kish Avi-Saul Avi-Jonathan, Malchi-shua, Abinadab, and Eshbaal.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> The son of Jonathan was Merib-baal Avi-35Micah Avi Pithon, Melech, Tarea, and Ahaz. 36Ahaz was father of Jehoaddah Avi-Alemeth, Azmaveth, and Zimri; Zimri was father of 37Moza Avi-Binea Avi-Raphah Avi-Eleasah Avi-Azel. 38Azel had six sons: Azrikam, Bocheru, Ishmael, Sheariah, Obadiah, and Hanan. 39Azel's brother Eshek was father of Ulam his firstborn, Jeush second, and Eliphelet third. 40The sons of Ulam were brave soldiers, archers, and had many sons and grandsons—150 of them. Those were all descendants of Benjamin.			1 Ch 8:29-40</span>
