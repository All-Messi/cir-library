<span class="verse" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> The sons of Ham: Cush, Mizraim, Put, Canaan</span>

<span class="verse" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> The sons of Canaan were Sidon, his firstborn; Heth, 14the Jebusites, Amorites, Girgashites, 15Hivites, Arkites, Sinites, 16Arvadites, Zemarites, Hamathites;</span>

<span class="verse" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> The sons of Shem: Elam, Asshur, Arpachshad, Lud, Aram, Uz, Hul, Gether, and Meshech</span>

<span class="verse" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> The son of Arpachshad was Shelah Avi-Eber.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> Eber had two sons: Peleg (during his life the earth was divided) and Joktan.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> Joktan was the father of Almodad, Sheleph, Hazarmaveth, Jerah,</span>

<span class="verse" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> Hadoram, Uzal, Diklah, 22Ebal, Abimael, Sheba, 23Ophir, Havilah, and Jobab. All those were the sons of Joktan.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> Shem was father of Arpachshad Avi-Shelah Avi-25Eber Avi-Peleg Avi-Reu Avi-26Serug Avi-Nahor Avi-Terah Avi-27Abram (Abraham).</span>

<span class="verse" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> The sons of Abraham: Isaac and Ishmael</span>

<span class="verse" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> These are their genealogies: Ishmael's firstborn was Nebaioth, then Kedar, Adbeel, Mibsam, 30Mishma, Dumah, Massa, Hadad, Tema, 31Jetur, Naphish, and Kedemah.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> The sons by Keturah, Abraham's concubine: Zimram, Jokshan, Medan, Midian, Ishbak, and Shuah. The sons of Jokshan: Sheba and Dedan.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> The sons of Midian: Ephah, Epher, Hanoch, Abida, and Eldaah. All those were the descendants of Keturah.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> Abraham was the father of Isaac Avi-Esau and Israel.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> The sons of Esau: Eliphaz, Reuel, Jeush, Jalam, and Korah</span>

<span class="verse" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> The sons of Eliphaz: Teman, Omar, Zephi, Gatam, Kenaz, Timna, and Amalek</span>

<span class="verse" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> The sons of Reuel: Nahath, Zerah, Shammah, and Mizzah   	 1 Ch 1:28-37</span>

<span class="verse" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> The sons of Seir: Lotan, Shobal, Zibeon, Anah, Dishon, Ezer, and Dishan</span>

<span class="verse" id="v39"><sup class="verse-num">39</sup> The sons of Lotan: Hori and Homam; Lotan's sister was Timna.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v40"><sup class="verse-num">40</sup> The sons of Shobal: Alian, Manahath, Ebal, Shephi, and Onam The sons of Zibeon: Aiah and Anah</span>

<span class="verse" id="v41"><sup class="verse-num">41</sup> The son of Anah: Dishon The sons of Dishon: Hamran, Eshban, Ithran, and Cheran</span>

<span class="verse" id="v42"><sup class="verse-num">42</sup> The sons of Ezer: Bilhan, Zaavan, and Jaakan The sons of Dishan: Uz and Aran				1 Ch 1:38-42</span>

<span class="verse" id="v43"><sup class="verse-num">43</sup> The kings that ruled in Edom before Israel had a king Bela was son of Beor; his city was Dinhabah.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v44"><sup class="verse-num">44</sup> When Bela died, Jobab Ben-Zerah of Bozrah became king.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v45"><sup class="verse-num">45</sup> When Jobab died, Husham from the land of the Temanites became king.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v46"><sup class="verse-num">46</sup> When Husham died, Hadad Ben-Bedad, who defeated Midian in the field of Moah, became king. His city was Avith.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v47"><sup class="verse-num">47</sup> When Hadad died, Samlah of Masrekah became king.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v48"><sup class="verse-num">48</sup> When Samlah died, Shaul of Rehoboth by the Euphrates River became king.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v49"><sup class="verse-num">49</sup> When Shaul died, Baal-hanan Ben-Achbor became king.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v50"><sup class="verse-num">50</sup> When Baal-hanan died, Hadad became king. His city was Pai; his wife was Mehetabel Bat-Matred Bat-Mezahab. 51Then Hadad died.
