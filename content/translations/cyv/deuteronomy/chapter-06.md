## Deuteronomy Chapter 6

<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Tie them like signs on your hand and make them like frontlets on your forehead. </span></i></span>
