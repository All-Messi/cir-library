## Deuteronomy Chapter 29

Your clothes and sandals haven't worn out. 6You didn't eat bread or drink wine or alcohol, but he provided for you so you would know he's Yahveh your God. 7When you got here, Sihon and Og came out to meet us, but we defeated them. 8We gave their land to Reuben, Gad, and the Manasseh half-tribe. 9So keep this covenant so you can prosper in everything you do. 10"You're standing today in Yahveh's presence: your chiefs, tribes, elders, officers, all the men in Israel, 11your children and wives, foreigners in your camps from the ones that chop your wood to the ones that draw your water. 12You're standing here to enter the covenant with Yahveh and into the oath he's taking with you today 13to establish you as his people and himself as your Godâ€”like he swore to Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob. 14"It's not just with you that he's making this covenant and taking this oath. 15It's with the ones that aren't standing here yet.
