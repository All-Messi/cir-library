## Daniel Chapter 7

<span class="verse" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> Previously, in the first year of Belshazzar, king of Babylon, Daniel had a dream while he was in bed. He wrote down this summary of it: 2"During the night I had a vision. Four winds were churning up the Mediterranean Sea, 3and four different beasts emerged from it.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> The first was like a lion with wings like an eagle. As I watched, its wings were pulled off, and it was made to stand on its two hind legs like a human being and receive a human mind.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> A second beast resembling a bear came up. It was rearing up on one side and had three ribs in its mouth between its teeth. They told it, 'Go ahead and gorge on many people!'</span>

<span class="verse" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> As I continued to watch, another beast came up, like a leopard with four heads and with four wings on its back; it received authority.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> Then a fourth beast came up, dreadful, terrifying, very strong with big iron teeth. It devoured, crushed, and tramped down its victims' remains. It wasn't like the other beasts and had ten horns. The Ancient of Days Reigns 8"While I was thinking about its horns, a little horn grew up among them, and three of the first horns were pulled out by the roots in its presence. This horn had eyes like a human and a mouth that bragged.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> I kept watching and thrones were set, and the Ancient of Days took his seat. His clothes were white like snow; his hair was like clean wool. He sat on a fiery throne with wheels of blazing fire.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> A molten river was flowing out from him. Thousands upon thousands attended him; millions upon millions stood in front of him. Court began to hold session, and the books were opened. The Son of Man Receives Dominion 11"I kept watching because the horn kept bragging. The beast was killed, and its body thrown into the blazing fire and destroyed. 12The other beasts had their authority revoked, but they were allowed to live for a season and a time longer. 13"I kept watching the vision. One like a son of man was coming with the clouds in the sky. He approached the Ancient of Days, led into his presence.</span>

<span class="verse" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> He received dominion, honor, and a kingdom so all peoples, nations, and language groups would serve him. His dominion was eternal; his kingdom would never be destroyed. The Vision Interpreted 15"The vision troubled me. 16I went to one of those standing nearby and asked what it meant. He told me, 17'Those four huge beasts are kings that will arise in the world. 18But the holy people of the Most High will receive the kingdom and have it for all ages to come.' 19I wanted to know what the fourth beast meant. It will devastate the whole world, tramp it down, and crush it. 24The ten horns in that kingdom are ten kings. Another, different kind of king will rise up after them and overthrow them. 25He'll defy the Most High and oppress his holy people. His kingdom will last forever, and all rulers will serve him.' 28"The revelation ended. My thoughts scared me, and my face grew pale. I kept the matter to myself."
