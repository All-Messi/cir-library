## Daniel Chapter 6


### Daniel in the Lions’ Den


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>King </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Darius thought it good to appoint 120 satraps to oversee the empire. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He put three commissioners over them. (</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Daniel was one of them.</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>) The satraps would report to them, and they would protect the king’s interests. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Daniel soon distinguished himself among the satraps and other commissioners because he had exceptional ability. The king planned to appoint him over the whole empire.
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then the commissioners and satraps tried to find some grounds for accusing him of mismanaging government affairs, but they couldn’t find any evidence of corruption. He was dependable, and they couldn’t find him negligent. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They said, <i>“We won’t find any grounds for accusing him unless it’s in connection with the Law of his god.”</i><b>           </b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Dan 6:1-5</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They went to the king together and said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:.5in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Long live King Darius! </span></i><span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The commissioners, prefects and satraps, high officials and governors have agreed that you should enforce a statute that anybody who asks for anything from any mortal or god but you for 30 days will be thrown in the lions’ den. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Now, establish the injunction and sign the document so it can’t be revoked—like the law of the Medes and Persians.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So Darius signed the injunction.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When Daniel found out he’d signed it, he went home. In his roof room with windows open toward Jerusalem, he continued to kneel three times a day, praying and giving thanks before his God. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Those men came to his house together and found him making petition to his God; </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>so they went to the king, <i>“Didn’t you sign an injunction that anybody who asks anything from any mortal or god besides you for 30 days would be thrown into the lions’ den?”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>He said, <i>“It’s true; the law of the Medes and Persians can’t be revoked.”</i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They answered, <i>“Daniel, an exile from Judah, is not honoring your injunction. He keeps praying to his God three times a day.”</i><b>        </b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Dan 6:6-13</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>When the king heard it, he was deeply distressed and tried all day to figure out some way to rescue Daniel. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then those men came back and said, <i>“You know, O King, it’s the law of the Medes and Persians that no injunction or statute the king makes can be revoked.”</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So he gave the order, and Daniel was brought in and thrown into the lions’ den. He told Daniel, <i>“The God you constantly serve will deliver you himself.”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They brought a stone and laid it over the mouth of the den. The king sealed it with his signet ring and his nobles’ signet rings so nothing could be changed. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He spent the night in his palace fasting. No entertainment was brought to him, and he couldn’t sleep.<b>         </b></span></span></p>

<p class="section-ref">Dan 6:14-18</p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He got up at dawn and hurried to the lions’ den. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Worried, he called out, <i>“Daniel, servant of the living God, has the God you constantly serve, delivered you from the lions?” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Daniel said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“Long live the King! </span></i><span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>My God sent his angel and shut their mouths. They haven’t hurt me because he considered me innocent. I haven’t committed any crime against you either.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The king was really relieved and had him brought up out of the den. They didn’t find any injury on him because he’d trusted in his God. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The king ordered the men to be brought who had maliciously accused Daniel, and threw them, their children, and wives into the den. They hadn’t hit the <i>den </i>floor before the lions pounced on them and crushed their bones.</span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         </span></b></span></p>


### Dan 6:19-24


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then Darius wrote to the peoples, nations, and language groups in the empire:</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.25in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Peace and prosperity! </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in; '><span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I decree that in all my kingdom, people tremble </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>in fear <i>before Daniel’s God.</i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.25in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  He’s the living God that endures forever;</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.25in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        his kingdom will never be destroyed;</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.25in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        his rule will never end.</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.25in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                  </span></i><span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He delivers and rescues, and performs signs and wonders</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.25in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        on earth and in the sky— </span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.25in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                        and delivered Daniel from the lions.”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v28" id="v28"><sup class="verse-num">28</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So Daniel enjoyed success during the regency of Darius and the reign of Cyrus the Persian. </span></span></p>
