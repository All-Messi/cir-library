### Ehud Judges Israel

<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>He gathered the Amorites and Amalekites, defeated Israel, and took the City of Palms. </span></span>

<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Ehud went out to the anteroom, closed the doors to the roof room, and locked them.                                                                                                              </span></span></p>
