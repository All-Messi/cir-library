## Judges Chapter 20


### Israel Fights the Benjamites


<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v1" id="v1"><sup class="verse-num">1</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The people came out from Dan to Beersheba, including Gilead, and gathered in unity to Yahveh at Mizpah. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v2" id="v2"><sup class="verse-num">2</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The chiefs from all the tribes presented themselves in the assembly of God’s people, 400,000 infantry that drew sword. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v3" id="v3"><sup class="verse-num">3</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>(</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Now the Benjamites heard that the people had gone up to Mizpah</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.) They said, <i>“How did this wickedness happen?”</i> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v4" id="v4"><sup class="verse-num">4</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Levite, husband of the murdered woman, said, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            <i>“My concubine and I came to Gibeah, in Benjamin, to spend the night. </i></span><span class="verse verse-v5" id="v5"><sup class="verse-num">5</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The men there rose against me and set on the house around me after dark. They meant to kill me. They raped my concubine, and she’s dead. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v6" id="v6"><sup class="verse-num">6</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I cut her up in pieces and sent them all over Israel because they’ve committed a detestable, disgraceful act. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v7" id="v7"><sup class="verse-num">7</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>People of Israel, all of you, give your advice and counsel here.”</span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v8" id="v8"><sup class="verse-num">8</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The people arose in agreement, </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:.5in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“None of us will go to his tent or house. </span></i><span class="verse verse-v9" id="v9"><sup class="verse-num">9</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We’ll go up against Gibeah by lot. </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v10" id="v10"><sup class="verse-num">10</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>We’ll take 10 men from 100 from all the tribes, 100 from 1,000, 1,000 from 10,000, to bring provisions for the people, so when they come, they can pay back Gibeah for that awful crime.” </span></i></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-bottom:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v11" id="v11"><sup class="verse-num">11</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The men of Israel gathered in unity against the town.
</span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jud 20:1-11</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v12" id="v12"><sup class="verse-num">12</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The tribes of Israel sent men through the whole tribe of Benjamin, and said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“What’s this wickedness that’s taken place among you? </span></i></span>
<span class="verse verse-v13" id="v13"><sup class="verse-num">13</sup> <i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Give up the base men of Gibeah, so we can put them to death, and purge sin from Israel.” </span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>But the Benjamites wouldn’t listen. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v14" id="v14"><sup class="verse-num">14</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They came together out of the towns to Gibeah to fight. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v15" id="v15"><sup class="verse-num">15</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>That day they mustered 26,000 swordsmen, besides 700 elite men in Gibeah. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v16" id="v16"><sup class="verse-num">16</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Among them were 700 elite men that were left-handed. Every one could sling a stone at a hair, and not miss. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v17" id="v17"><sup class="verse-num">17</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Israel, not including those from Benjamin, mustered 400,000 swordsmen.</span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v18" id="v18"><sup class="verse-num">18</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The people of Israel went up to Bethel and asked God, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Which of us should go up first to fight Benjamin?” </span></i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh said, <i>“Judah.”</i>
</span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v19" id="v19"><sup class="verse-num">19</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The people got up in the morning and camped against Gibeah. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v20" id="v20"><sup class="verse-num">20</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They went out to fight Benjamin and drew up the battle line against them at Gibeah. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v21" id="v21"><sup class="verse-num">21</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Benjamites came out of Gibeah and felled 22,000 Israelites that day. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v22" id="v22"><sup class="verse-num">22</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But the people took courage and formed the battle line again in the same place. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v23" id="v23"><sup class="verse-num">23</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They went up and cried to </span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh till evening, and asked him, <i>“Should we go again against our relatives the Benjamites?”</i> </span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Yes.”                                                                                                                   </span></i><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jud 20:12-23</span></b></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v24" id="v24"><sup class="verse-num">24</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So they attacked the Benjamites the second day. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v25" id="v25"><sup class="verse-num">25</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Benjamin went against them out of Gibeah the second day and felled 18,000 swordsmen. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v26" id="v26"><sup class="verse-num">26</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then the entire army of Israel went up to Bethel and cried. They sat in front of </span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh and fasted that day till evening and offered burnt offerings and peace offerings to Yahveh. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v27" id="v27"><sup class="verse-num">27</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>(</span><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Ark was there at that time, </span><b><sup><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>28</span></sup></b><span style='font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>and Phinehas Ben-Eleazar Ben-Aaron performed service in front of it</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%; font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>.) They asked </span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>him,
<i>“Should we go up again against our fellow Jews?” </i></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Yes, I’ll deliver them to you tomorrow.”</span></i></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span class="verse verse-v29" id="v29"><sup class="verse-num">29</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Israel set men in ambush around Gibeah. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v30" id="v30"><sup class="verse-num">30</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>On the third day, they went up against the Benjamites and set themselves in formation against Gibeah like before. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v31" id="v31"><sup class="verse-num">31</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Benjamites came out against them and drew away from the town. As at other times, they began to strike down and kill some people in the highways—one that goes up to Bethel, the other to Gibeah—and in the countryside, about 30 men of Israel. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v32" id="v32"><sup class="verse-num">32</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The Benjamites said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family: "Times New Roman",serif'>“They’re routed like the first time.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> But the men of Israel said, </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height: 115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“Let’s retreat and draw them away from the town onto the highways.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span>
<span class="verse verse-v33" id="v33"><sup class="verse-num">33</sup> They rose up out of their place and set themselves in formation at Baal-tamar, and the men lying in ambush rushed out of their place west of Geba. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v34" id="v34"><sup class="verse-num">34</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>10,000 elite men out of all Israel attacked Gibeah. The battle was hard, but the Benjamites didn’t know that disaster was coming. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v35" id="v35"><sup class="verse-num">35</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Yahveh defeated Benjamin in front of Israel. Israel destroyed 25,100 Benjamite swordsmen that day. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v36" id="v36"><sup class="verse-num">36</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then the Benjamites realized they were defeated. </span><b><span style='font-size: 10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jud 20:24-36a</span></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListBulletCxSpMiddle" style='margin-top:0in; margin-bottom:0in;margin-left:0in;'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The men of Israel gave ground, because they relied on the ones in ambush to attack the town. </span><span class="verse verse-v37" id="v37"><sup class="verse-num">37</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The men in ambush hurried out and struck the whole town with the sword. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v38" id="v38"><sup class="verse-num">38</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The signal by the men in ambush was that when they made a big cloud of smoke rise out of the town, </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v39" id="v39"><sup class="verse-num">39</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>the men of Israel should turn around in battle. Benjamin said, </span><i><span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>“We’ve struck them down like in the first battle.”</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v40" id="v40"><sup class="verse-num">40</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But when the signal began to rise, the Benjamites looked back. The whole town was going up in smoke. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v41" id="v41"><sup class="verse-num">41</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Then Israel turned around and Benjamin panicked when they saw that disaster was on them. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v42" id="v42"><sup class="verse-num">42</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>So they turned their backs on Israel toward open country, but the battle overtook them. Those who came out of the towns </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v43" id="v43"><sup class="verse-num">43</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>cut them down and pursued them down from Nohah all the way across from Gibeah to the east. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v44" id="v44"><sup class="verse-num">44</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>18,000 Benjamites fell. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v45" id="v45"><sup class="verse-num">45</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt; line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>They turned and fled toward open country to the Rock of Rimmon. On the roads they cut down 5,000 </span><i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>more</span></i><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'> and pursued them hard to Gidom and killed another 2,000. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v46" id="v46"><sup class="verse-num">46</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>25,000 Benjamites fell that day, trained soldiers. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v47" id="v47"><sup class="verse-num">47</sup> <span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>But 600 fled toward open country to the Rock of Rimmon and stayed there four months. </span></span>
<span class="verse verse-v48" id="v48"><sup class="verse-num">48</sup> <span style='font-size: 12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The men of Israel turned back and struck them with the sword, people and animals and everybody they found. They set on fire the towns they found.
</span><b><span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Jud 20:36b-48</span></b></span></p>
