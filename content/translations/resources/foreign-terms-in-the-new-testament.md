Abaddon (Ἀβαδδών): <em>“Destroyer” </em>(Revelation<strong> 9</strong>:11**)

Abba (Αββα): <em>“father”</em> (Mark <strong><sup>14</sup></strong>:36; Romans <strong>8:</strong>15; Galatians <strong><sup>4</sup></strong>:6**)

Akeldama (Ἀκελδαμάχ): <em>“Field of Blood”</em> (Acts<strong> 1</strong>:19**)

anathema (ἀνάθεμα): Galatians <strong><sup>1</sup></strong>:9; 1 Corinthians <strong><sup>16</sup></strong>:2

Armageddon (Ἁρμαγεδών): <em>“The Hill of Megiddo”</em> (Revelation <strong><sup>16</sup></strong>:16**)

Bar-Jesus (Βαριησούς): Acts <strong><sup>13</sup></strong>:6

(Simon) Bar-Jonah/Bar-Jonah (Βαιωνά/Βαριωνᾶς): <em>“(Simon), son-of John” </em>(Matthew

<strong><sup>16</sup></strong>:17)

Barnabas (Βαρναβᾶς): <em>“Son of Consolation”</em> (Acts<strong> 4</strong>:36 “<em>which is interpreted</em> [ὅ

ἐστιν μεθερμηνευόμενον]<em>”</em>)

Barsabbas (Βαρσαββᾶς): Joseph Barsabbas (Acts <strong><sup>1</sup></strong>:23); Judas Barsabbas (Acts <strong><sup>15</sup></strong>:22)

(Timaeus) Bar-Timaeus (Βαρτιμαῖος): <em>“Timaeus, son of Timaeus”</em> (Mark <strong><sup>10</sup></strong>:46)

Bethesda (Βηθζαθά /Βελζεθά/Βηθεσδά < בֵּית חֶסֶד): <em>“House of Mercy</em>” (?) John<strong> 5</strong>:2

Boanerges (Βοανεργές/Βοανεργής < בְּנֵי רֶגֶשׁ <em>“Sons of Thunder”</em> (Mark<strong> 3</strong>:17)

(Simon the) Cananaean (Καναναῖος): <em>“(Simon the) Zealot”</em> (Matthew <strong><sup>10</sup></strong>:4; Mark<strong> 3</strong>:18)

Cephas (Κηφᾶς < כֵּיפָא): <em>“Rock”</em> (John <strong><sup>1</sup></strong>:42, <em>“which is interpreted, ‘Peter</em> [ὃ

ἑρμηνεύεται Πετρός]<em>’”</em>)

Corban (Κορβᾶν < קָרְבָּן ): <em>“gift, offering”</em> (Mark <strong><sup>7</sup></strong>:11, “<em>Given [to God]”</em>)

Eli Eli lama sabachthani (Ηλι ηλι λεμα σαβαχθανι): <em>“My God, my God, why have you </em>

<em>	forsaken me?”</em> (Matthew <strong><sup>27</sup></strong>:46)

Eloi Eloi lema sabachthani (Ελωι ελωι λεμα σαβαχθανι): (Mark <strong><sup>15</sup></strong>:34)

Elymas (Ἐλύμας): Acts <strong><sup>13</sup></strong>:8, <em>“for thus his name is interpreted</em> [οὕτως γὰρ

μεθερμηνεύεται ὁ ὄνομα αὐτοῦ]<em>”</em>

Emmanuel (Ἐμμανουήλ < עִמָּנוּ אַל): (Matthew<strong> 1</strong>:23, <em>“which is interpreted</em> [ὅ ἐστιν

μεθερμηνευόμενον<em>”</em>]

Ephphatha (ἐφφαθα): <em>“Be opened”</em> (Mark <strong><sup>7</sup></strong>:34, <em>“that is</em> [ὅ εστιν]”)

Euroclydon (Εὐρακύλων/Εὐροκλύδων/Εὐρυκλύδων/Εὐροκοίδον/Εὐροκλύδω/

Εὐρουκλύδων): <em>“southwest wind”</em> (Acts<strong> 27</strong>:14)

Forum of Appius (Ἀππίου Φόρον): Acts <strong><sup>28</sup></strong>:15

Gabbatha (Γαββαθα = Λιθόστρωτον): <em>“The Pavement” </em>(<em>“stone pavement”</em>) John

<strong><sup>19</sup></strong>:13**

Gehenna (γέενα <הִנֹּם גּי(א/גֵּיהִנָּם): Matthew <strong><sup>23</sup></strong>:33, <em>etc.</em>

Golgotha (Γολγοθᾶ): <em>“Place of the Skull”</em> (Matthew <strong><sup>27</sup></strong>:33; Mark <strong><sup>15</sup></strong>:22, “which is

interpreted [μεθερμηνεύω”]) John <strong><sup>19</sup></strong>:27**)

Hallelujah (ἁλληλουϊά < חַלְלוּ־יָהּ): <em>“Praise Yahveh”</em> (Revelation<strong> 19</strong>:1, 3, 4, 6)

manna (μάννα): manna (John <strong><sup>6</sup></strong>:3, 49; Hebrews<strong> 9</strong>:4; Revelation <strong><sup>2</sup></strong>:17)

marana tha (μαρανα θα): <em>“Our Lord! Come!”</em> [or μαρανα αθα, <em>“Our Lord has come!”</em>],

1 Corinthians <strong><sup>16</sup></strong>:22)

Melchizedek (Μελχισέδεκ < מַלְכִּי־צֶדֶק): <em>“king of righteousness”</em> (Hebrews <strong><sup>7</sup></strong>:1-2,

<em>“which interpreted</em> [ἑρμηνευόμενος]<em>”</em>)

Messiah/Messias (Μεσσίας < מָשִׁיחַ, “to anoint”): <em>“the Messiah”</em>; <em>“The Anointed One”</em> =

<em>Christos”</em> < χρίω, “to anoint” (John <strong><sup>1</sup></strong>:41, <em>“which is interpreted”</em> [ὅ ἐστιν

μεθερμηνευόμενον Χριστός])

rabbi (ῥαββί/ῥαββεί < רַבִּי): teacher (John<strong> 1</strong>:38, <em>“which is interpreted, ‘teacher’”</em> [ὃ

λεγεται μεθερμηνευόμενον Διδάσκαλε])

rabboni (ῥαββουνι/ῥα/ββουωεί/ῥββονί/ῥαββονεί < רַבּןֹנִי): <em>teacher </em>(John <strong><sup>20</sup></strong>:16, <em>“which </em>

<em>	means, ‘teacher’”</em> [ὃ λεγεται διδάσκαλε])

Raka (ῥακά/ῥαχά < רֵיקָא): <em>“Fool” </em>(Matthew<strong> 5</strong>:22)

Sabaoth (Σαβαώθ < צְבָאוּת): <em>“[Lord of] hosts”</em> (Romans<strong> 9</strong>:29 ASV)

Salem (Σαλήμ): <em>“peace”</em> (Hebrews <strong><sup>7</sup></strong>:2, <em>“that is, King of Peace</em> [ὅ ἐστιν βασιλεὺς

εἰρήνης]<em>”</em>)

Siloam (Σιλωάμ < שִׁלַֹח): <em>“Sent”</em> (John <strong><sup>9</sup></strong>:7<em>, “which is interpreted</em> [ὃ ἑρμηνεύεται

Ἀπεσταλμένος]<em>”</em>)

Tabitha (Ταβιθά < טַבְיְתָא): <em>Dorcas</em> (Acts<strong> 9</strong>:36, 40; <em>“which is interpreted</em> [ἣ

διερμηνευομένη λέγεται] <em>Dorcas”</em>)

Talitha cumi (Ταλιθα κουμ < טַלְיְתָא): (Mark <strong><sup>5</sup></strong>:41, <em>“which is interpreted, ‘Little girl,</em>

<em>get up</em> [ὅ ἐστιν μεθερμηνεύω Τὸ κοράσιον, ἔγειρε]<em>’”</em>)

Three Taverns (Τριῶν Ταβερνῶν): Acts<strong> 28</strong>:15
