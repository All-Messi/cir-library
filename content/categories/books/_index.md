Dr. Warren's book-length studies provide in-depth exploration of theological topics, interpretation methods, and Christian doctrine. Each book is a collection of related essays that build upon one another.

## Available Books

### Interpretation
- **Getting the Point** - A comprehensive guide to biblical hermeneutics and interpretation principles

### Theology
- **Basic Christianity** - Foundational studies in Christian doctrine with an interpersonal emphasis
- **The Nature of Man** - An exploration of biblical anthropology

### Commentaries  
- **Sermon on the Mount Commentary** - Detailed exposition of Matthew 5-7

### Evidences
- **Apologetics** - Defending the Christian faith through evidence and reason
