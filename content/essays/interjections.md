INTERJECTIONS

ἄγε, James 4:13

ἴδε, John 1:29

δεῦρο, Mark 10:21

ἰδού, Matthew 11:10 absolutely; Revelation 4:1 (with article)

ἔα, Luke 4:34

οὐά, Mark 15:29 (with vocative)

οὐαί, Luke 6:25 (with vocative);

used absolutely οὐαί οὐαί (Ρεωελατιον 18:10, 16, 19

used with the dative as in Matthew 11:21; Luke 6:24-25

ὦ, with the vocative in Matthew 15:28

ὡς exclamatory particle in Romans 10:15

τί exclamatory particle in Lk. 12:49
