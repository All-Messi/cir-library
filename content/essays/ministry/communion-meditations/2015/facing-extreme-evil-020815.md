<p>We serve God even in the face of extreme evil. The Christian faith provides a unique perspective on why a loving God lets the righteous suffer. Whatever the reason, it was important enough for him in the person of the Son to expose himself to its full force. By implication, he calls on us to persevere against evil threats.</p>
<p>This sobering observance identifies us with him in his final hour; so we do not participate superficially, because the emblems implicate us in our <em>“last measure of devotion.”</em> We cast our lot with those who even today are saying by their ultimate obedience, <em>“</em><em>N</em><em>evertheless, your will be done.”</em></p>
