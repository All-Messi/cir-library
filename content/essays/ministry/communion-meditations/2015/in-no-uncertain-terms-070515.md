<p>Have you ever wondered whether somebody loved you?<em> “Loves me, loves me not.” </em>You weren’t sure. You were looking for times when the person paid attention to you, took time for you, helped you with something. We are hardwired with a desire to be loved; we want other people to care about us.</p>
<p>This loaf and cup tell us in no uncertain terms that God cares, that Christ cares; otherwise, he would never have laid down his life for us. That is the best thing one person can do for another (John<strong> 15</strong>:13), and that, in fact, is how he chose to show us that, yes, he cares; we can be sure he cares (Romans <strong>5</strong>:5-11).</p>
<p>Hymn: <em>“Does Jesus Care”</em> by Frank E. Graeff</p>
