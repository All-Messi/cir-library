<p><em>“For the joy that was set before him, Jesus endured the cross”</em> (Hebrews <strong>12</strong>:2). Among the host of things scripture teaches us is how to deal with adversity. One mechanism for coping is remembering that <em>“this too shall pass,”</em> and even more that future good awaits us when we endure hard things meantime.</p>
<p>We carry in our hands emblems of his hands, representations of his life poured out for us as an example to us. Taking these elements into ourselves visually portrays a valuable lesson learned and makes tribute to the Teacher who demonstrated the truth we profit from.</p>
