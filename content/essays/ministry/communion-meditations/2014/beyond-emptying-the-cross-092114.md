<p>Incredibly, while Jesus was suffering on the cross, the religious leaders unleashed the taunt, <em>“He trusts in God; let him deliver him now if he wants him”</em> (Matthew <strong>27</strong>:43). God delivered him from the tomb beyond the cross rather than from the cross.</p>
<p>Should we face such an experience, we resolve here to persevere in the hope of a better resurrection (Hebrews <strong>11</strong>:35) on the other side of what people might do to us (Luke <strong>12</strong>:4). In this hope, Christ has forged the way. We partake, we remember, we go and live likewise.</p>
