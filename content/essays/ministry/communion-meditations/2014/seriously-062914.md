<p>What we are about to do here is serious. It is a memorial act remembering a person, to be sure, but more specifically remembering a specific thing the person did. His life culminated in crucifixion. That is why we have the kind of elements we’re using.</p>
<p>Even more, we are entering into that experience as something we do. Previously Jesus had said, <em>“Take up your cross and follow me.”</em> We are putting the weight of a cross on our shoulders and following him uphill. That is something serious for us to be doing.</p>
