<p>The Lord’s Supper is like a peg on a wall. It is useless aside from what we hang on it; yet it is crucial for holding up what needs to hang there.</p>
<p>Likewise, these emblems have no significance in themselves—only in what they’re for. They mean what we mean into them. Instead of eating a piece of flatbread and taking a sip of grape juice, we hang on them a picture of our Lord at the conclusion of his life.</p>
