<p class=MsoNormal align=center style='text-align:center'><b><span
style='font-size:14.0pt'>SURPRISED AT SUNRISE</span></b></p>

<p class=MsoNormal align=center style='text-align:center'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal align=center style='text-align:center'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal align=center style='text-align:center'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            Surprised at sunrise
were two women,</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>                                                    
two women making their way to the tomb of a friend,</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>                                                                                                the
tomb of a revered teacher,</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>                                                    
two women who came to perform an unwelcome task</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>                                                                                                that
somebody had to do,</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>                                                    
two women who had watched in helpless horror the                                                            dying
agony of a man condemned by jealousy. </p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            Since the mock trials
and the gruesome visages that followed, they had had uncounted hours for the
sights of that Friday to become fixed in their minds.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            Theirs was the sad
surprise that the long-awaited One unbelievably had been killed—which meant
they had hoped in him in vain.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            Numb from fitful
sleep and crushed anticipations, these two women made their lonely pilgrimage
in the ebbing darkness for more than a mile, wondering how they could even do
what they had to do.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            Puzzled by the stone
rolled away from that sealed sepulcher,</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            puzzled by an empty
burial niche—of all things a stolen body of a dead man!</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            Then there came an
unexpected long shadow cast by the early-morning sun, </p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>                                                      
a shadow that turned one of the women around,</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>                                                      
the shadow of an unknown gardener who said, “Mary.”</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'> </p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>Surprised by an angel and his
announcement: <i>“He is not here; he has resurrected.”</i></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>Surprised by a joy proportionate
to their sorrow,</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>Surprised by the dawning, not of
a new day, but of a new age, a new history, </p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>Surprised, not by renewed hope,
but by a new kind of hope entirely.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>Surprised are we all especially
in the stark darkness of our disappointments, our despondencies, our
depressions—all dispelled by the light of the One who conquered our greatest
fears and ascended above them on high, who has not left us orphans in the world,
but draws us ever onward, ever upward after himself in proportion to our
surprise at what he has prepared for those who diligently follow him.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>Today . . . we . . . here . . .
relive the sunrise to keep the surprise alive.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>
