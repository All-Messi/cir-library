PHILIPPIANS: THE TIME OF WRITING

WITHIN THE ROMAN IMPRISONMENT

1:12-14: Enough time for Paul to influence the praetorian guard and others because of

their exposure to him while he was in prison

1:22-26; 2:24: Near the end of the imprisonment because Paul anticipates release from the

present circumstance
