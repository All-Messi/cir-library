style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  Revelation gives three answers to
the atheistic antilogism on God’s omnipotence, God’s holiness, and the fact of
evil. The Christian God does not exist, so the argument proceeds, because one
of the three elements above must be denied to have a consistent system. But
Revelation reminds Christians that God was not the origin of evil. Satan by his
free will was the ultimate source of it. God is responsible, however, and takes
care of it in the following way: Christ provides the conquest of evil, and in
the end evil will be overcome.  Until then God <u>righteously</u> gives
opportunity for <u>evil</u> people to take advantage of the salvation provided
by his <u>omnipotence</u> through the slain and resurrected Lamb.</span></p>
