<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:.5in'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>The
situation is like a broad arrow. Neither freedom nor determinism is absolute.
Freedom is within a framework determined by several factors. Nature is the
outermost limit, revelation is inside of that, and then there are other factors
like circumstance and providence. Our individual freedom will never allow us to
move outside the limit that nature imposes, but to a certain extent we may move
outside the margin that revelation or circumstance presents. </span></p>
