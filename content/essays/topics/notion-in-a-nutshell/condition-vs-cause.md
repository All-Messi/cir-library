<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:.5in'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>A
condition is an action required of one person by another. The other person act
does not produce the result; it meets the condition for the first person to
give the result. Jesus’ reference to the brazen serpent in the Old Testament
illustrates idea (John <b>3</b>:14-15). The Israelites were to look on the
brazen serpent to be cured from snakebite. Looking at the brazen serpent did
not cause the cure. God gave the cure <u>if</u> a person did the looking.</span></p>
