<p class=MsoNormal align=center style='text-align:center'><b>&nbsp;</b></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'><b>            </b>Baptism is for
personal identification with Jesus Christ. In consequence of identity with
Christ, God forgives sin, gives the Holy Spirit, and make us member in the body
of Christ.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            “baptism <i><u>eis</u></i><u>/<i>en</i>/<i>epi</i></u>
Christ”</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            “being <u>in</u>
Christ”</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            “putting <u>on</u>
Christ</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            </p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>Values of the identification
approach to baptism</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-left:.5in;text-align:justify'>Helps makes
sense of certain things that happen in scripture</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-left:.5in;text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-left:.5in;text-align:justify'>Gives a
framework for dealing with issues not addressed in scripture by direct
commandment, apostolic president, or necessary inference</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-left:.75in;text-align:justify;text-indent:
-.25in'>1.<span style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Makes
sense of Jesus’ being baptized by John</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-left:.75in;text-align:justify;text-indent:
-.25in'>2.<span style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Makes
sense of John’s disciples being baptized into Christ        </p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-left:.75in;text-align:justify;text-indent:
-.25in'>3.<span style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Avoids
the appearance of salvation by works</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-left:.75in;text-align:justify;text-indent:
-.25in'>4.<span style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>An
unimmersed believer does not have to view himself as lost before accepting
being baptized. In baptism a person is identifying with Christ, and God gives
whatever results appropriate to the situation.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-left:.75in;text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-left:1.0in;text-align:justify'>Saved Jews
being baptized by John</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-left:1.0in;text-align:justify'>Disciples of
John being baptized into Christ  </p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-left:1.0in;text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-left:1.0in;text-align:justify'>A minister does
not need to think or say to the paedobaptist that he is or is not in a saved
condition with God. The paedobaptist does not need to decide whether he is or
is not in a saved condition.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-left:1.0in;text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-left:1.0in;text-align:justify'>Just because a
minster now says to someone that he needs to be baptized as an “adult” does not
mean that person is now responsible for the truth of the matter. That person
does not have any reason ahead of the case to believe that the truth is what
the new minister is telling him.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-left:1.0in;text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-left:.75in;text-align:justify;text-indent:
-.25in'>5.<span style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Shows
why Jesus could be baptized by John </p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-left:.75in;text-align:justify;text-indent:
-.25in'>6.<span style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Answers
the problem of the age of accountability</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-left:.75in;text-align:justify;text-indent:
-.25in'>7.<span style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Provides
a framework that makes it sensible to say that we may not be the only
Christians, but we are Christians only </p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-left:.75in;text-align:justify;text-indent:
-.25in'>8.<span style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Provides
a framework for thinking about the unevangelized before, at, or after the time
of Christ.</p>
