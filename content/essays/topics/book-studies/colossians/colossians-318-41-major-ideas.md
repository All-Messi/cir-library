<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            These relationships
are specialized IP processes that have additional features.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            </p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            </p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            Additional features

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>Theory of Christian leadership</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            </p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            “Headship”/“deference”
refers to relative responsibility. </p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            “Authority” comes
into the picture only as a reinforcement for responsibility.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>Four types of systems driven
respectively by four different things</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            Interpersonal driven
by influence</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            Legal driven by
authority</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            Natural driven by
force</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            Metaphysical driven
by form</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>Three means of affecting the
actions of others.  In descending order of idealness, they are:</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>&nbsp;</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            Influence</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            Authority</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify'>            Force</p>
