<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-right:-.5in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>Christian baptism is an interpersonal act in which a repentant
believer identifies with Jesus Christ. On the basis of identity with Christ,
God promises to forgive our sins, grant us the presence of his Spirit, and add us
to Christ's body, the church. </span></p>
