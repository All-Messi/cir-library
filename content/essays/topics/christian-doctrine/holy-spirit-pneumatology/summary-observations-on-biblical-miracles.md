<p class=MsoNormal align=center style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:center'><b><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>&nbsp;</span></b></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><b><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>     </span></b><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>I.  Types
of Occurrences: divine miracle, demonic miracle, answered prayer,
psychological, </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                        
       magic</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>   II.  Characteristics
of Biblical Miracle</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         A.  Many:
<i>vs.</i> occasional</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         B.  Immediate:
<i>vs</i>. taking place over a period of hours, days, or months as perhaps in </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                        
natural healing</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         C.  Always
successful: <i>vs.</i> occasional success as perhaps something merely unusual </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         D.  Varied:
<i>vs</i>. just certain kinds of things that might be more easily faked or
misread</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>               1.
 healing all kinds of diseases and deformities: Acts <b>5</b>:16</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>               2. 
nature miracles: stilling the tempest (Matthew <b>8</b>:23-27)</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>               3. 
exorcisms of demons</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>               4. 
foreknowledge (across time): Luke <b>22</b>:7-13 </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>               5. 
from a distance (across space): Matthew<b> 8</b>:5-13</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>               6. 
special miracles: by way of objects carried from the person (Acts <b>19</b>:11)</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>               7. 
not dependent on the faith of the one affected: Malchus (John <b>18</b>:10-11)</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         E.   Permanent:
<i>vs</i>. something that “wore off” as perhaps with psychological effects</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         F.   Beneficial:
not harmful or detrimental to the persons involved</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                 
Note, however, cases where miracles were used to curb evil and opposition.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         G.  Positive:
not sheer demonstration of supposed supernatural power (levitation, bending </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    objects,
swallowing razor blades, eating fire, <i>etc</i>.)</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                              
sometimes without contact </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         H. 
Secondary: <i>vs</i>. the center of the miracle worker’s activity</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    secondary
to proclamation of a message</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>           I.  Purposeful:
<i>vs</i>. entertainment </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    to
prove the claim of the miracle worker; </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    to
increase faith in the one benefitted</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>          J.  Extreme:
obviously not by natural cause</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                               
blind from birth, lame from birth, resurrection, leprosy</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:45.0pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:-45.0pt'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>         K.  Simple:
merely with a word, not an elaborate process that involved agonizing to create </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:45.0pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:-45.0pt'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    the
effect (as in elaborate exorcisms; Matthew<b> 8</b>:16; Lk. <b>7</b>:7)</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:45.0pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:-45.0pt'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>          L.  Altruistic:
free <i>vs</i>. done for the benefit of the miracle worker—for money, fame,<i>
etc</i>. </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:45.0pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:-45.0pt'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    Perhaps
not even done on Christians: Trophimus (2 Timothy <b>4</b>:20) and</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:45.0pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:-45.0pt'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>                                    Epaphroditus
(Philippians. <b>2</b>:25-27) (?) </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:45.0pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:-45.0pt'><span

<i>vs</i>. known by rumor</span></p>
