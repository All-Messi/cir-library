<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>1. 
The Spirit is a <u>person</u>, not a force, idea, emotional surge.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>2. 
The Spirit is <u>deity</u>: Psalm<b> 138</b>:7-10; Matthew <b>28</b>:19; Acts <b>5</b>:3
(+ 4/9?); 1 Corinthians<b> 2</b>:10-</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify;text-indent:
.5in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>11;
<b>12</b>:4-6; <b>13</b>:14; 2 Corinthians <b>1</b>:21-22; Ephesians <b>4</b>:4-6;
Hebrews <b>9</b>:14.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>3. 
The Spirit “proceeds” from the Father and the Son: John <b>14</b>:16.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>4. 
There is a difference between the Spirit (personal) and manifestations of the
Spirit:</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            miraculous
manifestation via direct descent, laying on of hands, and answered prayer;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>            characteristic
positive interpersonal behavior: John <b>13</b>:35; 1 Corinthians <b>13</b>.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>5. 
The Spirit is not perceivable in himself without being manifested: Judges <b>16</b>:20.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>6. 
We know we have the Spirit on the basis of promise, not perception; not feeling
(see above), </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify;text-indent:
.5in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>miraculous
gifts, frenzy, altered states of consciousness, ecstasy. </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify;text-indent:
.5in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>7. 
What the Spirit gives a person will not contradict what Christ has taught: John
<b>16</b>:15; </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify;text-indent:
.5in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Deuteronomy
<b>13</b>:1-5.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify;text-indent:
.5in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;
line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>8.  Rule of Usage: If we
cannot tell the difference between our experience from other people’s </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:.5in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:
"Times New Roman",serif'>experiences, we do not claim our <u>idea</u> or plan
of <u>action</u> is from the Spirit; and we do not bank on it ourselves. To the
extent that God’s will is specific, his leading will be explicit.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:.5in;text-align:justify'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:
115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>9. 
Miraculous manifestation is not as important as interpersonal <u>behavior</u>: 1
Corinthians<b> 12</b>:30.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:.5in;text-align:justify'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:
115%;font-family:"Times New Roman",serif'>Miracle is in the service of <u>prophecy</u>:
1 Corinthians <b>14</b>:5.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;text-align:justify'><span
