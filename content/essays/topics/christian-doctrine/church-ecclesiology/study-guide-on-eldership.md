<strong>Observations</strong>


General observations on the work sheet above

1.	There is no mention of elders of household churches.

2.	Elders are always at the city level. That leaves unaddressed how the elders of a city related to multiple household churches in cities like Rome, where ther were at least four house churches at the time Paul addressed his letter to the saints there.

3.	Every example of an elder is local, and every local church whose organization is mentioned had elders.

4.	Elders existed in Jewish and Gentile territories.

5.	Elders existed in connection with the work or Peter (1 Peter <strong><sup>5</sup></strong>:1) and the other original apostles as well as Paul.
