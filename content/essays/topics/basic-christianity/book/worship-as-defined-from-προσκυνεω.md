<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-.5in;margin-bottom:0in;

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-.5in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:1.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:-1.0in'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  1.   Worship maintains the personal
identity of the worshiper. The idea is not to lose yourself. SELF-IDENTITY <i>vs</i>.
SELF-NEGATION</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-.5in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-.5in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:1.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:-1.0in'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  2.   Worship calls for centering
attention on God. The idea is not to draw attention to the leader as a
performer.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-.5in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-.5in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-.5in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-.5in;margin-bottom:0in;

not act silly before their maker.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-.5in;margin-bottom:0in;

hence, it is not wrapped up in form, ritual, </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-.5in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        externals, over-repetition.   
 </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-.5in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        SELF-EXPRESSION <i>vs</i>. SELF-CONSCIOUSNESS</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-.5in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-.5in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>            The approach above contrasts with using
Romans <b>12</b>:1, for example, as the characterizing statement about worship:
<i>“So I urge you brothers, by God’s mercies to present your bodies living and
holy sacrifices, acceptable to God, which is your spiritual service of <u>worship</u>.</i>”
</span><span lang=EL style='font-size:12.0pt'>Λατρεύω</span><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>/</span><span lang=EL style='font-size:12.0pt'>λατρεια</span><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>, the root Paul uses here, has a broader meaning: <i>service</i>.
That includes <i>“rites and duties”</i> as in Hebrews<b> 9</b>:6: <i>“Now when
these things have been thus prepared, the priests are continually entering the
outer tabernacle, performing the divine <u>worship</u>”</i> (NASB). </span><span
lang=EL style='font-size:12.0pt'>Λατρεύω</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt'>/</span><span
lang=EL style='font-size:12.0pt'>λατρεία</span><span lang=EL style='font-size:
12.0pt'> </span><span style='font-size:12.0pt'>ends up meaning what is done <u>in
connection with</u> worshiping God not just what is done <u>to</u> God.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-.5in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>            Such a broad definition makes everything
that happens during a gathering of worshipers an aspect of worship. Identifying
a common thread through such a variety of activities makes worship difficult to
define. Fellowship, exhortation, singing the content of the Christian faith,
preaching the message fall within the term just as surely as prayer, giving, and
the Lord’s Supper. The result is to create an attitude toward God expressed in
action. It ends up getting spread to life as a whole. Note Barth’s statement
that <i>“life is prayer.”</i></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-.5in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>            Keeping the emphasis on the vertical and
on its second-person character makes the experience identifiable, more focused,
more specific. It also drives home the fact that we do not do as much of it as
we should.</span></p>
