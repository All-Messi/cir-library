<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        a.   Verbal skills (preach,
teach, counsel, writing)</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                              “Be articulate” (1
Timothy <b>3</b>:2), which involves fluency and coherence. </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                              Be able to make a <u>through</u>
presentation (not just be a discussion leader who plays off of the comments of
others).</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                              Be able to reason
through matters with people, not just state conclusions.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        b.   Social skills</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                              Develop an
outward-looking personality.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                              Strive for a healthy
kind of extrovert personality through simple things like making eye contact,
speaking up/projecting, demonstrating holy boldness, smiling.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                              Be able to get up in
front of people, not just to engage in one-on-one </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                                       presentation.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                              Be able to counsel with
people one-on-one, which calls for approach-</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                                       ableness.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-right:-27.0pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  3.   Behavior traits (DO)</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        Get away from the
entertainment mentality. Work is goal oriented; play is not. Be willing to work
on your personality to modify it rather than treat it as a given, set, or
unalterable thing. Get away from too low a level of expectancy.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        a.   Initiative:                    A
leader has to be a self-starter.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        b.   Positive attitude:         focus
on the <u>good</u> (<i>vs.</i> weaknesses).</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                                                                  focus
on <u>possibilities</u> (<i>vs.</i> hindrances).</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        c.   Enthusiasm,                A
slow-moving, unemotional person is not able to </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                              aggressiveness                    keep
other people’s attention.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        d.   Tenacity (Philippians<b>
1</b>:12-14)</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                              (1)    Stability</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-right:-27.0pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                                    (2)    Carry through;
be a finisher.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                              (3)    Not quitting or
giving in too easily when opposed or tested: “refute</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                                       critics” (Titus
<b>1</b>:9)</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  Start helping with the area of
service; be around its people.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>            C.  Be involved.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  1.   Leadership for the church comes
from <u>within</u> the active membership.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        Israel was to choose a king
from among its own people.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        Leaders are not hired in from
the outside as they often are in the business </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                              world.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  2.   Willingness to follow precedes
opportunity to lead.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>      </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        The values of this approach
include the following observations:</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        a.   Willingness to support
implies that we will not abuse leadership when it is given because we are not
self-centered, or do not have an overly high opinion of ourselves. It
communicates concern for the purpose of the group first and foremost,
regardless of our place in it.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        b.   Willingness to support
implies that we will not consider leadership as just a job if it is given to us.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        c.   Willingness to support
implies that you have a cooperative spirit and that you will not just run off
heedlessly doing whatever you want if you become leader.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        d.   Willingness to support
implies a basic willingness to work even if there is no prestige in it for you.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                              Do not wait until a
situation is as it should be before we “lower ourselves” to associate with it. In
the meantime, we will probably be criticizing and complaining about the present
leadership.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                              Do not implicitly demand
leadership as a condition for being involved.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  3.   Much learning must come by
doing.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        Learn by doing so we can lead
those who are doing.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        Leaders are leading in an
activity, the <u>art</u> of living.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                                                                       
</span><span style='font-size:6.0pt'>            </span><span style='font-size:
12.0pt'>the <u>art</u> of doing church work.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        We must win the confidence of
doers by being able to do the job well </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                              ourselves.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        Much of what needs to be known
must be learned in the situation itself: </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                              internships, associate
worker, two-by-two activity.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>            D.  Be available.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  Be at the right place at the right
time: the person makes the situation, but the situation also makes the person.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  Know the right people and keep
yourself in contact with them.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  Do not have your life so filled you
cannot take the opportunities if they come. </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  Have your life flexible enough that you
can accept opportunities of being called into leadership roles. Make your life
as uncluttered as possible.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  Be willing to let go of lesser
things when bigger ones come along.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>II. LEADERSHIP GIVEN</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>            E.   Be faithful.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  <i>“Be faithful in little to become
responsible for much.”</i></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                         The Parable of the Pounds
(Luke <b>19</b>:11-27)</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                         The Parable of the Talents
(Matthew<b> 25</b>:11-30)</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>               </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        <img width=263 height=116
src="/cir-library/images/essays/obtaining-christian-leadership/image002.png"> </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        The right to lead comes to us from
beyond us. There can be various degrees of divine involvement even to the point
of supernatural involvement. </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        Even Christ did not make
himself a priest but was called like Aaron.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        a.   Not all who are qualified
for a task are necessarily needed for <u>that</u> task. </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        b.   Other people may be
better qualified to evaluate us.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                                       They are not
biased by our personal aspirations.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                                       They can see
the total impression we make on them.  </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                                                The
hardest thing in the world to see is ourselves.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>            2.   Lower to higher (Be faithful here to
be called higher.)</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span

answers some problems:</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        a.   How to <u>get</u> the experience
required for a job that requires experience. </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                              Minimize the lack of
experience by doing related, lesser jobs. In the process, you will probably be
around the higher job to see how it is done.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        b.   How to <u>give</u>
leadership without risking the destruction of the body.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        c.   How to <u>give</u> people
a significant job when we do not know whether </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                              If there is failure at a
lower level, the negative effects are not so great.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>      Becoming and choosing leaders best occurs by
adopting procedures like these:</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:1.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:
-1.0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  1.   Give a lower,
introductory job so that if there is a failure, the effects will not be wide-ranging.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:1.5in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:
-1.0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt'>            2.   Choose from those who
are involved, because they show an interest in the task for its own sake,
uncluttered by self-ambition.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:1.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:
-1.0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        Those around the
tasks have also seen someone <u>else</u> do it.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:1.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:
-1.0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  3.   Show yourself
willing to follow as evidence that when you lead it will not</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:1.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:
-1.0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt'>                               be
self-seeking.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:1.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:
-1.0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  4.   Make sure that
the accompanying factors in your life are well in order:</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:1.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:
-1.0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt'>                              personal
life, children, spouse.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:1.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:
-1.0in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt'>      </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
