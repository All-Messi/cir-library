style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  Saying #1: <i>“The meaning of an

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        Life is in the living, rather
than out ahead that we never to reach.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  The concept can be represented by a
rolling wheel, which pictures the ever-</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        expanding horizon that opens
up before us toward future goals.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  Saying #2: <i>“The future of an

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        The present does not equal a
discrete entity; it is not disjoined from all else.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        No act happens by itself.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        As memories of the past impact
the present, so anticipations of the future should impact the present.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        The law of the harvest teaches
that how we act now issues in future experiences that come back on us.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        No one lives in complete
disregard for the future, but we may not regard it enough. It is greatly
determined by what we mix into it through present action.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  Saying #3: <i>“The meaning of an
event <u>becomes</u> in the future of that event.”</i></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        Meaning “becomes” as it echoes
along in time. The decision we make now limits the decisions we have left to
choose from in the upcoming future. We speak of direction-setting events
because they impact significantly the subsequent direction of our life. Getting
married leads to many other things that would not have been the case without
that decision; getting married also removes some options that would have been
possible otherwise. Since one decision leads to another in this ongoing way,
the full significance of a past decision becomes clearer as the domino effect
of that decision unfolds itself over time.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        To be complete and fulfilled
persons, we must live in light of the “fulfillment” of what we do as well as
enjoy the experiences along the way. In more than one sense, actions have
consequences.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>            A.  The present grows toward the future.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  1.   Not just eventuation but

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        Life does not just eventuate; it
goes somewhere intentional because God has set natural, providential, and
mandatory limitations in its range of possibilities.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        There is more to it all than
one experience leading to the next.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  2.   Not just what it produces from
inside of history (<i>futurum</i>),</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        but what God brings into it
from the outside (<i>adventus</i>)</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        The Bible is a history of God’s
mighty acts in-breaking into the world.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        Not the communistic view, but
the Christian view of history.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        Not the communist charge that
religion is an excuse for present irresponsibility, but the Christian
recognition that there is more to the future than what <u>we</u> put into it.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        Not evolution, but creation. Creative
acts of God add to the potentialities of the present. Consequently, things are
not limited to what has already been; there is the possibility of the truly
new.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>            B.  The future shapes the present.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  The future affects the present.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  The present causes the future by
natural causation; the future shapes the present by mental anticipation;
projecting the natural future back into the present causes us to act
differently than we would otherwise.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  Mental penetration of the future
adjusts perceptions and behavior in the present.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>  </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                      </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                                      <img width=177
height=82 src="/cir-library/images/essays/projecting-into-the-future/image004.png"></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:8.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  Mind makes the connection both ways:
it interprets present experience so that action results and shapes tomorrow; it
projects into the future by planning and revealed prophecy to qualify present
actions.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>Since life does not just eventuate, we should live by
planning. We can get so caught up in living life that we do not plan the
future.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
