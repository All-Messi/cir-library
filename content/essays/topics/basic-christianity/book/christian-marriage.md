28-31, 33</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-right:-27.0pt;text-align:justify'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

style='font-size:12.0pt'>            B.  What does <u>responsibility</u> mean?</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  Having responsibility is the only
reason for having authority. In all cases where responsibility is involved,
those who do not have final responsibility have a right to say, “I do not have
to have my <u>way</u>, but I do want to have my <u>say</u>.” A respectful leader
does not make choices without consulting those affected by the choices. In
leadership of any kind, there can be some parceling up of areas of
responsibility by mutual consent. A wife and husband can exercise complete
control over certain parts of the whole operation of the home. Such an
arrangement does not infringe on the husband’s final responsibility for that
operation of the home.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  The husband’s responsibility . . .</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  1.   <u>Reinforces the many elements

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        Not only does a husband want
to fulfill his wife’s needs; God holds him responsible for doing so.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  2.   <u>Means someone is above</u><i>
</i>to be responsible to.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

style='font-size:12.0pt'>                  3.   <u>Is for good order in getting

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

style='font-size:12.0pt'>                                    not for being

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        Husband and wife are unequal in

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        The complexity of <u>married
life</u> is greater than the complexity level of <u>single life</u>, but the
setup of the home is calculated to hold for the highest complexity level—<u>family
life</u>: husband, wife, multiple children, extended family.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        A problem sometimes posed: <i>“There
is no reason to put one person over another unless he is better.”</i></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><i><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></i></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        a.   Good order is a reason
beyond equal ability. Equal ability does not require equal station. <i>“Everybody’s
business is nobody’s business.”</i> Besides, in unusual cases, when there is
disagreement on something important, someone has to finalize the course of
action.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        b.   Being better at being
head does not equal being better period.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        As with other kinds of social

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        Authority must be kept in low
profile—as at best a back-up mechanism.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                              The psychology of
authority creates a sense of <u>distance</u>.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                              The psychology of
interpersonal relationship creates a sense of <u>nearness</u>.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        There is the same relationship
between husband and wife as between individuals in any organized setting: business,
church, school, home.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        Interpersonal relationship
needs to overshadow all other considerations if for no other reason than to
make the structure work right.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>            C.  Why is the husband specified as

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        So there will not be a contest
to see who will fulfill that role. It helps avoid </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

established in part to play to the typical strengths of both men and women—to
optimize relative gifting.</span></p>

</i>childbearing ability and attendant abilities that go </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        b.   Physically stronger tends
toward a psychology about life.  </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                                    It is emotionally draining
enough to raise children, without then having the responsibility of the home
and earning a living on top of that.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                                    Women may be more cautious
because of their weaker physical constitution and their propensity to be
protective of their children.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                                    Deference may be
easier for a woman than a man since women tend to be more narrowly focused than
men.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                                    Men tend to get
their sense of identity from work (goal orientation) while women tend to get
their sense of identity from relationships. This tendency may have implications
for leadership emphasis and nurturing emphasis.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>                        c.   Personality correlates
with sexual pattern (restraint and response), which epitomizes marriage.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

style='font-size:12.0pt'>                              The psychology of that
distinctive in married life is crucial enough that it can permeate the
operation of the home, hence, the church.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:

style='font-size:12.0pt;color:windowtext'>affectively </span><span
