<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;line-height:
130%'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:130%'>            How much of our

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;line-height:

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;line-height:
130%'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:130%'>      <u>Importance of
meaningfulness  </u></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;line-height:
130%'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:130%'>            Results of
meaninglessness are boredom, pleasure seeking, escape (drugs, suicide, </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;line-height:
130%'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:130%'>                  <i>etc</i>.),
hedonism, materialism, busyness, struggle for power.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;line-height:
130%'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:130%'>            John <b>10</b>:10b:
Jesus came that people might have “abundant life.”</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;line-height:
130%'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:130%'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;line-height:
130%'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:130%'>      <u>Definition of
meaningfulness </u></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;line-height:
130%'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:130%'>            The term <i>life</i>
does not mean “existence,” but “meaningful existence.” It has to do with
quality, not quantity. It includes a state of perceived “blessedness.”
Meaningfulness comes from being part of a guaranteed purpose that is bigger
than us.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;line-height:
130%'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:130%'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;line-height:
130%'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:130%'>      <u>Basis of
meaningfulness  </u></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;line-height:
130%'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:130%'>            Meaningfulness
is based on persons acting together toward a common goal. Meaning does not come
from individuals in competition with each other, but from <u>belonging</u> to
the group (existential) and <u>contributing</u> toward its needs and purposes
(eschatological). It is <u>love</u>, not competition. Only what is omnipotent
can determine its own meaning.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;line-height:
130%'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:130%'>            Meaningfulness
in mankind comes from nature (image of God), relationship (love of God and
man), and purpose (to the praise of the glory of his grace; God’s delight).</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-27.0pt;margin-bottom:
0in;margin-left:.25in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;line-height:
130%'><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:130%'>&nbsp;</span></p>
