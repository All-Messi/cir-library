style='font-size:12.0pt'>            The following analysis of a Christian’s
growth process produces a schematic based on the inner-outer differential. By <u>inner</u>
we mean the understanding, emotions, and motives we have; <u>outer</u> means
the actions we do. Each group of scriptures given below affirms something about
the inner, the outer, or their relationship. Determine what that fact is and
why it is important. Then synthesize all the parts into a working model of the
growth process with observations on its implications for witnessing and
applications to Christian education and defense of the faith.</span></p>
