Christian freedom are diagrammed here in three sets of logical relationships: (1)
<u>subjective</u> refers to our feelings about the matter; <u>objective</u>
refers to what is there regardless of our feelings about it. (2) Plus (+) and
minus (-) mean the presence or absence of the category at the left for those
who are in Christ. (3)<u> Jurisdiction of law</u> is divine regulation of human
behavior. <u>Penalty of broken law</u> is the guilt consequence we have for
breaking God’s law. <u>Reality</u> means the barriers, boundaries,
limitations—personal and external—in the reality where we live and what we are
a part of.</span></p>
