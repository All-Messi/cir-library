margin-left:58.5pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:-22.5pt'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>I. A study of assurance requires an understanding of
the relationship between four sets of variables:</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-.5in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:81.0pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:-45.0pt'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-.5in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:81.0pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:-45.0pt'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>        (1)   Objective-subjective evidence. <u>Objective</u>
refers to the real basis upon which our assurance rests; <u>subjective</u>
refers to whether we feel assured of salvation.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-.5in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:81.0pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:-45.0pt'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>        (2)   Primary-confirmatory evidence. <u>Primary</u>
evidence fits only with one conclusion; <u>confirmatory</u> evidence fits with
the correct conclusion, but it might fit with other possibilities as well.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-.5in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:81.0pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:-45.0pt'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>        (3)   Absolute-relative. <u>Absolute</u>
certainty means there is no possibility of doubt; <u>relative</u> certainty is
the kind that is sufficient for practical purposes.</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-.5in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:81.0pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify;text-indent:-45.0pt'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>        (4)   Now-later. Our assurance about our
present state of salvation and our present assurance about our salvation later
in life</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:0in;margin-right:-.5in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:.5in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-align:justify'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt'>&nbsp;</span></p>
