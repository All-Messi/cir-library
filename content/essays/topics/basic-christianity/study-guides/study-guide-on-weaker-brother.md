style='font-size:12.0pt'>        Relating to our brothers and sisters in Christ
would be easier if we were all at the same stage of growth in understanding and
virtue and if we all viewed things the same way. As a matter of fact, we are
not all equally strong and do not see things alike. We must learn to make
allowance for differences i to keep the unity of the faith in the bond of peace
and to avoid stumbling and discouragement.</span></p>
